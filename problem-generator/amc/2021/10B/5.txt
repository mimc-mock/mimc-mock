<p>The ages of Jonie's four cousins are distinct single-digit positive integers. Two of the cousins' ages multiplied together give <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/9/e290f2cba0ba0e11c0b91630da15a408da2fcf1c.png" class="latex" alt="$24$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="12" />, while the other two multiply to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/1/651ba418a810d971dbc8a326d49a71fe1541fae0.png" class="latex" alt="$30$" width="17" height="12" />. What is the sum of the ages of Jonie's four cousins?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/b/6/ab6639febe524efcafd620bb7d3423c7bf67019a.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)} ~21 \qquad\textbf{(B)} ~22 \qquad\textbf{(C)} ~23 \qquad\textbf{(D)} ~24 \qquad\textbf{(E)} ~25$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>First look at the two cousins' ages that multiply to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/9/e290f2cba0ba0e11c0b91630da15a408da2fcf1c.png" class="latex" alt="$24$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="12" />. Since the ages must be single-digit, the ages must either be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/3/b739da9e6a60394e30101ea97328f450bc4a3e97.png" class="latex" alt="$3 \text{ and } 8$" width="55" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/1/d11c43c6f280fd3331f7a164d2bbe36a1a0c91a9.png" class="latex" alt="$4 \text{ and } 6.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="59" height="13" />
</p><p>Next, look at the two cousins' ages that multiply to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/1/651ba418a810d971dbc8a326d49a71fe1541fae0.png" class="latex" alt="$30$" width="17" height="12" />. Since the ages must be single-digit, the only ages that work are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/9/d/69dcd8ed23eec9db6222bdd404b2725fe7061bcd.png" class="latex" alt="$5 \text{ and } 6.$" width="59" height="12" /> Remembering that all the ages must all be distinct, the only solution that works is when the ages are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/d/f/8dfef01e13a10c05c574df88837784074e43d775.png" class="latex" alt="$3, 8$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="25" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/d/b/4db002ceacafc85dbc305a626c9260988817cdb4.png" class="latex" alt="$5, 6$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="25" height="16" />.
</p><p>We are required to find the sum of the ages, which is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/8/a/08a7d7abac9e4a0a4564b8008acc3e1aded4f510.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[3 + 8 + 5 + 6 = \boxed{\textbf{(B)} ~22}.\]" width="194" height="28" />
</p><p>-<a href="/wiki/index.php/User:PureSwag" title="User:PureSwag">PureSwag</a>
</p>
