<p>How many integer values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> satisfy <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/3/d/23d2e52b15a018d3bc52ad07b27d1e263429003d.png" class="latex" alt="$|x|&lt;3\pi$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="64" height="18" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/2/35211975309cd6693960267faef3e7d1e4ae7b02.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)} ~9 \qquad\textbf{(B)} ~10 \qquad\textbf{(C)} ~18 \qquad\textbf{(D)} ~19 \qquad\textbf{(E)} ~20$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="385" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/0/8e061d35afcd7937b7ee34765c01f166217bef83.png" class="latex" alt="$3\pi\approx9.42$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="79" height="14" />, we multiply <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> for the integers from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> and the integers from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/e/bae5aba07d37ff6ff813107e76260fb31ad5794e.png" class="latex" alt="$-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="22" height="12" /> to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/4/8/a483ee25b066703890405f0127fef9c31572fb81.png" class="latex" alt="$-9$" width="22" height="12" /> and add <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> to account for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" /> to get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/6/1/861c03cf6935c7b1e8407ffd155ae0adb0a1d8b9.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D)} ~19}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="65" height="28" />.
</p><p>~smarty101 and edited by Tony_Li2007 and sl_hc

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/3/d/23d2e52b15a018d3bc52ad07b27d1e263429003d.png" class="latex" alt="$|x|&lt;3\pi$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="64" height="18" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/b/7/4b7822aebffad0fc070fac8929eb45da399e369f.png" class="latex" alt="$\iff$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="37" height="10" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/a/a/2aa10c7e5a9aea31792d5e989114807588693c0e.png" class="latex" alt="$-3\pi&lt;x&lt;3\pi$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="112" height="13" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/2/c/f2ca003a7da0de4994b4733e203b74ff52d42553.png" class="latex" alt="$\pi$" width="10" height="8" /> is approximately <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/b/9/1b96e281382af234b80bf4333d96fe86bd3bdaf9.png" class="latex" alt="$3.14$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="32" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/d/f5d4a39c601726949a5cfde4a9bbd8d30a64fb56.png" class="latex" alt="$3\pi$" width="19" height="12" /> is approximately <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/9/119d44ee0c69bf7e5ec27134d9c942bfb181298a.png" class="latex" alt="$9.42$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="31" height="13" />. We are trying to solve for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/b/96b03ab08c0e5eebded485889b9570b90b95780c.png" class="latex" alt="$-9.42&lt;x&lt;9.42$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="137" height="13" />, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/4/e/14e1fc2610d0ab9747b552684df1356df224e62a.png" class="latex" alt="$x\in\mathbb{Z}$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="44" height="13" />. Hence, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/b/96b03ab08c0e5eebded485889b9570b90b95780c.png" class="latex" alt="$-9.42&lt;x&lt;9.42$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="137" height="13" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/f/4/ef48ac956824fcaea163a530a717e6df694aa006.png" class="latex" alt="$\implies$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="33" height="10" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/a/1/ea1231634d16d089bc562963f732dac67fe6a36c.png" class="latex" alt="$-9\leq x\leq9$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="90" height="15" />, for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/4/e/14e1fc2610d0ab9747b552684df1356df224e62a.png" class="latex" alt="$x\in\mathbb{Z}$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="44" height="13" />. The number of integer values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/d/f/0df7a361594dd6cb22938d7a64903c2e1b9c0fbb.png" class="latex" alt="$9-(-9)+1=19$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="141" height="18" />. Therefore, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/6/1/861c03cf6935c7b1e8407ffd155ae0adb0a1d8b9.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D)} ~19}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="65" height="28" />.
</p><p>~ {TSun} ~

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/f/6ef865683595098e666dbb647ac5b423687a61a6.png" class="latex" alt="$3\pi \approx 9.4.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="71" height="13" /> There are two cases here.
</p><p>When <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/8/c888ef4fe1ac7c3b193604cde5135a3eca9393c0.png" class="latex" alt="$x&gt;0, |x|&gt;0,$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="109" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/7/887dbb5f4f2e1b208c0a4eb2899feea2713808a1.png" class="latex" alt="$x = |x|.$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="58" height="18" /> So then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/0/8c00c2ae1d38f39da1967f39ffb11112cd92514a.png" class="latex" alt="$x&lt;9.4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="57" height="13" />
</p><p>When <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/c/8/5c87c23a846ee2b8dcc4a6b0cfe55c462623df00.png" class="latex" alt="$x&lt;0, |x|&gt;0,$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="109" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/0/8/308042f00628d2ceffb1f8d11f035f051b32be2b.png" class="latex" alt="$x = -|x|.$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="72" height="18" /> So then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/b/f/3bf14003a50e66fda402ea988ff9ec577b52f7ec.png" class="latex" alt="$-x&lt;9.4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="72" height="13" />. Dividing by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/e/bae5aba07d37ff6ff813107e76260fb31ad5794e.png" class="latex" alt="$-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="22" height="12" /> and flipping the sign, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/e/d/9edca71ef142917c734ff50272895c549514da51.png" class="latex" alt="$x&gt;-9.4.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="75" height="13" />
</p><p>From case 1 and 2, we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/7/2077cb8c73f7518efc3846743a68a5422ac36e6c.png" class="latex" alt="$-9.4 &lt; x &lt; 9.4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="119" height="13" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is an integer, we must have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/4/8/a483ee25b066703890405f0127fef9c31572fb81.png" class="latex" alt="$-9$" width="22" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />. There are a total of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/6/1c62150a3dcfbbbd3518e32f4a819392326e7eef.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[9-(-9) + 1 = \boxed{\textbf{(D)} ~19} \text{ integers}.\]" width="253" height="28" />
~<a href="/wiki/index.php/User:PureSwag" title="User:PureSwag">PureSwag</a>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_4">Solution 4</span></h2>
<p>Looking at the problem, we see that instead of directly saying <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, we see that it is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/9/2697836c7457680f6639d776d0b2438cdbc33c99.png" class="latex" alt="$|x|.$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="24" height="18" /> That means all the possible values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> in this case are positive and negative. Rounding <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/2/c/f2ca003a7da0de4994b4733e203b74ff52d42553.png" class="latex" alt="$\pi$" width="10" height="8" /> to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/a/bcae34926788f40c2b1fceb43db3b5aeea3d94bf.png" class="latex" alt="$3(3)=9.$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="69" height="18" /> There are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> positive solutions and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> negative solutions: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/3/3/e333e2e0f3ea0fe475c21a43e32e7174b8e7d4cd.png" class="latex" alt="$9+9=18.$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="86" height="14" /> But what about zero? Even though zero is neither negative nor positive, but we still need to add it into the solution. Hence, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/a/e1aca8973f0c13090d5a07936f3b1f2d6e42cd7c.png" class="latex" alt="$9+9+1=18+1=\boxed{\textbf{(D)} ~19}.$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="241" height="30" />
</p><p>~DuoDuoling0

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_5">Solution 5</span></h2>
<p>There are an odd number of integer solutions <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> to this inequality since if any non-zero integer <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> satisfies this inequality, then so does <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/f/0ff0f5de19e70a0c97c8330b86e0289e27bfd025.png" class="latex" alt="$-x,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="28" height="11" /> and we must also account for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/d/ced6b9b2e75857b566f1e516560c4a4d63977299.png" class="latex" alt="$0,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="16" /> which gives us the desired. Then, the answer is either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/4/6/a46f6af01fcf3bb5f6cac2316bc4de9bb3906bec.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="28" height="17" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/6/2d6d83615701ee2c7146b42a9ddaec6d734aca3b.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(D)},$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="33" height="17" /> and since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/2/a/42a1f7b59384fdd70994c82a3bde5ffab68e1f1f.png" class="latex" alt="$3 \pi &gt; 3 \cdot 3 &gt; 9,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="113" height="16" /> the answer is at least <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/3/a/63ac7e4fceeb0b51b095db32bd166fbf242c08ee.png" class="latex" alt="$9 \cdot 2 + 1 = 19,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="109" height="16" /> yielding <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/8/578f2e9e8a0f7ac924d5489e5b659346590e5c0d.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D)} ~19}.$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="68" height="28" />
