<p>What is the value of<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/a/9/8a93eb4b50cbbb0b051fa1401e2e58d5ab02d33e.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{\log_2 80}{\log_{40}2}-\frac{\log_2 160}{\log_{20}2}?\]" width="149" height="42" /><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601678842e7e593b100fa0ddeb7bb056fe626233.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }0 \qquad \textbf{(B) }1 \qquad \textbf{(C) }\frac54 \qquad \textbf{(D) }2 \qquad \textbf{(E) }\log_2 5$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="394" height="38" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/c/a/ecaa739cc3d887ba1766dc6a6e1c4f7ff7eba881.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{\log_{2}{80}}{\log_{40}{2}}-\frac{\log_{2}{160}}{\log_{20}{2}}\]" width="140" height="42" />
</p><p>Note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/0/b/f0b7591b43bf77f6425bd79a2e1908b97c5991af.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{40}{2}=\frac{1}{\log_{2}{40}}$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="128" height="41" />, and similarly <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/f/cbfe5c3db5e5b532c30eeae811fc1c4c98fbf45b.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{20}{2}=\frac{1}{\log_{2}{20}}$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="128" height="41" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/a/8eae9612f64d6e8ddf97686a8e74c8309b6d6ef7.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[= \log_{2}{80}\cdot \log_{2}{40}-\log_{2}{160}\cdot \log_{2}{20}\]" width="283" height="17" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/3/6/736771a0eba460514a82ed669ee1b1d9855e7047.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[=(\log_{2}{4}+\log_{2}{20})(\log_{2}{2}+\log_{2}{20})-(\log_{2}{8}+\log_{2}{20})\log_{2}{20}\]" width="478" height="18" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/b/2/4b21abc78fc7647612ff5c9ecfd62e29c9773171.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[=(2+\log_{2}{20})(1+\log_{2}{20})-(3+\log_{2}{20})\log_{2}{20}\]" width="388" height="18" />
</p><p>Expanding, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/2/be239702516f13b4dd8831ce1f08e672926ba2ca.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[2+2\log_{2}{20}+\log_{2}{20}+(\log_{2}{20})^2-3\log_{2}{20}-(\log_{2}{20})^2\]" width="446" height="19" />
</p><p>All the log terms cancel, so the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/3/0/e3052f8450ae02ed5d0a6413ef6d3618a4fa154e.png" class="latex" alt="$2\implies\boxed{\text{(D)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="96" height="28" />.
</p><p>~ SoySoy4444
</p>
