<p>A sequence of numbers is defined by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/8/7/387579bb5f6b91cdb56f311531458bec3e937f3d.png" class="latex" alt="$D_0=0,D_1=0,D_2=1$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="182" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/8/b88d64e47b0213d4dd7d804fa2cf72ee50a59b22.png" class="latex" alt="$D_n=D_{n-1}+D_{n-3}$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="151" height="15" /> for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/b/e/6be91d8532fe2a50e227f2fec7c3cecdd8758df3.png" class="latex" alt="$n\ge 3$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="43" height="15" />. What are the parities (evenness or oddness) of the triple of numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/6/5460a056945c4e59f49df293ed03f9cfc423425f.png" class="latex" alt="$(D_{2021},D_{2022},D_{2023})$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="156" height="18" />, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/a/2/fa2fa899f0afb05d6837885523503a2d4df434f9.png" class="latex" alt="$E$" width="14" height="12" /> denotes even and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/d/51da37d984564162c87710ca27bea422f657fb73.png" class="latex" alt="$O$" width="13" height="12" /> denotes odd?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/d/6/9d6285992c2b7388a5448300fcd21e8809999379.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }(O,E,O) \qquad \textbf{(B) }(E,E,O) \qquad \textbf{(C) }(E,O,E) \qquad \textbf{(D) }(O,O,E) \qquad \textbf{(E) }(O,O,O)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="674" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>We construct the following table:
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/6/c66194484f13cb67490c65bf30cd239601814333.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\begin{array}{c||c|c|c|c|c|c|c|c|c|c|c}    &amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp; \\ [-2.5ex] \textbf{Term} &amp;\boldsymbol{D_0}&amp;\boldsymbol{D_1}&amp;\boldsymbol{D_2}&amp;\boldsymbol{D_3}&amp;\boldsymbol{D_4}&amp;\boldsymbol{D_5}&amp;\boldsymbol{D_6}&amp;\boldsymbol{D_7}&amp;\boldsymbol{D_8}&amp;\boldsymbol{D_9}&amp;\boldsymbol{\cdots} \\  \hline \hline &amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp; \\ [-2.25ex] \textbf{Value} &amp; 0&amp;0&amp;1&amp;1&amp;1&amp;2&amp;3&amp;4&amp;6&amp;9&amp;\cdots \\ \hline   &amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp;&amp; \\ [-2.25ex] \textbf{Parity} &amp; E&amp;E&amp;O&amp;O&amp;O&amp;E&amp;O&amp;E&amp;E&amp;O&amp;\cdots \end{array}\]" width="536" height="74" />
Note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/8/b/e8bad1ac53393a3dd4fb0031f0ecf00df7979318.png" class="latex" alt="$(D_7,D_8,D_9)$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="100" height="20" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/b/26b4cb70d1e8884c792f578bb2f66e67014287c8.png" class="latex" alt="$(D_0,D_1,D_2)$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="100" height="20" /> have the same parities, so the parity is periodic with period <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/7/b17ce6fcb2994d4241d780dd5311d828db133411.png" class="latex" alt="$7.$" width="12" height="12" /> Since the remainders of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/d/cbd084c1a25cd3a64cc3a696316f62cafcc2bfe6.png" class="latex" alt="$(2021\div7,2022\div7,2023\div7)$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="235" height="20" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/d/bede160b1e42a1c83a44358d56a1f3115230d5cb.png" class="latex" alt="$(5,6,7),$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="64" height="20" /> we conclude that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/6/5460a056945c4e59f49df293ed03f9cfc423425f.png" class="latex" alt="$(D_{2021},D_{2022},D_{2023})$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="156" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/5/9/25973bf36fb45c7a8f7ab4fa2841b830b5c50ed3.png" class="latex" alt="$(D_5,D_6,D_7)$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="100" height="20" /> have the same parities, namely <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/d/a/4dae2e5a2515ce5d70ef62481d814b7a8f16d44c.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) }(E,O,E)}.$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="127" height="32" />
</p><p>~JHawk0224 ~MRENTHUSIASM
</p>
