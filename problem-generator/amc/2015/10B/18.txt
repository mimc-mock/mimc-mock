<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>We can simplify the problem first, then apply reasoning to the original problem. Let's say that there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> coins. Shaded coins flip heads, and blank coins flip tails. So, after the first flip;
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/d/a/3dac3cb762d73e6a8d3b8ec7c535a5096bb79831.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] filldraw(circle((-5,0),0.35),white); filldraw(circle((-4,0),0.35),white); filldraw(circle((-3,0),0.35),white); filldraw(circle((-2,0),0.35),white); filldraw(circle((-1,0),0.35),black); filldraw(circle((-0,0),0.35),black); filldraw(circle((1,0),0.35),black); filldraw(circle((2,0),0.35),black); [/asy]" width="252" height="25" />
</p><p>Then, after the second (new heads in blue);
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/8/8/688ee107045ce8085b5b481a7c8405dea7edbd7c.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] filldraw(circle((-5,0),0.35),white); filldraw(circle((-4,0),0.35),white); filldraw(circle((-3,0),0.35),blue); filldraw(circle((-2,0),0.35),blue); filldraw(circle((-1,0),0.35),black); filldraw(circle((-0,0),0.35),black); filldraw(circle((1,0),0.35),black); filldraw(circle((2,0),0.35),black); [/asy]" width="252" height="25" />
</p><p>And after the third (new head in green);
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/9/1/49134c0babdbc3bc848608ad22e9888c3d851db6.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] filldraw(circle((-5,0),0.35),white); filldraw(circle((-4,0),0.35),green); filldraw(circle((-3,0),0.35),blue); filldraw(circle((-2,0),0.35),blue); filldraw(circle((-1,0),0.35),black); filldraw(circle((-0,0),0.35),black); filldraw(circle((1,0),0.35),black); filldraw(circle((2,0),0.35),black); [/asy]" width="252" height="25" />
</p><p>So in total, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/a/e0a0db32027a732ac57d37ef2ae9bb150f65b108.png" class="latex" alt="$7$" width="9" height="12" /> of the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> coins resulted in heads. Now we have the ratio of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/1/7/717366d5cd3f9573014d073ca33467c37c12a364.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{7}{8}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="11" height="37" /> of the total coins will end up heads. Therefore, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/2/8/128085102a982ed75c2d4968eddf4b0de1c427b6.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{7}{8}\cdot64=\boxed{\mathbf{(D)}\ 56}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="134" height="37" />
