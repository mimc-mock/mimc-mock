<p>Ann made a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />-step staircase using <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/e/7/5e7a1b6098a8c98b8e4adae526aeef4b91712620.png" class="latex" alt="$18$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> toothpicks as shown in the figure. How many toothpicks does she need to add to complete a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />-step staircase?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/6/a/36a31b40816a756ca9badfb17bc6c75eb2015c6d.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 9\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 18\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 20\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 22\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 24$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="385" height="17" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/4/d/64dae9d5742d72398d8f4ea4711a8da0029f8bac.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] size(150); defaultpen(linewidth(0.8)); path h = ellipse((0.5,0),0.45,0.015), v = ellipse((0,0.5),0.015,0.45); for(int i=0;i&lt;=2;i=i+1) { for(int j=0;j&lt;=3-i;j=j+1) { filldraw(shift((i,j))*h,black); filldraw(shift((j,i))*v,black); } }[/asy]" width="252" height="252" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>We can see that a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />-step staircase requires <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> toothpicks and a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />-step staircase requires <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> toothpicks. Thus, to go from a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />-step to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />-step staircase, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> additional toothpicks are needed and to go from a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />-step to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />-step staircase, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> additional toothpicks are needed. Applying this pattern, to go from a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />-step to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />-step staircase, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> additional toothpicks are needed and to go from a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />-step to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />-step staircase, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> additional toothpicks are needed. Our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/f/adf5621375f8930c1b9f37fb79b46fa600f0ff66.png" class="latex" alt="$10+12=\boxed{\textbf{(D)}\ 22}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="148" height="28" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Alternatively, we can see with the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />-step staircase has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/d/0/6d0c32a93c666d81841954c631c6c973dc70aa3e.png" class="latex" alt="$2[2(3)+2+1]=18$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="156" height="18" /> toothpicks. Generalizing, we see that a staircase with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> steps has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/e/c7e1a24ea54a25898a7986abad7e2ed3da880f1a.png" class="latex" alt="$2[2x+(x-1)+(x-2)+...+1]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="261" height="18" /> toothpicks. So, for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/c/51c80ea5862b45877f08d698a0c893bd2806a2fd.png" class="latex" alt="$x=5$" width="43" height="12" /> steps, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/6/a/a6a7f2e13996699a3c1eb9842b0ffa5ee23c5672.png" class="latex" alt="$2[2(5)+4+3+2+1]=40$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="218" height="18" /> toothpicks. So our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/8/2/582c6346ca0223bad33de4c48860b1b457a71604.png" class="latex" alt="$40-18=22$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="100" height="13" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/f/9ffb448918db29f2a72f8f87f421b3b3cad18f95.png" class="latex" alt="$D$" width="15" height="12" />.
</p>
