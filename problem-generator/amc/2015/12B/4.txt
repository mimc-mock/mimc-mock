<p>Lian, Marzuq, Rafsan, Arabi, Nabeel, and Rahul were in a 12-person race with 6 other people. Nabeel finished 6 places ahead of Marzuq. Arabi finished 1 place behind Rafsan. Lian finished 2 places behind Marzuq. Rafsan finished 2 places behind Rahul. Rahul finished 1 place behind Nabeel. Arabi finished in 6th place. Who finished in 8th place?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/8/0f8a9e10c8fb3b0aa8d8f17671731dcf716c8377.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\; \text{Lian} \qquad\textbf{(B)}\; \text{Marzuq} \qquad\textbf{(C)}\; \text{Rafsan} \qquad\textbf{(D)}\; \text{Nabeel} \qquad\textbf{(E)}\; \text{Rahul}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="531" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/d/add22d9bda21e86390bc74fa4dde17730a442da7.png" class="latex" alt="$-$" style="vertical-align: 4px" width="13" height="1" /> denote any of the 6 racers not named. Then the correct order following all the logic looks like:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/c/3/ec364be22d6d93081d6a952a2a475fea025d3694.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[-, \text{Nabeel}, \text{Rahul}, -, \text{Rafsan}, \text{Arabi}, -, \text{Marzuq}, -, \text{Lian}, -, -\]" width="442" height="15" />
</p><p>Clearly the 8th place runner is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/a/c3a473cba8a93748da2f8fbad5582fda147fa059.png" class="latex" alt="$\fbox{\textbf{(B)}\; \text{Marzuq}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="105" height="28" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>We can list these out vertically to ensure clarity, starting with Marta and working from there.
</p><p><br />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/5/105aa601b74d255fdec68cea63a561c4634d492d.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[1 -\]" width="20" height="12" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/2/f721a5b009d17bdf473b8944f860e5118b860d7c.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[2 Nabeel\]" width="64" height="12" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/c/05c991ab9ec67e5b408ad328f3fb83c40ed8b7d9.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[3 Rahul\]" width="58" height="12" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/d/34dde0e8318825de00c6c696b1c5e715454d4420.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[4 -\]" width="21" height="12" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/b/c/bbc8eb8fb4eca84c52ebaa9998651c2499f9ace9.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[5 Rafsan\]" width="71" height="16" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/1/ce16a03a4e7f138594cb581ee42ba189623aa0c1.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[6 Arabi\]" width="54" height="13" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/4/f/14fa5dbaae18d5eaff20f23b696f5eac2a6254be.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[7 -\]" width="21" height="12" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/e/88eef4f42e9ecb7c6834e8e77e0b7b9613916639.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[8 Marzuq\]" width="75" height="16" />
</p><p>Thus our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/a/c3a473cba8a93748da2f8fbad5582fda147fa059.png" class="latex" alt="$\fbox{\textbf{(B)}\; \text{Marzuq}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="105" height="28" />.
