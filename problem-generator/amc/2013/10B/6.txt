<p>The average age of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/f/b6f3bccae16d5e31266370fced24edc41538d5da.png" class="latex" alt="$33$" width="17" height="12" /> fifth-graders is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/8/c6878713578626763c38433b3f4c8c2205ad0c15.png" class="latex" alt="$11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />. The average age of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/9/b79030abeb688469b5ffc2e1c4e37fee225a529e.png" class="latex" alt="$55$" width="17" height="12" /> of their parents is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/f/b6f3bccae16d5e31266370fced24edc41538d5da.png" class="latex" alt="$33$" width="17" height="12" />. What is the average age of all of these parents and fifth-graders?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/3/ad3e5a1473047ea23f5ee22efe3c215dca74d19a.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 22 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 23.25 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 24.75 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 26.25 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 28$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="463" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The sum of the ages of the fifth graders is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/5/f/a5f218f469819b421a08bd78803c925c67beb862.png" class="latex" alt="$33 * 11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="13" />, while the sum of the ages of the parents is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/f/90f68c7c3cf0745f9a309199c56e4219bba89cfd.png" class="latex" alt="$55 * 33$" width="53" height="12" />. Therefore, the total sum of all their ages must be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/e/d/2edde2ab3c1980467a20a22d1e6f980f91e8d62b.png" class="latex" alt="$2178$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />, and given <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/f/0ff56ff939af69c05034e731a55db507fca772db.png" class="latex" alt="$33 + 55 = 88$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="100" height="14" /> people in total, their average age is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/8/9/589df7163053ab31a550f9a6d57d2cee4d8fba4f.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2178}{88} = \frac{99}{4} = \boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 24.75}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="198" height="38" />.
</p>
