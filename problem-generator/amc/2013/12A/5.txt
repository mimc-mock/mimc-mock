<p>Tom, Dorothy, and Sammy went on a vacation and agreed to split the costs evenly. During their trip Tom paid &#36;<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/1/791f15f656db201fa9116a2f762a4b292fc50883.png" class="latex" alt="$105$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />, Dorothy paid &#36;<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/2/b829a6f0810a25e9ca9f85d690f2bafd0f2c1773.png" class="latex" alt="$125$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />, and Sammy paid &#36;<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/a/a/caa2c5db610c6d7c961d76200b986305e4f13db0.png" class="latex" alt="$175$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />. In order to share the costs equally, Tom gave Sammy <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/d/e0d2bf360290fd61d1c1557e763f2622363b3d35.png" class="latex" alt="$t$" width="6" height="11" /> dollars, and Dorothy gave Sammy <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> dollars. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/3/6/236c8b639a26e618cb78c2befb89f78fc61694cc.png" class="latex" alt="$t-d$" width="38" height="12" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/f/67f6e43038efd8cf47d657684adf291a269b6e9e.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 15\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 20\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 25\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 30\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 35$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Simply write down two algebraic equations. We know that Tom gave <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/d/e0d2bf360290fd61d1c1557e763f2622363b3d35.png" class="latex" alt="$t$" width="6" height="11" /> dollars and Dorothy gave <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> dollars. In addition, Tom originally paid <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/1/791f15f656db201fa9116a2f762a4b292fc50883.png" class="latex" alt="$105$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> dollars and Dorothy paid <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/2/b829a6f0810a25e9ca9f85d690f2bafd0f2c1773.png" class="latex" alt="$125$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> dollars originally. Since they all pay the same amount, we have: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/6/f/a6fe9782e94c6b81531413f701ac7f149a57878e.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[105 + t = 125 + d.\]" width="141" height="14" /> Rearranging, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/b/5/6b5b9202249b931c46ff886e2ea004aded3c478b.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[t-d = \boxed{\textbf{(B)} 20}.\]" width="124" height="28" />
</p><p>Solution <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/1/6c1e8d532dc235b1644948411b4762c042c63c90.png" class="latex" alt="$\textcopyright 2018$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="52" height="16" /> RandomPieKevin. All Rights Reserved. skrrt...
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Add up the amounts that Tom, Dorothy, and Sammy paid to get $<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/d/c/3dc228bd77701d8bed244703342dd3cee93bd56c.png" class="latex" alt="$405$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />, and divide by 3 to get $<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/8/e/d8efc4595ab8d0d7d43d5ec1988b1b7dc8ecc7fc.png" class="latex" alt="$135$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />, the amount that each should have paid.
</p><p>Tom, having paid $<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/1/791f15f656db201fa9116a2f762a4b292fc50883.png" class="latex" alt="$105$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />, owes Sammy $<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/1/651ba418a810d971dbc8a326d49a71fe1541fae0.png" class="latex" alt="$30$" width="17" height="12" />, and Dorothy, having paid $<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/2/b829a6f0810a25e9ca9f85d690f2bafd0f2c1773.png" class="latex" alt="$125$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />, owes Sammy $<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />.
</p><p>Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/e/3/1e351fc81d05c7e3572da1c8542707a7992c7c95.png" class="latex" alt="$t - d = 30 - 10 = 20$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="163" height="13" />, which is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/1/a2194b81820e8380be586c1564c3f5d03d5c100d.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="40" height="28" />
</p>
