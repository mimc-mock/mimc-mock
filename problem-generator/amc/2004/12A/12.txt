<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/d/c8d5794f9399d77b8ab4892ac69bbc45316f0f45.png" class="latex" alt="$A = (0,9)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="76" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/6/8c6020dc377e3989766367a2183f4fa785a2ba3b.png" class="latex" alt="$B = (0,12)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="86" height="18" />. <a href="/wiki/index.php/Point" title="Point">Points</a> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/9/1090530ae883b627e090bfb5e450ef796febca40.png" class="latex" alt="$A&#39;$" width="17" height="14" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/c/7/4c71f19552a05ee5e2b31e94aff3fb986884bf31.png" class="latex" alt="$B&#39;$" width="18" height="14" /> are on the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Line" title="Line">line</a> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/4/0/a40fee4603b4536f856dde720ee499468ace443f.png" class="latex" alt="$y = x$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="44" height="11" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/e/6cee29e8c46b0074ecb11ec9eed51e26715a850c.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{AA&#39;}$" width="32" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/e/0/2e0e06feaa3c61ff1386e726362f646529472c84.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{BB&#39;}$" width="34" height="15" /> intersect at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/5/f85d9313599e5037ba421c042dd287ad3dc89350.png" class="latex" alt="$C = (2,8)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="77" height="18" />. What is the length of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/6/6/a66bcf1b702aa4823d591a3a43757c06b3e45ca2.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{A&#39;B&#39;}$" width="38" height="15" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/2/c/12c90e204599e741692ecd916ba260cfa607f5e3.png" class="latex" alt="$\text {(A)} 2 \qquad \text {(B)} 2\sqrt2 \qquad \text {(C)} 3 \qquad \text {(D)} 2 + \sqrt 2\qquad \text {(E)}3\sqrt 2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="398" height="21" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/index.php/File:2004_AMC_12A-12.png" class="image"><img alt="2004 AMC 12A-12.png" src="https://wiki-images.artofproblemsolving.com//4/43/2004_AMC_12A-12.png" width="210" height="300" /></a>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/index.php/Equation" title="Equation">equation</a> of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/e/6cee29e8c46b0074ecb11ec9eed51e26715a850c.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{AA&#39;}$" width="32" height="15" /> can be found using points <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/a/a/faafcc2f53726bb09ac20d940deb797970210fc7.png" class="latex" alt="$A, C$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="35" height="16" /> to be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/5/10596bd295e55f2f82b9d33edcb8db8723bbad96.png" class="latex" alt="$y - 9 = \left(\frac{9-8}{0-2}\right)(x - 0) \Longrightarrow y = -\frac{1}{2}x + 9$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="336" height="43" />. Similarily, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/e/0/2e0e06feaa3c61ff1386e726362f646529472c84.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{BB&#39;}$" width="34" height="15" /> has the equation <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/0/a/80a4e846d85c98f0d6aa9e2a25430d0b90e0b91f.png" class="latex" alt="$y - 12 = \left(\frac{12-8}{0-2}\right)(x-0) \Longrightarrow y = -2x + 12$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="359" height="43" />. These two equations intersect the line <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/4/1/741a74d86df899c10724dc2f80638f5e7f599194.png" class="latex" alt="$y=x$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="44" height="11" /> at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/7/8e7eedad8b0b501a894378bd3d1fdaf959e4eabf.png" class="latex" alt="$(6,6)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/e/7/9e74a8a996f28bdc445eb3beaf3c3f14594eac27.png" class="latex" alt="$(4,4)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />. Using the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Distance_formula" title="Distance formula">distance formula</a> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/2/2/22231a5cdd4fe05a11f4b577e3b81221f3017a85.png" class="latex" alt="$45-45-90$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="98" height="13" /> <a href="/wiki/index.php/Right_triangle" title="Right triangle">right triangles</a>, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/6/0964a141b4f843a45886830d0d036cef9890ce4c.png" class="latex" alt="$2\sqrt{2}\ \mathrm{(B)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="64" height="21" />. 
</p>
