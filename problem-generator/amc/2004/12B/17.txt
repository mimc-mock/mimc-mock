<p>For some real numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />, the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Equation" title="Equation">equation</a>
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/7/c776fa3cc39e7fb51e32ea87c738b36491d7ed49.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[8x^3 + 4ax^2 + 2bx + a = 0\]" width="199" height="16" />
has three distinct positive roots. If the sum of the base-<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> <a href="/wiki/index.php/Logarithm" title="Logarithm">logarithms</a> of the roots is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />, what is the value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />? 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/8/e28c484d71f1b32f74d35a07a64103fea571117b.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ -256 \qquad\mathrm{(B)}\ -64  \qquad\mathrm{(C)}\ -8 \qquad\mathrm{(D)}\ 64  \qquad\mathrm{(E)}\ 256$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="463" height="18" /> 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Let the three roots be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/0/d500fb815f92dec3dba9e71115d728583cb171d6.png" class="latex" alt="$x_1,x_2,x_3$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="68" height="11" />. 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/e/a/9eaba1d48b6d1b7d491b25a3503629ff84a91bee.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\log_2 x_1 + \log_2 x_2 + \log_2 x_3 = \log_2 x_1x_2x_3= 5 \Longrightarrow x_1x_2x_3 = 32\]" width="479" height="17" />
By <a href="/wiki/index.php/Vieta%E2%80%99s_formulas" class="mw-redirect" title="Vieta’s formulas">Vieta’s formulas</a>,
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/6/b/06b76979c050b8824bb4a74150704bf5dd0867a8.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[8(x-x_1)(x-x_2)(x-x_3) = 8x^3 + 4ax^2 + 2bx + a\]" width="393" height="19" />
gives us that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/9/619713ce9b26e87814b3e3537ea60625142657d4.png" class="latex" alt="$a = -8x_1x_2x_3 = -256 \Rightarrow \mathrm{(A)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="230" height="18" />.
</p>
