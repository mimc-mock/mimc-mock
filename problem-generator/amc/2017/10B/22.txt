<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Notice that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/0/4/70422100db8de33f8e5e0ab5062c5bb6a5ac969d.png" class="latex" alt="$ADE$" width="43" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/a/e2a559986ed5a0ffc5654bd367c29dfc92913c36.png" class="latex" alt="$ABC$" width="42" height="13" /> are right triangles. Then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/1/f/f1fcef95953a7e4b0d76c852aa286c915c29869c.png" class="latex" alt="$AE = \sqrt{7^2+5^2} = \sqrt{74}$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="180" height="19" />. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/a/6/1a618782f89163afb80f42c69db2a240c9be70f9.png" class="latex" alt="$\sin{DAE} = \frac{5}{\sqrt{74}} = \sin{BAE} = \sin{BAC} = \frac{BC}{4}$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="370" height="42" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/f/c/cfc3ef6be3f2d01defa5c4b71380407ecfcb4206.png" class="latex" alt="$BC = \frac{20}{\sqrt{74}}$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="89" height="42" />. We also find that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/9/5/495ccf41bb0c350e534652f3d30e5d0aa39f6542.png" class="latex" alt="$AC = \frac{28}{\sqrt{74}}$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="88" height="42" /> (You can also use power of point ~MATHWIZARD2010), and thus the area of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/a/e2a559986ed5a0ffc5654bd367c29dfc92913c36.png" class="latex" alt="$ABC$" width="42" height="13" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/9/7/a971a06e5e15bd9ce2b9a72d7d63a061a5cc3aff.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{\frac{20}{\sqrt{74}}\cdot\frac{28}{\sqrt{74}}}{2} = \frac{\frac{560}{74}}{2} = \boxed{\textbf{(D) } \frac{140}{37}}$" style="vertical-align: -18px" width="228" height="48" />.
