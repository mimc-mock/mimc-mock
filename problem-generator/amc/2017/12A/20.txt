<p>How many ordered pairs <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/8/8e8da0aef2d19017da7471378386d61620f288f5.png" class="latex" alt="$(a,b)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" /> such that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> is a positive real number and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" /> is an integer between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/f/3/8f37626d49b4082c9102bf930bca51277ff2eb43.png" class="latex" alt="$200$" width="26" height="12" />, inclusive, satisfy the equation <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/a/41a72b6421a07fbc143fd3395e4d4f127479b174.png" class="latex" alt="$(\log_b a)^{2017}=\log_b(a^{2017})?$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="197" height="19" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/0/84094ce7e7cfe509ed4b01b908816b6da2949e57.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 198\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 199\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 398\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 399\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 597$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="440" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>By the properties of logarithms, we can rearrange the equation to read <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/f/61f7420950be368537019af630a6a5211fbe675f.png" class="latex" alt="$x^{2017}=2017x$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="108" height="15" /> with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/0/d/f0d8cd5d80b686350cb965284eda805574719983.png" class="latex" alt="$x=\log_b a$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="77" height="17" />. If <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/4/7/7470aeade5f47b768b503c1547f7e7cbf4540361.png" class="latex" alt="$x\neq 0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="43" height="17" />, we may divide by it and get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/a/b/8abffee04a7f764b732d794187e96e84dee13309.png" class="latex" alt="$x^{2016}=2017$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="98" height="15" />, which implies <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/e/6/2e6b62c88bfce6097063806b817127b088043249.png" class="latex" alt="$x=\pm \root{2016}\of{2017}$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="118" height="18" />. Hence, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> possible values <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, namely 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/a/4/4a4b102daf67f32fa414ab464cf8c24c8b5102a7.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[x=0,\qquad x=2017^{\frac1{2016}},\, \text{and}\quad  x=-2017^{\frac1{2016}}.\]" width="353" height="21" />
</p><p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/7/e/a7e1caeb47b395be37894dadc747fdd04e00c16d.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_b a=x$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="77" height="17" /> is equivalent to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/0/f40914c53c366d8c20ec5c64a4340a5ed3243df5.png" class="latex" alt="$a=b^x$" width="49" height="12" />, each possible value <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> yields exactly <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/3/0f31ff033089cb9cfa5a6ce6cd9a0ce6b5c57422.png" class="latex" alt="$199$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> solutions <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/3/963a27195c32f1708f62bb2c0e82add2d45486dd.png" class="latex" alt="$(b,a)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />, as we can assign <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/0/f40914c53c366d8c20ec5c64a4340a5ed3243df5.png" class="latex" alt="$a=b^x$" width="49" height="12" /> to each <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/1/3/713da23ee5c6dfc40cbda4683597fa3b499b51fa.png" class="latex" alt="$b=2,3,\dots,200$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="125" height="16" />. In total, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/7/e2771d3bf37a7b22fdd05c29c38d167ea0a1420c.png" class="latex" alt="$3\cdot 199=\boxed{\textbf{(E) } 597}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="146" height="28" /> solutions.
</p>
