<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_5">Solution 5</span></h2>
<p>A faster ending to Solution 1 is as follows.
We shall solve for only <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" />. Then,
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/6/e/56e818895e4bcfb7877937b864be03ee2ae093ef.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} f(1)&amp;=(1-r)(1^3+a\cdot1^2+1+10)\\ &amp;=(91)(-77)\\ &amp;=\boxed{\bold{(C)}\, -7007}.\\ \end{align*}" width="274" height="82" />
</p>
