<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/6/0965bf432b818b46740e3cd4d042899628c7ad0e.png" class="latex" alt="$S(n)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="35" height="18" /> equal the sum of the digits of positive integer <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" />. For example, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/2/4/02474038a7a0bace6997fbad80cbade79e0557a4.png" class="latex" alt="$S(1507) = 13$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="104" height="18" />. For a particular positive integer <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/2/26204ebf156e73a07fea0cb94965810f874e0f3d.png" class="latex" alt="$S(n) = 1274$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="97" height="18" />. Which of the following could be the value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/9/6/e964a2172d005c1230f8830bea0e78dbb447b779.png" class="latex" alt="$S(n+1)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="67" height="18" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/6/1/d610f8a4da3999d9e8b039e9b74e99b85bed9d08.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 1 \qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 3\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 12\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 1239\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 1265$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="412" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/4/1/941113d3e86c6cf28c06c6bd8dc63e9ec3fb063a.png" class="latex" alt="$n\equiv S(n)\bmod 9$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="126" height="18" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/b/c/4bce7d275958334d23d6d55833f5a1ea21712da1.png" class="latex" alt="$S(n+1)-S(n)\equiv n+1-n = 1\bmod 9$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="314" height="18" />. So, since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/e/54ec6ede978bfcb59805acc64ef08f8a593fe8bb.png" class="latex" alt="$S(n)=1274\equiv 5\bmod 9$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="184" height="18" />, we have that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/a/09af86c88f8b613ffd8a9455d4c5b4279f340035.png" class="latex" alt="$S(n+1)\equiv 6\bmod 9$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="155" height="18" />. The only one of the answer choices <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/6/e0669ab1ac6f384dea96899df7f3004300771ce6.png" class="latex" alt="$\equiv 6\bmod 9$" width="81" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/7/83703e2249d1d655eab27dc4e819f2fbc76c9f3e.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{(D)=\ 1239}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="107" height="29" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>One possible value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/6/0965bf432b818b46740e3cd4d042899628c7ad0e.png" class="latex" alt="$S(n)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="35" height="18" /> would be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/2/7/c273834136229c984b3fb5c1b0df6447b7cb84d0.png" class="latex" alt="$1275$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />, but this is not any of the choices. Therefore, we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> ends in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />, and after adding <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />, the last digit <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> carries over, turning the last digit into <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" />. If the next digit is also a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />, this process repeats until we get to a non-<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> digit. By the end, the sum of digits would decrease by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> multiplied by the number of carry-overs but increase by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> as a result of the final carrying over. Therefore, the result must be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/5/f95f32a6f63bcf721910e22fc1fc2c6261470502.png" class="latex" alt="$9x-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="49" height="13" /> less than original value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/6/0965bf432b818b46740e3cd4d042899628c7ad0e.png" class="latex" alt="$S(n)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="35" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/5/e/c5e64ba09d81cfe56e269ab704e517be6f941bae.png" class="latex" alt="$1274$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="36" height="12" />, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is a positive integer. The only choice that satisfies this condition is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/d/4/9d4832a1054d148cdc5ef8b0c7bebd4947db8c25.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{1239}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="48" height="23" />, since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/b/8/6b81de5dbb8f6697933fb112a19fe38382eb8cca.png" class="latex" alt="$(1274-1239+1) \bmod 9 = 0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="227" height="18" />. The answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/5/b/55bdade53f471aab49f63d1a734979cc0c636640.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{D}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="27" height="24" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>Another way to solve this is to realize that if you continuously add the digits of the number <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/b/ffb59f877fef713561086df83d4127d9c9f91b58.png" class="latex" alt="$1274 (1 + 2 + 7 + 4 = 14, 1 + 4 = 5)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="276" height="18" />, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />. Adding one to that, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" />. So, if we assess each option to see which one attains <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" />, we would discover that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/9/179c1a815c7919a1c68e9d2f92e619ea9c416080.png" class="latex" alt="$1239$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" /> satisfies the requirement, because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/0/8804385919d9bcff3583ed39f7e06847c99d53f7.png" class="latex" alt="$1 + 2 + 3 + 9 = 15$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="145" height="14" />. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/a/a/eaa37f6d11d98366a5acf61364c2ce485c52b7de.png" class="latex" alt="$1 + 5 = 6$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="73" height="14" />. The answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/5/b/55bdade53f471aab49f63d1a734979cc0c636640.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{D}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="27" height="24" />.
</p>
