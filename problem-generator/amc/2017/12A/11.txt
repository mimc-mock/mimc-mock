<p>Claire adds the degree measures of the interior angles of a convex polygon and arrives at a sum of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/6/1/5617f6d8def6056c5921630439d64b56cfa519ab.png" class="latex" alt="$2017$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="36" height="13" />. She then discovers that she forgot to include one angle. What is the degree measure of the forgotten angle?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/6/0/4602ac77968a2052245a6a2d8209741c16b202d5.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 37\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 63\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 117\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 143\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 163$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="421" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>We know that the sum of the interior angles of the polygon is a multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/c/b/5cb1b41850ab676e4d1cf15c69b1609af4befbc5.png" class="latex" alt="$180$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />. Note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/2/2f221761edb8401bbc37a552144212f5cb670485.png" class="latex" alt="$\left\lceil\frac{2017}{180}\right\rceil = 12$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="103" height="43" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/4/1/0414f4f3151ba9c0d1bf976fa139f347e7580474.png" class="latex" alt="$180\cdot 12 = 2160$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="118" height="13" />, so the angle Claire forgot is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/2/a/72a1a471bf7a6105363740687d757aaf47511816.png" class="latex" alt="$\equiv 2160-2017=143\mod 180$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="245" height="13" />. Since the polygon is convex, the angle is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f3740c0fa809d82273bccfa801d1ab2cb35f7e2f.png" class="latex" alt="$\leq 180$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="45" height="15" />, so the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/e/b5e740baa06be8a53ea4c5fc54c12b39a4238eee.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{(D)\ =\ 143}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="104" height="29" />.
</p>
