<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>We must first get an idea of what <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/6/c96dd6ec1dc4ad7520fbdc78fcdbec9edd068d0c.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="34" height="18" /> looks like:
</p><p>Between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/6/c96dd6ec1dc4ad7520fbdc78fcdbec9edd068d0c.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="34" height="18" /> starts at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> and increases; clearly there is no zero here.
</p><p>Between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/e/e/aeebb746f8c0d3dac5219c3635617032430afee9.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{\pi}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="13" height="33" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/6/c96dd6ec1dc4ad7520fbdc78fcdbec9edd068d0c.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="34" height="18" /> starts at a positive number and increases to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/7/b671f1bb7e4ee86584347d5d22f1dc8abdb5bef2.png" class="latex" alt="$\infty$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="9" />; there is no zero here either.
</p><p>Between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/e/e/aeebb746f8c0d3dac5219c3635617032430afee9.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{\pi}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="13" height="33" /> and 3, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/6/c96dd6ec1dc4ad7520fbdc78fcdbec9edd068d0c.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="34" height="18" /> starts at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/f/a/afac538740054d4786c170ef0123850adea5cdf9.png" class="latex" alt="$-\infty$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="31" height="9" /> and increases to some negative number; there is no zero here either.
</p><p>Between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/2/c/f2ca003a7da0de4994b4733e203b74ff52d42553.png" class="latex" alt="$\pi$" width="10" height="8" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/6/c96dd6ec1dc4ad7520fbdc78fcdbec9edd068d0c.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="34" height="18" /> starts at some negative number and increases to -2; there is no zero here either.
</p><p>Between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/2/c/f2ca003a7da0de4994b4733e203b74ff52d42553.png" class="latex" alt="$\pi$" width="10" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/9/d/49dcbd25f27327131b19f2bc28921237c77a34d3.png" class="latex" alt="$\pi+\frac{\pi}{4} &lt; 4$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="81" height="33" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/6/c96dd6ec1dc4ad7520fbdc78fcdbec9edd068d0c.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="34" height="18" /> starts at -2 and increases to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/3/c/23c1dd3ab0577ebedfb405201f4489deb014c0bd.png" class="latex" alt="$-\frac{\sqrt2}{2} + 2\left(-\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\right) + 3\left(1\right)=3\left(1-\frac{\sqrt2}{2}\right)&gt;0$" style="vertical-align: -22px" width="361" height="53" />. There is a zero here by the Intermediate Value Theorem.
