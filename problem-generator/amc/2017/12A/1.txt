<p>Pablo buys popsicles for his friends. The store sells single popsicles for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/3/6739c8a3ef02d0f557bfbe01c9c92e562ff9a7f1.png" class="latex" alt="$$1$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="17" height="14" /> each, 3-popsicle boxes for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/6/ee6e8cd66adab965db3a961cd262a4ba54e9a132.png" class="latex" alt="$$2$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="17" height="14" />, and 5-popsicle boxes for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/1/831d26f39684cd7cfa4ae5f2a0098b432dc8a6ab.png" class="latex" alt="$$3$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="17" height="14" />. What is the greatest number of popsicles that Pablo can buy with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/f/f7f9b76b8f6db99961a78ece506186b6bfb5eb00.png" class="latex" alt="$$8$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="17" height="14" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/7/2/272bf57e5f8300936fc1b5c5435f7285fdfa886f.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 8\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 11\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 12\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 13\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 15$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="385" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>By the greedy algorithm, we can take two 5-popsicle boxes and one 3-popsicle box with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/f/f7f9b76b8f6db99961a78ece506186b6bfb5eb00.png" class="latex" alt="$$8$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="17" height="14" />. To prove that this is optimal, consider an upper bound as follows: at the rate of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/1/831d26f39684cd7cfa4ae5f2a0098b432dc8a6ab.png" class="latex" alt="$$3$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="17" height="14" /> per 5 popsicles, we can get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/b/7/9b7827743bb9f90b1fffbd88a18904b0c979925a.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{40}{3}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="24" height="38" /> popsicles, which is less than 14. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/7/c/37cfb0d12967a18f3020a605b42d458edc87eea8.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{D}}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="26" height="23" />.
</p>
