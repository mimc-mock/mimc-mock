<p>Every week Roger pays for a movie ticket and a soda out of his allowance. Last week, Roger's allowance was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> dollars. The cost of his movie ticket was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/8/1183a12017e48198b3352789cc39b223b17945d6.png" class="latex" alt="$20\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the difference between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> and the cost of his soda, while the cost of his soda was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/b/0fbf33b81900fa9e836a608be7484b4c4d6ea3ec.png" class="latex" alt="$5\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="23" height="14" /> of the difference between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> and the cost of his movie ticket. To the nearest whole percent, what fraction of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> did Roger pay for his movie ticket and soda?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/e/5dea2c7ddd6eb1f8d96d33089ca47eea6366d985.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{\textbf{(A)} \ }9\%\qquad \mathrm{\textbf{(B)} \ } 19\%\qquad \mathrm{\textbf{(C)} \ } 22\%\qquad \mathrm{\textbf{(D)} \ } 23\%\qquad \mathrm{\textbf{(E)} \ }25\%$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="460" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/0/f5047d1e0cbb50ec208923a22cd517c55100fa7b.png" class="latex" alt="$m$" width="15" height="8" /> = cost of movie ticket<br />
Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f37bba504894945c07a32f5496d74299a37aa51c.png" class="latex" alt="$s$" width="8" height="8" />  = cost of soda
</p><p>We can create two equations:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/2/2/c225bfe998b71a7dfb2713ba8bcda44671f41eb3.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[m = \frac{1}{5}(A - s)\]" width="109" height="37" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/5/d/75d93a0482e185d50835ef040cd4c9f05a47cd87.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[s  = \frac{1}{20}(A - m)\]" width="119" height="37" />
</p><p>Substituting we get: <br />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/3/4/234f13fb1a857e5e12b6b63e8e0fd26606023db0.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[m = \frac{1}{5}(A - \frac{1}{20}(A - m))\]" width="189" height="37" />
which yields:<br />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/f/cbfad79ad2eab599b87d92603ba7d070d7858a3d.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[m = \frac{19}{99}A\]" width="75" height="37" />
</p><p>Now we can find s and we get:<br />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/1/a212bfb55c0fc18e3d650bd713be3bfe86181961.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[s = \frac{4}{99}A\]" width="68" height="37" />
</p><p>Since we want to find what fraction of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> did Roger pay for his movie ticket and soda, we add <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/0/f5047d1e0cbb50ec208923a22cd517c55100fa7b.png" class="latex" alt="$m$" width="15" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f37bba504894945c07a32f5496d74299a37aa51c.png" class="latex" alt="$s$" width="8" height="8" /> to get:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/0/a/c0aef4fb2cf37753e4197badb9f069a4e2fbc5ba.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{19}{99}A + \frac{4}{99}A \implies \boxed{\textbf{(D)}\ 23\%}\]" width="221" height="37" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>We have two equations from the problem:
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/7/b/47bfa39a87c248bac1a1d1b59c2b2e757c8b9c6e.png" class="latex" alt="$5M=A-S$" width="101" height="13" /> and 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/a/0/0a0c583e67f7a1c5610a9beff26cb5df814d67a8.png" class="latex" alt="$20S=A-M$" width="109" height="13" />
If we replace <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> we get a system of equations, and the sum of the values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/1/5d1e4485dc90c450e8c76826516c1b2ccb8fce16.png" class="latex" alt="$M$" width="19" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> is the percentage of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" />.
Solving, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/c/0/0c03e533cce5511813a29479961395e04ad05a5a.png" class="latex" alt="$S=\frac{400}{99}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="66" height="37" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/6/ff6f8f3034e152671736e6a583f0d81e38b4dab6.png" class="latex" alt="$M=\frac{1900}{99}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="82" height="37" />.
Adding, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/f/41fd1d9ca27a3fa46fc99fc898383503fd9c2586.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2300}{99}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="38" height="37" />, which is closest to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/1/9a1b2497e7b125cd721bab4c6e2385f954fe35cb.png" class="latex" alt="$23$" width="17" height="12" /> which is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/d/a/8daf487d58e02b7c0245375d4c9b48dca3a1be99.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D)}\ 23\%}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="81" height="31" />.
</p><p>-Harsha12345
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_4">Solution 4</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/0/f5047d1e0cbb50ec208923a22cd517c55100fa7b.png" class="latex" alt="$m$" width="15" height="8" /> be the price of a movie ticket and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f37bba504894945c07a32f5496d74299a37aa51c.png" class="latex" alt="$s$" width="8" height="8" /> be the price of a soda.
</p><p>Then, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/4/964a45b626532717f5633050f16e733665ec70a4.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[m=\frac{A-s}{5}\]" width="86" height="37" />
and
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/7/6/7763a6ca0c2da3c9425cd4b61bd1d11d0a452a90.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[s=\frac{A-m}{20}\]" width="86" height="37" />
Then, we can turn this into
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/a/d/2ad5b10753ec9316381e0ec7034bc5b90050f5e0.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[5m=A-s\]" width="93" height="13" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/9/7/d97d376c4e735e690fe46042f1d020e647bc8cca.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[20s=A-m\]" width="102" height="13" />
</p><p>Subtracting and getting rid of A, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/5/c3525a8e4d115b3fc5697ef217b3bd5d4ae357f8.png" class="latex" alt="$20s-5m=-m+s \rightarrow 19s=4m$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="265" height="15" />. Assume WLOG that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/8/e18f0adcd75f4954ae25630f7714a0a3d1d2ba92.png" class="latex" alt="$s=4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="42" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/4/f/44f8f8d5e5a6438ff5a29a239933e0b5f433af3a.png" class="latex" alt="$m=19$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="57" height="13" />, thus making a solution for this equation. Substituting this into the 1st equation, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/4/7a45f818c2b0d1748a46888cfb47370480076876.png" class="latex" alt="$A=99$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="58" height="14" />. Hence, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/9/3/393cc7196091fba3ddec5cf9e2f654a73b270f66.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{m+s}{A} = \frac{19+4}{99} \approx \boxed{\textbf{(D)}\ 23\%}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="234" height="38" />
</p><p>~MrThinker
</p>
