<p>Ana and Bonita were born on the same date in different years, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> years apart. Last year Ana was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> times as old as Bonita. This year Ana's age is the square of Bonita's age. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/8/a28578f91569670ce8f5d1c27b4ea637a09a75a9.png" class="latex" alt="$n?$" width="19" height="12" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/3/f/53f76bc53611aeda52dbb0a875264b0760ff8884.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } 3 \qquad\textbf{(B) } 5 \qquad\textbf{(C) } 9 \qquad\textbf{(D) } 12 \qquad\textbf{(E) } 15$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="371" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h3>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> be the age of Ana and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" /> be the age of Bonita. Then,
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/b/eeb3cc039f87fb845971069da564891c3e1d6dc6.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[A-1 = 5(B-1)\]" width="136" height="18" />
and
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/0/f405bc2666154b29ef09e9275cbcd1340f1b0df9.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[A = B^2.\]" width="63" height="15" />
</p><p>Substituting the second equation into the first gives us
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/6/c/66cbc568f3f79ad7a644b1e0924952e50d1316be.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[B^2-1 = 5(B-1).\]" width="149" height="19" />
</p><p>By using difference of squares and dividing, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/f/5/1f5012a6c282123c8eb3aae880eff075846e2451.png" class="latex" alt="$B=4.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="51" height="13" /> Moreover, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/7/1/271902a5cb802a3f16249cae320ad16fead53ca6.png" class="latex" alt="$A=B^2=16.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="106" height="15" />
</p><p>The answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/0/a/b0a7cdf81a838899350f67c2be46eb660ee37c60.png" class="latex" alt="$16-4 = 12 \implies \boxed{\textbf{(D)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="182" height="28" />
</p>
<h3><span id="Solution_2_(Guess_and_Check)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2_.28Guess_and_Check.29">Solution 2 (Guess and Check)</span></h3>
<p>Simple guess and check works. Start with all the square numbers - <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/5/9a5b4928c8fe50ce3c2428da3bee3505e891b788.png" class="latex" alt="$16$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/6/6/66644fd9f411299c3c1eabf151b5f601c9ef6c21.png" class="latex" alt="$25$" width="17" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/5/ce5d3cbb9ab0a992ed503c2d4499e4f4af2f9d65.png" class="latex" alt="$36$" width="17" height="12" />, etc. (probably stop at around <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> since at that point it wouldn't make sense). If Ana is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />, then Bonita is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />, so in the previous year, Ana's age was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> times greater than Bonita's. If Ana is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/5/9a5b4928c8fe50ce3c2428da3bee3505e891b788.png" class="latex" alt="$16$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />, then Bonita is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />, and Ana's age was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> times greater than Bonita's in the previous year, as required. The difference in the ages is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/a/d/5ad77b42eacd29d4d5039f4317834b17c17a6c5b.png" class="latex" alt="$16 - 4 = 12. \boxed{\textbf(D)}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="138" height="29" />
</p>
<h3><span id="Solution_3_(Answer_Choices)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3_.28Answer_Choices.29">Solution 3 (Answer Choices)</span></h3>
<p>The second sentence of the problem says that Ana's age was once <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> times Bonita's age. Therefore, the difference of the ages <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> must be divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/1/cb15a16cca90d201094265ee529e9e2cfb113de8.png" class="latex" alt="$4.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="12" height="12" /> The only answer choice which is divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/2/172e520c9532df8137f87274b99eb7d56d76520c.png" class="latex" alt="$12 \rightarrow \boxed{\textbf(D)}.$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="91" height="29" />
</p><p>- awesome_weisur
</p>
