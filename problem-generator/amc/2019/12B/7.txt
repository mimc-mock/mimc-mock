<p>What is the sum of all real numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> for which the median of the numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/3/0/230bc458ceeaea15cd0491fec243b566d79d85c2.png" class="latex" alt="$4,6,8,17,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="73" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is equal to the mean of those five numbers?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/0/a301b35be79f9351be0b150736cf035bf69e1070.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } -5 \qquad\textbf{(B) } 0 \qquad\textbf{(C) } 5 \qquad\textbf{(D) } \frac{15}{4} \qquad\textbf{(E) } \frac{35}{4}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="400" height="38" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The mean is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/e/9/ae9f9556e12ccb79f969db41a030497755044394.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{4+6+8+17+x}{5}=\frac{35+x}{5}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="226" height="37" />.
</p><p>There are three possibilities for the median: it is either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />.
</p><p>Let's start with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/c/7/3c727dd6d2ba6fce7326c9eb92ac9d645a76731a.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{35+x}{5}=6$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="87" height="37" /> has solution <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/2/c82313374f44e5d6d2b748519cb6fd317c9a303c.png" class="latex" alt="$x=-5$" width="57" height="12" />, and the sequence is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/4/8/24867466fb06f96c22017397639586da0f0130f6.png" class="latex" alt="$-5, 4, 6, 8, 17$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="101" height="16" />, which does have median <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" />, so this is a valid solution.
</p><p>Now let the median be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/b/e/9becbff18e9e451492f4b52a3658a37428005ca5.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{35+x}{5}=8$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="87" height="37" /> gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/c/51c80ea5862b45877f08d698a0c893bd2806a2fd.png" class="latex" alt="$x=5$" width="43" height="12" />, so the sequence is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/8/f/18f6c0739c4df8167300ada42c4e42e7607b4a05.png" class="latex" alt="$4, 5, 6, 8, 17$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="86" height="16" />, which has median <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" />, so this is not valid.
</p><p>Finally we let the median be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/0/990f5d51258400b815f105e55f8db8d1cf397ca5.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{35+x}{5}=x \implies 35+x=5x \implies x=\frac{35}{4}=8.75$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="395" height="38" />, and the sequence is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/0/3/d03446319c4849afb57fb642e38ee9ba5bda1340.png" class="latex" alt="$4, 6, 8, 8.75, 17$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="110" height="16" />, which has median <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />. This case is therefore again not valid.
</p><p>Hence the only possible value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/6/be6c2ca194c9a748ec6a129efe2303819574e5a8.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(A) }-5}.$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="82" height="28" />
</p>
