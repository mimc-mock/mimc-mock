What is the probability that after the bell has rung <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/9/f49e1f42131f302f2b4a45393cdc3fed5be2655c.png" class="latex" alt="$2019$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" /> times, each player will have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/3/6739c8a3ef02d0f557bfbe01c9c92e562ff9a7f1.png" class="latex" alt="$$1$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="17" height="14" />? (For example, Raashan and Ted may each decide to give <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/3/6739c8a3ef02d0f557bfbe01c9c92e562ff9a7f1.png" class="latex" alt="$$1$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="17" height="14" /> to Sylvia, and Sylvia may decide to give her dollar to Ted, at which point Raashan will have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/e/5/de5552aa8609c6804707c747d7b7aba076e1f740.png" class="latex" alt="$$0$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="17" height="14" />, Sylvia will have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/6/ee6e8cd66adab965db3a961cd262a4ba54e9a132.png" class="latex" alt="$$2$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="17" height="14" />, and Ted will have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/3/6739c8a3ef02d0f557bfbe01c9c92e562ff9a7f1.png" class="latex" alt="$$1$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="17" height="14" />, and that is the end of the first round of play.
