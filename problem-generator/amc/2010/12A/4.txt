<p>If <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/d/6/cd6ee81a7a535d87853e848c77654cc36104a75e.png" class="latex" alt="$x&lt;0$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="43" height="13" />, then which of the following must be positive?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/a/6/8a6b210377d3c8faaed5b00e17cbd935d20a0295.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ \frac{x}{\left|x\right|} \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ -x^2 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ -2^x \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ -x^{-1} \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ \sqrt[3]{x}$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="484" height="37" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is negative, so we can just place a negative value into each expression and find the one that is positive. Suppose we use <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/e/bae5aba07d37ff6ff813107e76260fb31ad5794e.png" class="latex" alt="$-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="22" height="12" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/7/0/470aec9b861027e027277a18306e5e2b59973252.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)} \Rightarrow \frac{-1}{|-1|} = -1$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="149" height="41" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/b/6/1b61197dc3bbff7f49f8631f7aa08382f8b4e6d2.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(B)} \Rightarrow -(-1)^2 = -1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="162" height="19" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/4/104c257fc1443e221cd256e4b475b3e694c3d9c3.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(C)} \Rightarrow -2^{(-1)} = -\frac{1}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="158" height="37" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/5/bf5d4d05f2ca6f77c96d6e3bbc2cdc2a2e956cd9.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(D)} \Rightarrow -(-1)^{(-1)} = 1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="169" height="20" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/a/5da4b91ca769554cfbff384adaa5aa432a2ef63a.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(E)} \Rightarrow \sqrt[3]{-1} = -1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="141" height="20" />
</p><p><br />
Obviously only <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/9/3/693c4044f8526a8179956e8ac7a3f2f9d61e2c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="41" height="28" /> is positive.
</p>
