<p>The first four terms of an arithmetic sequence are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/6/f/36f73fc1312ee0349b3f3a0f3bd9eb5504339011.png" class="latex" alt="$p$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="10" height="11" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/7/887203ae2a5566a5e9cb1e1004e272e273272f56.png" class="latex" alt="$3p-q$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="49" height="16" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/d/d/7ddd48d29cffa16ed411bc5af512994a0dfd8358.png" class="latex" alt="$3p+q$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="49" height="16" />. What is the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/d/5dda3ff4bb1d82073aec197e361469fed00ef6b1.png" class="latex" alt="$2010^\text{th}$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="48" height="16" /> term of this sequence?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/6/7/167a0135ba0d2f295924cc8823881e6f957c2ea2.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 8041 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 8043 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 8045 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 8047 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 8049$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="484" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/7/887203ae2a5566a5e9cb1e1004e272e273272f56.png" class="latex" alt="$3p-q$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="49" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/d/d/7ddd48d29cffa16ed411bc5af512994a0dfd8358.png" class="latex" alt="$3p+q$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="49" height="16" /> are consecutive terms, so the common difference is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/a/0/3a022cc72396f68e0fafc418444e0ea1bf15751c.png" class="latex" alt="$(3p+q)-(3p-q) = 2q$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="190" height="18" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/b/0/2b05b52a00485a85933f7b6b5006ea13aff78d88.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*}p+2q &amp;= 9\\ 9+2q &amp;= 3p-q\\ q&amp;=2\\ p&amp;=5\end{align*}" width="123" height="91" />
</p><p>The common difference is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />. The first term is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> and the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/d/5dda3ff4bb1d82073aec197e361469fed00ef6b1.png" class="latex" alt="$2010^\text{th}$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="48" height="16" /> term is
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/9/c/b9cc4cffb29eceab27e0e2bdaabebbbf4992345b.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[5+4(2009) = \boxed{\textbf{(A) }8041}\]" width="198" height="28" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Since all the answer choices are around <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/1/991c128f326a51cc6931634891f6fc2a1455ff1c.png" class="latex" alt="$2010 \cdot 4 = 8040$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="118" height="13" />, the common difference must be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />. The first term is therefore <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/3/333757668d52e09ec9241dc3c22f498933af63e6.png" class="latex" alt="$9 - 4 = 5$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="73" height="13" />, so the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/d/5dda3ff4bb1d82073aec197e361469fed00ef6b1.png" class="latex" alt="$2010^\text{th}$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="48" height="16" /> term is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/9/519a1c0201fb48fde82827472809097561734c2d.png" class="latex" alt="$5 + 4 \cdot 2009 = \boxed{\textbf{(A) }8041}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="198" height="28" />.
</p>
