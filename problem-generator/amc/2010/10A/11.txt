<p>The length of the interval of solutions of the inequality <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/4/674753086e317e7178f2d5b0766a170f04805793.png" class="latex" alt="$a \le 2x + 3 \le b$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="117" height="15" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/4/8e45c4e68b83385dff499a021e38f007c9f18b3a.png" class="latex" alt="$b - a$" width="39" height="12" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/a/65a67d9c9f67f8c4b3c216e63ba0cf0129f6433f.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ 6 \qquad \mathrm{(B)}\ 10 \qquad \mathrm{(C)}\ 15 \qquad \mathrm{(D)}\ 20 \qquad \mathrm{(E)}\ 30$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="378" height="18" />
</p><p><br />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Since we are given the range of the solutions, we must re-write the inequalities so that we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> in terms of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />. 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/4/3/7437ea0ecd121b92415d9b8e3d7102494fae4f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$a\le 2x+3\le b$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="117" height="15" />
</p><p>Subtract <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> from all of the quantities:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/7/f/77f7480135de8bb4272fb17f02be91dbbb7c27dd.png" class="latex" alt="$a-3\le 2x\le b-3$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="147" height="15" />
</p><p>Divide all of the quantities by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/0/c90132c9fb1867159105335256c8a1390a6aa9fa.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{a-3}{2}\le x\le \frac{b-3}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="145" height="37" />
</p><p>Since we have the range of the solutions, we can make it equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/0/880604578e3c6d6ab33ce3262682d34e32d1ab73.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{b-3}{2}-\frac{a-3}{2} = 10$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="152" height="37" />
</p><p>Multiply both sides by 2.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/4/90455c0987d5b593db94c03acfdb2b38e4b5351a.png" class="latex" alt="$(b-3) - (a-3) = 20$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="172" height="18" />
</p><p>Re-write without using parentheses.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/b/b/2bb0458c9c2814b25fb866d6c981de91cb9b685d.png" class="latex" alt="$b-3-a+3 = 20$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="144" height="14" />
</p><p>Simplify.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/8/7c8ac68e96bee128bc1431524a3f1accbf484763.png" class="latex" alt="$b-a = 20$" width="81" height="12" />
</p><p>We need to find <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/4/8e45c4e68b83385dff499a021e38f007c9f18b3a.png" class="latex" alt="$b - a$" width="39" height="12" /> for the problem, so the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/d/4fde5d6fbd79ae6f1b5a6383682861176c49d17d.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{20\ \textbf{(D)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="65" height="28" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/index.php/Without_loss_of_generality" title="Without loss of generality">Without loss of generality</a>, let the interval of solutions be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/7/2872eb0dc1de0c6367b541403e6997b3e04d53ce.png" class="latex" alt="$[0, 10]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="47" height="20" /> (or any real values <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/2/f927920459b87899f56bc84f479c2082146d3600.png" class="latex" alt="$[p, 10+p]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="79" height="20" />). Then, substitute <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />. This gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/f/a/dfaa2e0a1ed5c4da9745b8f3df10967e103e0408.png" class="latex" alt="$b=23$" width="49" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/0/340605c5f88cf6864adc5b146c3dffca7a45df22.png" class="latex" alt="$a=3$" width="42" height="12" />. So, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/0/f50609e29fbc19cb52507c972815e16094540501.png" class="latex" alt="$23-3=\boxed{20\ \textbf{(D)}}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="140" height="30" />.
~ bearjere
</p>
