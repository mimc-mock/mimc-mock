<p>Tony works <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> hours a day and is paid &#36;<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/1/aa10fc328b191089b850fd630e4c04747653da00.png" class="latex" alt="$0.50$" width="31" height="12" /> per hour for each full year of his age. During a six month period Tony worked <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/6/906734e2f1010e9a171ffee8050cbc0649cea37c.png" class="latex" alt="$50$" width="17" height="12" /> days and earned &#36;<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/7/0/970a9644fb50e7a7520c3046182fe4c6e6adc11f.png" class="latex" alt="$630$" width="26" height="12" />. How old was Tony at the end of the six month period?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/e/4/0e40887bcacbbc9aa999041ddd50e9009c17b06d.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ 9 \qquad \mathrm{(B)}\ 11 \qquad \mathrm{(C)}\ 12 \qquad \mathrm{(D)}\ 13 \qquad \mathrm{(E)}\ 14$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="379" height="18" />
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h3>
<p>Tony works <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> hours a day and is paid <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/1/aa10fc328b191089b850fd630e4c04747653da00.png" class="latex" alt="$0.50$" width="31" height="12" /> dollars per hour for each full year of his age. This basically says that he gets a dollar for each year of his age. So if he is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> years old, he gets <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> dollars a day. We also know that he worked <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/6/906734e2f1010e9a171ffee8050cbc0649cea37c.png" class="latex" alt="$50$" width="17" height="12" /> days and earned <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/7/0/970a9644fb50e7a7520c3046182fe4c6e6adc11f.png" class="latex" alt="$630$" width="26" height="12" /> dollars. If he was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> years old at the beginning of his working period, he would have earned <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/4/7/a4708d47c30ad7b2a5939e039cdfccc1ac9da690.png" class="latex" alt="$12 * 50 = 600$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="104" height="13" /> dollars. If he was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/0/030e19e4e2f1c074127cb50581e8496470c5419f.png" class="latex" alt="$13$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> years old at the beginning of his working period, he would have earned <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/1/7a19b6a004bcaeb48badb4a5af73fb1b53cf2ac1.png" class="latex" alt="$13 * 50 = 650$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="104" height="13" /> dollars. Because he earned <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/7/0/970a9644fb50e7a7520c3046182fe4c6e6adc11f.png" class="latex" alt="$630$" width="26" height="12" /> dollars, we know that he was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/0/030e19e4e2f1c074127cb50581e8496470c5419f.png" class="latex" alt="$13$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> for some period of time, but not the whole time, because then the money earned would be greater than or equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/2/0/12060badf8be82c1d7ae05f240e571d68f73ddfa.png" class="latex" alt="$650$" width="26" height="12" />. This is why he was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> when he began, but turned <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/0/030e19e4e2f1c074127cb50581e8496470c5419f.png" class="latex" alt="$13$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> sometime during the six month period and earned <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/7/0/970a9644fb50e7a7520c3046182fe4c6e6adc11f.png" class="latex" alt="$630$" width="26" height="12" /> dollars in total. So the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/0/030e19e4e2f1c074127cb50581e8496470c5419f.png" class="latex" alt="$13$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />.The answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/5/b/55bdade53f471aab49f63d1a734979cc0c636640.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{D}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="27" height="24" />. We could find out for how long he was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/0/030e19e4e2f1c074127cb50581e8496470c5419f.png" class="latex" alt="$13$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/5/f7562cdc646f661f79785c66ba2d32fdc0af3df1.png" class="latex" alt="$12 \cdot x + 13 \cdot (50-x) = 630$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="211" height="18" />. Then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/3/f8366cd6196dd8a9da6d38a3e9eafb109e99d53e.png" class="latex" alt="$20$" width="17" height="12" /> and we know that he was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/3/f8366cd6196dd8a9da6d38a3e9eafb109e99d53e.png" class="latex" alt="$20$" width="17" height="12" /> days, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/0/030e19e4e2f1c074127cb50581e8496470c5419f.png" class="latex" alt="$13$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/1/651ba418a810d971dbc8a326d49a71fe1541fae0.png" class="latex" alt="$30$" width="17" height="12" /> days. Thus, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/0/030e19e4e2f1c074127cb50581e8496470c5419f.png" class="latex" alt="$13$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h3>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> equal Tony's age at the end of the period. We know that his age changed during the time period (since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/7/0/970a9644fb50e7a7520c3046182fe4c6e6adc11f.png" class="latex" alt="$630$" width="26" height="12" /> does not evenly divide <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/6/906734e2f1010e9a171ffee8050cbc0649cea37c.png" class="latex" alt="$50$" width="17" height="12" />). Thus, his age at the beginning of the time period is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/0/4/b0495af1d7b094561fcade343847d464778c925d.png" class="latex" alt="$x - 1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="40" height="12" />.
</p><p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> be the number of days Tony worked while his age was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />. We know that his earnings every day equal his age (since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/3/be3c5a19c906e25bba9cf2b759682a11739f96f9.png" class="latex" alt="$2 \cdot 0.50 = 1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="87" height="13" />). Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/c/2/5c26a9949828947c54a73b4373bb229cb19b5c09.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[x \cdot d + (x - 1)(50 - d) = 630\]" width="225" height="18" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/845d1bcd6fa794739291be04cf262f8c0cab48ba.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[x\cdot d + 50x - x\cdot d - 50 + d = 630\]" width="261" height="14" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/a/3/6a3e43583ccd6a7d893f4ed92346e54c8670dce0.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[50x + d = 680\]" width="110" height="14" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/4/3440645b3bb386d007d6362b8eee6333f7dd2eed.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[x = \dfrac{680 - d}{50}\]" width="95" height="37" />
Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/1/0/210e79232fe84e26bc1ead08cd41206370f3e7dc.png" class="latex" alt="$0 &lt; d &lt;50$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="84" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/a/1dacac204a1ac6c9a81ae3277ced90ccbaa5a4c5.png" class="latex" alt="$d = 30$" width="51" height="12" />. Then we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/8/aa808b493f01f66b0bfdeb7ab0ab4f85d8c3e468.png" class="latex" alt="$50x = 650$" width="79" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/2/e/02e5b2ed0c958e21e728ed4d11539179bdbc8eb6.png" class="latex" alt="$x = \boxed{(D) 13}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="94" height="29" />
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h3>
<p>Since Tony worked for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/6/906734e2f1010e9a171ffee8050cbc0649cea37c.png" class="latex" alt="$50$" width="17" height="12" /> days, he has worked for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> hours. Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" /> be his hourly wage. Then, we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/4/6/2466dbff7735c257f244386761bfecbda4b977ec.png" class="latex" alt="$100k = 630$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="91" height="14" />. 
</p><p>Dividing both sides by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/6/906734e2f1010e9a171ffee8050cbc0649cea37c.png" class="latex" alt="$50$" width="17" height="12" />, we get that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/e/b8ea7ee8a282949fd853a01537ec7a2b55e56f75.png" class="latex" alt="$2k$" width="18" height="12" />(Daily wage, which is equal to his age) <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/c/b/acbc54ef8feabfc5d0a2329f0cfbd10af8a6a809.png" class="latex" alt="$\approx$" style="vertical-align: 1px" width="13" height="8" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/9/0f9f10489f5c393568d444e1fab55c655a98f53d.png" class="latex" alt="$12.6$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="31" height="13" />. Since we now know that his age was either 12 or 13 during the 6 month span, and we are asked to find his age at the end of this time, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/e/0/2e03ea24ebe7ef7a0073ab912955227fef362069.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{(D) 13}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="59" height="29" />
</p>
