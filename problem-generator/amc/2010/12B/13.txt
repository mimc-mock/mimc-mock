<p>In <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c3a2d2224f7d163b46d702132425d47828bf538.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle ABC$" width="58" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/9/8/e98d64db5a980245a2a80f65b02422d69dda7424.png" class="latex" alt="$\cos(2A-B)+\sin(A+B)=2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="240" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/b/4/4b44beb78e8be18179bfb7fe6b3424e7fb1aef32.png" class="latex" alt="$AB=4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="61" height="13" />. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/5/6c52a41dcbd739f1d026c5d4f181438b75b76976.png" class="latex" alt="$BC$" width="28" height="12" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/0/4/80444b2078dcbf5536467ec12e55ba9a49cf3ac3.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ \sqrt{2} \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ \sqrt{3} \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 2 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 2\sqrt{2} \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 2\sqrt{3}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="428" height="21" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>We note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/e/bae5aba07d37ff6ff813107e76260fb31ad5794e.png" class="latex" alt="$-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="22" height="12" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/b/4/3b4cd01885cb569775f5d1143bc5139fff104bdc.png" class="latex" alt="$\le$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="13" height="14" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/8/6e8f3a072de64627a11a2df663e577eb43d60c77.png" class="latex" alt="$\sin x$" width="35" height="12" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/b/4/3b4cd01885cb569775f5d1143bc5139fff104bdc.png" class="latex" alt="$\le$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="13" height="14" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/e/bae5aba07d37ff6ff813107e76260fb31ad5794e.png" class="latex" alt="$-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="22" height="12" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/b/4/3b4cd01885cb569775f5d1143bc5139fff104bdc.png" class="latex" alt="$\le$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="13" height="14" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/f/11f986c49f29d7a2ed4c28cab7319fa2ee3ee978.png" class="latex" alt="$\cos x$" width="37" height="8" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/b/4/3b4cd01885cb569775f5d1143bc5139fff104bdc.png" class="latex" alt="$\le$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="13" height="14" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />. 
Therefore, there is no other way to satisfy this equation other than making both <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/7/f/37f692d234cb2b78dcd44da712acab259b5be10d.png" class="latex" alt="$\cos(2A-B)=1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="130" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/0/0/c0057eae27aa77e7065d652802a5d7662a7a4182.png" class="latex" alt="$\sin(A+B)=1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="119" height="18" />, since any other way would cause one of these values to become greater than 1, which contradicts our previous statement.
From this we can easily conclude that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/2/012f676cbb0f480244eda7d4b65d9ead8f4418b0.png" class="latex" alt="$2A-B=0^{\circ}$" width="99" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/5/a/55aad741a85d37efd124d5ab050c93812a486f81.png" class="latex" alt="$A+B=90^{\circ}$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="99" height="14" /> and solving this system gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/9/879286e09e2bf96bfc751ebea2e86e0997eee930.png" class="latex" alt="$A=30^{\circ}$" width="62" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/6/7/a6733d5f7e73eb78da58bfc895ba17dc3a1b9c11.png" class="latex" alt="$B=60^{\circ}$" width="63" height="13" />. It is clear that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c3a2d2224f7d163b46d702132425d47828bf538.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle ABC$" width="58" height="13" /> is a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/0/9009e0cc70c1165f60661f68e84136b0a8dee1cc.png" class="latex" alt="$30^{\circ},60^{\circ},90^{\circ}$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="92" height="16" /> triangle with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/b/1/8b1929493a2042af1208c7b6bb285c11b19a6108.png" class="latex" alt="$BC=2$" width="61" height="12" />　<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/8/1/981cc6a2adc50878a6da811d05c34e5cfaf6cef7.png" class="latex" alt="$\Longrightarrow$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="28" height="10" />　<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/b/d/abd337e8b85dc471b3cb7c333dea9fd5d610a2f2.png" class="latex" alt="$(C)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="27" height="18" />.
</p>
