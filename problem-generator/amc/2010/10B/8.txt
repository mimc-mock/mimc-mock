<p>A ticket to a school play cost <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> dollars, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is a whole number. A group of 9th graders buys tickets costing a total of $<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/a/f/5afbad0b221f0ef70186fe70b08e6fc1efdd8dfa.png" class="latex" alt="$48$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />, and a group of 10th graders buys tickets costing a total of $<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/e/05e228ac5bee7b9ffb60cacf50f3f2c7c31f8038.png" class="latex" alt="$64$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="13" />. How many values for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> are possible?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/d/d4de85cf3f5978446555260f2de6dbc927a7bfac.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 1 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 2 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 3 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 4 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 5$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="348" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>We find the greatest common factor of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/a/f/5afbad0b221f0ef70186fe70b08e6fc1efdd8dfa.png" class="latex" alt="$48$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/e/05e228ac5bee7b9ffb60cacf50f3f2c7c31f8038.png" class="latex" alt="$64$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="13" /> to be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/5/9a5b4928c8fe50ce3c2428da3bee3505e891b788.png" class="latex" alt="$16$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />. The number of factors of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/5/9a5b4928c8fe50ce3c2428da3bee3505e891b788.png" class="latex" alt="$16$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> which is the answer <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/2/a/02ad991c2821130f0d7e456d453252c19a320752.png" class="latex" alt="$(E)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="27" height="18" />.
</p>
