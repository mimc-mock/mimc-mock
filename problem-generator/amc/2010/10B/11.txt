<p>A shopper plans to purchase an item that has a listed price greater than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/5/1/751f8354c64df7687207c0df786c41cc883ab3cb.png" class="latex" alt="$\textdollar 100$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="35" height="14" /> and can use any one of the three coupons. Coupon A gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/a/87a8787ca8fcdf64f42e938d20fa64f63871646d.png" class="latex" alt="$15\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> off the listed price, Coupon B gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/2/a22960968e6c093c4eae61303577e4f90fd4051c.png" class="latex" alt="$\textdollar 30$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="26" height="14" /> off the listed price, and Coupon C gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/6/96633b73a86b9d9110e1ff824fce08def96ca296.png" class="latex" alt="$25\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> off the amount by which the listed price exceeds
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/5/1/751f8354c64df7687207c0df786c41cc883ab3cb.png" class="latex" alt="$\textdollar 100$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="35" height="14" />. <br />
Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" /> be the smallest and largest prices, respectively, for which Coupon A saves at least as many dollars as Coupon B or C. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/3/d/e3d2d36ed8ae2ac20e4bcec402980a11639c40c5.png" class="latex" alt="$y - x$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="42" height="11" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/4/1/441abba6b58bf5f746fc848106bba86a7dbf1fed.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 50 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 60 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 75 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 80  \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 100$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="403" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Let the listed price be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/4/0/9409f7f27a664469d23090938f11f296adb48d00.png" class="latex" alt="$(100 + p)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="71" height="18" />, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/b/19bf07ceb43df8c7388742c5c1a3667791306791.png" class="latex" alt="$p &gt; 0$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="43" height="16" />
</p><p>Coupon A saves us: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/8/6e8ff7c32806fecbd109777eb7f7841c5f5ca2c9.png" class="latex" alt="$0.15(100+p) = (0.15p + 15)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="223" height="18" />
</p><p>Coupon B saves us: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/1/651ba418a810d971dbc8a326d49a71fe1541fae0.png" class="latex" alt="$30$" width="17" height="12" />
</p><p>Coupon C saves us: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/3/d4350c0f34738387e88729bb2d1d2f57043f0b21.png" class="latex" alt="$0.25p$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="41" height="16" />
</p><p>Now, the condition is that A has to be greater than or equal to either B or C which gives us the following inequalities:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/7/c/e7c313515101fd135bcc4c84f65708b151c738ba.png" class="latex" alt="$A \geq B \Rightarrow 0.15p + 15 \geq 30 \Rightarrow p \geq 100$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="293" height="16" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/5/8/2586348ff8cc0eaf479802af67fe8278a418e7f0.png" class="latex" alt="$A \geq C \Rightarrow 0.15p + 15 \geq 0.25p \Rightarrow p \leq 150$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="316" height="16" />
</p><p>We see here that the greatest possible value for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/6/f/36f73fc1312ee0349b3f3a0f3bd9eb5504339011.png" class="latex" alt="$p$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="10" height="11" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/e/f5e10e5f3aa7c766cb27c3ad319fa526f71588cd.png" class="latex" alt="$150$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />, thus <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/6/0/f60a455408724093e429b12e66bf2afb874f050c.png" class="latex" alt="$y = 100 + 150 = 250$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="161" height="16" /> and the smallest value for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/6/f/36f73fc1312ee0349b3f3a0f3bd9eb5504339011.png" class="latex" alt="$p$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="10" height="11" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/1/2013c432d62fd99b89da7804b8124ddb2918c61c.png" class="latex" alt="$x = 100 + 100 = 200$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="162" height="14" />. 
</p><p>The difference between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/f/6/1f6cb0f062afdb3a20d26b182b2a640e0779156d.png" class="latex" alt="$y - x = 250 - 200 = \boxed{\textbf{(A)}\ 50}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="232" height="28" />
</p>
