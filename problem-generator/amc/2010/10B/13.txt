<p>What is the sum of all the solutions of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/2/2f2ff20665ba333547d6ab7a4b5c3de71ec30a31.png" class="latex" alt="$x = \left|2x-|60-2x|\right|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="154" height="18" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/f/f9fd4b80ff9df85b040285e38acb6f963653e0ff.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 32 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 60 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 92 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 120 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 124$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="412" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>We evaluate this in cases:
</p><p><i>Case 1</i>
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/8/b/98b5fdb8e401d5a15d14d77e0c22e0cda6f09d54.png" class="latex" alt="$x&lt;30$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="13" /> 
</p><p>When <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/8/b/98b5fdb8e401d5a15d14d77e0c22e0cda6f09d54.png" class="latex" alt="$x&lt;30$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="13" /> we are going to have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/e/a2e1a0da7ba493213579252b0a1e2bfabbaab6f1.png" class="latex" alt="$60-2x&gt;0$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="92" height="13" />.  When <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/2/e/02e28efa00c4f2c66d1aa381c0cb1f8e0ef8d6e4.png" class="latex" alt="$x&gt;0$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="43" height="13" /> we are going to have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/a/b/2abd8287a4224ac51ecdee7bbbff038e1efabca2.png" class="latex" alt="$|x|&gt;0\implies x&gt;0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="146" height="18" /> and when <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/6/9/7690cc5b82324b605e2deeadb933cdd7959775ff.png" class="latex" alt="$-x&gt;0$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="57" height="13" /> we are going to have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/9/f99aa8af23c45d189e4148c6d84fb72b9ef5479a.png" class="latex" alt="$|x|&gt;0\implies -x&gt;0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="160" height="18" />.  Therefore we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/d/c/9dc1d3953a1501775ef2399942076b509221d4ec.png" class="latex" alt="$x=|2x-(60-2x)|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="158" height="18" />.
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/4/8/048b88559008f9da73eb7a7c073a8b1ed62d73b7.png" class="latex" alt="$x=|2x-60+2x|\implies x=|4x-60|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="298" height="18" />
</p><p><i>Subcase 1 </i><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/b/0fbe6ff21d707e32f97031ca6a554c92061af18b.png" class="latex" alt="$30&gt;x&gt;15$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="94" height="13" />
</p><p>When <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/b/0fbe6ff21d707e32f97031ca6a554c92061af18b.png" class="latex" alt="$30&gt;x&gt;15$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="94" height="13" /> we are going to have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/d/91d34e8f319df3f31ec3e8572f503a884f54f210.png" class="latex" alt="$4x-60&gt;0$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="92" height="13" />. When this happens, we can express <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/1/541815e380af6b8c31a4c1f4d3779f80d4cdc88a.png" class="latex" alt="$|4x-60|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="68" height="18" /> as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/8/e/38ef0c1a68f4490fb89d33ddf9fdec2fb494994b.png" class="latex" alt="$4x-60$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="59" height="13" />.
Therefore we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/8/198dd0fc25a4e001b0a3bca0732a9478fc9912a6.png" class="latex" alt="$x=4x-60\implies -3x=-60\implies x=20$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="336" height="13" />.  We check if <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/f/1cf1342f460285dfd226094ac3973558576c9982.png" class="latex" alt="$x=20$" width="52" height="12" /> is in the domain of the numbers that we put into this subcase, and it is, since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/c/6/ac66fb4e76bfd9facccb4a4ebfe3ebc126a8b0b7.png" class="latex" alt="$30&gt;20&gt;15$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="102" height="13" />. Therefore <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/3/f8366cd6196dd8a9da6d38a3e9eafb109e99d53e.png" class="latex" alt="$20$" width="17" height="12" /> is one possible solution.  
</p><p><i> Subcase 2 </i> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/0/4/704ba6849575779e2bfdebd0b1607344ee852577.png" class="latex" alt="$x&lt;15$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="13" />
</p><p>When <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/0/4/704ba6849575779e2bfdebd0b1607344ee852577.png" class="latex" alt="$x&lt;15$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="13" /> we are going to have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/7/9/979fcd1e6b3877cffb0cd28b76d9d8f1b103bc9f.png" class="latex" alt="$4x-60&lt;0$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="92" height="13" />, therefore <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/1/541815e380af6b8c31a4c1f4d3779f80d4cdc88a.png" class="latex" alt="$|4x-60|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="68" height="18" /> can be expressed in the form <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/2/3/52322520ae09503c9d2af7fd3a50a3f49fd8bebc.png" class="latex" alt="$60-4x$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="59" height="13" />.
We have the equation <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/e/8/7e85ff592b99c98ec755cd237c7d1069da0912d1.png" class="latex" alt="$x=60-4x\implies 5x=60\implies x=12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="307" height="13" />.  Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> is less than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/1/9/f1965fae079a9ba2c0726c307070c2355dfcb213.png" class="latex" alt="$15$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> is another possible solution.
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/0/7/707b2e898da40a17b8bd9e12cf06c3d299bf7b57.png" class="latex" alt="$x=|2x-|60-2x||$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="154" height="18" />
</p><p><i>Case 2 </i>:  <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/1/0514d2a96cfa22637b6c23769ac931a0dd82603e.png" class="latex" alt="$x&gt;30$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="13" />
</p><p>When <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/1/0514d2a96cfa22637b6c23769ac931a0dd82603e.png" class="latex" alt="$x&gt;30$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/3/bf3f34f82112c090d2927af1b24e6d5adbaa969f.png" class="latex" alt="$60-2x&lt;0$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="92" height="13" />. When <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/d/6/cd6ee81a7a535d87853e848c77654cc36104a75e.png" class="latex" alt="$x&lt;0$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="43" height="13" /> we can express this in the form <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/2/9/52969fdeeb90f7e24cd2a2f1a6cfe124776fee2a.png" class="latex" alt="$-x$" width="24" height="8" />.  Therefore we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/a/c/dacf3632e35e3df121360cf6e8e47be937ec76e7.png" class="latex" alt="$-(60-2x)=2x-60$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="171" height="18" />.  This makes sure that this is positive, since we just took the negative of a negative to get a positive.  Therefore we have 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/6/c/a6c5206155db22bd192fce664717f4217e63d161.png" class="latex" alt="$x=|2x-(2x-60)|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="158" height="18" /> 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/6/9/369aa53d20815ba6d7575b3e4ea96138263d97f8.png" class="latex" alt="$x=|2x-2x+60|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="144" height="18" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/f/8/df83d8b108283b7088617f0eac01ee66d0d2192c.png" class="latex" alt="$x=|60|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="60" height="18" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/f/43fe0b5d105ca27faf685588f5682110c5e78f21.png" class="latex" alt="$x=60$" width="52" height="12" />
</p><p>We have now evaluated all the cases, and found the solution to be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/2/5424a1640af76b14e8854a7a3c35cb5031c74f28.png" class="latex" alt="$\{60,12,20\}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="87" height="18" /> which have a sum of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/a/2da25e7283fe43ae90aff069326aac4a37bd5adf.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 92}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="64" height="28" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>From the equation <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/2/2f2ff20665ba333547d6ab7a4b5c3de71ec30a31.png" class="latex" alt="$x = \left|2x-|60-2x|\right|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="154" height="18" /> , we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/1/6/c16d1da212951c8656478b097fc8bca825fb5b70.png" class="latex" alt="$x = 2x-|60-2x|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="144" height="18" /> , or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/5/5/755ae6066fc92a79ddb2b1217b0ff12ddedef6a4.png" class="latex" alt="$-x = 2x-|60-2x|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="158" height="18" />. Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/6/0f69726a37c6d4addb8f1fa962feb716f9801378.png" class="latex" alt="$x=|60-2x|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="102" height="18" /> , or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/4/2842f7f7588d3c92dfca1f9b0d9e6cf4b299bbfd.png" class="latex" alt="$3x=|60-2x|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="111" height="18" />. From here we have four possible cases:
</p><p>1. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/d/2/0d29333f1df172ccedd9b1f767a31a9879261662.png" class="latex" alt="$x=60-2x$" width="94" height="12" />; this simplifies to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/0/3303641da2aa9acafa4e52b7b15cb190be79604d.png" class="latex" alt="$3x=60$" width="61" height="12" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/f/1cf1342f460285dfd226094ac3973558576c9982.png" class="latex" alt="$x=20$" width="52" height="12" />.
</p><p>2. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/e/7ae713a11c76464a8657d76afc50307059753ff6.png" class="latex" alt="$-x=60-2x$" width="108" height="12" />; this simplifies to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/f/43fe0b5d105ca27faf685588f5682110c5e78f21.png" class="latex" alt="$x=60$" width="52" height="12" />.
</p><p>3. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/0/1/d0151d9b44c73d875b5c6aff4453585acbbcaf90.png" class="latex" alt="$3x=60-2x$" width="103" height="12" />; this simplifies to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/7/f57b5d7d28b26fe161ba876436ee9fab57048a7d.png" class="latex" alt="$5x=60$" width="61" height="12" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/f/20fce41465f6ac44f0c78fbacabb9cb5e9682f9a.png" class="latex" alt="$x=12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="12" />.
</p><p>4. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/9/fc931469cad1bb0d1c56ed7b28b2dd3f2ba0cd29.png" class="latex" alt="$-3x=60-2x$" width="117" height="12" />; this simplifies to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/2/0/d20197d09edf3f98b56761014dc0f96e6a14e957.png" class="latex" alt="$-x=60$" width="66" height="12" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/2/c62d3fcc27aa677aa13958cf4462efa7f08c5a1a.png" class="latex" alt="$x=-60$" width="66" height="12" />. However, this solution is extraneous because the absolute value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/9/d/d9db8530ad3df8452be8c925d7e775c3164df12a.png" class="latex" alt="$2x-|60-2x|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="109" height="18" /> cannot be negative.
</p><p>The sum of all of the solutions of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/5/4/9547a00296ebd11a090828cf4e86546224b7d295.png" class="latex" alt="$20+60+12=\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 92}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="187" height="28" />
</p>
