<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> be real numbers with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/4/fe4d43f71032393d0d0520382809500472efa1ee.png" class="latex" alt="$|a-b|=2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="82" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/d/8/8d8d6299e0e7fdc47b12c8adf827ef273d10968e.png" class="latex" alt="$|b-c|=3$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="80" height="18" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/f/a/1fab66b7e187eb8891bca0a760733d0ed8a6564a.png" class="latex" alt="$|c-d|=4$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="83" height="18" />. What is the sum of all possible values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/d/8/fd854138acc7441594b654eeaad99f7f385dddfe.png" class="latex" alt="$|a-d|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="49" height="18" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/2/5/5253e48bddc542b28687c32df8c47d39f69f1d8b.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ 9 \qquad \mathrm{(B)}\ 12 \qquad \mathrm{(C)}\ 15 \qquad \mathrm{(D)}\ 18 \qquad \mathrm{(E)}\ 24$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="379" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>From <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/4/fe4d43f71032393d0d0520382809500472efa1ee.png" class="latex" alt="$|a-b|=2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="82" height="18" /> we get that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/e/ceec9b656cf09dd809819318388626312bf923a2.png" class="latex" alt="$a=b\pm 2$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="72" height="13" />
</p><p>Similarly, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/d/33d406cf7ec512499743c9c8040f7404fc579953.png" class="latex" alt="$b=c\pm3$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="70" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/c/1/cc1578566672deda0b1987d721a0df4d87c923d1.png" class="latex" alt="$c=d\pm4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="73" height="13" />.
</p><p>Substitution gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/d/b/3dbf4b6148adff50788fc5d749cda7cb4cf73a79.png" class="latex" alt="$a=d\pm 4\pm 3\pm 2$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="136" height="13" />. This gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/e/61e7a73ac984b5e7efc55e13a693f692a77ba317.png" class="latex" alt="$|a-d|=|\pm 4\pm 3\pm 2|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="177" height="18" />. There are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/4/57421b8557f6aa4384653806bfc7a5b80faf557a.png" class="latex" alt="$2^3=8$" width="49" height="15" /> possibilities for the value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/5/0158be400a293c5c0f08a88172a7e2af3b487a3a.png" class="latex" alt="$\pm 4\pm 3\pm2$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="85" height="13" />:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/b/3/4b3a8918ae74d79fbeef19cabd0d6996a54e10a8.png" class="latex" alt="$4+3+2=\boxed{9}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="116" height="23" />, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/4/5d4518dbe1095af4c242b37665ceece938623dbf.png" class="latex" alt="$4+3-2=\boxed{5}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="116" height="23" />, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/b/5/7b5f32d220c1e9e1b0f39bd29eacdefe9939e674.png" class="latex" alt="$4-3+2=\boxed{3}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="116" height="23" />, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/4/284f30a386dc47817f41c0de434a06db5adc461c.png" class="latex" alt="$-4+3+2=\boxed{1}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="131" height="23" />, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/9/d490fc46f7b8d65fe066d5d28c81f168c739e9ad.png" class="latex" alt="$4-3-2=\boxed{-1}$" style="vertical-align: -7px" width="131" height="24" />, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/2/0/120eb525d7f54612beeb4548e8c1313129e15bdf.png" class="latex" alt="$-4+3-2=\boxed{-3}$" style="vertical-align: -7px" width="145" height="24" />, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/0/d/80dcad471946c282ea48bbb33eed92a900f167f5.png" class="latex" alt="$-4-3+2=\boxed{-5}$" style="vertical-align: -7px" width="145" height="24" />, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/b/d/abdfc73d55b339efffdcbdafb5a8f1ba73254f1f.png" class="latex" alt="$-4-3-2=\boxed{-9}$" style="vertical-align: -7px" width="145" height="24" />
</p><p>Therefore, the only possible values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/d/8/fd854138acc7441594b654eeaad99f7f385dddfe.png" class="latex" alt="$|a-d|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="49" height="18" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />. Their sum is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/0/7/707b150be2f7a929f705d880caa88481445f9a87.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{18}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="30" height="23" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>If we add the same constant to all of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" />, we will not change any of the differences. Hence we can assume that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/9/c/69cefa6855d2216036df094012f7ad2a7e4e204f.png" class="latex" alt="$a=0$" width="42" height="12" />.
</p><p>From <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/4/fe4d43f71032393d0d0520382809500472efa1ee.png" class="latex" alt="$|a-b|=2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="82" height="18" /> we get that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/3/d/23ddfa3971034072dd95423bfbdb9f0fa06265a9.png" class="latex" alt="$|b|=2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="50" height="18" />, hence <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/0/8/00833f168d1d520a18190802f1e91d8f17c635c5.png" class="latex" alt="$b\in\{-2,2\}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="87" height="18" />.
</p><p>If we multiply all four numbers by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/e/bae5aba07d37ff6ff813107e76260fb31ad5794e.png" class="latex" alt="$-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="22" height="12" />, we will not change any of the differences. (This is due to the fact that we are calculating <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/e/2/4e220f29757fa2574952a2718dde54b0b7e914c8.png" class="latex" alt="$|d|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="17" height="18" /> at the end ~Williamgolly) <a href="/wiki/index.php/WLOG" class="mw-redirect" title="WLOG">WLOG</a> we can assume that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/d/6/7d65baaf9671227780dd04ab952911036aa5c868.png" class="latex" alt="$b=2$" width="40" height="12" />.
</p><p>From <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/d/8/8d8d6299e0e7fdc47b12c8adf827ef273d10968e.png" class="latex" alt="$|b-c|=3$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="80" height="18" /> we get that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/6/8365da656b939e6c420da127876cefcca01de70b.png" class="latex" alt="$c\in\{-1,5\}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="87" height="18" />.
</p><p>From <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/f/a/1fab66b7e187eb8891bca0a760733d0ed8a6564a.png" class="latex" alt="$|c-d|=4$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="83" height="18" /> we get that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/7/e57a60b37b33462d02c6343ac2d3f7393a82159f.png" class="latex" alt="$d\in\{-5,1,3,9\}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="123" height="18" />.
</p><p>Hence <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/8/8781f33b6f57645d5d0cd496fb3d19b6697db3ef.png" class="latex" alt="$|a-d|=|d|\in\{1,3,5,9\}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="195" height="18" />, and the sum of possible values is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/6/6/866535a83532e2c165d47c82919736fc9f736a23.png" class="latex" alt="$1+3+5+9 = \boxed{18}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="157" height="23" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/6/8/368407d649475cdabb8385d7cdcf9b3ae7ba57ac.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\begin{cases} |a-b| = 2, \\ |b-c| = 3, \\ |c-d| = 4, \\ |d-a| = X. \\ \end{cases}\]" width="107" height="96" /> Note that we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/0/a/00a3981a4ece0f816c6db5584a859b2ce2b3a853.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\begin{cases} a-b = \pm 2, \\ b-c = \pm 3, \\ c-d = \pm 4 \\ d-a = \pm X, \\ \end{cases} \ \ \implies\]" width="181" height="96" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/7/2/272461d1344d393fdead81820d91c94872bd2010.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\pm X \pm 2 \pm 3 \pm 4 = 0, \ \ \implies X \pm 2 \pm 3 \pm 4 = 0,\]" width="368" height="16" /> from which it follows that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/6/8c66e5fc96544ef88946069db4754891d465de51.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[X = \pm 4 \pm 3 \pm 2.\]" width="130" height="13" />
</p><p>Note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/c/60cbf439a187d0082e4a19add4b69a4075d83fd3.png" class="latex" alt="$X = |a-d|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="90" height="18" /> must be positive however, the only arrangements of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/7/c/77cf26be132ef93923e082ee4153b2cb0ef44a50.png" class="latex" alt="$+$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="13" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/d/add22d9bda21e86390bc74fa4dde17730a442da7.png" class="latex" alt="$-$" style="vertical-align: 4px" width="13" height="1" /> signs on the RHS which make <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/a/4/6a47ca0fe7cb276abc022af6ac88ddae1a9d6894.png" class="latex" alt="$X$" width="15" height="12" /> positive are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/f/aaf7b683db1d24d9930f54c516411025b72e2e8d.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[(4, 3, 2) \ \ \implies \ \ X = 9\]" width="177" height="18" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/0/e101d8f148de6ba0c171ff11e3338f27566be822.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[(4, 3, -2) \ \ \implies \ \ X = 5\]" width="191" height="18" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/e/5/0e56599c6cfdd101e1fa58312afd3f69a743fa26.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[(4, -3, 2) \ \ \implies \ \ X = 3\]" width="191" height="18" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/d/fcd49947d5d66a824f86f0aa3f42b2ec4d1e42b8.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[(-4, 3, 2) \ \ \implies \ \ X = 1.\]" width="195" height="18" /> (There are no cases with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> or more negative as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/f/f/5ffa95498f3b4bf364b1d5e7921db46108b20da5.png" class="latex" alt="$4-3-2&lt;0.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="108" height="13" />)
</p><p>Thus, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/a/6/ca6c96a883d5e1463bb9f9973250d8ab2f10f648.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[1+3+5+9 = \boxed{18}.\]" width="159" height="23" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_4">Solution 4</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/9/c/69cefa6855d2216036df094012f7ad2a7e4e204f.png" class="latex" alt="$a=0$" width="42" height="12" />.
</p><p>Then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/c/e/3ce53bd8393ce444387d04c475e4869dae2a3502.png" class="latex" alt="$\begin{cases} b=2 \\ b=-2 \\ \end{cases}$" style="vertical-align: -23px" width="90" height="55" />
</p><p>Thus <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/a/f3abbebde40d1b796529da28c9beb70d1cdb87e2.png" class="latex" alt="$\begin{cases} c=5 \\ c=-1 \\ c=1 \\ c=-5 \\ \end{cases}$" style="vertical-align: -44px" width="92" height="98" />
</p><p>Therefore <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/b/43b8d26ba4751ee1494c85a9e4aeb63eb28945f4.png" class="latex" alt="$\begin{cases} d=9 \\ d=1 \\ d=3 \\ d=-5 \\ d=5 \\ d=-3 \\ d=-1 \\ d=-9 \\ \end{cases}$" style="vertical-align: -92px" width="93" height="194" />
</p><p>So <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/0/5/305f375111d77184ba65056fae568de10284f096.png" class="latex" alt="$|a-d|=1, 9, 3, 5$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="138" height="20" /> and the sum is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/8/6/98679df9a9626db768969306810168a3e819be96.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D) } 18}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="67" height="30" />.
</p><p>~JH. L
