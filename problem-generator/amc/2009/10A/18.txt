<p>At Jefferson Summer Camp, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/d/7/fd7edd3842c40dbc1e54e522917a6e07b43c92e2.png" class="latex" alt="$60\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the children play soccer, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/e/32e5939a7bdd847ec86582225cd653c240b4abce.png" class="latex" alt="$30\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the children swim, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/e/f4e4e5c6d549ce47918b94180cad5c1644d9ea88.png" class="latex" alt="$40\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the soccer players swim. To the nearest whole percent, what percent of the non-swimmers play soccer? 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/d/20dd71251610de894c9c128c298afa5d684c2f51.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ 30\% \qquad \mathrm{(B)}\ 40\% \qquad \mathrm{(C)}\ 49\% \qquad \mathrm{(D)}\ 51\% \qquad \mathrm{(E)}\ 70\%$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="463" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solutions">Solutions</span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h3>
<p>Out of the soccer players, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/e/f4e4e5c6d549ce47918b94180cad5c1644d9ea88.png" class="latex" alt="$40\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> swim. As the soccer players are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/d/7/fd7edd3842c40dbc1e54e522917a6e07b43c92e2.png" class="latex" alt="$60\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the whole, the swimming soccer players are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/b/5/eb50031587ae6266f5d611c9e3819ca959336d66.png" class="latex" alt="$0.4 \cdot 0.6 = 0.24 = 24\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="173" height="14" /> of all children.
</p><p>The non-swimming soccer players then form <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/5/fe53544367c7dec211d44c511d8e1c6a1ff2d23d.png" class="latex" alt="$60\% - 24\% = 36\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="145" height="14" /> of all the children.
</p><p>Out of all the children, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/e/32e5939a7bdd847ec86582225cd653c240b4abce.png" class="latex" alt="$30\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> swim. We know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/1/c615d810d6f88583b5ebe2e267cfba53fb72761d.png" class="latex" alt="$24\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of all the children swim and play soccer, hence <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/4/2/a429328b9213684d1978f84ed616ca8229474418.png" class="latex" alt="$30\%-24\% = 6\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="136" height="14" /> of all the children swim and don't play soccer.
</p><p>Finally, we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/2/282fdef75f579c92565cd7e0734d83ccfd38bfdc.png" class="latex" alt="$70\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of all the children are non-swimmers. And as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/1/9/219d19a2cd739683cc264acbc4340e000f0bfd5c.png" class="latex" alt="$36\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of all the children do not swim but play soccer, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/c/b/9cbc80025804b9f942e975e655d3b8f6482c4db9.png" class="latex" alt="$70\% - 36\% = 34\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="145" height="14" /> of all the children do not engage in any activity.
</p><p>A quick summary of what we found out:
</p>
<ul><li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/1/c615d810d6f88583b5ebe2e267cfba53fb72761d.png" class="latex" alt="$24\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" />: swimming yes, soccer yes</li>
<li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/1/9/219d19a2cd739683cc264acbc4340e000f0bfd5c.png" class="latex" alt="$36\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" />: swimming no, soccer yes</li>
<li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/2/1/021ef37ec8c9c772a954b9035e2c721a63edb3af.png" class="latex" alt="$6\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="23" height="14" />: swimming yes, soccer no</li>
<li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/b/4/4b4d2b3f68e64e49385b1fa8a72987dfa77dd5a6.png" class="latex" alt="$34\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" />: swimming no, soccer no</li></ul>
<p>Now we can compute the answer. Out of all children, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/2/282fdef75f579c92565cd7e0734d83ccfd38bfdc.png" class="latex" alt="$70\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> are non-swimmers, and again out of all children <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/1/9/219d19a2cd739683cc264acbc4340e000f0bfd5c.png" class="latex" alt="$36\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> are non-swimmers that play soccer. Hence the percent of non-swimmers that play soccer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/1/f/f1f634f2409614dfa0fa75b8de1dceb21ff0b5b0.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{36}{70} \approx 51\% \Rightarrow \boxed{D}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="135" height="37" />.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h3>
<p>Let us set the total number of children as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />. So <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/0/2/302e99c1c7cd91a6ae371139e5142ccef5977dca.png" class="latex" alt="$60$" width="17" height="12" /> children play soccer, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/1/651ba418a810d971dbc8a326d49a71fe1541fae0.png" class="latex" alt="$30$" width="17" height="12" /> swim, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/8/b5894ce0ddcc5574a1936647331dfdc49510a13d.png" class="latex" alt="$0.4\times60=24$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="106" height="13" /> play soccer and swim. 
</p><p>Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/a/4fa69439ea7e314f2b10521a8f179f03ce9bf212.png" class="latex" alt="$60-24=36$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="100" height="13" /> children only play soccer.
</p><p>So our numerator is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/5/ce5d3cbb9ab0a992ed503c2d4499e4f4af2f9d65.png" class="latex" alt="$36$" width="17" height="12" />.
</p><p>Our denominator is simply <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/b/8/6b8ea24f99519462589ccddfa2d12559d93c8233.png" class="latex" alt="$100-\text{Swimmers}=100-30=70$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="257" height="13" />
</p><p>And so we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/6/0/1604dc86e080e9b23f2814e5cc9a5d6c925c7a75.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{36}{70}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="20" height="37" /> which is roughly <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/6/056ce7147acc0657ef73a965ad2bbfcd61372bfd.png" class="latex" alt="$51.4\% \Rightarrow \boxed{\text{D}}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="100" height="23" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>WLOG, let the total number of students be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />. Draw a venn diagram with 2 circles encompassing these 4 regions: 
</p><p><br />
Non-soccer players, non-swimmers: 34 people
</p><p>Soccer players, non-swimmers: 36 people
</p><p>Soccer players, swimmers: 24 people
</p><p>Non-soccer players, swimmers: 6 people. 
</p><p><br />
Hence the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/4/90411a45fb588bf63a6cebdbc9c3fcddf760b60c.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{36}{70}=\frac{18}{35}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="70" height="38" />. We know this is a little bit larger than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/d/c/3dcd5e6a7667f87063bc60523fe5945cd667a2be.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac 12$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="11" height="37" /> because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/8/7/987dbfe4ee5c7a41de618ed169f35e9b5d44e33e.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{17.5}{35}=\frac 12$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="75" height="38" />. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/c/c/ccc81172a830bf887ed99c681bd245e70b8afc04.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D) } 51\%}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="82" height="31" />
</p><p>~BakedPotato66
</p>
