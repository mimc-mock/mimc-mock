<p>Suppose that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/e/a/aea489c18f09efe3835367785ebc5e043757a75f.png" class="latex" alt="$P = 2^m$" width="59" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/d/32dd7832ce97cd702126aaa8e7874aeb95d81282.png" class="latex" alt="$Q = 3^n$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="55" height="16" />. Which of the following is equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/b/26bf0cf9c0c6a643b9da8c3fe00fe59dcce55d3a.png" class="latex" alt="$12^{mn}$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="38" height="13" /> for every pair of integers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/a/7ca10ffe3531cf1faf6222be7eac407c47bef878.png" class="latex" alt="$(m,n)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="47" height="18" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/7/607b00eabe7c4813567f82e7f2786e94f47fa457.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ P^2Q \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ P^nQ^m \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ P^nQ^{2m} \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ P^{2m}Q^n \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ P^{2n}Q^m$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="559" height="19" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>We have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/7/2f7918e85dfd4318d456b3050a6fbe3ea83164cd.png" class="latex" alt="$12^{mn} = (2\cdot 2\cdot 3)^{mn} = 2^{2mn} \cdot 3^{mn} = (2^m)^{2n} \cdot (3^n)^m = \boxed{\bold{E)} P^{2n} Q^m}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="503" height="31" />.
</p>
