<p>Four coins are picked out of a piggy bank that contains a collection of pennies, nickels, dimes, and quarters. Which of the following could <em>not</em> be the total value of the four coins, in cents?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/9/f/a9fe01f43deb04600b2071d0f1e9f90487acd65b.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 15 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 25 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 35 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 45 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 55$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>As all five options are divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />, we may not use any pennies. (This is because a penny is the only coin that is not divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />, and if we used between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> pennies, the sum would not be divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />.)
</p><p>Hence the smallest coin we can use is a nickel, and thus the smallest amount we can get is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/3/1938aa019e80099775595c1748ef3c96dedd9ff3.png" class="latex" alt="$4\cdot 5 = 20$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="73" height="13" />. Therefore the option that is not reachable is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/3/f73a8dcb8bba5c7529a6cded96fa1226d78e7dae.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{15}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="30" height="23" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/3/843622567e12d686e4e2f94ddfcb444e8ecde0d2.png" class="latex" alt="$\Rightarrow$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="10" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/6/5/565ab5e3715db52b9176b04dd9991b71f4e93a86.png" class="latex" alt="$(A)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="26" height="18" />.
</p><p>We can verify that we can indeed get the other ones:
</p>
<ul><li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/c/bfca85059f47371e3b25dded638f6274a8ca75af.png" class="latex" alt="$25 = 10+5+5+5$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="154" height="14" /></li>
<li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/0/8/308fe9d9f1c099989e09c79065ff70f29365f068.png" class="latex" alt="$35 = 10+10+10+5$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="172" height="14" /></li>
<li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/c/b/ecbc3c178b5108957f84d878ef909417f46c023d.png" class="latex" alt="$45 = 25+10+5+5$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="163" height="14" /></li>
<li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/8/b8891fbe04a1abe1326b131f7cfd0cd33f9438ba.png" class="latex" alt="$55 = 25+10+10+10$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="181" height="14" /></li></ul>
