<p>LeRoy and Bernardo went on a week-long trip together and agreed to share the costs equally. Over the week, each of them paid for various joint expenses such as gasoline and car rental. At the end of the trip it turned out that LeRoy had paid A dollars and Bernardo had paid B dollars, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/f/4/af409e0b42351dc29bf63182d85cd1328cb8ffa5.png" class="latex" alt="$A &lt; B.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="56" height="13" /> How many dollars must LeRoy give to Bernardo so that they share the costs equally?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/8/2/982d956cf0abe1d1596c2b694670988ff691279f.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ \frac{A+B}{2} \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ \frac{A-B}{2} \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ \frac{B-A}{2} \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ B-A \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ A+B$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="568" height="38" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The total amount of money that was spent during the trip was

So each person should pay
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/5/8/2582e48b89cce9e96302e33b01566fe34a450d0d.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{A+B}{2}\]" width="51" height="37" />
if they were to share the costs equally. Because LeRoy has already paid <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> dollars of his part, he still has to pay
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/7/077a77a211a52462acd409a7e5bcaed528d683eb.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{A+B}{2} - A =\]" width="107" height="37" />
