<p>At a certain beach if it is at least <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/f/8/ef80c3481c7bf79d09a5fa5cda7c0919e0efd644.png" class="latex" alt="$80^{\circ} F$" width="40" height="13" /> and sunny, then the beach will be crowded. On June 10 the beach was not crowded. What can be concluded about the weather conditions on June 10?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/4/b/e4bd5e590c99814f38a3ab07b905631bd1e51d2c.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ \text{The temperature was cooler than } 80^{\circ} \text{F and it was not sunny.}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="480" height="17" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/a/1/3a1661a0c43b480e0f2c9b21104dc86047b207e0.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(B)}\ \text{The temperature was cooler than } 80^{\circ} \text{F or it was not sunny.}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="467" height="17" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/2/d/d2db072991cbb235d2a86bb96aef171117414221.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(C)}\ \text{If the temperature was at least } 80^{\circ} \text{F, then it was sunny.}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="443" height="17" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/5/7/157d9d3f84c0b4c2003f4938ffb877d757cbf293.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(D)}\ \text{If the temperature was cooler than } 80^{\circ} \text{F, then it was sunny.}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="472" height="17" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/a/f/eaf50be23c6434e6c9afbd2b0b9261725d422147.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(E)}\ \text{If the temperature was cooler than } 80^{\circ} \text{F, then it was not sunny.}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="499" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The beach not being crowded only means that it is not both hot and sunny. Equivalently, it is either cool or cloudy, which means <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/1/a2194b81820e8380be586c1564c3f5d03d5c100d.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="40" height="28" /> is correct.
</p><p>It could be in fact cool and sunny, which would violate <b>(A)</b> and <b>(E)</b>. It could also be cool and cloudy, which would violate <b>(D)</b>, or hot and cloudy, which would violate <b>(C)</b>.
</p>
