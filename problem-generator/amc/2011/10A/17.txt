<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/a/e/cae669897108d7198614e5dfacf7f54dbc2c734b.png" class="latex" alt="$A=x$" width="48" height="13" />. Then from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/c/43cee4d874df05794dd0fdab1f96843da8e8f926.png" class="latex" alt="$A+B+C=30$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="129" height="14" />, we find that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/e/01eab809ed4d7509f691db4e2b727c2baf4ac6f8.png" class="latex" alt="$B=25-x$" width="89" height="12" />. From <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/4/e/44ea608e6e494c690bc61195ef286ea82118a655.png" class="latex" alt="$B+C+D=30$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="130" height="14" />, we then get that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/2/3/2236e2d144171d3a8280308db5012bea5d1f1605.png" class="latex" alt="$D=x$" width="50" height="12" />. Continuing this pattern, we find <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/c/6/9c67abfce13bdee8edf3ac3970566da3aba5620d.png" class="latex" alt="$E=25-x$" width="89" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/9/5b96df75753b23a179f96a9857176c54bda92366.png" class="latex" alt="$F=5$" width="47" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/b/4/9b45a445ac00624cb098d9b4d228f8cb75c7bd7f.png" class="latex" alt="$G=x$" width="48" height="12" />, and finally <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/6/c/06c227aebf57f302a1e9f1b48e9aa3f312c56b75.png" class="latex" alt="$H=25-x$" width="91" height="12" />. So <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/1/a/c1a509c66193c1a7861a41f40baf33914a11d5d0.png" class="latex" alt="$A+H=x+25-x=25 \rightarrow \boxed{\textbf{C}}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="256" height="23" />
