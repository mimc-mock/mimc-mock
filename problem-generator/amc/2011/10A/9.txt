<p>A rectangular region is bounded by the graphs of the equations <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/e/0/9e01d0114b268c0f142d64013a1b2f7446d54c8f.png" class="latex" alt="$y=a, y=-b, x=-c,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="176" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/4/8/e48af5d12d4180555c6c6b761696646008f7d826.png" class="latex" alt="$x=d$" width="44" height="12" />, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/8/b/d8b8490a47ba3063da2731316f5d65dc5cf731ed.png" class="latex" alt="$a,b,c,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="45" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> are all positive numbers. Which of the following represents the area of this region?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/f/b6fe3ae5283cdada50c2eb0af0b996e5204e19dd.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ ac+ad+bc+bd\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ ac-ad+bc-bd\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ ac+ad-bc-bd   \quad\quad\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ -ac-ad+bc+bd\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ ac-ad-bc+bd$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="1040" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>We have a rectangle of side lengths <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/a/c/0ac3fc24d21c85e8d9c759e9fb8d6a9a714551ac.png" class="latex" alt="$a-(-b)=a+b$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="132" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/e/c/0ec509414e084df5c8f49a8977014dfe31f119ef.png" class="latex" alt="$d-(-c)=c+d.$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="135" height="18" /> Thus the area of this rectangle is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/a/82a251c46ceb5620fc7a602a91f42e0d8741fcf5.png" class="latex" alt="$(a + b)(c + d) = \boxed{\textbf{(A)}\ ac + ad + bc + bd}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="314" height="28" />.
</p>
