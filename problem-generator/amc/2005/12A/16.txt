<p>Three <a href="/wiki/index.php/Circle" title="Circle">circles</a> of <a href="/wiki/index.php/Radius" title="Radius">radius</a> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f37bba504894945c07a32f5496d74299a37aa51c.png" class="latex" alt="$s$" width="8" height="8" /> are drawn in the first <a href="/wiki/index.php/Quadrant" title="Quadrant">quadrant</a> of the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/f/7/3f79c3676b62fb47243e0d357c87115b6f3395e4.png" class="latex" alt="$xy$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="19" height="11" />-<a href="/wiki/index.php/Plane" title="Plane">plane</a>. The first circle is tangent to both axes, the second is <a href="/wiki/index.php/Tangent_(geometry)" title="Tangent (geometry)">tangent</a> to the first circle and the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />-axis, and the third is tangent to the first circle and the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" />-axis. A circle of radius <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/a/4/1a4ec87b04efc15ef2bcc345def35b515d3c6f49.png" class="latex" alt="$r &gt; s$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="41" height="10" /> is tangent to both axes and to the second and third circles. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/a/0/0a0a9b50f5cff9336e7ae351829db68fa17a94df.png" class="latex" alt="$r/s$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="25" height="18" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/7/657afd2eef0760abe2ccc3fe7453a83a0b22e681.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] unitsize(3mm); defaultpen(linewidth(.8pt)+fontsize(10pt)); dotfactor=3; pair O0=(9,9), O1=(1,1), O2=(3,1), O3=(1,3); pair P0=O0+9*dir(-45), P3=O3+dir(70); pair[] ps={O0,O1,O2,O3}; dot(ps); draw(Circle(O0,9)); draw(Circle(O1,1)); draw(Circle(O2,1)); draw(Circle(O3,1)); draw(O0--P0,linetype(&quot;3 3&quot;)); draw(O3--P3,linetype(&quot;2 2&quot;)); draw((0,0)--(18,0)); draw((0,0)--(0,18)); label(&quot;$r$&quot;,midpoint(O0--P0),NE); label(&quot;$s$&quot;,(-1.5,4)); draw((-1,4)--midpoint(O3--P3));[/asy]" width="285" height="258" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/f/4/1f4f3c4be181b59565f91e0d9d1b32cbeae48d89.png" class="latex" alt="$(\mathrm {A}) \ 5 \qquad (\mathrm {B}) \ 6 \qquad (\mathrm {C})\ 8 \qquad (\mathrm {D}) \ 9 \qquad (\mathrm {E})\ 10$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="351" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/index.php/File:2005_12A_AMC-16b.png" class="image"><img alt="2005 12A AMC-16b.png" src="https://wiki-images.artofproblemsolving.com//d/d9/2005_12A_AMC-16b.png" width="491" height="497" /></a>
</p><p>Set <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/d/1dd4f44ebb556940a82828ee08adff439bec8ae6.png" class="latex" alt="$s =1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="41" height="12" /> so that we only have to find <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" />. Draw the segment between the center of the third circle and the large circle; this has length <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/8/0/080bdd4b53cf3c5d997f0ee91d272b451f0827d6.png" class="latex" alt="$r+1$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="39" height="13" />. We then draw the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Radius" title="Radius">radius</a> of the large circle that is perpendicular to the <a href="/wiki/index.php/X-axis" title="X-axis">x-axis</a>, and draw the perpendicular from this radius to the center of the third circle. This gives us a <a href="/wiki/index.php/Right_triangle" title="Right triangle">right triangle</a> with legs <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/2/e/22e23531f2d96690263a699c55f20b7b639c53fe.png" class="latex" alt="$r-3,r-1$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="87" height="16" /> and <a href="/wiki/index.php/Hypotenuse" title="Hypotenuse">hypotenuse</a> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/8/0/080bdd4b53cf3c5d997f0ee91d272b451f0827d6.png" class="latex" alt="$r+1$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="39" height="13" />. The <a href="/wiki/index.php/Pythagorean_Theorem" title="Pythagorean Theorem">Pythagorean Theorem</a> yields:
</p>
<div style="text-align:center;"><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/3/a234f93c17da5e55cc6d61dcb2dd96c94b967a8b.png" class="latex" alt="$(r-3)^2 + (r-1)^2 = (r+1)^2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="229" height="19" /><br /><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/f/5/cf5947e2aa4bf9c1f4187105b5fcc854f8bddee5.png" class="latex" alt="$r^2 - 10r + 9 = 0$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="129" height="16" /><br /><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/8/2/482f67dafc8ed1baa5cf823440067133e5dc45fa.png" class="latex" alt="$r = 1, 9$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="58" height="16" /></div>
<p>Quite obviously <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/b/a2b4e485bf802fab7cae595eefd961523b68ffc3.png" class="latex" alt="$r &gt; 1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="41" height="12" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/7/3/d734708bab402bfd5dd0bd521b6889935f78ea4a.png" class="latex" alt="$r = 9 \boxed{(D)}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="83" height="29" />.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h3><p>
Applying <a href="/wiki/index.php/Wildin%27s_Theorem" title="Wildin&#39;s Theorem">Wildin's Theorem</a> directly yields </p><div style="text-align:center;"><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/3/a234f93c17da5e55cc6d61dcb2dd96c94b967a8b.png" class="latex" alt="$(r-3)^2 + (r-1)^2 = (r+1)^2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="229" height="19" /><br /><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/f/5/cf5947e2aa4bf9c1f4187105b5fcc854f8bddee5.png" class="latex" alt="$r^2 - 10r + 9 = 0$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="129" height="16" /><br /><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/8/2/482f67dafc8ed1baa5cf823440067133e5dc45fa.png" class="latex" alt="$r = 1, 9$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="58" height="16" /></div>
<p>As before, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/b/a2b4e485bf802fab7cae595eefd961523b68ffc3.png" class="latex" alt="$r &gt; 1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="41" height="12" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/7/3/d734708bab402bfd5dd0bd521b6889935f78ea4a.png" class="latex" alt="$r = 9 \boxed{(D)}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="83" height="29" />.
</p>
