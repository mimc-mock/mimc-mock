<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> be the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Set" title="Set">set</a> of the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/5/9f5b442c275b4324f100c365b774e872b6b21f1e.png" class="latex" alt="$2005$" width="35" height="12" /> smallest positive multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />, and let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/5/5/2554b6496c3b678897e9b060ef00aa9f0a7d7ece.png" class="latex" alt="$T$" width="13" height="12" /> be the set of the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/5/9f5b442c275b4324f100c365b774e872b6b21f1e.png" class="latex" alt="$2005$" width="35" height="12" /> smallest positive multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" />. How many elements are common to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/5/5/2554b6496c3b678897e9b060ef00aa9f0a7d7ece.png" class="latex" alt="$T$" width="13" height="12" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/9/3/29333cbd86e095eaab0c3b33a1d0da663e1ef6c5.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } 166\qquad \textbf{(B) } 333\qquad \textbf{(C) } 500\qquad \textbf{(D) } 668\qquad \textbf{(E) } 1001$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="455" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Since the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Least_common_multiple" title="Least common multiple">least common multiple</a> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/5/3/453edcf37b92ae9c651d8f77279cf14da68a612b.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{lcm}(4,6)=12$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="110" height="18" />, the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Element" title="Element">elements</a> that are common to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/5/5/2554b6496c3b678897e9b060ef00aa9f0a7d7ece.png" class="latex" alt="$T$" width="13" height="12" /> must be <a href="/wiki/index.php/Multiple" title="Multiple">multiples</a> of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />. 
</p><p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/7/b171f709008f0f8d97ee798793dde6c72a271e5f.png" class="latex" alt="$4\cdot2005=8020$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="118" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/c/bec90f9eea03be47425154a9b458f1016cf20900.png" class="latex" alt="$6\cdot2005=12030$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="127" height="13" />, several multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> that are in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/5/5/2554b6496c3b678897e9b060ef00aa9f0a7d7ece.png" class="latex" alt="$T$" width="13" height="12" /> won't be in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" />, but all multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> that are in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> will be in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/5/5/2554b6496c3b678897e9b060ef00aa9f0a7d7ece.png" class="latex" alt="$T$" width="13" height="12" />. So we just need to find the number of multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> that are in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" />. 
</p><p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/9/f798312a4e70e1051ee5e87382166e9fe660c760.png" class="latex" alt="$4\cdot3=12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="73" height="13" />, every <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />rd element of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> will be a multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />.
</p><p>Therefore the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/6/e2658921165a03ede976469c03b23e967ddacf22.png" class="latex" alt="$\left \lfloor\frac{2005}{3} \right \rfloor=\boxed{\textbf{(D) }  668}$" style="vertical-align: -18px" width="162" height="45" />
</p>
