<p>Three tiles are marked <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/a/4/6a47ca0fe7cb276abc022af6ac88ddae1a9d6894.png" class="latex" alt="$X$" width="15" height="12" /> and two other tiles are marked <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/d/51da37d984564162c87710ca27bea422f657fb73.png" class="latex" alt="$O$" width="13" height="12" />. The five tiles are randomly arranged in a row. What is the probability that the arrangement reads <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/a/8/ca817a927cd019ed18dd40916c250ca3e66ef03b.png" class="latex" alt="$XOXOX$" width="77" height="12" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/e/e/9eee8640bc2b4a06d3e5bd96d1cbdd970b840ef1.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } \frac{1}{12}\qquad \textbf{(B) } \frac{1}{10}\qquad \textbf{(C) } \frac{1}{6}\qquad \textbf{(D) } \frac{1}{4}\qquad \textbf{(E) } \frac{1}{3}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="393" height="38" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>There are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/6/9/4697875f08b629286eaab99b6fe458827327c416.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{5!}{2!3!}=10$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="74" height="38" /> distinct arrangements of three <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/a/4/6a47ca0fe7cb276abc022af6ac88ddae1a9d6894.png" class="latex" alt="$X$" width="15" height="12" />'s and two <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/d/51da37d984564162c87710ca27bea422f657fb73.png" class="latex" alt="$O$" width="13" height="12" />'s. 
</p><p>There is only <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> distinct arrangement that reads <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/a/8/ca817a927cd019ed18dd40916c250ca3e66ef03b.png" class="latex" alt="$XOXOX$" width="77" height="12" />.
</p><p>Therefore the desired <a href="/wiki/index.php/Probability" title="Probability">probability</a> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/8/b/a8beea5e8eb5f3675f7740a255ec30bc337cc7f6.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B) }\frac{1}{10}}$" style="vertical-align: -19px" width="70" height="49" />
</p>
