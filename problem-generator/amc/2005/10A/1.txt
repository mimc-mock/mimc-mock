<p>While eating out, Mike and Joe each tipped their server <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> dollars. Mike tipped <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/b/5/2b56bd637cb2c936d539be1b5000a64a898af389.png" class="latex" alt="$10\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of his bill and Joe tipped <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/8/1183a12017e48198b3352789cc39b223b17945d6.png" class="latex" alt="$20\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of his bill. What was the difference, in dollars between their bills? 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/7/6/7765e7e5412a3216b1eecc3dd9550ee9b16d4322.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } 2\qquad \textbf{(B) } 4\qquad \textbf{(C) } 5\qquad \textbf{(D) } 10\qquad \textbf{(E) } 20$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="373" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/0/f5047d1e0cbb50ec208923a22cd517c55100fa7b.png" class="latex" alt="$m$" width="15" height="8" /> be Mike's bill and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/2/8122aa89ea6e80784c6513d22787ad86e36ad0cc.png" class="latex" alt="$j$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="8" height="16" /> be Joe's bill. 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/7/417bee6f509d22ac44915254a85ae52cde1673d5.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{10}{100}m=2$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="80" height="38" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/d/7/4d760e7a2e0efdfd3ed388b138b385ce235a5229.png" class="latex" alt="$m=20$" width="57" height="12" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/1/2813660828780e0397d0425af425f5126adcae7a.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{20}{100}j=2$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="72" height="38" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/2/b/02ba5e6e060124e8403e94c7a429975ba8ce1b24.png" class="latex" alt="$j=10$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="51" height="16" />
</p><p>So the desired difference is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/b/b/abb23968af070724034034ab4dc5c036fa053e6d.png" class="latex" alt="$m-j=20-10=10 \Rightarrow \boxed{\textbf{(D) }10}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="267" height="30" />
</p>
