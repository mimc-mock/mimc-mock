<p>Josh and Mike live <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/0/030e19e4e2f1c074127cb50581e8496470c5419f.png" class="latex" alt="$13$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> miles apart. Yesterday Josh started to ride his bicycle toward Mike's house. A little later Mike started to ride his bicycle toward Josh's house. When they met, Josh had ridden for twice the length of time as Mike and at four-fifths of Mike's rate. How many miles had Mike ridden when they met? 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/2/a/d2aeabbef06dcadef265041da5c42a40a971a9a7.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } 4\qquad \textbf{(B) } 5\qquad \textbf{(C) } 6\qquad \textbf{(D) } 7\qquad \textbf{(E) } 8$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="355" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/0/f5047d1e0cbb50ec208923a22cd517c55100fa7b.png" class="latex" alt="$m$" width="15" height="8" /> be the distance in miles that Mike rode. 
</p><p>Since Josh rode for twice the length of time as Mike and at four-fifths of Mike's rate, he rode <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/3/0/630230083a7f3e6a1350758c82ad2fdc63f6d5d2.png" class="latex" alt="$2\cdot\frac{4}{5}\cdot m = \frac{8}{5}m$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="117" height="37" /> miles. 
</p><p>Since their combined distance was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/0/030e19e4e2f1c074127cb50581e8496470c5419f.png" class="latex" alt="$13$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> miles, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/a/1/1a1d58be65774d633abf7ff460162ae58bc836d8.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{8}{5}m + m = 13$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="109" height="37" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/d/5bdae4dd033a433dd0e967a7311ec0bd0a566c61.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{13}{5}m = 13$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="80" height="37" /> 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/8/5/585f7bc137b0c993c7ba5b65a112a78745f5da0d.png" class="latex" alt="$m = \boxed{\textbf{(B) }5}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="97" height="30" />
</p>
