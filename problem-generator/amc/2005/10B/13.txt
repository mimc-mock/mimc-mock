<p>How many numbers between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/5/9f5b442c275b4324f100c365b774e872b6b21f1e.png" class="latex" alt="$2005$" width="35" height="12" /> are integer multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> but not <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/3/b/b3b0c4eed5f5b18e1f30816616e7e22cff1b97ef.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } 501 \qquad \textbf{(B) } 668 \qquad \textbf{(C) } 835 \qquad \textbf{(D) } 1002 \qquad \textbf{(E) } 1169$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="464" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>To find the multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> but not <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />, you need to find the number of multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />, and then subtract twice the number of multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />, because you overcount and do not want to include them. The multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/d/8cd3085680b83259c37d4dfc4ad16b791e196ffd.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2005}{3} = 668\text{ }R1.$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="124" height="37" /> The multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/1/4/f14d24c610aeaee151c838236452a5f0d7901be7.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2005}{4} = 501 \text{ }R1$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="119" height="38" />. The multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/a/c/cacb2310a6fa834fb166c731dcb7b7de995b9c77.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2005}{12} = 167\text{ }R1.$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="124" height="38" /> So, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/6/2/862f16cfded1baa59e22bb88d7069acbfeacfe3d.png" class="latex" alt="$668+501-167-167 = \boxed{\textbf{(C) } 835}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="275" height="30" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>From <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />-<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />, the multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> but not <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/6/c/d6c4bc858cfeaff07b2ae4cab3dd26798e70d4d2.png" class="latex" alt="$3, 4, 6, 8,$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="67" height="17" />and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />, a total of five numbers. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/1/261d308e7ccf29a46d69981bb97112ce6bd7326b.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{5}{12}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="20" height="38" /> of positive integers are multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> but not <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />-<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />, the answer is approximately <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/3/7c3d776b964c342ade4e5a1288b03dac65559155.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{5}{12} \cdot 2005$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="71" height="38" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/f/4/ef450a01df9e988c3b41b212e0f5bd5c6ff8fa97.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) }835}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="75" height="30" />
</p>
