<p>On a certain math exam, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/b/5/2b56bd637cb2c936d539be1b5000a64a898af389.png" class="latex" alt="$10\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the students got <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/9/aa94edfb9739dcc66dad90f7dd5c148f44a57e2c.png" class="latex" alt="$70$" width="17" height="12" /> points, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/6/96633b73a86b9d9110e1ff824fce08def96ca296.png" class="latex" alt="$25\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> got <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/a/fea5fcc6d66685ee21f722efcd228eb44d38f262.png" class="latex" alt="$80$" width="17" height="12" /> points, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/8/1183a12017e48198b3352789cc39b223b17945d6.png" class="latex" alt="$20\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> got <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/e/7fea07b9bf739f8e420081f31186264b91ced2ce.png" class="latex" alt="$85$" width="17" height="12" /> points, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/a/87a8787ca8fcdf64f42e938d20fa64f63871646d.png" class="latex" alt="$15\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> got <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/c/bfcfee7bd8a5e15e5f6b7881c138de43651572a9.png" class="latex" alt="$90$" width="17" height="12" /> points, and the rest got <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/1/0510fa96a9909623c5966ad29945fabc84c704a4.png" class="latex" alt="$95$" width="17" height="12" /> points.  What is the difference between the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Mean" title="Mean">mean</a> and the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Median" title="Median">median</a> score on this exam?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/5/3/7532e7b5fbdf84cd69e12448f3f9a4201d2c54d4.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }\ 0 \qquad \textbf{(B) }\ 1 \qquad \textbf{(C) }\ 2 \qquad \textbf{(D) }\ 4 \qquad \textbf{(E) }\ 5$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="383" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>To begin, we see that the remaining <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/e/32e5939a7bdd847ec86582225cd653c240b4abce.png" class="latex" alt="$30\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the students got <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/1/0510fa96a9909623c5966ad29945fabc84c704a4.png" class="latex" alt="$95$" width="17" height="12" /> points. Assume that there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/3/f8366cd6196dd8a9da6d38a3e9eafb109e99d53e.png" class="latex" alt="$20$" width="17" height="12" /> students; we see that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> students got <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/9/aa94edfb9739dcc66dad90f7dd5c148f44a57e2c.png" class="latex" alt="$70$" width="17" height="12" /> points, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> students got <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/a/fea5fcc6d66685ee21f722efcd228eb44d38f262.png" class="latex" alt="$80$" width="17" height="12" /> points, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> students got <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/e/7fea07b9bf739f8e420081f31186264b91ced2ce.png" class="latex" alt="$85$" width="17" height="12" /> points, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> students got <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/c/bfcfee7bd8a5e15e5f6b7881c138de43651572a9.png" class="latex" alt="$90$" width="17" height="12" /> points, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> students got <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/1/0510fa96a9909623c5966ad29945fabc84c704a4.png" class="latex" alt="$95$" width="17" height="12" /> points. The median is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/e/7fea07b9bf739f8e420081f31186264b91ced2ce.png" class="latex" alt="$85$" width="17" height="12" />, since the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/c/b/2cb190f950e911576a55d3d3f27d28fdd6e40493.png" class="latex" alt="$10^{\text{th}}$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="30" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/5/7/857cdd774f39c0d45a2a091c4d75dd55222b9b05.png" class="latex" alt="$11^{\text{th}}$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="30" height="16" /> terms are both <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/e/7fea07b9bf739f8e420081f31186264b91ced2ce.png" class="latex" alt="$85$" width="17" height="12" />. The mean is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/0/ce0aceb5d94680129e865440a19e5247636f161b.png" class="latex" alt="$\dfrac{70\,(2)+80\,(5)+85\,(4)+90\,(3)+95\,(6)}{20}=\dfrac{1720}{20}=86$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="421" height="38" />. The difference between the mean and median, therefore, is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/c/2/4c2f45ba306e2fa3a8fbeae63fa939779e2b8a16.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B)}\ 1}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="54" height="28" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>The remaining <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/e/32e5939a7bdd847ec86582225cd653c240b4abce.png" class="latex" alt="$30\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the students got <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/1/0510fa96a9909623c5966ad29945fabc84c704a4.png" class="latex" alt="$95$" width="17" height="12" /> points.
The mean is equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/4/6/e467833e275bd106abff315cb7ff7c74265ec43f.png" class="latex" alt="$10\%\cdot70 + 25\%\cdot80 + 20\%\cdot85 + 15\%\cdot90 + 30\%\cdot95 = 86$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="454" height="15" />.
The score greater than or equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/7/9f7f4de02215a8c5319886c13b2e291811346e1c.png" class="latex" alt="$50\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of other scores is the median. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/f/5/af59f5a81354c1925a58cb897e1d142111b1d7c6.png" class="latex" alt="$35\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> scored <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/a/fea5fcc6d66685ee21f722efcd228eb44d38f262.png" class="latex" alt="$80$" width="17" height="12" /> or lower and the next <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/8/1183a12017e48198b3352789cc39b223b17945d6.png" class="latex" alt="$20\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> scored <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/e/7fea07b9bf739f8e420081f31186264b91ced2ce.png" class="latex" alt="$85$" width="17" height="12" />, the median is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/e/7fea07b9bf739f8e420081f31186264b91ced2ce.png" class="latex" alt="$85$" width="17" height="12" />. The difference between the mean and the median is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/c/2/4c2f45ba306e2fa3a8fbeae63fa939779e2b8a16.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B)}\ 1}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="54" height="28" />.
</p><p>~mobius247
</p>
