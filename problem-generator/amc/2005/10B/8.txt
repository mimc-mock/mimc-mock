<p>An <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />-foot by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />-foot bathroom floor is tiled with square tiles of size <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> foot by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> foot.  Each tile has a pattern consisting of four white quarter circles of radius <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/f/3/1f3cc0ad966b3e637cd199607cbf0d1befc0e491.png" class="latex" alt="$1/2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="26" height="18" /> foot centered at each corner of the tile.  The remaining portion of the tile is shaded.  How many square feet of the floor are shaded?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/e/cbe2bb4966beb1c1cbc889938c8e120b7d80639b.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] unitsize(2cm); defaultpen(linewidth(.8pt)); fill(unitsquare,gray); filldraw(Arc((0,0),.5,0,90)--(0,0)--cycle,white,black); filldraw(Arc((1,0),.5,90,180)--(1,0)--cycle,white,black); filldraw(Arc((1,1),.5,180,270)--(1,1)--cycle,white,black); filldraw(Arc((0,1),.5,270,360)--(0,1)--cycle,white,black); [/asy]" width="98" height="98" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/9/5/d953ba9d249c20aa4700d32d3d861407f59f7ccc.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }\ 80-20\pi      \qquad \textbf{(B) }\ 60-10\pi      \qquad \textbf{(C) }\ 80-10\pi      \qquad \textbf{(D) }\ 60+10\pi      \qquad \textbf{(E) }\ 80+10\pi$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="685" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>There are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/a/fea5fcc6d66685ee21f722efcd228eb44d38f262.png" class="latex" alt="$80$" width="17" height="12" /> tiles.  Each tile has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/7/617c9a81577193c333f0b80c64514805a1e55857.png" class="latex" alt="$[\mbox{square} - 4 \cdot (\mbox{quarter circle})]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="210" height="18" /> shaded.  Thus:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/6/196e2b269a7dde966c160cf65bde833a9d4a2840.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} \mbox{shaded area} &amp;= 80 \left( 1 - 4 \cdot \dfrac{1}{4} \cdot \pi \cdot \left(\dfrac{1}{2}\right)^2\right) \\ &amp;= 80\left(1-\dfrac{1}{4}\pi\right) \\ &amp;= \boxed{\textbf{(A) }80-20\pi}. \end{align*}" width="305" height="137" />
4 quarters of a circle is a circle so that may save you 0.5 seconds&#160;:)
</p>
