<p>Six ants simultaneously stand on the six <a href="/wiki/index.php/Vertex" title="Vertex">vertices</a> of a regular <a href="/wiki/index.php/Octahedron" title="Octahedron">octahedron</a>, with each ant at a different vertex. Simultaneously and independently, each ant moves from its vertex to one of the four adjacent vertices, each with equal <a href="/wiki/index.php/Probability" title="Probability">probability</a>. What is the probability that no two ants arrive at the same vertex?
