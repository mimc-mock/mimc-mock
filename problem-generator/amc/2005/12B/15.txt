<p>The sum of four two-digit numbers is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/9/f490c9585fc04684a97f897f87d49d972658089a.png" class="latex" alt="$221$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="12" />. None of the eight digits is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" /> and no two of them are the same. Which of the following is <b>not</b> included among the eight digits?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/5/1/551ca347bd4c5ec53a5e5a468ad7169fc6436b28.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ 1      \qquad \mathrm{(B)}\ 2      \qquad \mathrm{(C)}\ 3      \qquad \mathrm{(D)}\ 4      \qquad \mathrm{(E)}\ 5$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="342" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/9/f490c9585fc04684a97f897f87d49d972658089a.png" class="latex" alt="$221$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="12" /> can be written as the sum of four two-digit numbers, let's say <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/e/8/ae877b98a8087facc8bc45fc78cc11275dbbe762.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{ae}$" width="18" height="11" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/6/1/a61f32129f477c872cb0ccc9052582df36a4e2d9.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{bf}$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="19" height="19" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/0/f30d17b315655b847e9d60c5fd04b5051d7f5fd8.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{cg}$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="17" height="14" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/e/1/de145983e02ef025597688e972e9f72c0532f5b8.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{dh}$" width="20" height="16" />. Then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/3/bf308ba2fd8dbc746c32e42a4ffa8b3b8b56a497.png" class="latex" alt="$221= 10(a+b+c+d)+(e+f+g+h)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="326" height="18" />. The last digit of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/9/f490c9585fc04684a97f897f87d49d972658089a.png" class="latex" alt="$221$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/a/f/5af0dfa14fb3d44abc1c0a1097aa928942fb7c88.png" class="latex" alt="$10(a+b+c+d)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="132" height="18" /> won't affect the units digits, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/4/6c4aa20de87963ddbc50901806fc4a6a423f1bae.png" class="latex" alt="$(e+f+g+h)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="118" height="18" /> must end with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />. The smallest value <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/4/6c4aa20de87963ddbc50901806fc4a6a423f1bae.png" class="latex" alt="$(e+f+g+h)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="118" height="18" /> can have is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/c/2dc05ccf6afdfd2f971a8ffdcafe00dc69fe0a41.png" class="latex" alt="$(1+2+3+4)=10$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="159" height="18" />, and the greatest value is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/c/2/cc2bf57e840c2c88ba5b474b4ab25261b8f9a306.png" class="latex" alt="$(6+7+8+9)=30$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="159" height="18" />. Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/4/6c4aa20de87963ddbc50901806fc4a6a423f1bae.png" class="latex" alt="$(e+f+g+h)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="118" height="18" /> must equal <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/8/c6878713578626763c38433b3f4c8c2205ad0c15.png" class="latex" alt="$11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/2/5/f25d31faf472ddb4c698a14522fe8125c06f8dd8.png" class="latex" alt="$21$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />. 
</p><p>Case 1: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/7/2/37289f5a2159593a5d6aa777602142d031069707.png" class="latex" alt="$(e+f+g+h)=11$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="161" height="18" />
</p><p>The only distinct positive integers that can add up to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/8/c6878713578626763c38433b3f4c8c2205ad0c15.png" class="latex" alt="$11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/6/b863f57a3646a8f054189824b188eb87498f64bd.png" class="latex" alt="$(1+2+3+5)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="115" height="18" />. So, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />,<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />,<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> must include four of the five numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/3/ad363aba6941e57637c0fe53fe17894a532933e6.png" class="latex" alt="$(4,6,7,8,9)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="90" height="18" />. We have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/d/4/3d4d1b4e90af962d9ce1af266f291c58356a79cd.png" class="latex" alt="$10(a+b+c+d)=221-11=210$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="276" height="18" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/6/d/f6da3eded38b7827e922b5144181dea7e418215f.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+c+d=21$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="142" height="14" />. We can add all of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/4/3/243f1f33b799e12dfb67b5c44d6c7822d402b1de.png" class="latex" alt="$4+6+7+8+9=34$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="176" height="14" />, and try subtracting one number to get to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/2/5/f25d31faf472ddb4c698a14522fe8125c06f8dd8.png" class="latex" alt="$21$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />, but none of them work. Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/4/6c4aa20de87963ddbc50901806fc4a6a423f1bae.png" class="latex" alt="$(e+f+g+h)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="118" height="18" /> cannot add up to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/8/c6878713578626763c38433b3f4c8c2205ad0c15.png" class="latex" alt="$11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />.
</p><p>Case 2: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/0/0/a007e12098024777fae6dfc6b2a0dc6d2ed1cf5c.png" class="latex" alt="$(e+f+g+h)=21$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="161" height="18" />
</p><p>Checking all the values for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/a/a3a59bb1293ee3f6dec19de4019a7178874219ae.png" class="latex" alt="$e$" width="8" height="8" />,<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/b/2/bb2c93730dbb48558bb3c4738c956c4e8f816437.png" class="latex" alt="$f$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="10" height="16" />,<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/1/1/311cabda3a9b09f0dde217303ca9d1cd9201dcf6.png" class="latex" alt="$g$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" />,and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/8/8189a5b5a0917b8c93350827be4038af1839139d.png" class="latex" alt="$h$" width="10" height="12" /> each individually may be time-consuming, instead of only having <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> solution like Case 1. We can try a different approach by looking at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/1/e21f0f14ddab72c13689bfd3c660312c319b70af.png" class="latex" alt="$(a+b+c+d)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="114" height="18" /> first. If <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/0/0/a007e12098024777fae6dfc6b2a0dc6d2ed1cf5c.png" class="latex" alt="$(e+f+g+h)=21$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="161" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/4/5742bdeb9f18712995c1d2e0dc74005dd8e9f855.png" class="latex" alt="$10(a+b+c+d)=221-21=200$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="276" height="18" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/5/1/a5133cc4a8357e64fe28882b2c9a32d3a19887cc.png" class="latex" alt="$(a+b+c+d)=20$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="157" height="18" />. That means <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/2/b/42bc3818ffbfc51db9687e727db508a5ee6e4fd6.png" class="latex" alt="$(a+b+c+d)+(e+f+g+h)=21+20=41$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="382" height="18" />. We know <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/9/2f9edd41500b863fbfa631a64fecccb5a3f0cc3f.png" class="latex" alt="$(1+2+3+4+5+6+7+8+9)=45$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="315" height="18" />, so the missing digit is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/a/5/1a5190f7afcf3602bdcb78249985318389cb898f.png" class="latex" alt="$45-41=\boxed{\mathrm{(D)}\ 4}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="137" height="29" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Alternatively, we know that a number is congruent to the sum of its digits mod 9, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/c/b8c4db07d572ac12b61de3315483df34351ee81b.png" class="latex" alt="$221 \equiv 5 \equiv 1 + 2 + 3 + 4 + 5 + 6 + 7 + 8 + 9 - d \equiv -d$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="424" height="14" />, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> is some digit. Clearly, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/0/9/c09dc653bc392b8d7af044c15476a2ec1f115db4.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{d = 4}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="54" height="24" />.
</p>
