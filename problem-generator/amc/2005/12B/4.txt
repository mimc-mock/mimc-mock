<p>At the beginning of the school year, Lisa's goal was to earn an <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> on at least <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/b/7/bb7e03ccc8985ba9d029ea4add6e3e15ab9a953a.png" class="latex" alt="$80\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of her <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/6/906734e2f1010e9a171ffee8050cbc0649cea37c.png" class="latex" alt="$50$" width="17" height="12" /> quizzes for the year.  She earned an <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> on <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/1/1d111a5e00ee5ea6700bb629994dc629874c505b.png" class="latex" alt="$22$" width="17" height="12" /> of the first <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/1/651ba418a810d971dbc8a326d49a71fe1541fae0.png" class="latex" alt="$30$" width="17" height="12" /> quizzes.  If she is to achieve her goal, on at most how many of the remaining quizzes can she earn a grade lower than an <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/b/2/9b29396993e55c4496f2b9acf510f9191ebaefaa.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }\ 1      \qquad \textbf{(B) }\ 2      \qquad \textbf{(C) }\ 3      \qquad \textbf{(D) }\ 4      \qquad \textbf{(E) }\ 5$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="383" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Lisa's goal was to get an <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> on <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/4/f/24f5c3320e5a1ab3453ae60518e8bb9520d29b6f.png" class="latex" alt="$80\% \cdot 50 = 40$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="106" height="14" /> quizzes.  She already has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" />'s on <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/1/1d111a5e00ee5ea6700bb629994dc629874c505b.png" class="latex" alt="$22$" width="17" height="12" /> quizzes, so she needs to get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" />'s on <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/5/c/55c4beb7cb06f4d07e8205a8870c33deab24c5fa.png" class="latex" alt="$40-22=18$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="100" height="13" /> more.  There are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/0/d/c0d1fcc71445d5dd7e5699c1fb03b3beb4a7ffbd.png" class="latex" alt="$50-30=20$" width="100" height="12" /> quizzes left, so she can afford to get less than an <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> on <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/6/e16c5dc1a1e26ca2af8eddbc78abe2372d37c118.png" class="latex" alt="$20-18=\boxed{\textbf{(B) }2}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="140" height="30" /> of them.
</p><p>Here, only the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" />'s matter... No complicated stuff!
</p>
