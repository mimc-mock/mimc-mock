<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution</span></h2>
<p>First, notice that there must be two such numbers: one greater than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/3/34318c5cacc51b7ee802150c608c6d7c25f66eb5.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_69$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="45" height="19" /> and one less than it. Furthermore, they both have to be the same distance away, namely <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/a/d/3ad969b4887eb08f905ccda10b28d09f4452dbb7.png" class="latex" alt="$2(\log_610 - 1)$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="109" height="20" />. Let these two numbers be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/f/5/5f55565ea92519eacf5b63f33aa45c19c4dac14c.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_6a$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="45" height="19" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/8/b68bd1a422ae6413851b40b59fdc5f9520188b73.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_6b$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="44" height="19" />. Because they are equidistant from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/3/34318c5cacc51b7ee802150c608c6d7c25f66eb5.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_69$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="45" height="19" />, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/0/3/d037fd464aa1f7f0975b758ae0f4e3eadd6b6071.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{\log_6a + \log_6b}{2} = \log_69$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="181" height="39" />. Using log properties, this simplifies to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/9/4/b94b2298f5768ff17e7879c031cb8d6f33227237.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_6{\sqrt{ab}} = \log_69$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="136" height="24" />. We then have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/6/5469453711e2e256afe34caed13a6fba58fd2b41.png" class="latex" alt="$\sqrt{ab} = 9$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="68" height="21" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/0/e000a8157e74874c00631853e947016081c8c77a.png" class="latex" alt="$ab = \boxed{\textbf{(E) } 81}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="107" height="30" />.
</p><p>~ jamesl123456
