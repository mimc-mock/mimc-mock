<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> be a real number, and let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/e/a/9ea31e5c5613f025b5be8aca425d318168491d89.png" class="latex" alt="$z_1$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="14" height="10" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/9/5/29535bc04682b0b477c682ee52dded5983bc41cb.png" class="latex" alt="$z_2$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="14" height="10" /> be the two complex numbers satisfying the equation
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/7/d17aff4f0c19f56de7166766d623793d151bcbd3.png" class="latex" alt="$z^2 - cz + 10 = 0$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="132" height="18" />. <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> is real, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/7/7a7df6319d1825673afeef624f2ea7506ad87d07.png" class="latex" alt="$z_2 = \bar z_1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="58" height="15" />.
We have
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/6/9/0695439ca966c11ae4e95c70959efb6505475671.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} 10 &amp; = z_1 z_2 \\ &amp; = z_1 \bar z_1 \\ &amp; = |z_1|^2 , \end{align*}" width="78" height="69" />
where the first equality follows from Vieta's formula.
</p><p>Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/b/f/dbf25ddb335f1d698c50d6a8ac05d4e908567550.png" class="latex" alt="$|z_1| = \sqrt{10}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="85" height="24" />.
</p><p>We have
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/a/5/ea53f050917ca5705683851f1ab13c4a79aa38ee.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} c &amp; = z_1 + z_2 \\ &amp; = z_1 + \bar z_1 \\ &amp; = 2 {\rm Re}(z_1), \end{align*}" width="96" height="65" />
where the first equality follows from Vieta's formula.
</p><p>Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/4/1/e4109708c8ca34d9e814904244da99d24fe9bf8e.png" class="latex" alt="${\rm Re}(z_1) = \frac{c}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="91" height="34" />.
</p><p>We have
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/0/a/80a847e9a341df8d2147007e1a1e4e4b13764290.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} \frac{1}{z_1} &amp; = \frac{1}{10}\cdot\frac{10}{z_1} \\ &amp; = \frac{1}{10}\cdot\frac{z_1 z_2}{z_1} \\ &amp; = \frac{z_2}{10} \\ &amp; = \frac{\bar z_1}{10}. \end{align*}" width="111" height="166" />
where the second equality follows from Vieta's formula.
</p><p>We have
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/b/ffb6a76c6310fa3af7ba196478d21e33b7ae0641.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} \frac{1}{z_2} &amp; = \frac{1}{10}\cdot\frac{10}{z_2} \\ &amp; = \frac{1}{10}\cdot\frac{z_1 z_2}{z_2} \\ &amp; = \frac{z_1}{10}. \end{align*}" width="111" height="125" />
where the second equality follows from Vieta's formula.
</p><p>Therefore,
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/3/6/23658039a36cfa5bce315f3c7ce276b6dfd71923.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} {\rm Area} \ Q &amp; = \frac{1}{2} \left| {\rm Re}(z_1) \right| \cdot 2 \left| {\rm Im}(z_1) \right| \cdot \left( 1 - \frac{1}{10^2} \right) \\ &amp; = \frac{1}{2} |c| \sqrt{10 - \frac{c^2}{4}} \left( 1 - \frac{1}{10^2} \right) \\ &amp; = \frac{1 - \frac{1}{10^2}}{4} \sqrt{c^2 \left( 40 - c^2 \right)} \\ &amp; \leq \frac{1 - \frac{1}{10^2}}{4} \cdot \frac{c^2 + \left( 40 - c^2 \right)}{2} \\ &amp; = \frac{1 - \frac{1}{10^2}}{4} \cdot 20 , \end{align*}" width="343" height="238" />
where the inequality follows from the AM-GM inequality, and it is augmented to an equality if and only if <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/d/bade148ecd6d10ce5ce1a0811cc3566958e094a0.png" class="latex" alt="$c^2 = 40 - c^2$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="97" height="18" />.
Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/e/c3efd72234e5dd070484c169bd46b20f3f369dcc.png" class="latex" alt="$|c| = 2 \sqrt{5} \approx \boxed{\textbf{(A) 4.5}}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="173" height="30" />.
</p><p>~Steven Chen (Professor Chen Education Palace, www.professorchenedu.com)
</p>
