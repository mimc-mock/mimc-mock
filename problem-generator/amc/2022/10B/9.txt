<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/a/baa3a6af01ebb7998090d727e422467f22c2b3c2.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{n}{(n+1)!} = \frac{1}{n!} - \frac{1}{(n+1)!}$" style="vertical-align: -18px" width="199" height="43" />, and therefore this sum is a telescoping sum, which is equivalent to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/2/1/b21c826de6221e801705a145bb3263db4dbf2835.png" class="latex" alt="$1 - \frac{1}{2022!}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="79" height="38" />. Our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/d/41df2acb9508009f5ef8d8ae515752459fa176dd.png" class="latex" alt="$1 + 2022 = \boxed{\textbf{(D)}\ 2023}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="176" height="30" />.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>We add <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/f/9/df91601ae54a1e5332021338a6537be000732ab0.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{2022!}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="47" height="38" /> to the original expression <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/5/ad521749a741d5009324c6088b8538c3fb56a187.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\left(\frac{1}{2!}+\frac{2}{3!}+\frac{3}{4!}+\cdots+\frac{2021}{2022!}\right)+\frac{1}{2022!}=\left(\frac{1}{2!}+\frac{2}{3!}+\frac{3}{4!}+\dots+\frac{2020}{2021!}\right)+\frac{1}{2021!}.\]" width="615" height="43" />
This sum clearly telescopes, thus we end up with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/2/992faa4b4bb973257c17cb0170ba5b750ee4ad3f.png" class="latex" alt="$\left(\frac{1}{2!}+\frac{2}{3!}\right)+\frac{1}{3!}=\frac{2}{2!}=1$" style="vertical-align: -18px" width="203" height="45" />. Thus the original expression is equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/b/f9b340b9eb8fb0082f1ba15b865586e189249c6d.png" class="latex" alt="$1-\frac{1}{2022!}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="79" height="38" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/4/19429e6c387ec6d992281084c5bbe0d76122f947.png" class="latex" alt="$1+2022=\boxed{\textbf{(D)}\ 2023}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="176" height="30" />.
