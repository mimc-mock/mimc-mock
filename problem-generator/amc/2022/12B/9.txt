<p>The sequence <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/d/c/bdc8022d98e12bf303ba0ab97dba36f436877dcc.png" class="latex" alt="$a_0,a_1,a_2,\cdots$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="95" height="11" /> is a strictly increasing arithmetic sequence of positive integers such that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/d/11d8bfbe431d47e1ad7ca13f8645d23e4cc433c3.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[2^{a_7}=2^{27} \cdot a_7.\]" width="104" height="18" /> What is the minimum possible value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/e/9/5e97a8af68fbc8e357d3ee0eba452022b06c1875.png" class="latex" alt="$a_2$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="15" height="10" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/4/2f4ee657c5a94193e684c5d4d31d5c849f6e8e16.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 8 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 12 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 16 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 17 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 22$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="387" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>We can rewrite the given equation as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/9/8/a98c128b98b6544a3acfec20102273934a9289be.png" class="latex" alt="$2^{a_7-27}=a_7$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="90" height="20" />. Hence, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/2/012aa2573a77f1bfe01a31fdebe941ee94b10808.png" class="latex" alt="$a_7$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="16" height="10" /> must be a power of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> and larger than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/5/1051e7d41ec0b8bd688a872be17538413f4ee97b.png" class="latex" alt="$27$" width="18" height="12" />. The first power of 2 that is larger than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/5/1051e7d41ec0b8bd688a872be17538413f4ee97b.png" class="latex" alt="$27$" width="18" height="12" />, namely <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/4/4f4b8590f8aa9f9894b3891dd5dede0e354fa04e.png" class="latex" alt="$32$" width="17" height="12" />, does satisfy the equation: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/1/e0107ac7b39b9ca5c923188422cf2bbbf2574718.png" class="latex" alt="$2^{32 - 27} = 2^5 = 32$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="132" height="17" />. In fact, this is the only solution; <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/4/d141f5cf56f64a34b5c309729264741f21d6724a.png" class="latex" alt="$2^{a_7-27}$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="49" height="17" /> is exponential whereas <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/2/012aa2573a77f1bfe01a31fdebe941ee94b10808.png" class="latex" alt="$a_7$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="16" height="10" /> is linear, so their graphs will not intersect again.
</p><p>Now, let the common difference in the sequence be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" />. Hence, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/1/1/311d6baf84498333dc7e114a0533b837f03dce85.png" class="latex" alt="$a_0 = 32 - 7d$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="102" height="17" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/1/6/c16550fada5a905e20205eda538833559bb23d3f.png" class="latex" alt="$a_2 = 32 - 5d$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="102" height="17" />. To minimize <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/e/9/5e97a8af68fbc8e357d3ee0eba452022b06c1875.png" class="latex" alt="$a_2$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="15" height="10" />, we maxmimize <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" />. Since the sequence contains only positive integers, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/a/2/5a202f0e848c40caa2fc2ea05e5cf11a325c176e.png" class="latex" alt="$32 - 7d &gt; 0$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="94" height="16" /> and hence <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/9/9/e990a096990720905f4f1f39e83594723247cd39.png" class="latex" alt="$d \leq 4$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="42" height="15" />. When <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/9/b/e9b7a0f00374ac1ac5553a55795b9531d4bdf042.png" class="latex" alt="$d = 4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="42" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/f/1/df1f017c85f8fa319f5b68135c1e70e3d6e579e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a_2 = \boxed{\textbf{(B)}\ 12}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="107" height="30" />.
</p><p>~<a href="/wiki/index.php/User:Bxiao31415" title="User:Bxiao31415">Bxiao31415</a>
</p>
