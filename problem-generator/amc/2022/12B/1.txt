<p>Define <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/a/c/facd9b0a167d6512bf167e2bdc04e52b35cb200f.png" class="latex" alt="$x\diamond y$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="39" height="13" /> to be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/2/e526fbac5ee18e20a7293e9cb9c317989b31988a.png" class="latex" alt="$|x-y|$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="50" height="18" /> for all real numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/6/e/56e228943301b048f242377b3b24717037d5996f.png" class="latex" alt="$y.$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="11" /> What is the value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/0/a/30abfad2fabbf18f3fae4d47e45fdcc4bf8a8d94.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[(1\diamond(2\diamond3))-((1\diamond2)\diamond3)?\]" width="208" height="18" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/2/2/b22fcb9b44af2c1cc068fe270482746071e3e5af.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ {-}2 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ {-}1 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 0 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 1 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 2$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="379" height="18" />
