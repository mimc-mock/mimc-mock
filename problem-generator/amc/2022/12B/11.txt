<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Converting both summands to exponential form, 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/6/c760c36926a63e2393eabbe1f38e47291a539182.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} -1 + i\sqrt{3} &amp;= 2e^{\frac{2\pi i}{3}}, \\ -1 - i\sqrt{3} &amp;= 2e^{-\frac{2\pi i}{3}} = 2e^{\frac{4\pi i}{3}}. \end{align*}" width="216" height="49" />
Notice that both are scaled copies of the third roots of unity.
When we replace the summands with their exponential form, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/9/119d918eac02e11217a3156cbae18f23823776ff.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[f(n) = \left(e^{\frac{2\pi i}{3}}\right)^n + \left(e^{\frac{4\pi i}{3}}\right)^n.\]" width="210" height="32" />
When we substitute <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/6/32603da0074bd25bba0f5d638b0664ab695b67cc.png" class="latex" alt="$n = 2022$" width="70" height="12" />, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/4/9/94919bc4bcb8414ff8ca067e352db7cdcf351cf3.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[f(2022) = \left(e^{\frac{2\pi i}{3}}\right)^{2022} + \left(e^{\frac{4\pi i}{3}}\right)^{2022}.\]" width="272" height="35" />
We can rewrite <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/c/b/0cb39dbc06a6cd0a0f4e4dd1e3ff461fb7026ba6.png" class="latex" alt="$2022$" width="35" height="12" /> as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/5/1/251b065c19c721e41102509af48add97a3983246.png" class="latex" alt="$3 \cdot 674$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="51" height="14" />, how does that help?
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/0/e1052a9fe2051eb77ec32b33115b5551d1a1d2b0.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[f(2022) = \left(e^{\frac{2\pi i}{3}}\right)^{3 \cdot 674} + \left(e^{\frac{4\pi i}{3}}\right)^{3 \cdot 674} = \left(\left(e^{\frac{2\pi i}{3}}\right)^{3}\right)^{674} + \left(\left(e^{\frac{4\pi i}{3}}\right)^{3}\right)^{674} = 1^{674} + 1^{674} = \boxed{\textbf{(E)} \ 2}.\]" width="695" height="46" />
Since any third root of unity must cube to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />.
