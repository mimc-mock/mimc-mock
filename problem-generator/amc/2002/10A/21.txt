<p>The mean, median, unique mode, and range of a collection of eight integers are all equal to 8. The largest integer that can be an element of this collection is 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/d/b/3dbc1285494ac244dcaefe6d3beb466f339dcc19.png" class="latex" alt="$\text{(A) }11 \qquad \text{(B) }12 \qquad \text{(C) }13 \qquad \text{(D) }14 \qquad \text{(E) }15$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="375" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>As the unique mode is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />, there are at least two <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />s.
</p><p>As the range is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> and one of the numbers is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />, the largest one can be at most <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/5/9a5b4928c8fe50ce3c2428da3bee3505e891b788.png" class="latex" alt="$16$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />.
</p><p>If the largest one is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/5/9a5b4928c8fe50ce3c2428da3bee3505e891b788.png" class="latex" alt="$16$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />, then the smallest one is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />, and thus the mean is strictly larger than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />, which is a contradiction.
</p><p>If we have 2 8's we can add find the numbers 4, 6, 7, 8, 8, 9, 10, 12.
This is a possible solution but has not reached the maximum.
</p><p>If we have 4 8's we can find the numbers 6, 6, 6, 8, 8, 8, 8, 14.
</p><p>We can also see that they satisfy the need for the mode, median, and range to be 8. This means that the answer will be
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/3/3/633e41dd80e3df880bb39bc696a89d119fd27faf.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\text{(D)}\ 14 }$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="61" height="28" />. ~By QWERTYUIOPASDFGHJKLZXCVBNM
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>We could express this collection as integers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/0/b104a7cec6854200c0282dccf53f4311a306a1c9.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{1}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" /> through <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/1/ad1c6f33c16d12b510187e59253a3b9a69e6491f.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{8}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />, with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/0/b104a7cec6854200c0282dccf53f4311a306a1c9.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{1}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" /> being the smallest and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/1/ad1c6f33c16d12b510187e59253a3b9a69e6491f.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{8}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" /> being the largest.
</p><p>Since the mean is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />, we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/3/d53909d77d352b88e5a4f4d7034ae5e49dafcb4f.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{4}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/a/eeae93118449d53a43022ef1a6ca94a9db21a580.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{5}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" /> must also be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />. If they were not, the other numbers, which are lesser and greater than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/3/d53909d77d352b88e5a4f4d7034ae5e49dafcb4f.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{4}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/a/eeae93118449d53a43022ef1a6ca94a9db21a580.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{5}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" /> respectively, would not be able to satisfy the condition that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> is the mode.
</p><p>There are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> terms and the mean is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />. This tells us that the sum of all the numbers is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/e/05e228ac5bee7b9ffb60cacf50f3f2c7c31f8038.png" class="latex" alt="$64$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="13" />.
</p><p>We want to maximize the value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/1/ad1c6f33c16d12b510187e59253a3b9a69e6491f.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{8}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />, so we set  <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/b/e/0bea182db8605801e31b1adc79bc2012173dc980.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{6}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/3/79396341648ef156f24614916cf47f8d9e78d365.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{7}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" /> to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> as well.
</p><p>Knowing that we want to minimize numbers and that the range is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> , we set <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/0/b104a7cec6854200c0282dccf53f4311a306a1c9.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{1}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/5/9957b63459be81449ac74819db2000948ea0a835.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{2}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/3/4/b3486021d68eeaccbd4d3f79417a9cf7231cfbd6.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{3}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" /> equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/5/875e7d1aabb6d910db47867c8d59476346a3ac97.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{8} - 8$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="50" height="17" />.
</p><p>{<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/0/b104a7cec6854200c0282dccf53f4311a306a1c9.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{1}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/5/9957b63459be81449ac74819db2000948ea0a835.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{2}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/3/4/b3486021d68eeaccbd4d3f79417a9cf7231cfbd6.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{3}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/3/d53909d77d352b88e5a4f4d7034ae5e49dafcb4f.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{4}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/a/eeae93118449d53a43022ef1a6ca94a9db21a580.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{5}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/b/e/0bea182db8605801e31b1adc79bc2012173dc980.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{6}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/3/79396341648ef156f24614916cf47f8d9e78d365.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{7}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/1/ad1c6f33c16d12b510187e59253a3b9a69e6491f.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{8}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />} <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/e/d5eb8726a34932d28103ce7ce17456421222603f.png" class="latex" alt="$=$" style="vertical-align: 2px" width="13" height="5" /> {<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/5/875e7d1aabb6d910db47867c8d59476346a3ac97.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{8} - 8$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="50" height="17" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/5/875e7d1aabb6d910db47867c8d59476346a3ac97.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{8} - 8$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="50" height="17" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/5/875e7d1aabb6d910db47867c8d59476346a3ac97.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{8} - 8$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="50" height="17" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/1/ad1c6f33c16d12b510187e59253a3b9a69e6491f.png" class="latex" alt="$a\textsubscript{8}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="19" height="13" />}
</p><p>Letting the sum of all the numbers be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/e/05e228ac5bee7b9ffb60cacf50f3f2c7c31f8038.png" class="latex" alt="$64$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="13" />, we find that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/c/33c019d396974387dc3fca23a1f717a35b9b610d.png" class="latex" alt="$32 + 4a_8 - 24 = 64$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="151" height="17" />, which simplifies to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/b/7ab69cfbd1a9cb18424d23af8dfc091c16fbdaec.png" class="latex" alt="$4a_8 = 56$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="70" height="17" />. Solving, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/3/3/633e41dd80e3df880bb39bc696a89d119fd27faf.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\text{(D)}\ 14 }$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="61" height="28" />. ~By SK80, mod_x for minor edits
