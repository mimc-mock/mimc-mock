<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/1/28184d1392b9cc70daf5d2563f9f1be67c4d8112.png" class="latex" alt="$x=a + 1 = b + 2 = c + 3 = d + 4 = a + b + c + d + 5$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="423" height="14" />. Since one of the sums involves <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/0/2d02bb2275100261109b043261e702124460a83e.png" class="latex" alt="$a, b, c,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="45" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/2/3/9234823c756646d3c67387b3f73375eed1aebd6d.png" class="latex" alt="$d,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="16" /> it makes sense to consider <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/4/ed4977828049d99f20ccd3b4acb6bbd43c2eb70f.png" class="latex" alt="$4x$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="19" height="12" />. We have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/5/2/552a48d02c71f777e5fe4e7a35ff0271964b2e34.png" class="latex" alt="$4x=(a+1)+(b+2)+(c+3)+(d+4)=a+b+c+d+10=4(a+b+c+d)+20$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="681" height="18" />. Rearranging, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/b/f/4bfd1c4e4bf3913ac5f50ee548618dab77f6a353.png" class="latex" alt="$3(a+b+c+d)=-10$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="180" height="18" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/5/d/95db23f053189a4530c4ebeba9c5f2978851768c.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+c+d=\frac{-10}{3}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="160" height="37" />. Thus, our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/d/5/bd5904c715051d2b0c600bcf67eba46a274d1a6d.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\text{(B)}\ -10/3}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="100" height="29" />.
</p>
