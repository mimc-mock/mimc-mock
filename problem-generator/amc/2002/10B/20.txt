<p>Let a, b, and c be real numbers such that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/b/1dbe57d3e6c03521388f73c8abdedac3516eed3c.png" class="latex" alt="$a-7b+8c=4$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="121" height="14" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/9/4/894b914d7a91e26c592a4b9f78860b5192b43d33.png" class="latex" alt="$8a+4b-c=7$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="121" height="14" />. Then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/3/2d363059d912b28fc24dfc18c1acb67a2d5c53c6.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2-b^2+c^2$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="91" height="16" /> is 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/0/4/a0476c92e3a4383e1682d4f21b4cee5a61025491.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)\ }0\qquad\mathrm{(B)\ }1\qquad\mathrm{(C)\ }4\qquad\mathrm{(D)\ }7\qquad\mathrm{(E)\ }8$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="342" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h3>
<p>Rearranging, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/7/9/47968fd68093ef3c4e62bda4b655e20bca3fd612.png" class="latex" alt="$a+8c=7b+4$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="121" height="14" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/a/67a9549d883feb1cc57768a26a7047644843dcdd.png" class="latex" alt="$8a-c=7-4b$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="121" height="13" />
</p><p>Squaring both, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/2/e/12e6308451de85e79a2263f0871b07782ddca167.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2+16ac+64c^2=49b^2+56b+16$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="276" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/5/43504939754aa34485c0635d2f7aafb2e7338e83.png" class="latex" alt="$64a^2-16ac+c^2=16b^2-56b+49$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="276" height="16" /> are obtained.
</p><p>Adding the two equations and dividing by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/9/339baf6df71777af266e6259546917b0742caf2d.png" class="latex" alt="$65$" width="17" height="12" /> gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/0/c/b0c66ef0d7afe545819b7099e09ab6f833d43238.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2+c^2=b^2+1$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="124" height="16" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/b/d/dbdf2344d5b7de703c2dcdc107cc3c64a9cfbaee.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2-b^2+c^2=\boxed{(\text{B})1}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="163" height="29" />.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h3>
<p>The easiest way is to assume a value for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and then solve the system of equations. For <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/8/d/48d02444a59b917b64e8bd0963b88712fc7fa0a3.png" class="latex" alt="$a = 1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="42" height="12" />, we get the equations 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/e/e/2ee9e645af3557c7b02b1b103f57067a7ba09d68.png" class="latex" alt="$-7b + 8c = 3$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="103" height="14" /> and
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/a/c/eac9444fdfbffb7769d931055ec2d19d2d8f1c07.png" class="latex" alt="$4b - c = -1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="93" height="13" />
Multiplying the second equation by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />, we have 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/2/0/4207a6e228f0486a44085676a77f9491c8be3ca5.png" class="latex" alt="$32b - 8c = -8$" width="112" height="12" />
Adding up the two equations yields 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/3/c635f5f27100fc3a288d1eb292ab13b1d47cca7e.png" class="latex" alt="$25b = -5$" width="72" height="12" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/9/8/298232048b1b88bf92b829250bf394adbb110b0e.png" class="latex" alt="$b = -\frac{1}{5}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="57" height="37" />
We obtain <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/f/3/3f39103c7730cd58b226e5952f0c215448d238b3.png" class="latex" alt="$c = \frac{1}{5}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="43" height="37" /> after plugging in the value for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />.
Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/4/e04e516322b9d7a3fe61d94c9de2ebbebde6d703.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2-b^2+c^2 = 1-\frac{1}{25}+\frac{1}{25}=\boxed{1}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="259" height="37" /> which corresponds to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/e/2/5e226c2f5a445f0a9ab589c1337336ea7587b36e.png" class="latex" alt="$\text{(B)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="23" height="17" />.
This time-saving trick works only because we know that for any value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/3/2d363059d912b28fc24dfc18c1acb67a2d5c53c6.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2-b^2+c^2$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="91" height="16" /> will always be constant (it's a contest), so any value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> will work. This is also called <a href="/wiki/index.php/Without_loss_of_generality" title="Without loss of generality">without loss of generality</a> or WLOG.
</p>
