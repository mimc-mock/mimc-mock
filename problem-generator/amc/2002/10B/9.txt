<p>Using the letters <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/1/5d1e4485dc90c450e8c76826516c1b2ccb8fce16.png" class="latex" alt="$M$" width="19" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/d/51da37d984564162c87710ca27bea422f657fb73.png" class="latex" alt="$O$" width="13" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/b/e2bbebb3bd73f1ae5c64098ab0244f739abf7ca4.png" class="latex" alt="$U$" width="14" height="12" />, we can form five-letter "words". If these "words" are arranged in alphabetical order, then the "word" <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/9/f/39ff923e346c14334b1c7cfdcee7d47f9cadf18e.png" class="latex" alt="$USAMO$" width="73" height="13" /> occupies position
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/0/9/4094f025ce01ab6d611511649fe889425fb54882.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A) \ } 112\qquad \mathrm{(B) \ } 113\qquad \mathrm{(C) \ } 114\qquad \mathrm{(D) \ } 115\qquad \mathrm{(E) \ } 116$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="432" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>There are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/c/5/cc5ef4c011af453a9438ece8938f94c195af751b.png" class="latex" alt="$4!\cdot 4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="36" height="13" /> "words" beginning with each of the first four letters alphabetically. From there, there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/2/9f2dfeedc22ea0ff2877c9f98e571f73e2fc315e.png" class="latex" alt="$3!\cdot 3$" width="36" height="13" /> with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/b/e2bbebb3bd73f1ae5c64098ab0244f739abf7ca4.png" class="latex" alt="$U$" width="14" height="12" /> as the first letter and each of the first three letters alphabetically. After that, the next "word" is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/9/f/39ff923e346c14334b1c7cfdcee7d47f9cadf18e.png" class="latex" alt="$USAMO$" width="73" height="13" />, hence our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/3/5/935a614f719071855449cd2967d9481c8bc5b6f5.png" class="latex" alt="$4\cdot 4!+3\cdot 3!+1=\boxed{115\Rightarrow\text{(D)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="243" height="28" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/a/3/da3d600d21b2b1343040ecb85db0e354a60e96a5.png" class="latex" alt="$A = 1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="46" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/1/e/21e5206723f0ab8c3e17442ed4b794a3863f1c07.png" class="latex" alt="$M = 2$" width="52" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/7/d/d7de7ec3025a041bfade85fb2069573996150cc6.png" class="latex" alt="$O = 3$" width="47" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/e/51e60b61d5ced17fa0164ca18c4ef2fd703f478d.png" class="latex" alt="$S = 4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="45" height="13" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/0/9/709f6dffa4dea44cc417f27a36c2934cb07563ee.png" class="latex" alt="$U = 5$" width="47" height="12" />. Then counting backwards, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/0/ce0dae02d9625ca1d7de2a008e989fc9feeea3b1.png" class="latex" alt="$54321, 54312, 54231, 54213, 54132, 54123$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="312" height="16" />, so the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/8/8/788271465669af4cb58b6ec5f8a3b61db0ffb7ac.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{115\Rightarrow\text{(D)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="93" height="28" />
</p>
