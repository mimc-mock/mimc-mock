<p>For all positive integers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" />, let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/a/19ab85b892c52e90051151d17cf72c59a1d5caaa.png" class="latex" alt="$f(n)=\log_{2002} n^2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="131" height="19" />. Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/c/65c3d77f4f8c63e10436ae3259b4dce15eb4dd83.png" class="latex" alt="$N=f(11)+f(13)+f(14)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="213" height="18" />. Which of the following relations is true?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/8/2/4823c0e30e0a0b2b9d6cf5d8066d44eda5165c40.png" class="latex" alt="$\text{(A) }N&lt;1 \qquad \text{(B) }N=1 \qquad \text{(C) }1&lt;N&lt;2 \qquad \text{(D) }N=2 \qquad \text{(E) }N&gt;2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="567" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>First, note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/7/4/274a4637f71ab9e01081d83e83ddd1c7ff012e20.png" class="latex" alt="$2002 = 11 \cdot 13 \cdot 14$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="141" height="13" />.
</p><p>Using the fact that for any base we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/c/9acf0610d37f3f02859dfb700ae5b54134cca871.png" class="latex" alt="$\log a + \log b = \log ab$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="161" height="16" />, we get that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/9/839b8e52f143c016b33653fa72b9fe49591c83e1.png" class="latex" alt="$N = \log_{2002} (11^2 \cdot 13^2 \cdot 14^2) = \log_{2002} 2002^2 = \boxed{(D) N=2}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="446" height="29" />.
</p>
