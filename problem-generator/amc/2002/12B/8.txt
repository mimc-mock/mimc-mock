Which of the following must occurs five times in the August of year <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/9/fc97ef67268cd4e91bacdf12b8901d7036c9a056.png" class="latex" alt="$N$" width="16" height="12" />? (Note: Both months have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/9/6/296fac336caaa4265aa24b6ecbc408a348ad7e0b.png" class="latex" alt="$31$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> days.)
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/a/d1a6782fe960954ab4f9ff75912a4ccd61c879bf.png" class="latex" alt="$\textrm{(A)}\ \text{Monday} \qquad \textrm{(B)}\ \text{Tuesday} \qquad \textrm{(C)}\ \text{Wednesday} \qquad \textrm{(D)}\ \text{Thursday} \qquad \textrm{(E)}\ \text{Friday}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="599" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>If there are five Mondays, there are only three possibilities for their dates: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/0/f4022a8e2abffa7c5aa316027b60e732d7f282c5.png" class="latex" alt="$(1,8,15,22,29)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="117" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/5/f/85f6b741c00194f24d00c685cc19a77f92707c9f.png" class="latex" alt="$(2,9,16,23,30)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="117" height="18" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/b/d/1bdbcca00cd27b8dde0b44bc1c0daa3d3302b1fd.png" class="latex" alt="$(3,10,17,24,31)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="126" height="18" />. 
</p><p>In the first case August starts on a Thursday, and there are five Thursdays, Fridays, and Saturdays in August. 
</p><p>In the second case August starts on a Wednesday, and there are five Wednesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays in August.
</p><p>In the third case August starts on a Tuesday, and there are five Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays in August.
</p><p>The only day of the week that is guaranteed to appear five times is therefore <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/9/0/690b70710ad547103377d676922b6cc171d5aa12.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textrm{(D)}\ \text{Thursday}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="111" height="28" />.
</p>
