<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Looking at the numbers, you see that every set of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> positive numbers and 1 negative number. Calculating the sum of the first couple sets gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/b/8/eb809fb6b20557c60b5d1071d19f7ff090f733f3.png" class="latex" alt="$2+10+18...+394$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="154" height="14" />. Clearly, this pattern is an arithmetic sequence. By using the formula we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/8/0/a80b897db8d877c29b4d5c8b1ce2fbf958ad9e5b.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2+394}{2}\cdot 50=\boxed{\textbf{(B) }9900}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="201" height="37" />.
</p><p>-ViratKohli2018
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Split the even numbers and the odd numbers apart. If we group every 2 even numbers together and add them, we get a total of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/9/079545f27a4cfd4e9e510f51850e3c914f424e11.png" class="latex" alt="$50\cdot (-2)=-100$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="133" height="18" />. Summing the odd numbers is equivalent to summing the first 100 odd numbers, which is equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/3/d53b48e06adbe9aba421a4d1dd527a4a886821b7.png" class="latex" alt="$100^2=10000$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="103" height="15" />. Adding these two, we obtain the answer of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/b/54bc4cd2f5f14f8e1683790b4eefe605c413bd15.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B) }9900}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="83" height="28" />.
</p>
