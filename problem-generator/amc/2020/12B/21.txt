<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> be uniquely of the form <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/3/f/13fbbe8e4a4497625700613faecfd935005d1e3d.png" class="latex" alt="$n=k^2+r$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="83" height="16" /> where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/f/2/ef26663e727e7c8dac2b8ab85a474e5ab7c93904.png" class="latex" alt="$0 \le r \le 2k \; \bigstar$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="106" height="15" />. Then, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/3/6137909b1fb0639eddcd9ee699433c1be62b0163.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{k^2+r+1000}{70} = k\]" width="142" height="39" /> Rearranging and completeing the square gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/0/0301bb2accd6d21f81c16e0f8547396a322b05f6.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[(k-35)^2 + r = 225\]" width="152" height="19" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/a/d5afb0746e87395ffc9bff1518a7b16e452efff4.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\Rightarrow r = (k-20)(50-k)\; \smiley\]" width="202" height="18" /> This gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/6/7/e67c6c0697cc53c11885605aa167a4d437e12471.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[(k-35)^2 \le (k-35)^2+r=225 \le (k-35)^2 + 2k\]" width="386" height="19" /> Solving the left inequality shows that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/2/81216811cfa021205a831f70fee83fa018a29c8f.png" class="latex" alt="$20 \le k \le 50$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="94" height="15" />. Combing this with the right inequality gives that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/3/ee30efe278c81302f02a51fc91ff19110055d97a.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[(k-35)^2+r=225 \le (k-35)^2 + 2k \le (k-35)^2+100\]" width="435" height="19" /> which implies either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/9/be96c93b21c39b0c4fb16650d5c6a6ffcdfacac9.png" class="latex" alt="$k \ge 47$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="52" height="15" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/0/9/b0958ddd207698987fc1488e1f10bb36d13ae2a2.png" class="latex" alt="$k \le 23$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="52" height="15" />. By directly computing the cases for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/8/7983cd99211b9c6719e0ef8a7330c1936523536e.png" class="latex" alt="$k = 20, 21, 22, 23, 47, 48, 49, 50$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="235" height="16" /> using <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/1/541e9aef85db57b7dbef677e59a04d09b2e8b58a.png" class="latex" alt="$\smiley$" width="12" height="11" />, it follows that only <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/5/f/25fd0da218a7d9c1cfe08691532b8c6eb8b7c1b2.png" class="latex" alt="$k = 22, 23$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="78" height="16" /> yield and invalid <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/0/4302e3b4e7057984ac3eb2e9018a9b64002550b8.png" class="latex" alt="$\bigstar$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="16" height="14" />. Since each <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" /> corresponds to one <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> and thus to one <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> (from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/1/541e9aef85db57b7dbef677e59a04d09b2e8b58a.png" class="latex" alt="$\smiley$" width="12" height="11" /> and the original form), there must be 6 such <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" />.  
</p><p><br />
~the_jake314
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_5">Solution 5</span></h2>
<p>Since the right-hand-side is an integer, so must be the left-hand-side. Therefore, we must have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/2/7/c27bf89b3c44fd2a7f24fc80dcadd020e04221fb.png" class="latex" alt="$n\equiv -20\pmod{70}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="151" height="20" />; let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/4/61470e134f5cf45d9ad6e2f70a0e86af7aa3a8d8.png" class="latex" alt="$n=70j-20$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="104" height="17" />. The given equation becomes<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/3/17345d91d4c4390830bd13f01a8e3d39b34ff1fb.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[j+14 = \lfloor \sqrt{70j-20} \rfloor\]" width="171" height="21" />
</p><p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/3/1/b3142bf21a2b7793990c70e7235171e587cb6dea.png" class="latex" alt="$\lfloor x \rfloor \leq x &lt; \lfloor x \rfloor +1$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="145" height="20" /> for all real <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, we can take <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/d/c/ddc3b77eabb14b69345ad78604739496272d867c.png" class="latex" alt="$x=\sqrt{70j-20}$" style="vertical-align: -6px" width="121" height="24" /> with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/7/e17664dc421aa4803f8f95b985b1a6883297e4f5.png" class="latex" alt="$\lfloor x \rfloor =j+14$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="102" height="20" /> to get
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/b/0/4b0d18580862fdd0c33e66f28c5572a6789c9c46.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[j+14 \leq \sqrt{70j-20} &lt; j+15\]" width="231" height="21" />
We can square the inequality to get<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/e/c/4ec1fe0ccb3f5cd3c8447833b2bfc1b4f83457d6.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[196+28j+j^{2} \leq 70j-20 &lt; 225 + 30j + j^{2}\]" width="341" height="19" />
The left inequality simplifies to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/0/f50d96e542de0534412e2d1810527d50257e7f11.png" class="latex" alt="$(j-36)(j-6) \leq 0$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="152" height="20" />, which yields <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/e/17e21226e7187580e20a8a84540900625b6ee432.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[6 \le j \le 36.\]" width="87" height="15" />
The right inequality simplifies to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/0/f/d0f6bb71436efce2a9eb20055df625947e3eb400.png" class="latex" alt="$(j-20)^2 - 155 &gt; 0$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="155" height="21" />, which yields <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/f/05f0cebd52176379eabb06f8d64308461dd6792b.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[j &lt; 20 - \sqrt{155} &lt; 8 \quad \text{or} \quad j &gt; 20 + \sqrt{155} &gt; 32\]" width="354" height="20" /> 
</p><p>Solving <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/e/6/2e665e335d27d53d390c9f97f364516d02ae3309.png" class="latex" alt="$j &lt; 8$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="42" height="16" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/5/905904e9db1cea65ba2da2ca615995c20e4307ec.png" class="latex" alt="$6 \le j \le 36$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="86" height="17" />, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/a/aaa789e1200f9fe8e69835a62e36ab3adbf2f8a5.png" class="latex" alt="$6 \le j &lt; 8$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="77" height="17" />, for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> values <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/9/6/8969966168497aab4b638b5145a1ee2302ea6c29.png" class="latex" alt="$j\in \{6, 7\}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="77" height="20" />.
</p><p>Solving <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/a/1ca858bc3e88e69019f554fee1edc6a8c358e112.png" class="latex" alt="$j &gt;32$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="53" height="17" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/5/905904e9db1cea65ba2da2ca615995c20e4307ec.png" class="latex" alt="$6 \le j \le 36$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="86" height="17" />, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/6/0/06026beff76fa99330fdd1c55b80c5d1aa965fa1.png" class="latex" alt="$32 &lt; j \le 36$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="95" height="17" />, for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> values <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/2/d/22d57d23a05c5b6a0de80c37f3c4d14359ade529.png" class="latex" alt="$k\in \{33, \ldots , 36\}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="129" height="20" />.
</p><p>Thus, our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/6/3367375feecbbc702c764df2793f0238a6bd4740.png" class="latex" alt="$2 + 4 = \boxed{\textbf{(C) }6}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="122" height="30" />
</p><p><br />
~KingRavi

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_8">Solution 8</span></h2>
<p>Set <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/9/0f9f36c136ecabc3b16aaff49a02c1cf19c78941.png" class="latex" alt="$x=\sqrt{n}$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="61" height="18" /> in the given equation and solve for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> to get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/3/b534a3c7ea06e7d611e6d0192129c80ecd0ea81b.png" class="latex" alt="$x^2 = 70 \cdot \lfloor x \rfloor - 1000$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="160" height="21" />. Set <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/e/4/5e4e91ed0da8e94b1fa9c932b5fe14455b69eb3c.png" class="latex" alt="$k = \lfloor x \rfloor \ge 0$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="96" height="20" />; since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/3/a/73aef428d1372f3246710e123735c5e5a739366d.png" class="latex" alt="$\lfloor x \rfloor^2 \le x^2 &lt; (\lfloor x \rfloor + 1)^2$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="182" height="21" />, we get  <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/e/87e5552926ab3dc33ebe1cc1064c3bf61b5553d7.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[k^2 \le  70k - 1000 &lt; k^2 + 2k + 1.\]" width="246" height="18" />
The left inequality simplifies to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/7/a27a71b5941e2539d092b02f753835702e8ad86d.png" class="latex" alt="$(k-20)(k-50) \le 0$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="164" height="20" />, which yields <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/0/a/b0a23e6943661779e194bcd07e1c285b03cea22e.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[20 \le k \le 50.\]" width="97" height="15" />
The right inequality simplifies to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/6/d16f5736a940b3bb81995fbce364d4c11ccf7395.png" class="latex" alt="$(k-34)^2 &gt; 155$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="125" height="21" />, which yields <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/e/2de4624197885f1a29da207f8825a40be0218b0d.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[k &lt; 34 - \sqrt{155} &lt; 22 \quad \text{or} \quad k &gt; 34 + \sqrt{155} &gt; 46\]" width="366" height="18" /> 
Solving <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/c/a/ccaf9eca69cedbe45440b6e9c2b81f059907f20d.png" class="latex" alt="$k &lt; 22$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="54" height="15" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/2/81216811cfa021205a831f70fee83fa018a29c8f.png" class="latex" alt="$20 \le k \le 50$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="94" height="15" />, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/7/8/7784b94ac988d97483140c00ff505ba793766700.png" class="latex" alt="$20 \le k &lt; 22$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="97" height="17" />, for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> values <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/3/d/73d378f907dd49d66e7b905e056f6b33ea14a06f.png" class="latex" alt="$k\in \{20, 21\}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="97" height="20" />.
</p><p>Solving <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/8/4/384321b92c13f5d57d2130839d9f167041ade3de.png" class="latex" alt="$k &gt;46$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="54" height="15" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/2/81216811cfa021205a831f70fee83fa018a29c8f.png" class="latex" alt="$20 \le k \le 50$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="94" height="15" />, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/a/54a13b14c90e1d4591f9ac9252e3a85d15a3df6e.png" class="latex" alt="$46 &lt; k \le 50$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="97" height="17" />, for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> values <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/c/26c9135cd9ca7334412fc97dc14fa8e4ec8d41c3.png" class="latex" alt="$k\in \{47, \ldots , 50\}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="129" height="20" />.
</p><p>Thus, our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/6/3367375feecbbc702c764df2793f0238a6bd4740.png" class="latex" alt="$2 + 4 = \boxed{\textbf{(C) }6}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="122" height="30" />
