<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/b/6ebabe6f737db7a0f7825d4a0f4023d395532098.png" class="latex" alt="$a_1 , a_2 , ...$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="64" height="11" /> be a sequence for which <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/b/a/0bad78c0b52d180bb51ac7f3f167374b4d38a73b.png" class="latex" alt="$a_1=2$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="50" height="14" /> , <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/c/ffce335a846c1048a9d985eb07371ffc96eaf752.png" class="latex" alt="$a_2=3$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="50" height="15" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/8/8/588fa958248d3d4f2963cf6110f8f99d7ad7ef4a.png" class="latex" alt="$a_n=\frac{a_{n-1}}{a_{n-2}}$" style="vertical-align: -15px" width="81" height="35" /> for each positive integer <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/f/1/8f1e2533d6abdb11a9a1626c0240fab1a9200fc6.png" class="latex" alt="$n \ge 3$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="43" height="15" />. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/0/b5033830f7a46e393b1f2c6cba9de59d64a7a87b.png" class="latex" alt="$a_{2006}$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="35" height="10" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/d/7/cd7bd183afdd316bad13ba5564c7a58ebcec1344.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A) \ } \frac{1}{2}\qquad \mathrm{(B) \ } \frac{2}{3}\qquad \mathrm{(C) \ } \frac{3}{2}\qquad \mathrm{(D) \ } 2\qquad \mathrm{(E) \ } 3$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="354" height="37" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Looking at the first few terms of the sequence:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/1/3/2137c56db5ae36da103beb38ea3975020c9dacc3.png" class="latex" alt="$a_1=2 , a_2=3 , a_3=\frac{3}{2}, a_4=\frac{1}{2} , a_5=\frac{1}{3} , a_6=\frac{2}{3} , a_7=2 , a_8=3 , ....$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="502" height="37" />
</p><p>Clearly, the sequence repeats every 6 terms. 
</p><p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/8/f/08f32923ade8881415a4b4d15ed5fdaccd34d655.png" class="latex" alt="$2006 \equiv 2\bmod{6}$" width="123" height="12" />,
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/5/ee5c513747796c173c75fbe6c10dc395088d3fef.png" class="latex" alt="$a_{2006} = a_2 = \boxed{\textbf{(E) }3}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="158" height="30" />
</p>
