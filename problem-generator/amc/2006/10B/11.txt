<p>What is the tens digit in the sum <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/2/bc23705486fc49308e04b5b5505b0bb2463bcb41.png" class="latex" alt="$7!+8!+9!+...+2006!$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="186" height="14" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/c/c/acc18e4cd0250c23ada5cb910be1d8c43f4cd752.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } 1\qquad \textbf{(B) } 3\qquad \textbf{(C) } 4\qquad \textbf{(D) } 6\qquad \textbf{(E) } 9$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="355" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/2/c824ea490ff073932a1c17d8ce0b9a4215f90d95.png" class="latex" alt="$10!$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="21" height="13" /> is divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />, any <a href="/wiki/index.php/Factorial" title="Factorial">factorial</a> greater than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/2/c824ea490ff073932a1c17d8ce0b9a4215f90d95.png" class="latex" alt="$10!$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="21" height="13" /> is also divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />. The last two <a href="/wiki/index.php/Digit" title="Digit">digits</a> of all factorials greater than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/2/c824ea490ff073932a1c17d8ce0b9a4215f90d95.png" class="latex" alt="$10!$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="21" height="13" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/f/b1f2c746327924273ba209f0879ea846a0b8dd6b.png" class="latex" alt="$00$" width="17" height="12" />, so the last two digits of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/4/d/14d3f16d533036ac71a6e8a1dbe968f49bc6eb3a.png" class="latex" alt="$10!+11!+...+2006!$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="168" height="14" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/f/b1f2c746327924273ba209f0879ea846a0b8dd6b.png" class="latex" alt="$00$" width="17" height="12" />. 
(*)
</p><p>So all that is needed is the tens digit of the sum <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/d/ffdb57a47ef31a1832edac4171282e89717560ef.png" class="latex" alt="$7!+8!+9!$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="85" height="14" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/8/b/a8bad5c6f48c8e464639619c6e7c5d88d3e389b0.png" class="latex" alt="$7!+8!+9!=5040+40320+362880=408240$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="369" height="14" />
</p><p>So the tens digit is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/a/9/fa9073850b901d86aa525ea6586176619fc33fad.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) }4}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="57" height="30" />.
</p><p>(*) A slightly faster method would be to take the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/8/0/0807d278db612869d21715b131a86b9bb74544d8.png" class="latex" alt="$\pmod {100}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="88" height="18" /> residue of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/3/9/9396c3aac08fb3ab1c124ee973cba2c5f2f06574.png" class="latex" alt="$7! + 8! + 9!.$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="90" height="14" /> Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/f/c7fa027eb0424ff984903cb27635bb754605b491.png" class="latex" alt="$7! = 5040,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="78" height="16" /> we can rewrite the sum as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/e/1de56c07aa4efc6f508d9d71c952d4b824f74e02.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[5040 + 8\cdot 5040 + 72\cdot 5040 \equiv 40 + 8\cdot 40 + 72\cdot 40 = 40 + 320 + 2880 \equiv 40 \pmod{100}.\]" width="599" height="18" /> Since the last two digits of the sum is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/8/7/78792fe4680a164f537ea72a4f900e4471433c3b.png" class="latex" alt="$40$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />, the tens digit is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/a/9/fa9073850b901d86aa525ea6586176619fc33fad.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) }4}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="57" height="30" />.
</p>
