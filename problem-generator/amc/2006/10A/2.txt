<p>Define <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/a/6cafb6fa7693c038b7920d8b898f1d6b671d5ac5.png" class="latex" alt="$x\otimes y=x^3-y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="115" height="18" />. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/d/b/0dbe8c4ddc90e70bd6a0101c5b339a4d0485b03e.png" class="latex" alt="$h\otimes (h\otimes h)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="88" height="18" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/b/9/1b9db39d37e489818077cb154986ca139d8dbee9.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ -h\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 0\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ h\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 2h\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ h^3$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="395" height="21" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>By the definition of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/8/b88c462e5d9319a7d2181ad94863658fa0a24736.png" class="latex" alt="$\otimes$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="13" height="12" />, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/4/5/c4577a4c10e373d1a00562b6ae0c09c0bd4521a0.png" class="latex" alt="$h\otimes h=h^{3}-h$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="117" height="16" />. 
</p><p>Then, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/8/918974382c0d579dab2b6fc1ab291a4834d8d76e.png" class="latex" alt="$h\otimes (h\otimes h)=h\otimes (h^{3}-h)=h^{3}-(h^{3}-h)=\boxed{\textbf{(C) }h}.$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="429" height="30" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Substitute <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/8/8189a5b5a0917b8c93350827be4038af1839139d.png" class="latex" alt="$h$" width="10" height="12" />. You get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/1/1/f112cab37e4549d75a1c07322341030700f4a4b0.png" class="latex" alt="$1^3-(1^3-1)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="99" height="19" /> which is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/0/b/f0b2d4c8f33e34b6675b8e392a77c6656a27d21d.png" class="latex" alt="$h=1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="42" height="13" />, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/0/6/40648958db52a32f5ed4e8459f6e1bc98dc1bb85.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) }h}.$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="64" height="30" />
</p><p>~dragoon also aop is a god
</p>
