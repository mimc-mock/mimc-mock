<p>Which of the following describes the graph of the equation <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/d/3/9d3c5de1665d772d27931cb0db25f2c0fa8839a5.png" class="latex" alt="$(x+y)^2=x^2+y^2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="143" height="19" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/3/3532ca04dcd0c040f8d63c76e6195e785bf2cfd2.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ \text{the empty set}\qquad\mathrm{(B)}\ \text{one point}\qquad\mathrm{(C)}\ \text{two lines}\qquad\mathrm{(D)}\ \text{a circle}\qquad\mathrm{(E)}\ \text{the entire plane}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="694" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/3/9937145e4db273bc0ce1869b57d1fa4dbbe49ef0.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*}(x+y)^2&amp;=x^2+y^2\\ x^2 + 2xy + y^2 &amp;= x^2 + y^2\\ 2xy &amp;= 0\end{align*}" width="188" height="71" />
Either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/3/2d348bde3e15456e71734dc2c56fc7425c95927f.png" class="latex" alt="$x = 0$" width="43" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/0/7a06334068417b1781d14e8b28af827907961eb3.png" class="latex" alt="$y = 0$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="42" height="16" />. The <a href="/wiki/index.php/Union" title="Union">union</a> of them is 2 lines, and thus the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/4/8/2484f88154fadcf9c781bba19becdae88696b77f.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(C)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="25" height="18" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/7/b/77bc9852606b67fe448031002c3e27a4b8a97bd1.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] draw((0,-50)--(0,50));draw((-50,0)--(50,0));[/asy]" width="252" height="252" />
