<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Since the swath Moe actually mows is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/9/e290f2cba0ba0e11c0b91630da15a408da2fcf1c.png" class="latex" alt="$24$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="12" /> inches, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> feet wide, he mows <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/4/9f4f407fa86791b87ad5286ea377c10b2111d0f0.png" class="latex" alt="$10000$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="44" height="13" /> square feet in one hour. His lawn has an area of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/7/b/a7bbfa0ba2963fe81be6c77d8f304ced9fef8d2c.png" class="latex" alt="$13500$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="44" height="13" />, so it will take Moe <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/7/3/97306b985de6c8e82811f9e1717c0e64d2bd7fbd.png" class="latex" alt="$1.35$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="31" height="13" /> hours to finish mowing the lawn.
