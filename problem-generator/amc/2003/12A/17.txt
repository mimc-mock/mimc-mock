<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_5">Solution 5</span></h2>
<p>Using the double-angle formula for sine, what we need to find is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/5/7f57c8f8881afc8329012d27eadccadedf786afe.png" class="latex" alt="$AP\cdot \sin(DAP) = AP\cdot  2\sin( DAM) \cos(DAM) = 4\cdot 2\cdot \frac{2}{\sqrt{20}}\cdot\frac{4}{\sqrt{20}} = \frac{16}{5}$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="575" height="42" />.
</p>
