<p>A point <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/3/e53b1fe25be1c679117fb44a6a886fe1247d189a.png" class="latex" alt="$(x,y)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="40" height="18" /> is randomly picked from inside the rectangle with  vertices <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/6/f9603ca3089464e548fc6f1366bc474e7efef8d9.png" class="latex" alt="$(0,0)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/6/b86f58cb609fa753024f1173510c877971f8e4e6.png" class="latex" alt="$(4,0)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/5/5/a55cbad9e2ef9542b8d4d3d8c21f3c887fd44df2.png" class="latex" alt="$(4,1)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/1/be11a56a55425d6c6b69f70f5fa98eab95e8ad18.png" class="latex" alt="$(0,1)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />. What is the probability that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/b/4/0b4aee29876af28821f3a35178c09561adaf5a4a.png" class="latex" alt="$x&lt;y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="43" height="13" />? 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/0/a30a03b6dc43679af3ca442123c383032d822bd3.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A) \ } \frac{1}{8}\qquad \mathrm{(B) \ } \frac{1}{4}\qquad \mathrm{(C) \ } \frac{3}{8}\qquad \mathrm{(D) \ } \frac{1}{2}\qquad \mathrm{(E) \ } \frac{3}{4}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="361" height="38" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The rectangle has a width of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> and a height of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />. 
</p><p>The area of this rectangle is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/0/3/a0362d563c30867dafad729983f553f9b2356a03.png" class="latex" alt="$4\cdot1=4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="64" height="12" />. 
</p><p>The line <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/1/0110f1bb04c51b2a4d431bf14489fc1b48b37421.png" class="latex" alt="$x=y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="43" height="11" /> intersects the rectangle at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/6/f9603ca3089464e548fc6f1366bc474e7efef8d9.png" class="latex" alt="$(0,0)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/e/7/ae7a465ffe5255961382f5777569608be052b783.png" class="latex" alt="$(1,1)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />. 
</p><p>The area which <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/b/4/0b4aee29876af28821f3a35178c09561adaf5a4a.png" class="latex" alt="$x&lt;y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="43" height="13" /> is the right isosceles triangle with side length <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> that has vertices at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/6/f9603ca3089464e548fc6f1366bc474e7efef8d9.png" class="latex" alt="$(0,0)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/e/7/ae7a465ffe5255961382f5777569608be052b783.png" class="latex" alt="$(1,1)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/1/be11a56a55425d6c6b69f70f5fa98eab95e8ad18.png" class="latex" alt="$(0,1)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />.  
</p><p>The area of this triangle is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/b/f/dbf674ec4ba523c1250f98c1f4789344349087ae.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{2}\cdot1^{2}=\frac{1}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="78" height="37" /> 
</p><p>Therefore, the probability that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/b/4/0b4aee29876af28821f3a35178c09561adaf5a4a.png" class="latex" alt="$x&lt;y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="43" height="13" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/2/0/220ebbdf7352bc97ca1fb9bbe5946fbae1eaa7e7.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{\frac{1}{2}}{4}=\frac{1}{8} \Rightarrow \boxed{\mathrm{(A)}\ \frac{1}{8}}$" style="vertical-align: -18px" width="138" height="48" />
</p>
