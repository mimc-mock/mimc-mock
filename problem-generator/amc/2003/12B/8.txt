<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/9/1/891efd9a27650e638592a8a274ea57c279725e33.png" class="latex" alt="$\clubsuit(x)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="37" height="18" /> denote the sum of the digits of the positive integer <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />. For example, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/b/2/eb2de7a9d2ecc7d2fb7dca82a7bc6de9e1e488de.png" class="latex" alt="$\clubsuit(8)=8$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="70" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/e/d/4edc76e725705a6ac9eca665fa4d6ddc4be7f910.png" class="latex" alt="$\clubsuit(123)=1+2+3=6$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="184" height="18" />. For how many two-digit values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/4/b749a8c7b00df2042ac25557fe341710db24f7b2.png" class="latex" alt="$\clubsuit(\clubsuit(x))=3$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="99" height="18" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/e/5/9e57faea979cf8d6f3aed0d93857be9bb505bf3a.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } 3 \qquad\textbf{(B) } 4 \qquad\textbf{(C) } 6 \qquad\textbf{(D) } 9 \qquad\textbf{(E) } 10$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="362" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/e/3/4e3f79707f61c6f51c60721ce914bec12320ef35.png" class="latex" alt="$y=\clubsuit (x)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="71" height="18" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/b/6/bb6a88c3c41c84ea0964afaeac9d7ba6f7c79961.png" class="latex" alt="$x \leq 99$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="52" height="15" />, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/c/f/ecf6c02f6e12699f850b5fda229808fce7cead85.png" class="latex" alt="$y \leq 18$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="51" height="16" />. Thus if <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/c/0/ac075f16724360b21252345292599c900211d65d.png" class="latex" alt="$\clubsuit (y)=3$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="70" height="18" />, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/b/a2b0e1404de4eca35c42876bcc83707b753e990e.png" class="latex" alt="$y=3$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="42" height="16" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/5/2/c52f2821f1a68dc474c90d7acb0e279c7fca51c6.png" class="latex" alt="$y=12$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="51" height="15" />. The 3 values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> for which <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/c/9/2c9b419643c3f5c150b94d73abd54c87f1f26da8.png" class="latex" alt="$\clubsuit (x)=3$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="71" height="18" /> are 12, 21, and 30, and the 7 values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> for which <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/4/d/14d57f230f71078d2b8cf5cb8e454982200fd023.png" class="latex" alt="$\clubsuit (x)=12$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="80" height="18" /> are 39, 48, 57, 66, 75, 84, and 93. There are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/c/8/cc812ebec7a8610978c54ad02fb91ca81587ffef.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{E=10}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="68" height="24" /> values in all.
</p>
