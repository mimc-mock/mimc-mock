<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/2/5/12517bbc31cb9026c71299b7a0d232d07e60f284.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{AC}$" width="28" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/7/4373e7b4be983c0315e82e1fda1df66ed34dd7fb.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{BD}$" width="30" height="15" /> intersect at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/d/51da37d984564162c87710ca27bea422f657fb73.png" class="latex" alt="$O$" width="13" height="12" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/e/f9efaf9474c5658c4089523e2aff4e11488f8603.png" class="latex" alt="$ABCD$" width="57" height="13" /> is a rhombus, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/2/5/12517bbc31cb9026c71299b7a0d232d07e60f284.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{AC}$" width="28" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/7/4373e7b4be983c0315e82e1fda1df66ed34dd7fb.png" class="latex" alt="$\overline{BD}$" width="30" height="15" /> are <a href="/wiki/index.php/Perpendicular_bisector" title="Perpendicular bisector">perpendicular bisectors</a>. Thus <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/8/6/58656922d810aa5ccda634029c19a7b65a14fbb7.png" class="latex" alt="$\angle POQ = 90^{\circ}$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="104" height="16" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/a/6/3a677ba660e288a7c378ac7a3fbd5764df2e60a1.png" class="latex" alt="$OPNQ$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="58" height="16" /> is a <a href="/wiki/index.php/Rectangle" title="Rectangle">rectangle</a>. Since the diagonals of a rectangle are of equal length, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/6/916523eac03a3253e7f2a729252e1b5beb1eb68b.png" class="latex" alt="$PQ = ON$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="83" height="16" />, so we want to minimize <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/f/b/afb3aa28627d70a5c4421063effb76bd2241a7e3.png" class="latex" alt="$ON$" width="31" height="12" />. It follows that we want <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/c/4/2c4ba875c55cd8b676dd6a55e8a05378708fa1b6.png" class="latex" alt="$ON \perp AB$" width="82" height="13" />.
