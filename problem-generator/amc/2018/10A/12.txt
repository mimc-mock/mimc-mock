<p>How many ordered pairs of real numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/3/e53b1fe25be1c679117fb44a6a886fe1247d189a.png" class="latex" alt="$(x,y)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="40" height="18" /> satisfy the following system of equations?
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/8/b58992285436b1813d31a4762ccfd7307d087e7e.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} x+3y&amp;=3 \\ \big||x|-|y|\big|&amp;=1 \end{align*}" width="104" height="46" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/6/8/668a1b4307255e720a0afa6005afbcff5a138064.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } 1 \qquad  \textbf{(B) } 2 \qquad  \textbf{(C) } 3 \qquad  \textbf{(D) } 4 \qquad  \textbf{(E) } 8$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="353" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>We can solve this by graphing the equations. The second equation looks challenging to graph, but start by graphing it in the first quadrant only (which is easy since the inner absolute value signs can be ignored), then simply reflect that graph into the other quadrants.
</p><p>The graph looks something like this:
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/0/5/305e6f8f4c7a9e170253eeb9b7d6c41df6dace78.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] draw((-3,0)--(3,0), Arrows); draw((0,-3)--(0,3), Arrows); draw((2,3)--(0,1)--(-2,3), blue); draw((-3,2)--(-1,0)--(-3,-2), blue); draw((-2,-3)--(0,-1)--(2,-3), blue); draw((3,-2)--(1,0)--(3,2), blue); draw((-3,2)--(3,0), red); dot((-3,2)); dot((3/2,1/2)); dot((0,1)); [/asy]" width="252" height="248" />
Now, it becomes clear that there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/4/e/44eb511c4f725b6b8f6c6a941d77324031885652.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) } 3}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="55" height="28" /> intersection points.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/d/88de73b3bb4592164414272fd69da97dc1e0c358.png" class="latex" alt="$x+3y=3$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="84" height="16" /> can be rewritten to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/0/b/c0b4adc658d91833cd64eee87a8097a3ac1f55c9.png" class="latex" alt="$x=3-3y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="84" height="16" />. Substituting <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/c/e/5ce4a84127cf042b6c7f601a326ebbbc8b965819.png" class="latex" alt="$3-3y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="49" height="16" /> for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> in the second equation will give <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/8/8182cbe24540085d81a28d26de66abd55c19f050.png" class="latex" alt="$||3-3y|-y|=1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="134" height="18" />. Splitting this question into casework for the ranges of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" /> will give us the total number of solutions. 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/7/f/d7fc8bd87f70c04687e145d72c7b03b4d902f80d.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{Case 1:}$" width="59" height="11" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/9/8/b9845c168b2536a28057556dfc680eef7f658833.png" class="latex" alt="$y&gt;1$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="42" height="15" />:
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/c/e/5ce4a84127cf042b6c7f601a326ebbbc8b965819.png" class="latex" alt="$3-3y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="49" height="16" /> will be negative so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/4/994f93bf824b6050080b9be498d3f263daf929b4.png" class="latex" alt="$|3-3y| = 3y-3.$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="137" height="18" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/6/ed6cbb3cbab587eaf856bd1c98f074e9cd0ad07e.png" class="latex" alt="$|3y-3-y| = |2y-3| = 1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="208" height="18" />
</p>
<pre>   Subcase 1: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/6/f/76f0395de7ab2f4ad89c0ccd6980eb30d1dcf302.png" class="latex" alt="$y&gt;\frac{3}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="45" height="37" />
</pre>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/8/1d8cbfc25f75226a38758b02348a6ab712af5937.png" class="latex" alt="$2y-3$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="49" height="16" /> is positive so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/4/e/b4ea8f6c7bcd9edb72841d7562dcb467186f7c00.png" class="latex" alt="$2y-3 = 1$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="82" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/d/b8db1c24a6c4d0ade195b8b098cfe96a64267735.png" class="latex" alt="$y = 2$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="42" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/4/b/94b456355fd253a2c0a97c454e856ee4fb1fbd7d.png" class="latex" alt="$x = 3-3(2) = -3$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="145" height="18" />
</p>
<pre>   Subcase 2: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/5/6/c568081a876195848640ce1a21b4fb9217933351.png" class="latex" alt="$1&lt;y&lt;\frac{3}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="78" height="37" />
</pre>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/8/1d8cbfc25f75226a38758b02348a6ab712af5937.png" class="latex" alt="$2y-3$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="49" height="16" /> is negative so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/f/bcf3cde4915a82a464f32ffc80c07225e19e920c.png" class="latex" alt="$|2y-3| = 3-2y = 1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="166" height="18" />. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/d/87dad8c1b2ad5c3debf2c670cf3b9364c11c42f6.png" class="latex" alt="$2y = 2$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="51" height="15" /> and so there are no solutions (<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" /> can't equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />)
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/1/331226e700ae378565242419f911b29b000845c5.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{Case 2:}$" width="59" height="11" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/3/4/234b330118777aba0bee513e3ade547ebd70d845.png" class="latex" alt="$y = 1$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="42" height="15" />:
It is fairly clear that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/b/4/db43f2c66699f1ec2d1988f8a220b6a09d1c91ba.png" class="latex" alt="$x = 0.$" width="47" height="12" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/4/9/949308f1d0158c4f33a3154470cc9db508bdb248.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{Case 3:}$" width="59" height="11" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/5/a25d4948a50ef14a700f9f1e2a21293f98ab9636.png" class="latex" alt="$y&lt;1$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="42" height="15" />:
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/c/e/5ce4a84127cf042b6c7f601a326ebbbc8b965819.png" class="latex" alt="$3-3y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="49" height="16" /> will be positive so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/a/7aa5d84eed98a70e3451d59df26d8b11a9a56a72.png" class="latex" alt="$|3-3y-y| = |3-4y| = 1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="208" height="18" />
</p>
<pre>   Subcase 1: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/b/6/7b64d05f34c10b3174e559539674625892e4794f.png" class="latex" alt="$y&gt;\frac{4}{3}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="45" height="37" />
</pre>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/0/3/d03a548fa969fc95e3c6f9bf0ad514150a0cd27c.png" class="latex" alt="$3-4y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="49" height="16" /> will be negative so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/0/c/80c93b9ef1159c135b509e04b760a38e1d917a2d.png" class="latex" alt="$4y-3 = 1$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="82" height="16" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/2/e12b6767375342ed57d27678e3ea1cdb97f47e15.png" class="latex" alt="$\rightarrow$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="10" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/2/f82a0912221c2fd05091c6c2b0537dfbe7dd300b.png" class="latex" alt="$4y = 4$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="52" height="15" />. There are no solutions (again, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" /> can't equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />)
</p>
<pre>   Subcase 2: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/a/9aa42509c7ebb2f4954c781119bd536681680f37.png" class="latex" alt="$y&lt;\frac{4}{3}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="45" height="37" />

<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/0/3/d03a548fa969fc95e3c6f9bf0ad514150a0cd27c.png" class="latex" alt="$3-4y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="49" height="16" /> will be positive so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/5/8855d076afc32b7cd78934d608e490fc9fe9b5d4.png" class="latex" alt="$3-4y = 1$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="82" height="16" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/2/e12b6767375342ed57d27678e3ea1cdb97f47e15.png" class="latex" alt="$\rightarrow$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="10" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/f/dcf23bb638ba9a7be15b2f9f2b1c9554c8ce7571.png" class="latex" alt="$4y = 2$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="51" height="15" />. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/2/d/72d9ffa7385dfa6d83b977f243e2dee69923bda6.png" class="latex" alt="$y = \frac{1}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="45" height="37" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/0/3/003bcebc12d2d3dd5107627be5b8f237dff6c147.png" class="latex" alt="$x = \frac{3}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="46" height="37" />.
