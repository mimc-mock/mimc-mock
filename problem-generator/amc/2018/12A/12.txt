<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> be a set of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> integers taken from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/f/35f0946f770710ad495222b344c4769f88116fc6.png" class="latex" alt="$\{1,2,\dots,12\}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="102" height="18" /> with the property that if <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" /> are elements of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/1/0/710e3e4f18d7c0270ab719a00adc61fa12363b1e.png" class="latex" alt="$a&lt;b$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="42" height="13" />, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" /> is not a multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />. What is the least possible value of an element in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/6/e/a6eaac66a9117c992b2cf0ddb957d528be912b3b.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 2\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 3\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 4\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 5\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 7$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="349" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>If we start with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/f/5bf27edd6ee0c25241392922eca9b9c9144f4d73.png" class="latex" alt="$1,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="15" /> we can include nothing else, so that won't work. By the way, note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> is not an answer choice.
</p><p>If we start with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/1/6e1d1407aac4125d6c453a365eb173d96ebb48e2.png" class="latex" alt="$2,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="15" /> we would have to include every odd number except <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> to fill out the set, but then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> would violate the rule, so that won't work.
</p><p>Experimentation with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> shows it's likewise impossible. You can include <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/e/4/1e4cef427ae68b14c28f505097095888911b162b.png" class="latex" alt="$7,11,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="39" height="15" /> and either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> (which are always safe). But after adding either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> we have no more valid numbers.
</p><p>Finally, starting with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/0/5/4054552638cf9f29e857a1306bd3ec31df3c19b6.png" class="latex" alt="$4,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="15" /> we find that the sequence <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/2/f7284b0d4975f21d8343a8175ba90960cfcd8301.png" class="latex" alt="$4,5,6,7,9,11$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="103" height="16" /> works, giving us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/d/a2d1eb7107858447cf70e9e6ce4073c1b1c3fc15.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 4}.$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="61" height="30" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>We know that all odd numbers except <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/f/5bf27edd6ee0c25241392922eca9b9c9144f4d73.png" class="latex" alt="$1,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="15" /> namely <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/f/baf41145ffe0022968456c3d3cd4fdd640960e51.png" class="latex" alt="$3, 5, 7, 9, 11,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="90" height="16" /> can be used.
</p><p>Now we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/a/e0a0db32027a732ac57d37ef2ae9bb150f65b108.png" class="latex" alt="$7$" width="9" height="12" /> possibilities to choose from for the last number (out of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/2/8/72869994b1623ccde5a157980b330daeb936a0c1.png" class="latex" alt="$1, 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 12$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="129" height="16" />). We can eliminate <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/b/6/8b653473b329b8ea370466d0c2a5ccd3ac92d9f2.png" class="latex" alt="$1, 2, 10,$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="59" height="17" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/3/1030ad28c571a1558f19ddc7b500bbd42cdf1b54.png" class="latex" alt="$12,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="22" height="15" /> and we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/0/9/d09e0e35d99e93ad7a136adc85fc54183602c8e7.png" class="latex" alt="$4, 6, 8$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="43" height="16" /> to choose from. However, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> is a multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/3/0/93032f1ab8b685d84a767a2fde0d9006a464b5e4.png" class="latex" alt="$3.$" width="12" height="12" /> Now we have to take out either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> from the list. If we take out <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/2/8/1288f16f77606bb83db76df0877615ef6e8b149e.png" class="latex" alt="$9,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="16" /> none of the numbers would work, but if we take out <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/8/2f885f96d2fb7e65f800de4ff71d7dcd0a5d2db5.png" class="latex" alt="$3,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="16" /> we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/6/a/d6a9c1d25a9c8c6c44c0ea9c1db554e56e2211e1.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[4, 5, 6, 7, 9, 11.\]" width="107" height="16" />
The least number is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/0/5/4054552638cf9f29e857a1306bd3ec31df3c19b6.png" class="latex" alt="$4,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="15" /> so the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/d/a2d1eb7107858447cf70e9e6ce4073c1b1c3fc15.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 4}.$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="61" height="30" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>We can get the multiples for the numbers in the original set with multiples in the same original set
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/0/200ee44dbf1d8f0a5c9f721596ea7678ea149b78.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} 1&amp;: \ \text{all elements of }\{1,2,\dots,12\} \\ 2&amp;: \ 4,6,8,10,12 \\ 3&amp;: \ 6,9,12 \\ 4&amp;: \ 8,12 \\ 5&amp;: \ 10 \\ 6&amp;: \ 12 \end{align*}" width="241" height="138" />
It will be safe to start with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> since they have the smallest number of multiples as listed above, but since the question asks for the least, it will be better to try others.
</p><p>Trying <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/0/5/4054552638cf9f29e857a1306bd3ec31df3c19b6.png" class="latex" alt="$4,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="15" /> we can get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/4/d/b4d77ebca741561940126fc509b383434569a474.png" class="latex" alt="$4,5,6,7,9,11.$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="111" height="17" /> So <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> works.
Trying <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/8/2f885f96d2fb7e65f800de4ff71d7dcd0a5d2db5.png" class="latex" alt="$3,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="16" /> it won't work, so the least is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/1/cb15a16cca90d201094265ee529e9e2cfb113de8.png" class="latex" alt="$4.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="12" height="12" /> This means the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/d/a2d1eb7107858447cf70e9e6ce4073c1b1c3fc15.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 4}.$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="61" height="30" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_4">Solution 4</span></h2>
<p>We partition <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/0/a/a0a45d4d00f382fb4ab24bc03e04e4d48e08cca4.png" class="latex" alt="$\{1,2,\ldots,12\}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="102" height="18" /> into six nonempty subsets such that for every subset, each element is a multiple of all elements less than or equal to itself: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/1/b/a1b51d74e9b9f113aff4007008b3ba7e706b2741.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\{1,2,4,8\}, \ \{3,6,12\}, \ \{5,10\}, \ \{7\}, \ \{9\}, \ \{11\}.\]" width="364" height="18" />
Clearly, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> must contain exactly one element from each subset:
</p>
<ul><li>For <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/5/d1513638c290a18912ca5f7b43a9d52e12ca33fb.png" class="latex" alt="$\{5,10\},$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="60" height="20" /> we can select either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/3/b833a70e5b34465a7e1cc445231d569f21b81da4.png" class="latex" alt="$10.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="21" height="13" /></li></ul>
<ul><li>For <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/2/3/923f53562faf95fc400fc6e0861d5911608dfacb.png" class="latex" alt="$\{3,6,12\},$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="78" height="20" /> we can select either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/4/0/b40dc5c54a0dac797341eefc0e3befa7caa8d74f.png" class="latex" alt="$12.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="21" height="12" /> Recall that since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/8/f/68fcf26fd71051c195b61498cdb3d37c88c18c1e.png" class="latex" alt="$9\in S,$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="49" height="18" /> we cannot select <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/3/0/93032f1ab8b685d84a767a2fde0d9006a464b5e4.png" class="latex" alt="$3.$" width="12" height="12" /></li></ul>
<ul><li>For <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/9/b/d9b6d92719b8d83ad2f007de4a0d45602575931b.png" class="latex" alt="$\{1,2,4,8\},$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="86" height="20" /> we can select either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> (provided that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/6/616f977c0858df66dc4135342375cf2c6574ec4b.png" class="latex" alt="$12\not\in S$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="54" height="18" />) or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/b/1/bb13a1ea7b9fd6892ebe9599c3587aa68288c873.png" class="latex" alt="$8.$" width="12" height="12" /> Recall that since either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/7/f/37f1f651b2d302e554f8a5530757045df5ced8bd.png" class="latex" alt="$6\in S$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="43" height="13" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/4/b14c1d3e5b6847e0ec041561a4d09a9e7488e740.png" class="latex" alt="$12\in S,$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="58" height="18" /> we can select neither <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> nor <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/4/7/e4797950b5e812b65381cbc5fc33ad2db5f57aff.png" class="latex" alt="$2.$" width="12" height="12" /></li></ul>
<p>If <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/d/28d5ff2137836a3dae35854740f384ed95ca54d4.png" class="latex" alt="$4\in S,$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="49" height="18" /> then the possibilities of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/7/c/37cabfa8bafebd871d5131f68335aebf5b1f0dca.png" class="latex" alt="$\{4,5,6,7,9,11\}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="124" height="20" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/4/b/c4bb1f88e7a75c0befe520032b814c3f0132a5ca.png" class="latex" alt="$\{4,6,7,9,10,11\}.$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="138" height="20" /> So, the least possible value of an element in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/d/a2d1eb7107858447cf70e9e6ce4073c1b1c3fc15.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 4}.$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="61" height="30" />
</p><p><u><b>Remark</b></u>
</p><p>There exist multiple such partitions of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/0/a/a0a45d4d00f382fb4ab24bc03e04e4d48e08cca4.png" class="latex" alt="$\{1,2,\ldots,12\}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="102" height="18" /> into six nonempty subsets, one of which is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/0/aa06facdffeaa11b463d5349f7df4aa937417769.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\{1,2,6,12\}, \ \{3,9\}, \ \{4,8\}, \ \{5,10\}, \ \{7\}, \ \{11\}.\]" width="364" height="18" />
Regardless of which partition we use, we will conclude that to minimize the least element of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/2/2f2e8e8dd78b26ddc9ac278cc179101d30644cae.png" class="latex" alt="$S,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="15" height="16" /> the only possibilities for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/7/c/37cabfa8bafebd871d5131f68335aebf5b1f0dca.png" class="latex" alt="$\{4,5,6,7,9,11\}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="124" height="20" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/4/b/c4bb1f88e7a75c0befe520032b814c3f0132a5ca.png" class="latex" alt="$\{4,6,7,9,10,11\}.$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="138" height="20" />
</p><p>~MRENTHUSIASM
