<p>A piece of cheese is located at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/6/836333fe92d63dccc6ba1e2ae80df30ea9ed5dbf.png" class="latex" alt="$(12,10)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="57" height="18" /> in a <a href="/wiki/index.php/Coordinate_plane" class="mw-redirect" title="Coordinate plane">coordinate plane</a>. A mouse is at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/4/3/043b3c9c08690a58e011eb238316a58295360bed.png" class="latex" alt="$(4,-2)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="52" height="18" /> and is running up the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Line" title="Line">line</a> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/4/f/c4fd99f6fd96f5b045d52ee984bd5f0ac3d16f55.png" class="latex" alt="$y=-5x+18$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="107" height="16" />. At the point <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/8/8e8da0aef2d19017da7471378386d61620f288f5.png" class="latex" alt="$(a,b)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" /> the mouse starts getting farther from the cheese rather than closer to it. What is  <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/d/add2609af1bb7632cc22b68fd49fdd94de06b327.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="40" height="14" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/a/91a8bc1f9043d0e64a70f4539ca34d59d0036be7.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ 6\qquad \mathrm{(B)}\ 10\qquad \mathrm{(C)}\ 14\qquad \mathrm{(D)}\ 18\qquad \mathrm{(E)}\ 22$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="378" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>We are trying to find the point where distance between the mouse and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/8/b68a82bba7425a629a6a8fb2101ae024cc8b8347.png" class="latex" alt="$(12, 10)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="57" height="18" /> is minimized. This point is where the line that passes through <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/8/b68a82bba7425a629a6a8fb2101ae024cc8b8347.png" class="latex" alt="$(12, 10)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="57" height="18" /> and is perpendicular to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/4/f/c4fd99f6fd96f5b045d52ee984bd5f0ac3d16f55.png" class="latex" alt="$y=-5x+18$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="107" height="16" /> intersects <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/4/f/c4fd99f6fd96f5b045d52ee984bd5f0ac3d16f55.png" class="latex" alt="$y=-5x+18$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="107" height="16" />. By basic knowledge of perpendicular lines, this line is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/f/79fb319e6bf764c7a3507aa7943f13e9254eb74a.png" class="latex" alt="$y=\frac{x}{5}+\frac{38}{5}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="91" height="37" />. This line intersects <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/4/f/c4fd99f6fd96f5b045d52ee984bd5f0ac3d16f55.png" class="latex" alt="$y=-5x+18$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="107" height="16" /> at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/0/990cfee9f8fcfcd28b839edff474bb09d81e4891.png" class="latex" alt="$(2,8)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />. So <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/f/03fd11fc14f3659bf9aab99365c46bf813adbe80.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b=\boxed{10}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="93" height="23" />. - MegaLucario1001
</p><p><br />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>If the mouse is at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/0/9/f096d8fa66ca5624ef03016d697c772f99318a99.png" class="latex" alt="$(x, y) = (x, 18 - 5x)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="157" height="18" />, then the square of the distance from the mouse to the cheese is
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/2/b/92bea7c497a5d8132a43fb499227c27e407cbd5c.png" class="latex" alt="$(x - 12)^2 + (8 - 5x)^2 = 26(x^2 - 4x + 8) = 26((x - 2)^2 + 4)$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="467" height="21" />.
The value of this expression is smallest when <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/e/f/2effc7979b1c93e59356b73253d75e2740b33105.png" class="latex" alt="$x = 2$" width="43" height="12" />, so the mouse is closest to the cheese at the point <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/c/bac0bd179c9337dea0f245160802ea78e9584a89.png" class="latex" alt="$(2, 8)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/4/1/1413115dc451a033eac3f6c16e0d3a2a16d1857f.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b=2+8 = \boxed{10}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="158" height="23" />.
-Paixiao
</p>
