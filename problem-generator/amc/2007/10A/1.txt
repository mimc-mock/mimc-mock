<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Official_Solution">Official Solution</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/6/5/d650ee71ee0530237c68c8017f7a7a9922e1a369.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{Answer: (C)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="103" height="17" /> 
Susan pays <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/4/7/b47260fcc4466689f3d9e25c92eecbdd49b71e82.png" class="latex" alt="$(4)(0.75)(20) = 60$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="144" height="18" /> dollars. Pam pays <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/8/d/58df5c262098772f3ef75b60ec5ca85f6e6e8c1d.png" class="latex" alt="$(5)(0.70)(20) = 70$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="144" height="18" /> dollars, so she pays <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/1/8/c188f9e369d611c166a2cb5e7358951c0f82fbfa.png" class="latex" alt="$70-60=10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="100" height="13" /> more dollars than Susan.
