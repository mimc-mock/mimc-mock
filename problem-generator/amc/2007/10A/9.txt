<p>Real numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" /> satisfy the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Equation" title="Equation">equations</a> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/d/5/4d5f27672e51ebe66a48c1fc6415b7431eb92bec.png" class="latex" alt="$3^{a} = 81^{b + 2}$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="81" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/9/9095cb3077ffb9a30ad90785c4470fbf0860ced0.png" class="latex" alt="$125^{b} = 5^{a - 3}$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="90" height="16" />. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/f/8/ef870ce9fefe09b80a97b05a7b9198414c1b35f7.png" class="latex" alt="$ab$" width="17" height="12" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/c/3/ac3a3ac2c5ea60d773c71b5e7728f30ac1bf5420.png" class="latex" alt="$\text{(A)}\ -60 \qquad \text{(B)}\ -17 \qquad \text{(C)}\ 9 \qquad \text{(D)}\ 12 \qquad \text{(E)}\ 60$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="411" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/2/0f2765a3b43aef194ebe11a4a63c5083344208ce.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[81^{b+2} = 3^{4(b+2)} = 3^a \Longrightarrow a = 4b+8\]" width="277" height="17" />
</p><p>And
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/e/c/decae380961b77b05f8b9876d54f4c9777d2e402.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[125^{b} = 5^{3b} = 5^{a-3} \Longrightarrow a - 3 = 3b\]" width="258" height="16" />
</p><p>Substitution gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/7/6/d76378f19645a86585270a984b337036636492f8.png" class="latex" alt="$4b+8 - 3 = 3b \Longrightarrow b = -5$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="214" height="14" />, and solving for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> yields <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/a/dcadf6b880a738849845ef4a0bb57403e9898359.png" class="latex" alt="$-12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="31" height="12" />. Thus <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/e/9ae4250fc6037cdf5180bfbd2f038b8cb76f87e4.png" class="latex" alt="$ab = 60\ \mathrm{(E)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="90" height="18" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1_another_similar_way">Solution 1 another similar way</span></h2>
<p>Simplify equation <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />, which is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/9/a39024d0ecd6ac28b56ff275454b85bf70a2071c.png" class="latex" alt="$3^a=81^{b+2}$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="81" height="16" />, to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/5/6/c56c25d3fba735464db559f146f747c31d423008.png" class="latex" alt="$3^a=3^{4b+8}$" width="79" height="15" />. 
</p><p>And
</p><p>Simplify equation <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />, which is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/845ad1dc31db5d2a0c969c402818acfd8dcbee96.png" class="latex" alt="$125^b=5^{a-3}$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="90" height="16" />, to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/1/9/7196dc08a396a617595bf10424caf8645951cc5d.png" class="latex" alt="$5^{3b}=5^{a-3}$" width="79" height="15" />.
</p><p>Now, eliminate the bases from the simplified equations <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> to arrive at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/d/eedb55816971473714a069b4032b21f1901f4fc6.png" class="latex" alt="$a=4b+8$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="81" height="14" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/2/8/228da89fe4ca22a6262f0d0946ee54a7ab6a5fa8.png" class="latex" alt="$3b=a-3$" width="81" height="12" />. Rewrite equation <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> so that it is in terms of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />. That would be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/c/f/ccf60ce78950a9b14dbb3cb52551dec7e151f445.png" class="latex" alt="$a=3b+3$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="81" height="14" />.
</p><p>Since both equations are equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />, and the values for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" /> are constant for both equations, set the equations equal to each other.
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/6/1/06162c381db3e01b7cfc28f35ad2d20f1f487e63.png" class="latex" alt="$4b+8=3b+3 \Longrightarrow b=-5$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="214" height="14" />
</p><p>Now plug <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />, which is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/5/2/a526e44e36254c196af103613bc724f312b6a209.png" class="latex" alt="$-5$" width="22" height="12" />, back into one of the two earlier equations.
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/0/ee0b5254c8391ef39ef8be0ec3d57f233ad8063c.png" class="latex" alt="$4(-5)+8=a \Longrightarrow -20+8=a \Longrightarrow a=-12$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="353" height="18" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/1/281fb71e368b30ac6d47e0c08c10214389bb92e2.png" class="latex" alt="$(-12)(-5)=60$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="125" height="18" />
</p><p>Therefore the correct answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/a/3/3a3faf1572d785ef1d5d72f120fb3a16190b4b61.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(E)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="25" height="18" />
</p>
