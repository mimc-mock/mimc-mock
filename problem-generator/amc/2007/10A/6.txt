<p>At Euclid High School, the number of students taking the <a href="/wiki/index.php/AMC_10" title="AMC 10">AMC 10</a> was <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/0/2/302e99c1c7cd91a6ae371139e5142ccef5977dca.png" class="latex" alt="$60$" width="17" height="12" /> in 2002, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/4/2d40f3652c1962e98ce050a88c04ed37997c67df.png" class="latex" alt="$66$" width="17" height="12" /> in 2003, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/9/aa94edfb9739dcc66dad90f7dd5c148f44a57e2c.png" class="latex" alt="$70$" width="17" height="12" /> in 2004, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/5/1d5db6ea360f9b1367e4db85c18474906401e2c0.png" class="latex" alt="$76$" width="17" height="12" /> in 2005, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/6/d/76d15d808807bfa561e72dc8111649ed0a204ebe.png" class="latex" alt="$78$" width="17" height="12" /> and 2006, and is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/e/7fea07b9bf739f8e420081f31186264b91ced2ce.png" class="latex" alt="$85$" width="17" height="12" /> in 2007. Between what two consecutive years was there the largest percentage increase?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/5/4/c540813196397eaf04ed89dae18091ec338fdb91.png" class="latex" alt="$\text{(A)}\ 2002\ \text{and}\ 2003 \qquad \text{(B)}\ 2003\ \text{and}\ 2004 \qquad \text{(C)}\ 2004\ \text{and}\ 2005 \qquad \text{(D)}\ 2005\ \text{and}\ 2006 \qquad \text{(E)}\ 2006\ \text{and}\ 2007$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="837" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>We compute the percentage increases:
</p>
<ol><li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/8/9/389e5ba4a88996c159c1963346c7a98af4590040.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{66 - 60}{60} = 10\%$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="119" height="37" /></li>
<li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/f/2/5f26a79b31be5df93c7f405ebe62ce0d8dad2ccf.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{70 - 66}{66} \approx 6\%$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="110" height="37" /></li>
<li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/5/0f55735d70f0b2afeb9ca709e0ee8c610f7375f9.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{76-70}{70} \approx 8.6\%$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="124" height="37" /></li>
<li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/f/d1fe473b8ca10c03899105f334f56489d34a531b.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{78-76}{76} \approx 2.6\%$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="124" height="37" /></li>
<li><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/3/6c30c6fe0cb92105bdf1d6ae6179d67c621ea070.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{85-78}{78} \approx 9\%$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="110" height="37" /></li></ol>
<p>The answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/a/2/ca2f4283cfd441270cb90bfd2cf89a533f3dde81.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="26" height="18" />. 
</p><p>In fact, the answer follows directly from examining the differences between each year. The largest differences are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/a/e0a0db32027a732ac57d37ef2ae9bb150f65b108.png" class="latex" alt="$7$" width="9" height="12" />. Due to the decreased starting number of students between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/e/96e470b7ce7a2f4d0c1ae064d2874ca377693897.png" class="latex" alt="$2002$" width="35" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/9/1/b910f4dafca80b54c6e7bedf63b6485eebff0764.png" class="latex" alt="$2003$" width="35" height="12" />, that interval will be our answer.
</p><p>~edited by mobius247
</p>
