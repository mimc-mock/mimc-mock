<p>Define <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/0/0/3001f8ca4ba92117ea7cbc8ce8b98a54d00f9b62.png" class="latex" alt="$a@b = ab - b^{2}$" width="109" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/4/844838873e2f9f1afc1053795f8d6e10b67bea34.png" class="latex" alt="$a\#b = a + b - ab^{2}$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="142" height="18" />. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/4/9f4c55991491fcb82afadd5d19474a0b7539bccc.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac {6@2}{6\#2}$" style="vertical-align: -16px" width="35" height="41" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/9/7/a97d9015d43afb56a01cbd1ef64aa583abcc6a52.png" class="latex" alt="$\text{(A)}\ - \frac {1}{2}\qquad \text{(B)}\ - \frac {1}{4}\qquad \text{(C)}\ \frac {1}{8}\qquad \text{(D)}\ \frac {1}{4}\qquad \text{(E)}\ \frac {1}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="393" height="37" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/5/4/7541531833ff6358250ac6796327414a2ad1e8ef.png" class="latex" alt="$6@2$" width="31" height="13" /> must be equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/2/f72dd26dc28445b623ee8839711017cc0e7ae8d9.png" class="latex" alt="$6*2-2^2$" width="72" height="15" /> which is 8. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/8/e/48ec4d369c3f5624c91ce4163cc8cf9548493f9e.png" class="latex" alt="$6\# 2$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="32" height="16" /> is equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/e/4/3e4dbf07bd487429fa07cd50327e6b1629bb11a6.png" class="latex" alt="$6+2-6*2^2$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="104" height="16" /> which is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/e/5/1e55578ccf9d13ad09cdcce181f0853a349018da.png" class="latex" alt="$8-24 = -16$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="105" height="13" />. Therefore <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/c/5bcdbe691ceafd760fdaf79de3825367e4cada62.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{6@2}{6\# 2}$" style="vertical-align: -16px" width="35" height="41" /> must be equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/c/82c9e4bd3061c0ca8afec87e7068ffda0a50c626.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{8}{-16} = -\frac{1}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="86" height="38" />. Therefore the solution is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" />.
</p>
