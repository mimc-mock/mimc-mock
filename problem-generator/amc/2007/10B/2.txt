<p>Define the operation <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/7/d17950ad2ab63c764778b133f95a03dcf2969d57.png" class="latex" alt="$\star$" width="9" height="9" /> by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/0/670f16c293beeb2b6896d90e1a0152b299183eb8.png" class="latex" alt="$a \star b = (a+b)b.$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="124" height="18" /> What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/c/7cc1492f8eecd03f649161a83acd42471bc7d508.png" class="latex" alt="$(3 \star 5) - (5 \star 3)?$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="129" height="18" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/7/e57a6bab7e7c92558d685a15cf1fb68822f915e5.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } -16 \qquad\textbf{(B) } -8 \qquad\textbf{(C) } 0 \qquad\textbf{(D) } 8 \qquad\textbf{(E) } 16$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="415" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Substitute and simplify.
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/a/17a3171fd336e8778f58d182a02e1600a87f8bcb.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[(3+5)5 - (5+3)3 = (3+5)2 = 8\cdot2 = \boxed{\textbf{(E) }16}\]" width="379" height="28" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Note that
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/8/1/a816cff0fb7159c0d8b06b735391098bfab42295.png" class="latex" alt="$(a \star b) - (b \star a) = (a+b)b - (b+a)a= (a+b)(b-a)= b^2 - a^2$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="504" height="21" />. We can substitute <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/0/340605c5f88cf6864adc5b146c3dffca7a45df22.png" class="latex" alt="$a=3$" width="42" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/6/4f69c1ec597d19324140f3130e9d63367f4b69c5.png" class="latex" alt="$b=5$" width="40" height="12" /> to get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/b/0/fb07763d0af51058046c986f07c9ce4ecf93429e.png" class="latex" alt="$5^2 - 3^2 = \boxed{\textbf{(E) }16}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="145" height="30" />.
</p><p>~MathFun1000 (Minor Edits)
</p>
