<p>It takes Clea 60 seconds to walk down an escalator when it is not operating, and only 24 seconds to walk down the escalator when it is operating. How many seconds does it take Clea to ride down the operating escalator when she just stands on it?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/8/0/a80d7c1af573197fcfe67ff334e6bec7a26baae8.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 36\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 40\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 42\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 48\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 52$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f37bba504894945c07a32f5496d74299a37aa51c.png" class="latex" alt="$s$" width="8" height="8" /> be the speed of the escalator and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> be the speed of Clea. Using <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/3/9/139e6850443c2cdcaf493dbb098c1dc31d53b472.png" class="latex" alt="$d = v t$" width="49" height="12" />, the first statement can be translated to the equation <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/2/dc2301c2948b48994e2fdea12343efb9b2bc3939.png" class="latex" alt="$d = 60c$" width="59" height="12" />. The second statement can be translated to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/c/cbcd19fe885fd502ff2e1bf246627edef1fb071b.png" class="latex" alt="$d = 24(c+s)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="103" height="18" />. Since the same distance is being covered in each scenario, we can set the two equations equal and solve for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f37bba504894945c07a32f5496d74299a37aa51c.png" class="latex" alt="$s$" width="8" height="8" />. We find that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/1/2/3129f725e045425e58bebaad2c2f5fae9519e491.png" class="latex" alt="$s = \dfrac{3c}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="52" height="37" />. The problem asks for the time it takes her to ride down the escalator when she just stands on it. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/d/60d392e34c1dbd8ba6667815e9cdb0609b4ff768.png" class="latex" alt="$t = \dfrac{d}{s}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="42" height="37" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/2/dc2301c2948b48994e2fdea12343efb9b2bc3939.png" class="latex" alt="$d = 60c$" width="59" height="12" />, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/e/2fe8f2e3bc00d4bb502d6d85ef8fdd2eb2266605.png" class="latex" alt="$t = \dfrac{60c}{\dfrac{3c}{2}} = 40$" style="vertical-align: -34px" width="102" height="59" /> seconds. Answer choice <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/2/e/f2e33ab9a00e021af49e888212645d4667903c49.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{B}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="26" height="24" /> is correct.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f37bba504894945c07a32f5496d74299a37aa51c.png" class="latex" alt="$s$" width="8" height="8" /> be the speed of the escalator and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> be the speed of Clea. Then <a href="/wiki/index.php/Without_loss_of_generality" title="Without loss of generality">without loss of generality</a>, assume that the length of the escalator be 1. Then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/1/b116c642f18120997f81719aba4b392803d8d35b.png" class="latex" alt="$c=\dfrac{1}{60}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="52" height="37" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/e/beeab7f81e53faf8fee7e090e5f737b0e8620631.png" class="latex" alt="$c+s=\dfrac{1}{24}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="83" height="37" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/6/3/46395b6ba6661c0c63e9d86070e2ccf3ca9eee5a.png" class="latex" alt="$s=\dfrac{1}{24}-\dfrac{1}{60}=\dfrac{1}{40}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="144" height="37" />. Thus the time it takes for Clea to ride down the operating escalator when she just stands on it is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/6/e/76eafe8b83058f2ecb66513446191fa177f227b3.png" class="latex" alt="$\dfrac{1}{\dfrac{1}{40}}=\boxed{\textbf{(B)}\ 40}$" style="vertical-align: -35px" width="114" height="59" />.
</p>
