<p>What is the sum of all integer solutions to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/e/9/3e97fbbee3f9961bdd22dba2f270967cbf2203fa.png" class="latex" alt="$1&lt;(x-2)^2&lt;25$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="138" height="19" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/c/34cc6451fb0390669afa420d6f86835c0700aefc.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 10\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 12\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 15\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 19\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 25$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/0/8/4089987a9c991bba4233d802131442ff4443263e.png" class="latex" alt="$(x-2)^2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="61" height="19" /> = perfect square.
</p><p>1 &lt; perfect square &lt; 25
</p><p>Perfect square can equal: 4, 9, or 16
</p><p>Solve for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/a/91adcfc7af2fcc8e2317ca2245816ea73449e5cf.png" class="latex" alt="$(x-2)^2=4$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="96" height="19" /> 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/9/5/495fd4bdf9a17185e56e55c840ad27a0608c4826.png" class="latex" alt="$x=4,0$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="60" height="16" />
</p><p>and
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/b/1/7b1c40a3cc9cb3f5a4cf82b844bc3d8e80e39620.png" class="latex" alt="$(x-2)^2=9$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="96" height="19" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/7/6/776f17d830499ea9a26ef90e87c50dff9dfcfe29.png" class="latex" alt="$x=5,-1$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="74" height="16" />
</p><p>and
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/4/0/0408ad3900c55f774fefb2b3754ae47822a15b66.png" class="latex" alt="$(x-2)^2=16$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="105" height="19" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/3/9036839d6eac7f360718876f66a4eeee34a82c95.png" class="latex" alt="$x=6,-2$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="74" height="16" />
</p><p>The sum of all integer solutions is
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/b/4/2b45036ac9e226090485117ea75aa357ae49377d.png" class="latex" alt="$4+5+6+0+(-1)+(-2)=\boxed{\textbf{(B)} 12}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="304" height="28" />
</p>
