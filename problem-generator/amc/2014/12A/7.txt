<p>The first three terms of a geometric progression are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/8/99867f5c473020300fc4d6c2da322e550a0bc2f6.png" class="latex" alt="$\sqrt 3$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="24" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/d/f/9df2b093dad7aff038bf8e76013debc0f33465dc.png" class="latex" alt="$\sqrt[3]3$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="25" height="18" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/6/f76bf02aeb1d529aef2a4953c053ddaef97334fb.png" class="latex" alt="$\sqrt[6]3$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="25" height="18" />.  What is the fourth term?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/e/1ce555f7402c58e7f2bd569b30fd28c2495d9532.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }1\qquad\textbf{(B) }\sqrt[7]3\qquad\textbf{(C) }\sqrt[8]3\qquad\textbf{(D) }\sqrt[9]3\qquad\textbf{(E) }\sqrt[10]3\qquad$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="422" height="21" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The terms are  <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/8/99867f5c473020300fc4d6c2da322e550a0bc2f6.png" class="latex" alt="$\sqrt 3$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="24" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/d/f/9df2b093dad7aff038bf8e76013debc0f33465dc.png" class="latex" alt="$\sqrt[3]3$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="25" height="18" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/6/f76bf02aeb1d529aef2a4953c053ddaef97334fb.png" class="latex" alt="$\sqrt[6]3$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="25" height="18" />, which are equivalent to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/8/4/684558343af1fdf8588efdbd141b495f6ef01027.png" class="latex" alt="$3^{\frac{3}{6}}$" width="17" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/0/cb0a85b22e4583b5f1f985ccb2b25ff4bb53e248.png" class="latex" alt="$3^{\frac{2}{6}}$" width="17" height="18" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/e/20e8cd6c3e49307173c3305081a9ee699c36ac97.png" class="latex" alt="$3^{\frac{1}{6}}$" width="17" height="18" />.  So the next term will be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c3d746089358315da3154c203094d6b9fa17e5f.png" class="latex" alt="$3^{\frac{0}{6}}=1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="18" />, so the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/e/35e8ea2d16518197b5113002b8c0c7da6c311a3e.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(A)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="41" height="28" />.
</p>
