</p><p>Case <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />&#160;: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/b/d4baf6d4b1cfa69b68b3204b5e2eba0c5755fe20.png" class="latex" alt="$x-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="40" height="12" />
</p><p>We know that this number takes the form <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/6/5/f65951a84bd3858470b381686eb32c48f093ee7b.png" class="latex" alt="$abcde... 124$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="87" height="14" /> where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/5/c850fa6c15da60232724cd75ab225a6dd814e8ae.png" class="latex" alt="$abcde...$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="60" height="14" /> is a integer that ends in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/1/c/71c1636bd2876630688f77deef38f6203e8a5875.png" class="latex" alt="$124$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="27" height="12" />. Because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/5/c850fa6c15da60232724cd75ab225a6dd814e8ae.png" class="latex" alt="$abcde...$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="60" height="14" /> is a multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> times an even number <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/a/a3a59bb1293ee3f6dec19de4019a7178874219ae.png" class="latex" alt="$e$" width="8" height="8" /> while <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/1/c/71c1636bd2876630688f77deef38f6203e8a5875.png" class="latex" alt="$124$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="27" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/e/1/9e172a5f04e2fa000988b79863d3e51fe3861e2c.png" class="latex" alt="$4(31)$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="43" height="20" />, we find that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/c/b/5cb16bfc3a50768461a7b417ebfb3da5789d9d25.png" class="latex" alt="$X-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="46" height="13" /> must be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/8/b/98b2ca62dd4d54e6e62ebd3829abbf293d26213f.png" class="latex" alt="$4e + 4 \cdot 31 = 4(e+31) = 4o$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="221" height="20" /> where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/e/a/3eabe6c97f03e413d1aa3c292e9478f286e64df1.png" class="latex" alt="$o$" width="9" height="8" /> is an odd number
</p><p>Case <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />&#160;: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/c/c/cccb103143393c0119f5ab61d6d53a852ce70fcf.png" class="latex" alt="$x+1$" width="40" height="13" />
</p><p>We know that this number <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/0/9/b09844ed5175e98951e1fd7726543c14261cf0d7.png" class="latex" alt="$fghijk...126$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="100" height="18" /> ends. Because it is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> more than the number <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/b/d4baf6d4b1cfa69b68b3204b5e2eba0c5755fe20.png" class="latex" alt="$x-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="40" height="12" />, which is a multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />, we find <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/6/906cca155f13e01f51c7a5067bdaac37da6f846d.png" class="latex" alt="$x+1 = 4o + 2$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="117" height="15" /> which is an even number that is not divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />. Thus, it must have a maximum of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />.
</p><p>This means that for any number <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> being in the form <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/0/a/f0a1ae7267b12fc456ea9e4bd495e17b31c987cb.png" class="latex" alt="$5^{n}$" width="17" height="12" /> where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> is an odd integer, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/b/d4baf6d4b1cfa69b68b3204b5e2eba0c5755fe20.png" class="latex" alt="$x-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="40" height="12" /> must have a maximum of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> factors of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> while <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/c/c/cccb103143393c0119f5ab61d6d53a852ce70fcf.png" class="latex" alt="$x+1$" width="40" height="13" /> must have a maximum of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> factor of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />. 
</p><p><br />
</p><p><br />
~ShangJ2
