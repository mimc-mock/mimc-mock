<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let the original price be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />. Then, for option <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />, the discounted price is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/3/9931b3325d2a93f7e4ffa98b11e31694b9fc1014.png" class="latex" alt="$(1-.15)(1-.15)x = .7225x$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="223" height="18" />. For option <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />, the discounted price is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/4/a/c4a6c88e41f5c9e24046ca43e2619393137790f5.png" class="latex" alt="$(1-.1)(1-.1)(1-.1)x = .729x$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="255" height="18" />. Finally, for option <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />, the discounted price is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/7/ad7d8717b0fb909896e25f274343beb054a1e453.png" class="latex" alt="$(1-.25)(1-.05) = .7125x$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="212" height="18" />. Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> must be greater than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/8/e/a8e325234dd23db5adccfa1f501d700c19003cd0.png" class="latex" alt="$\max(x - .7225x, x-.729x, x-.7125x)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="306" height="18" />. It follows <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/a/cbab8456e0c981b4c458623b4f8ce7e6334176a1.png" class="latex" alt="$n/100$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="46" height="18" /> must be greater than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/1/7f10c71e7d27bba42777221b4540cbcaca0dd387.png" class="latex" alt="$.2875$" width="40" height="12" />. We multiply this by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> to get the percent value, and then round up because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> is the smallest integer that provides a greater discount than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/c/1/3c1694abbbf2ecd583a1b4b06a8a701eccc6e2b3.png" class="latex" alt="$28.75$" width="40" height="12" />, leaving us with the answer of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/e/e/4ee346cac45bd4de313dd1ad479560253a2cd589.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) } 29}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="65" height="28" />
</p>
