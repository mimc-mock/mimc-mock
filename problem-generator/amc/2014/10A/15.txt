<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Note that he drives at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/6/906734e2f1010e9a171ffee8050cbc0649cea37c.png" class="latex" alt="$50$" width="17" height="12" /> miles per hour after the first hour and continues doing so until he arrives.
</p><p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> be the distance still needed to travel after <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> hour. We have that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/9/1/a9127b3a2da512577645cc96eefb074c8db19732.png" class="latex" alt="$\dfrac{d}{50}+1.5=\dfrac{d}{35}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="112" height="37" />, where the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/5/7/15788e198a53ced87268aea78fcde68a84de92c0.png" class="latex" alt="$1.5$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="22" height="13" /> comes from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> hour late decreased to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/2/192b4a258ee998961a99523fd0f0a029f9ea194a.png" class="latex" alt="$0.5$" width="22" height="12" /> hours early.
</p><p>Simplifying gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/5/b/85bf40351ff84d8a1c9f735c4a5118a69884d5aa.png" class="latex" alt="$7d+525=10d$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="120" height="14" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/6/9/d698b8565da4feee5af744c1937ec518087e9387.png" class="latex" alt="$d=175$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="60" height="13" />.
</p><p>Now, we must add an extra <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/7/8c7f6c79f2148f0cec592c9517b40ee1b82ef22e.png" class="latex" alt="$35$" width="17" height="12" /> miles traveled in the first hour, giving a total of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/d/7/0d74cf7d99446217735b58e414f4f8f94203d4ce.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) } 210}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="75" height="30" /> miles.
