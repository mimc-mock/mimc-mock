<p>On an algebra quiz, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/b/5/2b56bd637cb2c936d539be1b5000a64a898af389.png" class="latex" alt="$10\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the students scored <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/9/aa94edfb9739dcc66dad90f7dd5c148f44a57e2c.png" class="latex" alt="$70$" width="17" height="12" /> points, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/f/5/af59f5a81354c1925a58cb897e1d142111b1d7c6.png" class="latex" alt="$35\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> scored <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/a/fea5fcc6d66685ee21f722efcd228eb44d38f262.png" class="latex" alt="$80$" width="17" height="12" /> points, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/e/32e5939a7bdd847ec86582225cd653c240b4abce.png" class="latex" alt="$30\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> scored <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/c/bfcfee7bd8a5e15e5f6b7881c138de43651572a9.png" class="latex" alt="$90$" width="17" height="12" /> points, and the rest scored <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> points. What is the difference between the mean and median score of the students' scores on this quiz?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/5/9959d47fffb760b6cf89894df252b99436e4d8f5.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 1\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 2\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 3\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 4\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 5$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="348" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Without loss of generality, let there be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/3/f8366cd6196dd8a9da6d38a3e9eafb109e99d53e.png" class="latex" alt="$20$" width="17" height="12" /> students (the least whole number possible) who took the test. We have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> students score <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/9/aa94edfb9739dcc66dad90f7dd5c148f44a57e2c.png" class="latex" alt="$70$" width="17" height="12" /> points, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/a/e0a0db32027a732ac57d37ef2ae9bb150f65b108.png" class="latex" alt="$7$" width="9" height="12" /> students score <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/a/fea5fcc6d66685ee21f722efcd228eb44d38f262.png" class="latex" alt="$80$" width="17" height="12" /> points, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> students score <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/c/bfcfee7bd8a5e15e5f6b7881c138de43651572a9.png" class="latex" alt="$90$" width="17" height="12" /> points and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> students score <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> points. Therefore, the mean
is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/1/1/711f5fc6f8d53d2565cff62b15f932f02e0dbc58.png" class="latex" alt="$87$" width="18" height="12" /> and the median is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/c/bfcfee7bd8a5e15e5f6b7881c138de43651572a9.png" class="latex" alt="$90$" width="17" height="12" />.
</p><p>Thus, the solution is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/c/c/9cc0a8bdca61ca93db41147a74ef754c1822910c.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[90-87=3\implies\boxed{\textbf{(C)} \ 3}\]" width="196" height="28" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>The percentage who scored <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> points is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/9/1/591be0cd82614f40222b53f085648726cd07b1ca.png" class="latex" alt="$100\%-(10\%+35\%+30\%)=100\%-75\%=25\%$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="402" height="18" />. Now, we need to find the median, which is the score that splits the upper and lower <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/7/9f7f4de02215a8c5319886c13b2e291811346e1c.png" class="latex" alt="$50\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" />.The lower <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/a/99a5eeab881effc1c646145c490379892a56f0de.png" class="latex" alt="$10\%+35\%=45\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="145" height="15" /> scored <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/9/aa94edfb9739dcc66dad90f7dd5c148f44a57e2c.png" class="latex" alt="$70$" width="17" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/a/fea5fcc6d66685ee21f722efcd228eb44d38f262.png" class="latex" alt="$80$" width="17" height="12" /> points, so the median is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/c/bfcfee7bd8a5e15e5f6b7881c138de43651572a9.png" class="latex" alt="$90$" width="17" height="12" /> (since the upper <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/6/96633b73a86b9d9110e1ff824fce08def96ca296.png" class="latex" alt="$25\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> points and the lower <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/0/8/00890d0beefe80ec2a7d63ca91aaa8b8056ba355.png" class="latex" alt="$45\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/9/aa94edfb9739dcc66dad90f7dd5c148f44a57e2c.png" class="latex" alt="$70$" width="17" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/a/fea5fcc6d66685ee21f722efcd228eb44d38f262.png" class="latex" alt="$80$" width="17" height="12" />).The mean is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/5/99584be6abc05c3664c8dd6f1a9e74fd18e6f936.png" class="latex" alt="$10\%\cdot70+35\%\cdot80+30\%\cdot90+25\%\cdot100=7+28+27+25=87$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="531" height="15" />.
So, our solution is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/6/f3604acb055bc931852d1ceb7f11eb9f365b3ee1.png" class="latex" alt="$90-87=3\Rightarrow\boxed{3 \ \textbf{(C)} }$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="175" height="28" /> ~sosiaops
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>The <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/3/a/73a6210accc9d864f00e0d96769864fe7d1e01d7.png" class="latex" alt="$\le 80$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="36" height="15" />-point scores make up <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/9/2/292833fa853a0cd641c80817fa96fac2161a63a4.png" class="latex" alt="$10\%+35\% = 45\% &lt; 50\%$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="206" height="17" /> of the scores, but the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/9/1/d917725a96c70b49737efa3559c2a7b488c3f432.png" class="latex" alt="$\le 90$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="36" height="15" />-point scores make up <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/0/1905f2d5dfdff2d84405c4b97ad8943d498d50bc.png" class="latex" alt="$45\%+30\% = 75\% &gt; 50\%$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="206" height="17" /> of the scores, so the median is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/c/bfcfee7bd8a5e15e5f6b7881c138de43651572a9.png" class="latex" alt="$90$" width="17" height="12" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/b/5/2b56bd637cb2c936d539be1b5000a64a898af389.png" class="latex" alt="$10\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of scores were <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/3/c/63c31f63d47a6b698015522eef67e9c201b3534b.png" class="latex" alt="$70-90 = -20$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="117" height="15" /> more than the median, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/f/5/af59f5a81354c1925a58cb897e1d142111b1d7c6.png" class="latex" alt="$35\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> were <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/7/7/d7701e9469f26e22c106341791344b28f3196443.png" class="latex" alt="$-10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="31" height="13" /> more, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/5/8/95809f2c389dc2f2ca38c0d5b5618219ab83ad8a.png" class="latex" alt="$100\%-75\% = 25\%$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="157" height="17" /> were <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> more, and the rest were equal. This means that the mean score is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/8/e283aa94dc34434fe73743a156a1d91c13da2b57.png" class="latex" alt="$10\%\cdot(-20)+35\%\cdot(-10)+25\%\cdot10 = -2 + (-3.5) + 2.5 = -3$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="511" height="20" /> more than the median, so their difference is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/b/f/3bff83a02f1d2f349778b41410eaebdd80930a6b.png" class="latex" alt="$\left|-3\right| = \boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 3}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="114" height="30" />.
~<a href="/wiki/index.php/User:Emerald_block" title="User:Emerald block">emerald_block</a>
</p>
