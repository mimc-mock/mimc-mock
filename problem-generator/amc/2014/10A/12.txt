<p>A regular hexagon has side length 6. Congruent arcs with radius 3 are drawn with the center at each of the vertices, creating circular sectors as shown. The region inside the hexagon but outside the sectors is shaded as shown What is the area of the shaded region?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/d/e1d7da1287cb46e28237f0befa7b1a4f9d721ef9.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] size(125); defaultpen(linewidth(0.8)); path hexagon=(2*dir(0))--(2*dir(60))--(2*dir(120))--(2*dir(180))--(2*dir(240))--(2*dir(300))--cycle; fill(hexagon,grey); for(int i=0;i&lt;=5;i=i+1) { path arc=2*dir(60*i)--arc(2*dir(60*i),1,120+60*i,240+60*i)--cycle; unfill(arc); draw(arc); } draw(hexagon,linewidth(1.8));[/asy]" width="208" height="182" />

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>The area of the hexagon is equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/0/0/b00d964357b0c802520c6c6b23eea8d5d310ec54.png" class="latex" alt="$\dfrac{3(6)^2\sqrt{3}}{2}=54\sqrt{3}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="135" height="41" /> by the formula for the area of a hexagon.
</p><p>We note that each interior angle of the regular hexagon is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/f/f3fd424e953ac76b50bf6103875acc049d21d3c2.png" class="latex" alt="$120^\circ$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="33" height="13" /> which means that each sector is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/4/4/c443e0e31bcbc53302d23755444ed9e46190f1f1.png" class="latex" alt="$\dfrac{1}{3}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="11" height="37" /> of the circle it belongs to. The area of each sector is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/a/4/1a48d83bbbf4bafabdcf4fb511ad6c2fb240fda2.png" class="latex" alt="$\dfrac{9\pi}{3}=3\pi$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="68" height="37" />. The area of all six is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/a/0/6a071d5178fb89dc51c03a33689be4f2c4cc4f5c.png" class="latex" alt="$6\times 3\pi=18\pi$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="104" height="13" />. 
</p><p>The shaded area is equal to the area of the hexagon minus the sum of the area of all the sectors, which is equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/e/3/de3ce8825932b5ce20b130d1cd9a5ac3a4077d88.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 54\sqrt{3}-18\pi}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="139" height="33" />
</p>
