<p>Danica drove her new car on a trip for a whole number of hours, averaging 55 miles per hour. At the beginning of the trip, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96a81938ca5e71faa6894d9d52ac776c9efbe75d.png" class="latex" alt="$abc$" width="25" height="12" /> miles was displayed on the odometer, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96a81938ca5e71faa6894d9d52ac776c9efbe75d.png" class="latex" alt="$abc$" width="25" height="12" /> is a 3-digit number with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/6/5/76555552c60df1ccc621fd6a0c2f9a10885ce753.png" class="latex" alt="$a \geq{1}$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="42" height="14" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/c/c3c33805aaf4f2700a7a30b7ebb97ba8fd4612d7.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+c \leq{7}$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="103" height="15" />. At the end of the trip, the odometer showed <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/a/82a16fbef6d82c0de43b9512df7cf77b894b22ae.png" class="latex" alt="$cba$" width="25" height="12" /> miles. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/8/bf889907d1b955a87013cb264ed2fe2c0bafb283.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2+b^2+c^2?$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="100" height="16" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/a/b/fab1c7a15009d2774a015d6380f311a37f9c78cf.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 26\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 27\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 36\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 37\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 41$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="393" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>We know that the number of miles she drove is divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> must either be the equal or differ by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />. We can quickly conclude that the former is impossible, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> must be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> apart.  Because we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/2/602e211de3a0f69f7d22420aefcba5834d71bf8a.png" class="latex" alt="$c &gt; a$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="41" height="10" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/b/cbb8c900a7bbc3bca6374914bfb80c453c88b79e.png" class="latex" alt="$a + c \le 7$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="73" height="14" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/6/4/d644bfba7a125eab0feb6f52761c803a0215255e.png" class="latex" alt="$a \ge 1$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="42" height="14" />, we find that the only possible values for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" />, respectively.  Because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/a/7/da735a0c1eebf010d71281b3be9c0c35bfa4703c.png" class="latex" alt="$a + b + c \le 7$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="103" height="15" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/7/be7a58270d4285821716c6093a83541f3ea31dc7.png" class="latex" alt="$b = 0$" width="40" height="12" />.  Therefore, we have
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/a/3/1a3c061138770801170b4377f6deae8a9fb4a2a3.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[a^2 + b^2 + c^2 = 36 + 0 + 1 = \boxed{\textbf{(D)}\ 37}\]" width="286" height="28" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Let the number of hours Danica drove be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" />. Then we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/3/f/73fc6f823bbf8f9ea9d7d57c45cb55f837bb3d02.png" class="latex" alt="$100a + 10b + c + 55k$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="165" height="14" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/5/1758c3c9d08f2e758f3df83adcecdcbcfa81a415.png" class="latex" alt="$100c + 10b + a$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="115" height="14" />. Simplifying, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/6/1/f617075b5d64bd61f8282e28fd110c7e46b24200.png" class="latex" alt="$99c - 99a = 55k$" width="128" height="12" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/9/079ca23c65ac014db9f21ae62e63925afe6a1d32.png" class="latex" alt="$9c - 9a = 5k$" width="100" height="12" />. Thus, k is divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />. Because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/b/b/dbb288bd3a7d8c2594d9c81de1f739d91c516444.png" class="latex" alt="$55 * 18 = 990$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="104" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" /> must be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />, and therefore <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/d/8/8d8d5017e7cece634ed5994aa4e1c334a1074ee0.png" class="latex" alt="$c - a = 5$" width="72" height="12" />. Because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/0/a/30aac425ab13c6c060a7521a90d2ef5983d53df2.png" class="latex" alt="$a + b + c \leq{7}$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="103" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/6/5/76555552c60df1ccc621fd6a0c2f9a10885ce753.png" class="latex" alt="$a \geq{1}$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="42" height="14" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/8/d/48d02444a59b917b64e8bd0963b88712fc7fa0a3.png" class="latex" alt="$a = 1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="42" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/d/d/7dd32a3f4397c1dc8606e89d5a85198d87fa27fe.png" class="latex" alt="$c = 6$" width="40" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/7/be7a58270d4285821716c6093a83541f3ea31dc7.png" class="latex" alt="$b = 0$" width="40" height="12" />, and our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/2/0/7206c2b30aaad5caa439bfc8313be05daf6eaaac.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2 + b^2 + c^2 = 6^2 + 0^2 + 1^2 = 37$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="252" height="16" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/5/b/55bdade53f471aab49f63d1a734979cc0c636640.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{D}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="27" height="24" />.
</p><p><br />
</p>
