<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/b/4/4b4cade9ca8a2c8311fafcf040bc5b15ca507f52.png" class="latex" alt="$P$" width="14" height="12" /> be a cubic polynomial with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/b/ffb631ed8dbefd419c68127d53f1b52a6a415e8c.png" class="latex" alt="$P(0) = k$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="71" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/2/3/2232637215c031f57b90bc7d8ef40581276faf33.png" class="latex" alt="$P(1) = 2k$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="80" height="18" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/1/821deaae890cf1e7c3a3c365d0bc09b3e5720c8e.png" class="latex" alt="$P(-1) = 3k$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="94" height="18" />.  What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/e/0/4e0e984581f2dd8ece1e1e1a19a2598532e1e38e.png" class="latex" alt="$P(2) + P(-2)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="109" height="18" />&#160;?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/9/4/494c948def8b12368f59c67a34743f3a2caf5f52.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 0\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ k\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 6k\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 7k\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 14k$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="388" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/5/c/c5cfad4617f71e64e230a264fea4e4b682960aed.png" class="latex" alt="$P(x) = Ax^3+Bx^2 + Cx+D$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="233" height="19" />.   Plugging in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" /> for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, we find <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/b/2/bb23ecb9229709a1aa8fb9344c3db03e1ef45dc4.png" class="latex" alt="$D=k$" width="49" height="12" />, and plugging in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/e/bae5aba07d37ff6ff813107e76260fb31ad5794e.png" class="latex" alt="$-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="22" height="12" /> for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, we obtain the following equations:
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/5/0/1503648965acd04927e5bfe20f7c67db9d183748.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[A+B+C+k=2k\]" width="161" height="14" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/9/8499052701c9d424dbccc5271cdf6a44010d5503.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[-A+B-C+k=3k\]" width="174" height="14" />
Adding these two equations together, we get
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/f/10f65c247560ffb6b2c076f64436e7f7ece20e76.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[2B=3k\]" width="66" height="12" />
If we plug in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/2/a/62a76677add1d246708c86edfc7c4b8a40184914.png" class="latex" alt="$-2$" width="22" height="12" /> in for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, we find that 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/b/8/9b8e66e96cc12c547760e359facdc158eafca131.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[P(2)+P(-2) = 8A+4B+2C+k+(-8A+4B-2C+k)=8B+2k\]" width="494" height="18" />
Multiplying the third equation by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> and adding <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/e/b8ea7ee8a282949fd853a01537ec7a2b55e56f75.png" class="latex" alt="$2k$" width="18" height="12" /> gives us our desired result, so 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/1/57165eb04bd764255ebda7be8aceb512fd52372e.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[P(2)+P(-2)=12k+2k=\boxed{\textbf{(E)}\ 14k}\]" width="301" height="28" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>If we use <a href="/wiki/index.php?title=Gregory%27s_Triangle&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Gregory&#39;s Triangle (page does not exist)">Gregory's Triangle</a>, the following happens:
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/b/e/3beff45e6d4db2ab84f638835cbb87936060a724.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[P(-1), P(0), P(1)\]" width="140" height="18" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/f/c7f4beea8c5ea97ccc7e59771949c7183a715b34.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[3k ,  k  ,  2k\]" width="63" height="16" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/4/9/a4951d6bf8a8b5355aafd6d1094cce06cc04fc11.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[-2k , k\]" width="49" height="16" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/a/c/4acc8781e78a048e26ffcaed3c613285c076a872.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[3k\]" width="18" height="12" />
</p><p>Since this is cubic, the common difference is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/6/ce6582d1a31d1fd6bdab19f0d332c8d93adb9c66.png" class="latex" alt="$3k$" width="18" height="12" /> for the linear level so the string of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/6/ce6582d1a31d1fd6bdab19f0d332c8d93adb9c66.png" class="latex" alt="$3k$" width="18" height="12" />s are infinite in each direction.
If we put a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/6/ce6582d1a31d1fd6bdab19f0d332c8d93adb9c66.png" class="latex" alt="$3k$" width="18" height="12" /> on each side of the original <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/6/ce6582d1a31d1fd6bdab19f0d332c8d93adb9c66.png" class="latex" alt="$3k$" width="18" height="12" />, we can solve for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/1/57198378b469f85f987bdb53a28ed6fcb46ee824.png" class="latex" alt="$P(-2)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="49" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/7/1079eb7c4fc10d5c411bd0524a7f1f8c65a058f9.png" class="latex" alt="$P(2)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="35" height="18" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/7/b67a2b6a9191d789517bc9d680a97d83c61d1081.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[P(-2), P(-1), P(0), P(1), P(2)\]" width="245" height="18" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/6/286558e5d6aeada26eb0ca64a9d79af36cc82ee5.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[8k ,   3k ,  k  ,  2k ,  6k\]" width="117" height="16" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/c/33c324c78cee0b8ba190452713598846e2c7f92e.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[-5k  , -2k ,  k  ,  4k\]" width="118" height="16" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/4/1/641353e8447029c9d32d1887cea86293e0f2fdaa.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[3k   ,  3k ,  3k\]" width="72" height="16" />
</p><p>The above shows us that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/1/57198378b469f85f987bdb53a28ed6fcb46ee824.png" class="latex" alt="$P(-2)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="49" height="18" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/d/b/cdb46da3a4c78f6abaae56f9bb84befe410fe2a6.png" class="latex" alt="$8k$" width="18" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/7/1079eb7c4fc10d5c411bd0524a7f1f8c65a058f9.png" class="latex" alt="$P(2)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="35" height="18" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/e/11eefce055193a7d479d9c6ac55c16fc190a6795.png" class="latex" alt="$6k$" width="18" height="12" /> so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/3/1c3c61850caa5dd90ab685710d41a4fa5e41524e.png" class="latex" alt="$8k+6k=14k$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="112" height="14" />.
</p>
