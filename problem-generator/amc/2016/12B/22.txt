<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Solution by e_power_pi_times_i
</p><p>If <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/e/7fe35200ebda6e90fbff8ed6cdefd1b24208bad5.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{n} = 0.\overline{abcdef}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="107" height="37" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> must be a factor of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/e/d1e03516f20a1788b9bc2c80c6ab1f1edeb9cecc.png" class="latex" alt="$999999$" width="53" height="12" />. Also, by the same procedure, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/4/9/04901235ff573c150d7bedafe9510525f1ace480.png" class="latex" alt="$n+6$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="41" height="14" /> must be a factor of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/5/0/450352c42d8f7a2b16abf3323453aa1ab13cbe0d.png" class="latex" alt="$9999$" width="35" height="12" />. Checking through all the factors of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/e/d1e03516f20a1788b9bc2c80c6ab1f1edeb9cecc.png" class="latex" alt="$999999$" width="53" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/5/0/450352c42d8f7a2b16abf3323453aa1ab13cbe0d.png" class="latex" alt="$9999$" width="35" height="12" /> that are less than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/e/6ee927e1332358c96c62c277441c907c4f51057f.png" class="latex" alt="$1000$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />, we see that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/c/9/cc939937f42bf06ee6259e6d6f80a888bd6f14a7.png" class="latex" alt="$n = 297$" width="62" height="12" /> is a solution, so the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/1/a2194b81820e8380be586c1564c3f5d03d5c100d.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="40" height="28" />.
</p><p>Note: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/9/0/e9021f11dd1349049201973c740d94bdbfa8315d.png" class="latex" alt="$n = 27$" width="53" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c342b01f0cdef0f23d11a2bc9c5aa67f6d83ccb.png" class="latex" alt="$n = 3$" width="43" height="12" /> are both solutions, which invalidates this method. However, we need to examine all factors of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/e/d1e03516f20a1788b9bc2c80c6ab1f1edeb9cecc.png" class="latex" alt="$999999$" width="53" height="12" /> that are not factors of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/6/a/86a2f56dd0e57cb4603f97bc19530bf036969f2a.png" class="latex" alt="$99999$" width="44" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/0/0705411d92671faf1fe5602cfef8f353c7f0f83d.png" class="latex" alt="$999$" width="26" height="12" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/1/a313bf06349648df7be807bee95b651604ce7a7e.png" class="latex" alt="$99$" width="17" height="12" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />. Additionally, we need <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/4/9/04901235ff573c150d7bedafe9510525f1ace480.png" class="latex" alt="$n+6$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="41" height="14" /> to be a factor of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/5/0/450352c42d8f7a2b16abf3323453aa1ab13cbe0d.png" class="latex" alt="$9999$" width="35" height="12" /> but not <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/0/0705411d92671faf1fe5602cfef8f353c7f0f83d.png" class="latex" alt="$999$" width="26" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/1/a313bf06349648df7be807bee95b651604ce7a7e.png" class="latex" alt="$99$" width="17" height="12" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />. Indeed, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/3/2031a31b76c9001a3dab135ff60ce4d6f02b558a.png" class="latex" alt="$297$" width="27" height="12" /> satisfies these requirements.
</p><p>We can see that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/d/a/7da419fbae6e7d19cc3af06fbfe52efd524596d3.png" class="latex" alt="$n=27$" width="53" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/6/3/5635737307f5f0651cced8ee2e6558a426fd27b5.png" class="latex" alt="$n=3$" width="43" height="12" /> are not solutions by checking it in the requirements of the problem: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/b/7ab34943aa07822c7a88628da3d4d7a4eae5da8f.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{3}=0.3333\dots$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="110" height="37" />, period 1, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/a/5/1a5ba6b59e390d673c44717a8a2809685e242756.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{27}=0.037037\dots$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="138" height="37" />, period 3. Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/3/e/13e96a9e71076879ae02457ab5a4e6d8dc598cd0.png" class="latex" alt="$n=297$" width="62" height="12" /> is the only answer.
