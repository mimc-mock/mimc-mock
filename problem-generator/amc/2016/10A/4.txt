<p>The remainder can be defined for all real numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" /> with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/c/8/5c8eb2cb0cf3c53328e172b4cf6f48f09f2f8e9b.png" class="latex" alt="$y \neq 0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="42" height="17" /> by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/5/c/95c21b1ce04c5f3c86f558991d39aa35a13bca21.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\text{rem} (x ,y)=x-y\left \lfloor \frac{x}{y} \right \rfloor\]" width="170" height="43" />where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/3/b/93bb07b9858e6e26bc681f68395ff57e77363228.png" class="latex" alt="$\left \lfloor \tfrac{x}{y} \right \rfloor$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="26" height="32" /> denotes the greatest integer less than or equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/f/b/8fb4021a1e2f13045a5aef3df30db82490c8cc93.png" class="latex" alt="$\tfrac{x}{y}$" style="vertical-align: -8px" width="10" height="20" />. What is the value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/3/0f3dd332d0ae60d38aa4c3cefa4d52c7fc1c9135.png" class="latex" alt="$\text{rem} (\tfrac{3}{8}, -\tfrac{2}{5} )$" style="vertical-align: -6px" width="83" height="21" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/d/c3d0489296c9d022335d939e9236f5ee736bda92.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } -\frac{3}{8} \qquad \textbf{(B) } -\frac{1}{40} \qquad \textbf{(C) } 0 \qquad \textbf{(D) } \frac{3}{8} \qquad \textbf{(E) } \frac{31}{40}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="430" height="38" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>The value, by definition, is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/9/5/4952d3a491d52ed182ee666da9d7f3e062a95c68.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} \text{rem}\left(\frac{3}{8},-\frac{2}{5}\right) &amp;= \frac{3}{8}-\left(-\frac{2}{5}\right)\left\lfloor\frac{\frac{3}{8}}{-\frac{2}{5}}\right\rfloor \\ &amp;= \frac{3}{8}-\left(-\frac{2}{5}\right)\left\lfloor\frac{3}{8}\times\frac{-5}{2}\right\rfloor \\ &amp;= \frac{3}{8}-\left(-\frac{2}{5}\right)\left\lfloor\frac{-15}{16}\right\rfloor\\ &amp;= \frac{3}{8}-\left(-\frac{2}{5}\right)\left(-1\right)\\ &amp;= \frac{3}{8}-\frac{2}{5}\\ &amp;= \boxed{\textbf{(B) } -\frac{1}{40}}. \end{align*}" width="300" height="292" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Do note that the denominator of the answer will be a multiple of 5 and 8 (40) and that the answer will also be negative. The only answer choice that satisfies this is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/2/e/f2e33ab9a00e021af49e888212645d4667903c49.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{B}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="26" height="24" />
</p>
