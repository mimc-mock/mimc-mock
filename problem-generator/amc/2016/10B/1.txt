<p>What is the value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/d/6/7d674023a1aeeb093498a81cce51a90a0abf07db.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2a^{-1}+\frac{a^{-1}}{2}}{a}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="87" height="44" /> when <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/a/9/8a9d49204d0db99d687e764f3caa877aca867db5.png" class="latex" alt="$a= \tfrac{1}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -6px" width="43" height="21" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/4/034b1747cb6012c30926ce531a4431e2ae586fca.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 1\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 2\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ \frac{5}{2}\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 10\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 20$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="370" height="37" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Factorizing the numerator, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/8/7f807516bf1e77ef0503c8dd0b1ac46364ea2597.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{\frac{1}{a}\cdot(2+\frac{1}{2})}{a}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="82" height="40" /> then becomes <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/d/8/6d81eadc4f20c12f7b646ec3be41ad5938621aac.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{\frac{5}{2}}{a^{2}}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="19" height="40" /> which is equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/a/9/2a90f03c5595047822a09ac0ca8083e939558d42.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{5}{2}\cdot 2^2$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="41" height="37" /> which is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/c/2/3c2b4e1927beecd6f76ddf7eabf239a21f2eb38a.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D) }10}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="65" height="28" />.
</p><p><br />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Substituting <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/9/2f960094315d60883495f9b74148e17487ee9584.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="11" height="37" /> for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/8/7f807516bf1e77ef0503c8dd0b1ac46364ea2597.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{\frac{1}{a}\cdot(2+\frac{1}{2})}{a}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="82" height="40" /> gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/c/2/3c2b4e1927beecd6f76ddf7eabf239a21f2eb38a.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D) }10}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="65" height="28" />. ~peelybonehead
</p>
