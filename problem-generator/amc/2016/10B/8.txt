<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Notice that, for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/9/a/39abfe232e2ed5247aa4028089e5f651090e66da.png" class="latex" alt="$n\ge 2$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="43" height="14" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/8/d/08d5cbd326de28154006f0382373b7d5d3a3e8de.png" class="latex" alt="$2015^n\equiv 15^n$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="95" height="13" /> is congruent to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/b/8/fb8f7e86a67710596d7456d77284dab79cdffe10.png" class="latex" alt="$25\pmod{100}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="106" height="18" /> when <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> is even and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/3/1/231ca6446f84b84dbbc19f7c286788763e2f268a.png" class="latex" alt="$75\pmod{100}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="106" height="18" /> when <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> is odd. (Check for yourself).  Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/b/7/4b781fdf1cd172b567be38e2ffa6f20f0b8390c0.png" class="latex" alt="$2016$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" /> is even, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/5/1755b00aa4945ab7bd2b75310246956916908f5e.png" class="latex" alt="$2015^{2016} \equiv 25\pmod{100}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="194" height="19" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/9/cb9e62b30aa6bf257e35dadd52184a892703495b.png" class="latex" alt="$2015^{2016}-2017 \equiv 25 - 17 \equiv \underline{0}8\pmod{100}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="336" height="19" />.
</p><p>So the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/5/b85b17968b605cf6be4293b97638f79dd4006c30.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ \boxed{0}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="55" height="23" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>In a very similar fashion, we find that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/d/5/dd53ce1e2e7a16714f88d19a4fd2d4f921af11bf.png" class="latex" alt="$2015^{2016} \equiv 15^{2016} \pmod{100}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="222" height="19" />, which equals <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/6/876f1b262c58e2e253b0a68dd1231915c0f488bf.png" class="latex" alt="$225^{1008}$" width="52" height="15" />. Next, since every power (greater than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" />) of every number ending in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/6/6/66644fd9f411299c3c1eabf151b5f601c9ef6c21.png" class="latex" alt="$25$" width="17" height="12" /> will end in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/6/6/66644fd9f411299c3c1eabf151b5f601c9ef6c21.png" class="latex" alt="$25$" width="17" height="12" /> (which can easily be verified), we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/1/611726a945b6474515162cba97ee449c4333688a.png" class="latex" alt="$225^{1008} \equiv 25 \pmod{100}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="185" height="19" />. (In this way, we don't have to worry about the exponent very much.) Finally, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/9/e/89e9a5bf10cb4e377a400a3ab5743389ad548ee8.png" class="latex" alt="$2017 \equiv 17 \pmod{100}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="167" height="18" />, and thus <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/8/ce8c31442f2f4177622f14f12bbafb0024382d63.png" class="latex" alt="$2015^{2016}-2017 \equiv 25-17 \equiv 08 \pmod{100}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="336" height="19" />, as above.
</p>
