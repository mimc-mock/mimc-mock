<p>How many four-digit integers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/1/b7189fc05f58344dc86c4790cf960d504196f533.png" class="latex" alt="$abcd$" width="35" height="12" />, with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/a/67a8181c9d9b8253bd721b620aaabc5b01f4af96.png" class="latex" alt="$a \neq 0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="42" height="17" />, have the property that the three two-digit integers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/7/3/a731f52581dc7c4509940547711ec4f9166792b1.png" class="latex" alt="$ab&lt;bc&lt;cd$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="99" height="13" /> form an increasing arithmetic sequence? One such number is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/9/43989ea2c3eed6e6affca85c3720e354f1cce6cc.png" class="latex" alt="$4692$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/f/c7ffa3b8792f0170bce1ab6008e907343100d70e.png" class="latex" alt="$a=4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="43" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/2/282cf257220cfc0de7647945572e6529c1d4774f.png" class="latex" alt="$b=6$" width="40" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/b/c/2bcf069d8a6c65338cedd8d4d727c3dbbc81842b.png" class="latex" alt="$c=9$" width="40" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/8/8/588e07a0cfc1e617ac5d6552a759b3011aa96d6c.png" class="latex" alt="$d=2$" width="42" height="12" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/e/a3e7fb7684f150e91563f0e34d53c99b54e09e10.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 9\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 15\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 16\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 17\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 20$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="385" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>The numbers are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/c/b6c71f0685da9a6786c221d676b6726ef6209398.png" class="latex" alt="$10a+b, 10b+c,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="126" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/0/3/703f9e8f09c687fe7860e616422ef8f63eb4dbe3.png" class="latex" alt="$10c+d$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="58" height="14" />. Note that only <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> can be zero, the numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/f/8/ef870ce9fefe09b80a97b05a7b9198414c1b35f7.png" class="latex" alt="$ab$" width="17" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/2/fe207dd510fa3e2012d9747d93de6f9cc71bf3f6.png" class="latex" alt="$bc$" width="16" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/6/e/f6e1a0789128066a6b03855158dafabb8d84f63b.png" class="latex" alt="$cd$" width="17" height="12" /> cannot start with a zero, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/7/e57c4f96dc49aa339e443ef0b7d16f9ce178cf84.png" class="latex" alt="$a\le b\le c$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="74" height="15" />.
</p><p>To form the sequence, we need <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/8/f/98f7887b7c3f9da33726cc59714d39f2180406db.png" class="latex" alt="$(10c+d)-(10b+c)=(10b+c)-(10a+b)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="351" height="18" />. This can be rearranged as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/9/9f9358d94844e68420df4f2195d172a64b2922c8.png" class="latex" alt="$10(c-2b+a)=2c-b-d$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="214" height="18" />. Notice that since the left-hand side is a multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />, the right-hand side can only be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />. (A value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/7/7/d7701e9469f26e22c106341791344b28f3196443.png" class="latex" alt="$-10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="31" height="13" /> would contradict <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/7/e57c4f96dc49aa339e443ef0b7d16f9ce178cf84.png" class="latex" alt="$a\le b\le c$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="74" height="15" />.) Therefore we have two cases: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/1/4f198f93c3caa34097c36b3bca2075e179a3cac7.png" class="latex" alt="$a+c-2b=1$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="111" height="14" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/8/10800c22ac2ab364244410f6a13fd4a337ad6089.png" class="latex" alt="$a+c-2b=0$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="111" height="14" />.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Case_1">Case 1</span></h3>
<p>If <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/b/c/2bcf069d8a6c65338cedd8d4d727c3dbbc81842b.png" class="latex" alt="$c=9$" width="40" height="12" />, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/b/67b66723cd2a5fa95929308c581b7d13f1386786.png" class="latex" alt="$b+d=8,\ 2b-a=8$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="168" height="16" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/f/c6f11726ea5f26cf7bd8896817422209ec05bfb7.png" class="latex" alt="$5\le b\le 8$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="74" height="15" />. This gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/7/5/475fe7765c1cacb67454f3545e48a469937d4b6f.png" class="latex" alt="$2593, 4692, 6791, 8890$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="169" height="16" />.
If <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/a/3/fa39557a4d886b45466417ae28c5b0a3a92ba7ef.png" class="latex" alt="$c=8$" width="40" height="12" />, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/c/4/2c48597181d22d8924d5d1e0343fd09ec70038c8.png" class="latex" alt="$b+d=6,\ 2b-a=7$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="168" height="16" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/2/9/929e94c19420b06c320f3a07074630f8054b61a1.png" class="latex" alt="$4\le b\le 6$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="74" height="15" />. This gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/0/e/d0eb875db2d3c9543e149bc8a9b36eeccdff3d18.png" class="latex" alt="$1482, 3581, 5680$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="124" height="16" />.
If <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/f/4/df4c636bd4eadfbf8bf1447703d58c79214f3197.png" class="latex" alt="$c=7$" width="41" height="12" />, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/2/ce2f94321a79ac899b156f38178d299685dda92f.png" class="latex" alt="$b+d=4,\ 2b-a=6$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="168" height="16" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/d/a/fda5328bc4703ff88e62aa83ea58ae4e8361f5ee.png" class="latex" alt="$b=4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="41" height="13" />, giving <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/7/a/47ac6ce76de4f21345e05303d9d6490ece355112.png" class="latex" alt="$2470$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />.
There is no solution for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/e/b/4eb76404ee79952dcc555464b061509299789b09.png" class="latex" alt="$c=6$" width="40" height="12" />.
Added together, this gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> answers for Case 1.
</p><p><br />
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Case_2">Case 2</span></h3>
<p>This means that the digits themselves are in an arithmetic sequence. This gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> answers,
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/c/a/2ca730cb2ade6589e53aef8775e2f5ddbc48079e.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[1234, 1357, 2345, 2468, 3456, 3579, 4567, 5678, 6789.\]" width="393" height="16" />
Adding the two cases together, we find the answer to be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/b/5/ab5082c6e77e5a1067e6335e16b9010c54f73a96.png" class="latex" alt="$8+9=$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="59" height="14" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/3/7/e376c5ad07084b492f5806216673136956efe845.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D) }17}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="65" height="28" />.
