<p>The fraction
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26e933ebb23b926f939dea3997c79761c2d177d5.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{\left(3^{2008}\right)^2-\left(3^{2006}\right)^2}{\left(3^{2007}\right)^2-\left(3^{2005}\right)^2}\]" width="138" height="49" />
simplifies to which of the following?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/8/0/1804a1bd4aa0eb5c3b5a526c69fd5753cbc4fe89.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ 1\qquad\mathrm{(B)}\ \frac{9}{4}\qquad\mathrm{(C)}\ 3\qquad\mathrm{(D)}\ \frac{9}{2}\qquad\mathrm{(E)}\ 9$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="350" height="38" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Simplifying, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/1/cb1decaca126604949ba6ae3013d1d5716cbe164.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{3^{4016}-3^{4012}}{3^{4014}-3^{4010}}.\]" width="100" height="39" /> Factoring out <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/7/9a7cf7837c9032522cc3d1c15ce511847f1fbecd.png" class="latex" alt="$3^{4012}$" width="34" height="15" /> in the numerator and factoring out <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f370e928962d718502a87e790a42dec329e380a8.png" class="latex" alt="$3^{4010}$" width="34" height="15" /> in the denominator gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/5/d/45ddc8ab77e26848dcfe1758bdc31dbcead4dcb8.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{(3^4-1)(3^{4012})}{(3^4-1)(3^{4010})}.\]" width="118" height="43" /> Canceling out <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/9/5/d95537d28c4dead41332d14e7278cbaff593f0c3.png" class="latex" alt="$3^4-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="47" height="15" /> gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/1/1/311851b3ef939c145355f7389fa838adc570704b.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{3^{4012}}{3^{4010}}=\frac{3^2}{3^0}=9\ \mathrm{(E)}.$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="154" height="39" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Using Difference of Squares, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/8/b182ab05fb846f27f2bd1b21e720d71377c476d1.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{(3^{2008})^{2}-(3^{2006})^{2}}{(3^{2007})^{2}-(3^{2005}){^2}}$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="140" height="43" /> becomes
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/f/f/eff6068a5e7213b24c50c9d40d68a8b276ac57e4.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{(3^{2008}+3^{2006})(3^{2008}-3^{2006})}{(3^{2007}+3^{2005})(3^{2007}-3^{2005})}$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="220" height="43" />
</p><p><br />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/7/83772901e4da126c81fa7dd5d2b7015edd8f2eda.png" class="latex" alt="$= \frac{3^{2006}(9+1) \cdot 3^{2006}(9-1)}{3^{2005}(9+1) \cdot 3^{2005}(9-1)}$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="216" height="43" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/f/a/cfa60692fb48ed86def891a0615e0e33250bec2a.png" class="latex" alt="$= \boxed{\text{(E)}9}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="64" height="28" />
