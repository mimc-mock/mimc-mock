<p>In a collection of red, blue, and green marbles, there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/6/96633b73a86b9d9110e1ff824fce08def96ca296.png" class="latex" alt="$25\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> more red marbles than blue marbles, and there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/d/7/fd7edd3842c40dbc1e54e522917a6e07b43c92e2.png" class="latex" alt="$60\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> more green marbles than red marbles. Suppose that there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> red marbles. What is the total number of marbles in the collection?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/9/ba987aeced743390dba9679e97efe23bbce263f2.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ 2.85r\qquad\mathrm{(B)}\ 3r\qquad\mathrm{(C)}\ 3.4r\qquad\mathrm{(D)}\ 3.85r\qquad\mathrm{(E)}\ 4.25r$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="469" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The number of blue marbles is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/a/87a6fda2ab126ed2ab5603cf6eb8812f5696aaed.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{4}{5}r$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="21" height="37" />, the number of green marbles is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/5/4/1541cf18eb4764bec8e50c4515968d1b383717c8.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{8}{5}r$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="21" height="37" />, and the number of red marbles is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" />.
</p><p>Thus, the total number of marbles is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/2/7/027dca14b2871dd052c50b64a148db9e0b9b9368.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{4}{5}r+\frac{8}{5}r+r=3.4r$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="152" height="37" />, and the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/4/8/2484f88154fadcf9c781bba19becdae88696b77f.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(C)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="25" height="18" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Let the number of red marbles be 100. You have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/b/96ba6d82c22a3599c6ed8db287bcbb8171c8fab3.png" class="latex" alt="$b = 80,$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="57" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/d/19da3d1cc9c70f4a5e84b54f94b592f20e18059b.png" class="latex" alt="$g = 160.$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="68" height="17" /> 
</p><p>The answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/e/8eed414cb543ed3d52d12a35b26a3b45a7d75c9b.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{100+80+160}{100} = \boxed{3.4}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="182" height="38" />
</p>
