What is the units digit of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/c/0/0c01c032b94ec9ecda551db1d9b16f9a9655cd19.png" class="latex" alt="$k^2+2^k$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="55" height="17" />?

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/e/a/4eaa8b031286aa7c4bc545a29d2d6e9c4c9151a5.png" class="latex" alt="$k \equiv 2008^2 + 2^{2008} \equiv 8^2 + 2^4 \equiv 4+6 \equiv 0 \pmod{10}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="19" />. 
</p><p>So, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/1/0/f106ad36161ef3f49d444446a5487f12196bf547.png" class="latex" alt="$k^2 \equiv 0 \pmod{10}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="130" height="19" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/3/e13cd5724b8e4f9377fbcfb48191520cff017fb3.png" class="latex" alt="$k = 2008^2+2^{2008}$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="135" height="16" />   is a multiple of four and the units digit of powers of two repeat in cycles of four, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/1/8/f18032f679bbb6ac0c030edc4d536eaf8ab7bf08.png" class="latex" alt="$2^k \equiv 2^4 \equiv 6 \pmod{10}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="171" height="20" />. 
</p><p>Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/3/c/23ce952ee4ccce6bbee52f48b700515f0de4563c.png" class="latex" alt="$k^2+2^k \equiv 0+6 \equiv 6 \pmod{10}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="234" height="20" />. So the units digit is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/c/f/0cf377a856d1acf9a2910459cff3c3107cb9b2d3.png" class="latex" alt="$6 \Rightarrow \boxed{D}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="64" height="24" />.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Note">Note</span></h3>
<p>Another way to get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/f/91f809bbb021ebb0274fe226dbe88c63cd92c9ce.png" class="latex" alt="$k \equiv 0 \pmod{10}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="123" height="18" /> is to find the cycles of the last digit.
</p><p>For <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/4/e/e4ee918bf442fcbcdcae0eec2ae37e1b522b8957.png" class="latex" alt="$2008^2$" width="42" height="15" />, we need only be concerned with the last digit <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> since the other digits do not affect the last digit. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/3/b/e3b4e0e67cb52270a78ce9780704dbdcee457826.png" class="latex" alt="$8^{2} = 64$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="59" height="15" />, the last digit of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/4/e/e4ee918bf442fcbcdcae0eec2ae37e1b522b8957.png" class="latex" alt="$2008^2$" width="42" height="15" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />.
</p><p>For <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/1/a/31ac618038692e63ea0dff0f02a9290074cacee0.png" class="latex" alt="$2^{2008}$" width="34" height="15" />, note that the last digit cycles through the pattern <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/8/f/98fde074632956d7f8f5a08488198438f55098d8.png" class="latex" alt="${2, 4, 8, 6}$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="60" height="16" />. (You can try to see this by calculating the first powers of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />.)
</p><p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/b/c/abc1744a459ad135efd3fd45fca7285cae133717.png" class="latex" alt="$2008$" width="35" height="12" /> is a multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />, the last digit of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/1/a/31ac618038692e63ea0dff0f02a9290074cacee0.png" class="latex" alt="$2^{2008}$" width="34" height="15" /> is evidently <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/e/1de3ed5fb7fa9b1fa73395d65c43e5bc81cd9d61.png" class="latex" alt="$6.$" width="12" height="12" />
