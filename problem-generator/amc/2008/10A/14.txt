That is, the ratio of the width to the height is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/9/a/a9a03b059b84a40291c4c20ea6a12c7127bff4c3.png" class="latex" alt="$4: 3$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="32" height="13" />. The aspect ratio of many movies is not <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/9/a/a9a03b059b84a40291c4c20ea6a12c7127bff4c3.png" class="latex" alt="$4: 3$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="32" height="13" />, so they are sometimes shown on a television screen by "letterboxing" - darkening strips of equal height at the top and bottom of the screen, as shown.
