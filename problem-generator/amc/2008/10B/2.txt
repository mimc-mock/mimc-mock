<p>A <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/4/674dc6717f1938acff63480bd24b1270a30439ff.png" class="latex" alt="$4\times 4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="40" height="12" /> block of calendar dates is shown. First, the order of the numbers in the second and the fourth rows are reversed. Then, the numbers on each diagonal are added. What will be the positive difference between the two diagonal sums?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/9/b/b9b73fd2d51a903cdaf987fca13b101dde70d13c.png" class="latex" alt="$\begin{tabular}[t]{|c|c|c|c|} \multicolumn{4}{c}{}\\\hline 1&amp;2&amp;3&amp;4\\\hline 8&amp;9&amp;10&amp;11\\\hline 15&amp;16&amp;17&amp;18\\\hline 22&amp;23&amp;24&amp;25\\\hline \end{tabular}$" style="vertical-align: -89px" width="147" height="89" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/7/cb709aee0a0c76b0b7c94a6325bc9fe47183589f.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 2 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 4 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 6 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 8 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 10$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="357" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>After reversing the numbers on the second and fourth rows, the block will look like this: 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/a/3/fa3f4afefd9e2d1f2e7e867c0c2df60feae30bef.png" class="latex" alt="$\begin{tabular}[t]{|c|c|c|c|} \multicolumn{4}{c}{}\\\hline 1&amp;2&amp;3&amp;4\\\hline 11&amp;10&amp;9&amp;8\\\hline 15&amp;16&amp;17&amp;18\\\hline 25&amp;24&amp;23&amp;22\\\hline \end{tabular}$" style="vertical-align: -89px" width="147" height="89" />
</p><p>The positive difference between the two diagonal sums is then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/5/e056dbf7aff9822824c2d98ebff4f813e0f9337b.png" class="latex" alt="$(4+9+16+25)-(1+10+17+22)=3-1-1+3=\boxed{\textbf{(B) }}4$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="511" height="30" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>You can also see that the positive difference between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> a d the positive difference between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/1/1d111a5e00ee5ea6700bb629994dc629874c505b.png" class="latex" alt="$22$" width="17" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/6/6/66644fd9f411299c3c1eabf151b5f601c9ef6c21.png" class="latex" alt="$25$" width="17" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />. Adding gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/f/b/3fb79703b09bc33b6c018d1a76ba8cb8306b135c.png" class="latex" alt="$1+3=\boxed{\textbf{(B) }}4$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="122" height="30" />
</p>
