<p>A circle has a radius of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/2/b/f2b34d3cc8d92f18962c19e3ed3b93c265ea9db1.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{10}{(a^2)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="70" height="19" /> and a circumference of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/6/b/76b6b0e5c76430c7f97c07f631e6ad3bcb1c4ca6.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{10}{(b^4)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="68" height="19" />. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/0/c/00ca1d0113fd12d4b87405c9483f5c5fcd8d8184.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{a}{b}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="42" height="17" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/9/f/d9f239540e69528094eb2885577ebfd6d602e436.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ \frac{1}{4\pi} \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ \frac{1}{\pi} \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ \pi \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 2\pi \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 10^{2\pi}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="406" height="37" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/3/c3355896da590fc491a10150a50416687626d7cc.png" class="latex" alt="$C$" width="14" height="12" /> be the circumference of the circle, and let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> be the radius of the circle. 
</p><p>Using log properties, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/4/6/1469d2b32097eaa9edae11537c1edfb1b7cee1af.png" class="latex" alt="$C=\log_{10}{(b^4)}=4\log_{10}{(b)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="206" height="19" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/6/8e69a95abc3206b377156285907990351e4f07f8.png" class="latex" alt="$r=\log_{10}{(a^2)}=2\log_{10}{(a)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="203" height="19" />. 
</p><p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/a/07ad7caf69ba14640d888bda20e93c71474794ff.png" class="latex" alt="$C=2\pi r$" width="66" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/e/60e2cb262251b46de26399796c51bf00920de879.png" class="latex" alt="$4\log_{10}{(b)}=2\pi\cdot2\log_{10}{(a)} \Rightarrow \log_{a}{b} = \frac{\log_{10}{(b)}}{\log_{10}{(a)}}=\pi \Rightarrow C$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="449" height="43" />. 
</p>
