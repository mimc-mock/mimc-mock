<p>Sangho uploaded a video to a website where viewers can vote that they like or dislike a video. Each video begins with a score of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" />, and the score increases by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> for each like vote and decreases by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> for each dislike vote. At one point Sangho saw that his video had a score of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/c/bfcfee7bd8a5e15e5f6b7881c138de43651572a9.png" class="latex" alt="$90$" width="17" height="12" />, and that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/1/ff169c8eb4e8e515216bcd0f6f207a78af94c8b4.png" class="latex" alt="$65\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the votes cast on his video were like votes. How many votes had been cast on Sangho's video at that point?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/a/7cab29b78b1532e029b496cddcf0f2d74cdb54b8.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }   200   \qquad        \textbf{(B) }   300   \qquad    \textbf{(C) }   400   \qquad   \textbf{(D) }  500  \qquad  \textbf{(E) }   600$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="443" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>If <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/1/ff169c8eb4e8e515216bcd0f6f207a78af94c8b4.png" class="latex" alt="$65\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the votes were likes, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/f/5/af59f5a81354c1925a58cb897e1d142111b1d7c6.png" class="latex" alt="$35\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the votes were dislikes. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/d/10d179d73f652801e77f6343dab4bb9a645d8f27.png" class="latex" alt="$65\%-35\%=30\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="145" height="14" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/c/bfcfee7bd8a5e15e5f6b7881c138de43651572a9.png" class="latex" alt="$90$" width="17" height="12" /> votes is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/e/32e5939a7bdd847ec86582225cd653c240b4abce.png" class="latex" alt="$30\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the total number of votes. Doing quick arithmetic shows that the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/4/b/a4b50a29387f6f67a5ce1100e1be7c0a04d7e178.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B) } 300}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="73" height="28" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Let's consider that Sangho has received 100 votes. This means he has received 65 upvotes and 35 downvotes. Part of these upvotes and downvotes cancel out, so Sangho is now left with a total of 30 upvotes, or a score increase of 30. In order for his score to be 90, Sangho must receive three sets of 100 votes. Therefore, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/4/b/a4b50a29387f6f67a5ce1100e1be7c0a04d7e178.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B) } 300}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="73" height="28" />.
</p><p>-tryanotherangle
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> be the amount of votes cast, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/f/5/af59f5a81354c1925a58cb897e1d142111b1d7c6.png" class="latex" alt="$35\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> would be dislikes and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/1/ff169c8eb4e8e515216bcd0f6f207a78af94c8b4.png" class="latex" alt="$65\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> would be likes. Since a like earns the video 1 point and a dislike takes 1 point away from the video, the amount of points Sangho's video will have in terms of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/d/10d179d73f652801e77f6343dab4bb9a645d8f27.png" class="latex" alt="$65\%-35\%=30\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="145" height="14" /> of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/f/0/5f0cf2a41ca6fbaafe3d21b823a9bed30c7272fa.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{3}{10}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="20" height="38" /> of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />. Because Songho's video had a score of 90 points, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/f/6/af6a3357a6fcb3df8b9ba82cd6c26aeb60416aca.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{3x}{10} = 90$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="65" height="38" />. After we solve for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/c/4fc94a7e9cac05c83555bebfc594bb0f4aa2d3a6.png" class="latex" alt="$x = \boxed{\textbf{(B) } 300}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="108" height="28" />.
</p><p>-Mathandski
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_4">Solution 4</span></h2>
<p>Let x be the amount of like votes and y be the amount of dislike votes. Then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/1/5115e5b79fe9f20df789a20937246fb2b09c7591.png" class="latex" alt="$x-y=90$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="84" height="16" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/1/ff169c8eb4e8e515216bcd0f6f207a78af94c8b4.png" class="latex" alt="$65\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of the total votes are like votes,
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/b/3/0b3f1ccc9f4f9fc7b07dd84b0446d468d727f7f2.png" class="latex" alt="$0.65(x+y)=x$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="123" height="18" />. then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/b/01bfb1887409cda77efd69f0a7a6577471eae4c4.png" class="latex" alt="$.65y = .35x$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="90" height="16" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/e/09e25c90ed467955864d2d78846b45b791ec3f42.png" class="latex" alt="$13/7$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="36" height="18" />y = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />. Plugging that in to the original equation, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/f/3/8f3b882e2475cd6d091495b5aff4d6323bde6ee8.png" class="latex" alt="$6/7 y$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="36" height="18" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/c/bfcfee7bd8a5e15e5f6b7881c138de43651572a9.png" class="latex" alt="$90$" width="17" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/0/d/a0d494fa044170deeb85c95888b599c5b499db6c.png" class="latex" alt="$y =105$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="60" height="16" />
Then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/d/b/bdb923b95a19d4fc80f01faa9155bd7425133d7a.png" class="latex" alt="$x=195$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="61" height="13" /> and the total number of votes is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/2/a/02a55e28a411183bce3b6b95c9baf8c9e7938194.png" class="latex" alt="$105+195 = 300$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="127" height="14" />. The answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/4/b/a4b50a29387f6f67a5ce1100e1be7c0a04d7e178.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B) } 300}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="73" height="28" />.
</p><p>-goldenn
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_5">Solution 5</span></h2>
<p>Obviously, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/2/b/52bfe2af19719b9ad8686891cb2a1abc8345bdd1.png" class="latex" alt="$90/(65\%-35\%) = 90/30\% = 300$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="268" height="20" />. The answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/4/b/a4b50a29387f6f67a5ce1100e1be7c0a04d7e178.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B) } 300}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="73" height="28" />.
</p>
