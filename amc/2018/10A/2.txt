<p>Liliane has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/7/9f7f4de02215a8c5319886c13b2e291811346e1c.png" class="latex" alt="$50\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> more soda than Jacqueline, and Alice has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/6/96633b73a86b9d9110e1ff824fce08def96ca296.png" class="latex" alt="$25\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> more soda than Jacqueline. What is the relationship between the amounts of soda that Liliane and Alice have?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/8/7/e8738bbc91069d2db6a97b6ab9b1e13052ac4606.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="28" height="17" /> Liliane has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/8/1183a12017e48198b3352789cc39b223b17945d6.png" class="latex" alt="$20\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> more soda than Alice.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/0/6/506e909041d36bd739c8c959d341fcfbba3e9ad7.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(B) }$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="27" height="17" /> Liliane has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/6/96633b73a86b9d9110e1ff824fce08def96ca296.png" class="latex" alt="$25\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> more soda than Alice.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/a/b/0ab3a9c13a5c376b1660f46d00b9007dbae240cb.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(C) }$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="27" height="17" /> Liliane has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/0/8/00890d0beefe80ec2a7d63ca91aaa8b8056ba355.png" class="latex" alt="$45\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> more soda than Alice.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/7/8/3787f1b7ce783a57c3a0d02ca4d485ccc950ff0a.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(D) }$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="28" height="17" /> Liliane has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/2/e126f0d41c904d37ba2c332347a24b75d4ac3066.png" class="latex" alt="$75\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> more soda than Alice.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/d/81d05263c2e18459105296c8e3e06c9fddddb735.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(E) }$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="26" height="17" /> Liliane has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/1/b/71bd818354ff89f378b1c6b3d4df72f3c8c508b3.png" class="latex" alt="$100\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="41" height="14" /> more soda than Alice.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let's assume that Jacqueline has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> gallon(s) of soda. Then Alice has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/d/b7d3560fba12b97d35543b1e1028c14f5f1545b2.png" class="latex" alt="$1.25$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="31" height="13" /> gallons and Liliane has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/5/7/15788e198a53ced87268aea78fcde68a84de92c0.png" class="latex" alt="$1.5$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="22" height="13" /> gallons. Doing division, we find out that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/1/811dd7bc449eec57f31f8abab00b3d7b760234f8.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1.5}{1.25}=1.2$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="83" height="38" />, which means that Liliane has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/8/1183a12017e48198b3352789cc39b223b17945d6.png" class="latex" alt="$20\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> more soda. Therefore, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/e/35e8ea2d16518197b5113002b8c0c7da6c311a3e.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(A)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="41" height="28" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>WLOG, lets use <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> gallons instead of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />. When you work it out, you get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> gallons and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> gallons. We have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/2/1/c21a49745a84d560c817999141e2335dfa0cf9d0.png" class="latex" alt="$6 - 5 = 1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="72" height="13" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/8/1183a12017e48198b3352789cc39b223b17945d6.png" class="latex" alt="$20\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />. Thus, we reach <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/e/35e8ea2d16518197b5113002b8c0c7da6c311a3e.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(A)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="41" height="28" />.
</p><p>~Ezraft
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>If Jacqueline has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> gallons of soda, Alice has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/9/659ea026f238eb026d79c0605eff5b2a20c01900.png" class="latex" alt="$1.25x$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="42" height="13" /> gallons, and Liliane has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/0/4/70426e82add219c57d25b20bc2966f9f1588a222.png" class="latex" alt="$1.5x$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="33" height="13" /> gallons. Thus, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/1/811dd7bc449eec57f31f8abab00b3d7b760234f8.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1.5}{1.25}=1.2$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="83" height="38" /> -&gt; Liliane has <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/8/1183a12017e48198b3352789cc39b223b17945d6.png" class="latex" alt="$20\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> more soda. Our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/e/35e8ea2d16518197b5113002b8c0c7da6c311a3e.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(A)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="41" height="28" />.
</p><p>~lakecomo224
</p>
