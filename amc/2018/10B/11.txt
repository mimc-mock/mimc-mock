<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Because squares of a non-multiple of 3 is always <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/c/91c18a864fecc0092e5be9451ffbc9e9e3bd5d73.png" class="latex" alt="$1 \pmod{3}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="79" height="18" />, the only expression is always a multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/1/5d1c51ad580e5291c95b179641c9c6a4f140599e.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) } p^2+26}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="103" height="31" />. This is excluding when <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/4/5b4441e03cdf4b443e6d7476d1d681ae5c789f37.png" class="latex" alt="$p\equiv 0 \pmod{3}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="114" height="18" />, which only occurs when <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/c/54cfc82a82f113a7432ca68f71338c383bbcb44b.png" class="latex" alt="$p=3$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="43" height="16" />, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/0/6/80661505e712c57c5b7e1888fd01a978f4ac4733.png" class="latex" alt="$p^2+26=35$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="99" height="18" /> which is still composite.
</p>
