<p>The sum of three numbers is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/b/3/fb35ebe06c2c3b3e1ca9b1a7bb7717bc18d0b6fe.png" class="latex" alt="$96.$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="25" height="14" /> The first number is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> times the third number, and the third number is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/8/7/78792fe4680a164f537ea72a4f900e4471433c3b.png" class="latex" alt="$40$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> less than the second number. What is the absolute value of the difference between the first and second numbers?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/7/5/7752ab84e23c215afdc175282988b652ec621f8c.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } 1 \qquad \textbf{(B) } 2 \qquad \textbf{(C) } 3 \qquad \textbf{(D) } 4 \qquad \textbf{(E) } 5$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="353" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> be the third number. It follows that the first number is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/4/1/941cfe0c3317f1a321702f71a52c77f8fce22128.png" class="latex" alt="$6x,$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="26" height="17" /> and the second number is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/3/d430ab9f0bce27387c17c89456fc0c5c739cbd36.png" class="latex" alt="$x+40.$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="58" height="15" />
</p><p>We have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/8/2/a82848a0e0a81667b270afbd8560aba712f745c6.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[6x+(x+40)+x=8x+40=96,\]" width="269" height="18" /> from which <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/7/8/978156fbed1de4b6b0969d83a54656f307aedd7c.png" class="latex" alt="$x=7.$" width="47" height="12" />
</p><p>Therefore, the first number is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/c/3/cc3565157ec53e03f90f3d221f05b373850aafea.png" class="latex" alt="$42,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="22" height="15" /> and the second number is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/d/c8d06fc910703a003655e44dbcd4f3626ee6d02f.png" class="latex" alt="$47.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="21" height="12" /> Their absolute value of the difference is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/b/4/0b46bbe29e6811a5e2be2d2a882e9c116058a0e7.png" class="latex" alt="$|42-47|=\boxed{\textbf{(E) } 5}.$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="154" height="30" />
</p><p>~MRENTHUSIASM
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Solve this using a system of equations. Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/3/b13f21416d84e13708696f34dea81026cda583c9.png" class="latex" alt="$z$" width="8" height="8" /> be the three numbers, respectively. We get three equations:
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/b/1/7b1a37d92f3fed67554c9131cb214b74cca94762.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[x+y+z=96\]" width="115" height="15" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/0/1/b01547616152adc55ed1fa525c67c11f50e5c7c4.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[x=6z\]" width="51" height="12" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/c/3/0c38de59f105c823063b23e7f7bff60f0d250b91.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[z=y-40\]" width="82" height="15" />
Rewriting the third equation gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/8/d58f96a5b33bee288e0dd3c14008383b39b17600.png" class="latex" alt="$y=z+40$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="85" height="17" />, so we can substitute <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/0/8/708f8260f746cc398394cf9bbe649678fee5524e.png" class="latex" alt="$6z$" width="17" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" /> as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/4/6540118109b1ec7915e135b20a24a396a63b0f82.png" class="latex" alt="$z+40$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="51" height="15" />.
</p><p>Therefore, we get
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/8/e58ab559d1a251776e8b7f56a5ce02bd77595515.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[6z+(z+40)+z=96\]" width="177" height="18" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/6/6e63292dc055589dee5f08bb6834f3583a56b460.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[8z+40=96\]" width="100" height="13" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/2/b72684856a44f3af1e4477b01dda45f3ade44ea1.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[8z=56\]" width="59" height="13" />
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/7/f87bae25a67f81988ba1c9c7c92db6951fdef056.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[z=7\]" width="42" height="13" />
</p><p>Substituting 7 in for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/3/b13f21416d84e13708696f34dea81026cda583c9.png" class="latex" alt="$z$" width="8" height="8" /> gives us 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/7/7a7d6e8f5d00e35a078a72e7c223f7713ec52fe8.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[x=6z=6(7)=42\]" width="150" height="18" /> and 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/4/1/e410843a538ae1e8b348bedaf018725f0b2078e8.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[y=z+40=7+40=47\]" width="199" height="16" />
</p><p>So <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/9/4/594a73a07a746814d383063a55ed325d6acd639a.png" class="latex" alt="$|x-y|=|42-47|=\boxed{\textbf{(E) } 5}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="225" height="30" />
</p><p>~alexdapog A-A
</p>
