<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>First, notice that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/7/ce7c4e34f998676159910a3b2620c5bbf62cca7c.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[x_{2019} + 2x_{2020} + 4x_{2021} + 8x_{2022} = \frac{S_{2023} - S_{2019}}{2^{2019}}.\]" width="375" height="37" />

</p><p>Starting with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/9/f49e1f42131f302f2b4a45393cdc3fed5be2655c.png" class="latex" alt="$2019$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />,

<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/6/c/66cee7c2441da6f8e97e2a293a8ec9ff7077fc0b.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[0 \equiv 2^{2019}k + 1 \pmod{7}.\]" width="196" height="20" />

Since the maximum possible number that can be generated with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> bits is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/0/f4094b9af8c3306c0b04e796ad875cca555e74c7.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\underbrace{{11111\dotsc1}_2}_{n} = \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} 2^k = 2^n - 1 \leqslant 2^n.\]" width="279" height="55" />
Looking at our calculations for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/a/baaf0d4e84c0cb4e835e382d8f1ac4f0cd48793c.png" class="latex" alt="$S_{2019}$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="36" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/7/d47e4582b2590016478a048ccd61020454107469.png" class="latex" alt="$S_{2023}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="40" height="17" />, we see that the only valid integers that satisfy that constraint are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/2/c9226e412677907df8df4fc3be892f00a8e90321.png" class="latex" alt="$j = h = 0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="78" height="18" />.

</p><p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" /> are integers less than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/a/e0a0db32027a732ac57d37ef2ae9bb150f65b108.png" class="latex" alt="$7$" width="9" height="12" />, the only values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> respectively. 
</p><p>The requested sum is 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/2/a/92a99f60e000f85889ecaae96936e67c91f1bad6.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} \frac{S_{2023}-S_{2019}}{2^{2019}} &amp;= \frac{\frac{2^{2023}\cdot{x} + 1}{7} - \frac{2^{2019}\cdot{y} + 1}{7}}{2^{2019}} \\ &amp;= \frac{1}{2^{2019}}\left(\frac{2^{2023}\cdot{3} + 1}{7} -\left(\frac{2^{2019}\cdot{6} + 1}{7}  \right)\right) \\ &amp;= \frac{3\cdot{2^4}-6}{7} \\ &amp;= \boxed{\textbf{(A) } 6}. \end{align*}" width="427" height="174" />
-Benedict T (countmath1)
</p>
