<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" /> are relatively prime, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/a/1da3a71a29e6ce9aecf0304c375be0579ed13f55.png" class="latex" alt="$a^3-b^3$" width="53" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/f/a/8face52bbe7a4be1112b000c09130ca848a04c4a.png" class="latex" alt="$(a-b)^3$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="60" height="19" /> are both integers as well. Then, for the given fraction to simplify to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/0/e/a0e2cb352eb13aa7e751c83d1904a6aa3ecc80bd.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{73}{3}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="20" height="37" />, the denominator <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/f/a/8face52bbe7a4be1112b000c09130ca848a04c4a.png" class="latex" alt="$(a-b)^3$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="60" height="19" /> must be a multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/3/0/93032f1ab8b685d84a767a2fde0d9006a464b5e4.png" class="latex" alt="$3.$" width="12" height="12" /> Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/a/6/5a697bfdeadf2105048670e6f6c7d4dc6878dc4f.png" class="latex" alt="$a-b$" width="40" height="12" /> is a multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />. Looking at the answer choices, the only multiple of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/8/c88676177603d4e7134e7819bb31db2c8ca62f28.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 3}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="55" height="28" />.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Using difference of cubes in the numerator and cancelling out one <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/2/4f2aee761704bb62a779d1bbcb9fb09912bb516f.png" class="latex" alt="$(a-b)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="52" height="18" /> in the numerator and denominator gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/8/ee83ea9952aa78ab7f1decb195665d673ec9fcbf.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{a^2 + ab + b^2}{a^2 - 2ab + b^2} = \frac{73}{3}$" style="vertical-align: -14px" width="152" height="40" />.

Then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/5/83591b47b32156c1e01f12168ad51b1ab3e5c056.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{x + y}{x - 2y} = \frac{73}{3}$" style="vertical-align: -16px" width="100" height="41" />. <h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>

Now letting <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/5/f/95fe06c6ce538a7bb67c18fbdeecc307157c964c.png" class="latex" alt="$x = a - b$" width="74" height="12" />, we simplify the expression to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/9/f7983825d265bfeb8f41d4146d85c3375042cdb4.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{70x^2 + 3ab}{x^2} = \frac{73}{3}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="133" height="39" />. Cross multiplying and doing a bit of simplification, we obtain that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/a/f5a187fe938515d719996ae75bc985a269a284e9.png" class="latex" alt="$ab = \frac{70x^2}{9}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="80" height="39" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" /> are both integers, we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/5/2/952d28321e60cbae9c238467fb559bbde0581321.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{70x^2}{9}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="38" height="39" /> has to be an integer. Experimenting with values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, we get that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/9/0/690ef55a607beaf2389d1ee102df817721bc2392.png" class="latex" alt="$x = 3$" width="43" height="12" /> which means <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/7/3/3736bb5f649b92222053e13ccf7ff6b84a99d89c.png" class="latex" alt="$ab = 70$" width="59" height="12" />. We could prime factor from here to figure out possible values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />, but it is quite obvious that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/d/6/7d6cc5bbcaca2ea70d1998da025c3f0873bb8562.png" class="latex" alt="$a = 10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="51" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/f/e/3fe4d6ebef2a09e428f2984fb2d677c342dbe1bf.png" class="latex" alt="$b=7$" width="41" height="12" />, so our desired answer is  <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/8/c88676177603d4e7134e7819bb31db2c8ca62f28.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 3}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="55" height="28" /> ~triggod
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_7">Solution 7</span></h2>
<p>Since the two numbers are integers, and both <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/f/a/8face52bbe7a4be1112b000c09130ca848a04c4a.png" class="latex" alt="$(a-b)^3$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="60" height="19" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/a/1da3a71a29e6ce9aecf0304c375be0579ed13f55.png" class="latex" alt="$a^3-b^3$" width="53" height="15" /> would yield integers, for the denominator to have a factor of 3, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/2/4f2aee761704bb62a779d1bbcb9fb09912bb516f.png" class="latex" alt="$(a-b)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="52" height="18" /> must have a factor of 3. Only choice <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/f/fffc7368e8e7b77c88b0b4bf732c88c60e27b439.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="42" height="30" /> has a factor of 3. ~hh99754539
</p><p><br />
</p>
