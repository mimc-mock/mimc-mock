<p>Cagney can frost a cupcake every 20 seconds and Lacey can frost a cupcake every 30 seconds. Working together, how many cupcakes can they frost in 5 minutes?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/9/8/69884be70b739fb0f43cc9486b0969af0b47a6d1.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 10\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 15\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 20\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 25\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 30$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Cagney can frost one in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/3/f8366cd6196dd8a9da6d38a3e9eafb109e99d53e.png" class="latex" alt="$20$" width="17" height="12" /> seconds, and Lacey can frost one in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/1/651ba418a810d971dbc8a326d49a71fe1541fae0.png" class="latex" alt="$30$" width="17" height="12" /> seconds. Working together, they can frost one in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/b/5/eb5d947c20000e1bb912664c780ff3aa434b48af.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{20\cdot30}{20+30} = \frac{600}{50} = 12$" style="vertical-align: -14px" width="160" height="39" /> seconds. In <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/9/b899aba26cc33f0026125daebb44a60349c999b6.png" class="latex" alt="$300$" width="26" height="12" /> seconds (<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> minutes), they can frost <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/7/1/2719c093613425513312f5848aac60a585775556.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D)}\ 25}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="65" height="28" /> cupcakes.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>In <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/9/b899aba26cc33f0026125daebb44a60349c999b6.png" class="latex" alt="$300$" width="26" height="12" /> seconds (<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> minutes), Cagney will frost <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/6/9/d694ca1024350ea40a8c7be7b3cbd0cf218041df.png" class="latex" alt="$\dfrac{300}{20} = 15$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="73" height="37" /> cupcakes, and Lacey will frost <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/2/9/52931bb9292965c8e578e77fc79d7dfb8e944429.png" class="latex" alt="$\dfrac{300}{30} = 10$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="73" height="37" /> cupcakes. Therefore, working together they will frost <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/0/c805f451513457a9ee11957af83094397f5b6e5d.png" class="latex" alt="$15 + 10 = \boxed{\textbf{(D)}\ 25}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="148" height="28" /> cupcakes.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>Since Cagney frosts <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> cupcakes a minute, and Lacey frosts <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> cupcakes a minute, they together frost <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/f/d/efd511c1545dc62444789b5e1f7ec0c0fec0b442.png" class="latex" alt="$3+2=5$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="73" height="14" /> cupcakes a minute. Therefore, in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> minutes, they frost <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/5/be56bc664dc56b61d27e6f783e01a671e29bc7e3.png" class="latex" alt="$5\times5 = 25 \Rightarrow \boxed{\textbf{(D)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="152" height="28" />
</p>
