<p>Bernardo and Silvia play the following game. An integer between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/0/0705411d92671faf1fe5602cfef8f353c7f0f83d.png" class="latex" alt="$999$" width="26" height="12" /> inclusive is selected and given to Bernardo. Whenever Bernardo receives a number, he doubles it and passes the result to Silvia. Whenever Silvia receives a number, she adds <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/6/906734e2f1010e9a171ffee8050cbc0649cea37c.png" class="latex" alt="$50$" width="17" height="12" /> to it and passes the result to Bernardo. The winner is the last person who produces a number less than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/e/6ee927e1332358c96c62c277441c907c4f51057f.png" class="latex" alt="$1000$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />. Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/9/fc97ef67268cd4e91bacdf12b8901d7036c9a056.png" class="latex" alt="$N$" width="16" height="12" /> be the smallest initial number that results in a win for Bernardo. What is the sum of the digits of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/9/fc97ef67268cd4e91bacdf12b8901d7036c9a056.png" class="latex" alt="$N$" width="16" height="12" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/a/f/3af1025237725763f525a2be317da1aefcf3c13e.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 7\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 8\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 9\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 10\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 11$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="366" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h3>
<p>The last number that Bernado says has to be between 950 and 999. Note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/b/e/fbe4765d87bb99b878cce0677742a35406759c38.png" class="latex" alt="$1\rightarrow 2\rightarrow 52\rightarrow 104\rightarrow 154\rightarrow 308\rightarrow 358\rightarrow 716\rightarrow 766$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="425" height="13" /> contains 4 doubling actions. Thus, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/b/babb6205b8510ee62e0828644ef74781542e22dd.png" class="latex" alt="$x \rightarrow 2x \rightarrow 2x+50 \rightarrow 4x+100 \rightarrow 4x+150 \rightarrow 8x+300 \rightarrow 8x+350 \rightarrow 16x+700$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="641" height="14" />. 
</p><p>Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/f/a/dfa4dcd070dd4eca80d44f4747aa6a186b4a088f.png" class="latex" alt="$950&lt;16x+700&lt;1000$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="189" height="14" />. Then, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/d/b/0db19ae496b91d020d585a5f306721fbd24a09c2.png" class="latex" alt="$16x&gt;250 \implies x \geq 16$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="181" height="15" />. If <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/6/2/1628581d2c8bdf5dfbb9d5fafe360ec1773aaff6.png" class="latex" alt="$x=16$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="13" />, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/e/5/5e5cdbab780ed9b6c9f5db5a044270e53912a7a6.png" class="latex" alt="$16x+700=956$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="129" height="14" />. Working backwards from 956, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/a/9/4a9bec556ce1f066acaa89591b510964a49b6150.png" class="latex" alt="$956 \rightarrow 478 \rightarrow 428 \rightarrow 214 \rightarrow 164 \rightarrow 82 \rightarrow 32 \rightarrow 16$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="388" height="13" />. 
</p><p>So the starting number is 16, and our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/7/79793b0f12454bdb6e29a754735ce574555acae3.png" class="latex" alt="$1+6=\boxed{7}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="85" height="23" />, which is A.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h3>
<p>Work backwards. The last number Bernardo produces must be in the range <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/8/e/68e9cad028df7dec5e91c9b17f4adbbd69ff997f.png" class="latex" alt="$[950,999]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="70" height="18" />. That means that before this, Silvia must produce a number in the range <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/c/54c7141f734f081bb6dcfec5767313dfefb9eb26.png" class="latex" alt="$[475,499]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="70" height="18" />. Before this, Bernardo must produce a number in the range <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/d/fedca09904c2f859f7cc67a16d85048aeb3def61.png" class="latex" alt="$[425,449]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="70" height="18" />. Before this, Silvia must produce a number in the range <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/4/6c4ec6c90f7f9d147b6f37dab41f1cd4086899d6.png" class="latex" alt="$[213,224]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="70" height="18" />. Before this, Bernardo must produce a number in the range <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/c/7/2c74fa588f22b763406a0347561206cc531f389b.png" class="latex" alt="$[163,174]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="70" height="18" />. Before this, Silvia must produce a number in the range <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/0/b701bcc3d868f98630f9b05eab2c5406a46b0b01.png" class="latex" alt="$[82,87]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="52" height="18" />. Before this, Bernardo must produce a number in the range <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/b/7/eb7e9ad6309143501a3d157879690716fd251d2f.png" class="latex" alt="$[32,37]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="52" height="18" />. Before this, Silvia must produce a number in the range <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/e/82ea496e66a2dede47a10899b472c310d31ff34e.png" class="latex" alt="$[16,18]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="52" height="18" />. Silvia could not have added 50 to any number before this to obtain a number in the range <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/e/82ea496e66a2dede47a10899b472c310d31ff34e.png" class="latex" alt="$[16,18]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="52" height="18" />, hence the minimum <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/9/fc97ef67268cd4e91bacdf12b8901d7036c9a056.png" class="latex" alt="$N$" width="16" height="12" /> is 16 with the sum of digits being <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/d/b/7dbf74dc64cf0f4fb1e277e03bf74c5a8dff3880.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(A)}\ 7}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="55" height="28" />.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h3>
<p>If our first number is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/6/35672a19103f2ead1f02935bb8450addc3318b94.png" class="latex" alt="$N,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="19" height="16" /> then the sequence of numbers will be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/d/ffde9877dcef098fe8f38621d9042cece360806e.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[2N,~2N+50,~4N+100,~4N+150,~8N+300,~8N+350,~16N+700,~16N+750\]" width="584" height="16" /> Note that we cannot go any further because doubling gives an extra <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/2/6/726ceb374a9997197b7c47009e586ebf30a3d9a3.png" class="latex" alt="$1500$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" /> at the end, which is already greater than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/b/4/6b434ebc2071e706df740975a3c90aad3cfa1455.png" class="latex" alt="$1000.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="39" height="13" /> The smallest <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/9/fc97ef67268cd4e91bacdf12b8901d7036c9a056.png" class="latex" alt="$N$" width="16" height="12" /> will be given if <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/1/c/a1c6263957c3a5575f9bdfc622ce28ed5c55ee60.png" class="latex" alt="$16N+750&gt;1000&gt;16N+700 \implies 15&lt;N&lt;19.$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="407" height="14" /> Since the problem asks for the smallest possible value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/6/35672a19103f2ead1f02935bb8450addc3318b94.png" class="latex" alt="$N,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="19" height="16" /> we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/4/b/e4bb513e21508306b77c013aae74e9c4b165e9fc.png" class="latex" alt="$16,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="22" height="16" /> and the sum of its digits is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/6/c/16c3f53b077e4bd1c25a9b90219ffddee8c142b8.png" class="latex" alt="$1+6=\boxed{\textbf{(A)}~7}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="120" height="28" />
</p><p>~ 1001
</p>
