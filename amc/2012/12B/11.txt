<p>Change the equation to base 10: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/8/7f8c413a644a9cf4e811fa2f03795ac829834483.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[A^2 + 3A +2 + 4B +3= 6A + 6B + 9\]" width="298" height="16" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/a/d/6adfef05e4387aa3181bf060ddd0e5b33da04966.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[A^2 - 3A - 2B - 4=0\]" width="175" height="15" /> 
</p><p>Either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/4/0/440ebe4cd5f20bf4bdfe440636d1bde7ecef8ed0.png" class="latex" alt="$B = A + 1$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="83" height="14" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/c/d/9cdaefb299f34ceb2e9b4605182d6df7d39e11a2.png" class="latex" alt="$B = A - 1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="83" height="13" />, so either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/5/8/158b12166e67da719caa0f9f9ed9f9c629cce170.png" class="latex" alt="$A^2 - 5A - 6, B = A + 1$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="188" height="18" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/9/9/599a8d05da6ff13b3e456397de64137598d6ebf6.png" class="latex" alt="$A^2 - 5A - 2, B = A - 1$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="188" height="18" />.
