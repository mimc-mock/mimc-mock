<p>A bug crawls along a number line, starting at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/2/a/62a76677add1d246708c86edfc7c4b8a40184914.png" class="latex" alt="$-2$" width="22" height="12" />. It crawls to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/6/8/3683f227b0b53e6a30087db2fa3b752965ecf05d.png" class="latex" alt="$-6$" width="22" height="12" />, then turns around and crawls to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />. How many units does the bug crawl altogether?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/a/a/2aa490b381d1fda6afe4ce2e4ed380cdb908db88.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 9\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 11\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 13\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 14\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 15$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="385" height="17" />
