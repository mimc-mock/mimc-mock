<p>Two bees start at the same spot and fly at the same rate in the following directions. Bee <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> travels <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> foot north, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> foot east, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> foot upwards, and then continues to repeat this pattern. Bee <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" /> travels <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> foot south, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> foot west, and then continues to repeat this pattern. In what directions are the bees traveling when they are exactly <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> feet away from each other?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/8/9/189cd94747f9414d8c87bef0168013a057ec033f.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } A\ \text{east, } B\ \text{west} \qquad \textbf{(B) } A\ \text{north, } B\ \text{south} \qquad \textbf{(C) } A\ \text{north, } B\ \text{west} \qquad \textbf{(D) } A\ \text{up, } B\ \text{south} \qquad \textbf{(E) } A\ \text{up, } B\ \text{west}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="875" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Let A and B begin at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/e/9/4e9f36b13a11a14accae8e072c0cf34d69267bb6.png" class="latex" alt="$(0,0,0)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="56" height="18" />. In <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> steps, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> will have done his route twice, ending up at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/d/4/3d4d0787f258358551930ba1f3dbdab59fa1a9a6.png" class="latex" alt="$(2,2,2)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="56" height="18" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" /> will have done his route three times, ending at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/2/8/228200a2fef126868a5e7112d56ab857b682a7e6.png" class="latex" alt="$(-3,-3,0)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="84" height="18" />. Their distance is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/e/f9e20713213e444a2c9e445b314b53e319648052.png" class="latex" alt="$\sqrt{(2+3)^2+(2+3)^2+2^2}=\sqrt{54} &lt; 10$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="302" height="32" />. We now move forward one step at a time until they are ten feet away:
</p><p>7 steps: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> moves north to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/b/0/8b0280aebf8b4eb06b4eb9b0f8376eef9cec787a.png" class="latex" alt="$(2,3,2)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="56" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" /> moves south to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/6/9/86969448d0e1a3f29502f71281e6b58a98b37146.png" class="latex" alt="$(-3,-4,0)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="84" height="18" />, distance of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/b/0/0b0fa5c3dce98fc569b1f99473ac83d37133acbc.png" class="latex" alt="$\sqrt{(2+3)^2+(3+4)^2+2^2}=\sqrt{78} &lt; 10$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="302" height="32" />
</p><p>8 steps: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> moves east to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/7/4/3743da5af2793e9e268234fa98ec06299c8b1485.png" class="latex" alt="$(3,3,2)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="56" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" /> moves west to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/4/2/e42925b4c064fd3e0347c12b5cabb1fe5305e0d8.png" class="latex" alt="$(-4,-4,0)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="84" height="18" />, distance of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/d/7/fd759cd630154b9aa33342ceaafbd97ec253c056.png" class="latex" alt="$\sqrt{(3+4)^2+(3+4)^2+2^2}=\sqrt{102}&gt;10$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="311" height="32" />
</p><p>Thus they reach <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> feet away when <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> is moving east and B is moving west, between moves 7 and 8. Thus the answer is  <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/e/35e8ea2d16518197b5113002b8c0c7da6c311a3e.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(A)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="41" height="28" />.
</p>
