<p>The sequence <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/f/1/1f1d31dfac94050b4d1d7896c826f3630951b513.png" class="latex" alt="$S_1, S_2, S_3, \cdots, S_{10}$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="139" height="16" /> has the property that every term beginning with the third is the sum of the previous two.  That is, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/9/d/49d16ad7dec282dc9ee086290f9aa43ba22b0b78.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[S_n = S_{n-2} + S_{n-1} \text{ for } n \ge 3.\]" width="219" height="15" /> Suppose that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/2/f92300ed458272b30562cfdfaefdd2febf5376c1.png" class="latex" alt="$S_9 = 110$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="69" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/8/d182fd61fa45b7b0a73697cc1ffdaea87333df34.png" class="latex" alt="$S_7 = 42$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="60" height="15" />.  What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/2/7/927ca6f264730b7f22ea5e06ee7ac6872819f94e.png" class="latex" alt="$S_4$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="17" height="15" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/1/c31b1dc65ee21b37c677f982eff4bd0f94d80c9b.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 4\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 6\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 10\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 12\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 16\qquad$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="376" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/2/f92300ed458272b30562cfdfaefdd2febf5376c1.png" class="latex" alt="$S_9 = 110$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="69" height="15" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/1/8/d182fd61fa45b7b0a73697cc1ffdaea87333df34.png" class="latex" alt="$S_7 = 42$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="60" height="15" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/1/bf1340fd5ca32ae5e165acc39bafe3f1456c6fc7.png" class="latex" alt="$S_8 = S_9 - S_ 7 = 110 - 42 = 68$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="236" height="15" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/f/d/1fd6ce2fa2c4a18b3b8eb5ea9c237947c746a1e6.png" class="latex" alt="$S_6 = S_8 - S_7 = 68 - 42 = 26$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="227" height="15" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/d/6/0d6dd2209711d808f34a95d1bff70dac2cf0e0a7.png" class="latex" alt="$S_5 = S_7 - S_6 = 42 - 26 = 16$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="227" height="15" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/4/2/242bfdd4125ca626d9903d244d1ad5deca88ba32.png" class="latex" alt="$S_4 = S_6 - S_5 = 26 - 16 = 10$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="227" height="15" />
</p><p>Therefore, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/c/e2c7554b5d32e6696d5f2f14c5752b2569705654.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) }{10}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="65" height="28" />
</p>
