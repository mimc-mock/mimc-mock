<p>The sequence
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/7/41793eda7b06e911ab6c4c3098662f7baf5e8e0e.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{162}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="67" height="17" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/2/8c2ff51d33698fe6de1d1e0946ecddfe908724ac.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{x}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="50" height="17" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/b/8/8b88b9b218534416efde4dad6ba7a107a270b419.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{y}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="49" height="17" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/8/8/488dea8b04efcc8bb8d2513924ae6614d0b3634f.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{z}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="49" height="17" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/a/5ba0128ce278a9f2db95ca335f583425c5847c44.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{1250}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="76" height="17" />
</p><p>is an arithmetic progression. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/3/5131c9bb69bfc0d5f5dc8ace17da1e6998b49535.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)} \ 125\sqrt{3} \qquad \textbf{(B)} \ 270 \qquad \textbf{(C)} \ 162\sqrt{5} \qquad \textbf{(D)} \ 434 \qquad \textbf{(E)} \ 225\sqrt{6}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="513" height="21" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Since the sequence is arithmetic, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/7/41793eda7b06e911ab6c4c3098662f7baf5e8e0e.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{162}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="67" height="17" /> + <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/8/f/a8fba9005cb63bba4318f8b13049aa3006547cd5.png" class="latex" alt="$4d$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="13" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/a/5ba0128ce278a9f2db95ca335f583425c5847c44.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{1250}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="76" height="17" />, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> is the common difference.
</p><p><br />
Therefore,
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/8/f/a8fba9005cb63bba4318f8b13049aa3006547cd5.png" class="latex" alt="$4d$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="13" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/a/5ba0128ce278a9f2db95ca335f583425c5847c44.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{1250}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="76" height="17" /> - <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/7/41793eda7b06e911ab6c4c3098662f7baf5e8e0e.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{162}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="67" height="17" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/9/0/690dc1e1bf9a2baac30a441ecfdf0bab3d974d89.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{(1250/162)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="125" height="18" />, and
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/3/c339afa03e060d959e4942be7522c0c992f4c41c.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{4}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="11" height="37" />(<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/9/0/690dc1e1bf9a2baac30a441ecfdf0bab3d974d89.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{(1250/162)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="125" height="18" />) = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/1/d515e688ff60d2265c84ec61893b0a1cf9b7f65c.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{(1250/162)^{1/4}}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="147" height="20" />
</p><p><br />
</p><p>Now that we found <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" />, we just add it to the first term to find <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/7/41793eda7b06e911ab6c4c3098662f7baf5e8e0e.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{162}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="67" height="17" /> + <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/1/d515e688ff60d2265c84ec61893b0a1cf9b7f65c.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{(1250/162)^{1/4}}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="147" height="20" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/1/d517df7717629c17d41206e7cba4032479f9c571.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{((162)(1250/162)^{1/4})}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="202" height="20" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/4/c9427366a1925a9dc8714dadb30dc2d3565e0b84.png" class="latex" alt="$(162)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="39" height="18" /><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/3/a/53aeb262d0c6e3c3ed67edb4995f16250ee0b9a5.png" class="latex" alt="$(1250/162)^{1/4}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="106" height="20" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/4/c9427366a1925a9dc8714dadb30dc2d3565e0b84.png" class="latex" alt="$(162)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="39" height="18" /><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/2/57241059b4811497142e54be308d6392c963b537.png" class="latex" alt="$(625/81)^{1/4}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="88" height="20" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/d/7/8d798e93c654ffd478208e561701d33b49130a96.png" class="latex" alt="$(162)(5/3)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="81" height="18" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/9/6/496208bfed993b16f902b0edc179b34b89804160.png" class="latex" alt="$270$" width="26" height="12" />, which is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>As the sequence <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/7/41793eda7b06e911ab6c4c3098662f7baf5e8e0e.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{162}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="67" height="17" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/2/8c2ff51d33698fe6de1d1e0946ecddfe908724ac.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{x}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="50" height="17" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/b/8/8b88b9b218534416efde4dad6ba7a107a270b419.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{y}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="49" height="17" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/8/8/488dea8b04efcc8bb8d2513924ae6614d0b3634f.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{z}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="49" height="17" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/a/5ba0128ce278a9f2db95ca335f583425c5847c44.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{12}{1250}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="76" height="17" /> is an arithmetic progression, the sequence <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/7/2/9727f0421617708df7d1872db0469ab975fe00d9.png" class="latex" alt="$162,x,y,z,1250$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="124" height="16" /> must be a geometric progression.
</p><p>If we factor the two known terms we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/5/4f5f1b98d4914ae704000fad91be977e1ca2dfe6.png" class="latex" alt="$162=2\cdot 3^4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="89" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/3/3/e33ac73bcbbab2147b78d8644bb1eb0ec36b55a5.png" class="latex" alt="$1250=2\cdot 5^4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="98" height="15" />, thus the quotient is obviously <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/3/1d31460c82f927162bb7828441f6ee67c5f767c7.png" class="latex" alt="$5/3$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="26" height="18" /> and therefore <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/c/f/9cf9eab6d499b33c94faf10060c172f81e16bfc0.png" class="latex" alt="$x=162\cdot(5/3) = 270$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="167" height="18" />.
</p>
