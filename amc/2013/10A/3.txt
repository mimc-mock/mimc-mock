<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>We are given that the area of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/5/9057d4d264f5530ac63d366089bc3c17ce5d79a4.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle ABE$" width="58" height="13" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/8/7/78792fe4680a164f537ea72a4f900e4471433c3b.png" class="latex" alt="$40$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />, and that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/b/b6b5b6b078188523fb69a22aff2fbd2b3df42b14.png" class="latex" alt="$AB = 10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="70" height="13" />.
</p><p>The area of a triangle is:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/a/8/2a8a4739d6a26db5c4596d54cf765a7b63a53fb2.png" class="latex" alt="$A = \frac{bh}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="58" height="37" />
</p><p>Using <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/e/57ee5125358c0606c9b588580ddfa66f83e607b7.png" class="latex" alt="$AB$" width="27" height="13" /> as the height of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/5/9057d4d264f5530ac63d366089bc3c17ce5d79a4.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle ABE$" width="58" height="13" />,
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/d/3/4d3a4c3aa665381c1964f2f02b5f168fec979d68.png" class="latex" alt="$40 = \frac{10b}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="70" height="37" />
</p><p>and solving for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />,
