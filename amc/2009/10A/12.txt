<p>In quadrilateral <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/e/f9efaf9474c5658c4089523e2aff4e11488f8603.png" class="latex" alt="$ABCD$" width="57" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/8/8/d88d3b225d4dcf1a78380f5c98940f6107e3deca.png" class="latex" alt="$AB = 5$" width="61" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/d/add662a0bcea3258bd427832357a4d6a542bf69c.png" class="latex" alt="$BC = 17$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="71" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/d/9/4d99eb629f027ee953f4fce4c24425c6fb800a24.png" class="latex" alt="$CD = 5$" width="62" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/9/6790c30f7e0de64cd05074a11aa915120e09ad5a.png" class="latex" alt="$DA = 9$" width="62" height="13" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/9/f/89fff82bb65d0215e49c8c91cb7c553da52205e2.png" class="latex" alt="$BD$" width="29" height="12" /> is an integer. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/9/f/89fff82bb65d0215e49c8c91cb7c553da52205e2.png" class="latex" alt="$BD$" width="29" height="12" />?
</p>
<center><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/0/570b698fea7c6d66beab04dd6aee44dd24f2b6c1.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] unitsize(4mm); defaultpen(linewidth(.8pt)+fontsize(8pt)); dotfactor=4;  pair C=(0,0), B=(17,0); pair D=intersectionpoints(Circle(C,5),Circle(B,13))[0]; pair A=intersectionpoints(Circle(D,9),Circle(B,5))[0]; pair[] dotted={A,B,C,D};  draw(D--A--B--C--D--B); dot(dotted); label(&quot;$D$&quot;,D,NW); label(&quot;$C$&quot;,C,W); label(&quot;$B$&quot;,B,E); label(&quot;$A$&quot;,A,NE); [/asy]" width="358" height="85" /></center><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/1/c/f1c91317cfa55d222c95104286c1e90c8ee7c1bf.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 11 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 12 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 13 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 14 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 15$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p><h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>By the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Triangle_inequality" class="mw-redirect" title="Triangle inequality">triangle inequality</a> we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/b/60b0a18ae56f591d170e9486dc413779dc75b86a.png" class="latex" alt="$BD &lt; DA + AB = 9 + 5 = 14$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="241" height="14" />, and also <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/4/9/74970605a1dea225a719b814cb0428018a7de99f.png" class="latex" alt="$BD + CD &gt; BC$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="135" height="14" />, hence <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/c/83c2a0e0df682b1f94fd40dc2a9ef415d1b658b1.png" class="latex" alt="$BD &gt; BC - CD = 17 - 5 = 12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="251" height="13" />.
</p><p>We get that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/f/a/3fa71d1c756ae190f63e4fd935de6b83c722f676.png" class="latex" alt="$12 &lt; BD &lt; 14$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="115" height="13" />, and as we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/9/f/89fff82bb65d0215e49c8c91cb7c553da52205e2.png" class="latex" alt="$BD$" width="29" height="12" /> is an integer, we must have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/b/7abcbbd0f931cc53b4cce01a408c4fa4a4672ca8.png" class="latex" alt="$BD=\boxed{13}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="84" height="23" />.
</p>
