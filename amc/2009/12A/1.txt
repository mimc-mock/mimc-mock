<p>Kim's flight took off from Newark at 10:34 AM and landed in Miami at 1:18 PM. Both cities are in the same time zone. If her flight took <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/8/8189a5b5a0917b8c93350827be4038af1839139d.png" class="latex" alt="$h$" width="10" height="12" /> hours and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/0/f5047d1e0cbb50ec208923a22cd517c55100fa7b.png" class="latex" alt="$m$" width="15" height="8" /> minutes, with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/9/0/b9074389182b6bb3eb504c536a57c06d51512821.png" class="latex" alt="$0 &lt; m &lt; 60$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="91" height="13" />, what is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/e/b/3eb5079e64854a8781cd6ad26bb4b417ea9781c9.png" class="latex" alt="$h + m$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="48" height="14" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/9/f/39f8ccfd0c4976e8e7da0e7e949e5bc0798ab247.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 46 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 47 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 50 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 53 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 54$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>There is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> hour and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/d/f3dbc342d2af90f8e1044ffa0c70761eb827d531.png" class="latex" alt="$60-34 = 26$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="100" height="13" /> minutes between 10:34 AM and noon; and there is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> hour and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/e/7/5e7a1b6098a8c98b8e4adae526aeef4b91712620.png" class="latex" alt="$18$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> minutes between noon and 1:18 PM. Hence the flight took <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> hours and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/f/0/8f06817759dd750d4f14160b23b063952535a927.png" class="latex" alt="$26 + 18 = 44$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="101" height="14" /> minutes, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/2/1021cdf265f50fe4901c8f2c5b3c5fed67cb0ec6.png" class="latex" alt="$h+m= 46\ \textbf{(A)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="124" height="17" />.
</p>
