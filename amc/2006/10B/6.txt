<p>A region is bounded by semicircular arcs constructed on the side of a square whose sides measure <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/0/f80c5dddbc62faa2e29515da4b433c4c8dc9613f.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2}{\pi}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="13" height="37" />, as shown. What is the perimeter of this region?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/e/2/5e2f7d7427c74567de47a93edfad961d23cd8633.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] size(90); defaultpen(linewidth(0.7)); filldraw((0,0)--(2,0)--(2,2)--(0,2)--cycle,gray(0.5)); filldraw(arc((1,0),1,180,0, CCW)--cycle,gray(0.7)); filldraw(arc((0,1),1,90,270)--cycle,gray(0.7)); filldraw(arc((1,2),1,0,180)--cycle,gray(0.7)); filldraw(arc((2,1),1,270,90, CCW)--cycle,gray(0.7)); [/asy]" width="152" height="152" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/c/c/5cc3ae8fc4036a4e5f1dc1f776ed94cc7763c385.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } \frac{4}{\pi}\qquad \textbf{(B) } 2\qquad \textbf{(C) } \frac{8}{\pi}\qquad \textbf{(D) } 4\qquad \textbf{(E) } \frac{16}{\pi}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="380" height="38" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Since the side of the square is the diameter of the semicircle, the radius of the semicircle is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/6/8/0680bee2333595261957a2fd4ddc10f70252932f.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{2}\cdot\frac{2}{\pi}=\frac{1}{\pi}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="79" height="37" />.
</p><p>Since the length of one of the semicircular <a href="/wiki/index.php/Arc" title="Arc">arcs</a> is half the circumference of the corresponding circle, the length of one arc is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/6/5/a65f59645830f43b279e8cd97d70691d34e1d1ac.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{2}\cdot2\cdot\pi\cdot\frac{1}{\pi}=1$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="120" height="37" />. 
</p><p>Since the desired perimeter is made up of four of these arcs, the perimeter is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/2/b82f541406ff2ef1e80f69f30c8c17665bb6a916.png" class="latex" alt="$4\cdot1=\boxed{\textbf{(D) }4}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="114" height="30" />.
</p>
