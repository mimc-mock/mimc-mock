<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>The peanut butter and jam for each sandwich costs <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/3/4f346e8ccadc97a22ef850c4ab099ca302b09670.png" class="latex" alt="$4B\cent+5J\cent$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="83" height="15" />, so the peanut butter and jam for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/9/fc97ef67268cd4e91bacdf12b8901d7036c9a056.png" class="latex" alt="$N$" width="16" height="12" /> sandwiches costs <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/2/1/42122db365826f4842140fa0d7a7e789bae324c4.png" class="latex" alt="$N(4B+5J)\cent$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="105" height="18" />. 
</p><p>Setting this equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/e/c3eb545d940178e9f54be2a719b68c47fe863c1e.png" class="latex" alt="$253\cent$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="35" height="15" />:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/1/5/2154fabfa05cddf8f067d521d0121538dbd33454.png" class="latex" alt="$N(4B+5J)=253=11\cdot23$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="222" height="18" />
</p><p>The only possible positive integer pairs <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/5/915b70687a9cec368aa1f1b377aa86035892af09.png" class="latex" alt="$(N , 4B+5J)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="103" height="18" /> whose product is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/5/8350ad56249c4928110c3e8b558ca84a2df815af.png" class="latex" alt="$253$" width="26" height="12" /> are: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/d/3/0d356134e8321512527a0827d75018b4e5ed96d4.png" class="latex" alt="$(1,253)&#160;; (11,23)&#160;; (23,11)&#160;; (253,1)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="257" height="18" />
</p><p>The first pair violates <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/c/9/0c9c0479fbd8aad814e20f8d875602b38a115c4d.png" class="latex" alt="$N&gt;1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="49" height="13" /> and the third and fourth pairs have no positive integer solutions for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/b/b/abb5588023cfa1ac14643e9778699f03eecc57a3.png" class="latex" alt="$J$" width="12" height="12" />. 
</p><p>So, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/d/c/9dcaff3b5a28c5bc81229c7e0cb1216ad4bd7296.png" class="latex" alt="$N=11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="58" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/0/b/f0b91d254cadc578c6d7da2f0333fff14f59d98f.png" class="latex" alt="$4B+5J=23$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="108" height="14" />.
</p><p>The only integer solutions for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/b/b/abb5588023cfa1ac14643e9778699f03eecc57a3.png" class="latex" alt="$J$" width="12" height="12" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/1/3417d76d7ccdf011b0760de58dd37e42a924bdf7.png" class="latex" alt="$B=2$" width="47" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/9/bf9fa150888856bbc5c32a1c159404113d588134.png" class="latex" alt="$J=3$" width="44" height="12" />.
</p><p>Therefore, the cost of the jam Elmo uses to make the sandwiches is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/4/0/b40fd0c3449d40f287978a745c79044fe5fb8e4a.png" class="latex" alt="$3\cdot5\cdot11=165\cent$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="122" height="15" />  <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/d/c/6dc4d366b609f6108ca809cefa733e62bd432b30.png" class="latex" alt="$=  $1.65 \Rightarrow \boxed{\mathrm{D}}$" style="vertical-align: -6px" width="116" height="25" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Note as above, you get the equation <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/9/e/29e19a1f33504bc74dee76c7e101f88f13615552.png" class="latex" alt="$N(0.04B+0.05J=2.53)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="199" height="18" />
</p><p>Notice that we can multiply by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> on both sides to get whole numbers. Hence <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/e/8/7e86e64bc12e0fa37e4089e79378c6563d058a8a.png" class="latex" alt="$\implies N(4B+5J)=253$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="193" height="18" />
</p><p>Note that the prime factorization of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/8/0/3806c0e9287df04377bf99f6c938a80234633bb0.png" class="latex" alt="$253=11\cdot23$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="100" height="13" />.
</p><p>Hence, we want <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/0/b/f0b91d254cadc578c6d7da2f0333fff14f59d98f.png" class="latex" alt="$4B+5J=23$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="108" height="14" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/8/c6878713578626763c38433b3f4c8c2205ad0c15.png" class="latex" alt="$11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />
</p><p>Now, we have two cases to test.
</p><p>Case 1: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/0/b/f0b91d254cadc578c6d7da2f0333fff14f59d98f.png" class="latex" alt="$4B+5J=23$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="108" height="14" />
</p><p>Notice that we want <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/b/d/dbdfb34bb887dc087fe241fa6b454765dfc8e6c9.png" class="latex" alt="$B\le5$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="47" height="15" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/a/09a3887646f9d9cd203890d47294ae3e8d27a205.png" class="latex" alt="$J\le4$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="45" height="15" />
</p><p>Taking <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/6/916e3d564cd27e39fdb334f895a79b5d40b0deec.png" class="latex" alt="$\pmod{5}\implies 4B\equiv3\pmod{5}\implies B=2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="348" height="18" />
</p><p>Hence, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/4/6/c463b6b753e9160058aab0e839b49996682ea81e.png" class="latex" alt="$B=2,J=3$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="100" height="16" />.
</p><p>Hence, the price of the jam is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/6/2/6628f87aa1ac7ca23f087a2c474d62f55870f180.png" class="latex" alt="$3\cdot11\cdot{0.05}\implies 1.65 \implies\boxed{D}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="244" height="24" />.
