<p>Francesca uses 100 grams of lemon juice, 100 grams of sugar, and 400 grams of water to make lemonade.  There are 25 calories in 100 grams of lemon juice and 386 calories in 100 grams of sugar.  Water contains no calories.  How many calories are in 200 grams of her lemonade?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/3/65331d73a0442fdd5639b74fd9a57349db8c78b7.png" class="latex" alt="$\text {(A) } 129 \qquad \text {(B) } 137 \qquad \text {(C) } 174 \qquad \text {(D) } 223 \qquad \text {(E) } 411$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="420" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Francesca makes a total of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/1/d/a1d70b98a3db79c82ec0ca4b891078def7a689bc.png" class="latex" alt="$100+100+400=600$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="177" height="14" /> grams of lemonade, and in those <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/a/a/caa9bc3e1ca566d171ec6b80e39ee4cc913b5aa1.png" class="latex" alt="$600$" width="26" height="12" /> grams, there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/6/6/66644fd9f411299c3c1eabf151b5f601c9ef6c21.png" class="latex" alt="$25$" width="17" height="12" /> calories from the lemon juice and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/f/57f55ec01da5c55eb70a451645b9481f33d061e8.png" class="latex" alt="$386$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="29" height="14" /> calories from the sugar, for a total of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/e/baee11712ed309525c9cded04fc60d42d4c486f2.png" class="latex" alt="$25+386=411$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="118" height="14" /> calories per <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/a/a/caa9bc3e1ca566d171ec6b80e39ee4cc913b5aa1.png" class="latex" alt="$600$" width="26" height="12" /> grams. We want to know how many calories there are in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/2/01242907cd94c27bcaff552b46cfdd0cd7b665d6.png" class="latex" alt="$200=600/3$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="96" height="18" /> grams, so we just divide <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/a/7/2a7175687ac053fd16e00c047312692c905b65fd.png" class="latex" alt="$411$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="12" /> by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> to get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/5/b758f96f3fbf3fb8124a25bc53de308effcaa77d.png" class="latex" alt="$137\implies\boxed{(\text{B})}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="114" height="29" />.
</p>
