<p>Mary is about to pay for five items at the grocery store.  The prices of the items are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/2/07295bef10c97f742a1e3e5d529e9299a5425a9c.png" class="latex" alt="$7.99$" width="31" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/9/1d9e0dc7ab42d418e920f1f0fd7154d645e2ceb3.png" class="latex" alt="$4.99$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="31" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/7/1/a7117460b42030e57876b6c08386eefdfc2a4451.png" class="latex" alt="$2.99$" width="31" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/a/c7a170aa044b1a4775240768d3ee7cc3ba67e388.png" class="latex" alt="$1.99$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="31" height="13" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/d/6/0d647f8f49c91b95cc56a0a66f5dd2143533d6ca.png" class="latex" alt="$0.99$" width="31" height="12" />.  Mary will pay with a twenty-dollar bill.  Which of the following is closest to the percentage of the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/e/f5e300b5b3cfd4f614d1d5b105244798bca90f60.png" class="latex" alt="$20.00$" width="40" height="12" /> that she will receive in change?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/1/b/71b093adc3a4f4fb890240978eba5db1bd5910f5.png" class="latex" alt="$\text {(A) } 5 \qquad \text {(B) } 10 \qquad \text {(C) } 15 \qquad \text {(D) } 20 \qquad \text {(E) } 25$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="366" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The total price of the items is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/4/9/049b385136cfb85e7e91e37d83e762090bff151a.png" class="latex" alt="$(8-.01)+(5-.01)+(3-.01)+(2-.01)+(1-.01)=19-.05=18.95$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="585" height="18" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/7/4f7ec93d66f4ba53a8457499c6ac50280b98ac5b.png" class="latex" alt="$20-18.95=1.05$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="137" height="13" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/4/a/74a71e5c26a9306bf0acfa33e045a63101bf7c54.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1.05}{20}=.0525 \Rightarrow \text{(A)}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="154" height="37" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Alternative_Solution">Alternative Solution</span></h2>
<p>We can round the prices to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />. 
</p><p>So <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/8/f/18fd49bfe9eedbf7ce8769bfa332aed62ded072e.png" class="latex" alt="$20 - (8+5+3+2+1) = 1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="221" height="18" />
</p><p>We can make an equation: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/a/7/8a7074f5cda9169e4599a24134cb1532f964d4f4.png" class="latex" alt="$20* \frac{x}{100}=1$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="99" height="33" />
</p><p>If we simplify the equation to "x", we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/c/51c80ea5862b45877f08d698a0c893bd2806a2fd.png" class="latex" alt="$x=5$" width="43" height="12" />
</p>
