<p>A store normally sells windows at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> each. This week the store is offering one free window for each purchase of four. Dave needs seven windows and Doug needs eight windows. How many dollars will they save if they purchase the windows together rather than separately?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/d/3/fd3ce4f88a9742402d5a1c78d31330dd8cd99e63.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } 100\qquad \textbf{(B) } 200\qquad \textbf{(C) } 300\qquad \textbf{(D) } 400\qquad \textbf{(E) } 500$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="446" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The store's offer means that every <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />th window is free. 
</p><p>Dave would get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/c/0/5c041aa4e44a78078451569e16c026ae20ceabe9.png" class="latex" alt="$\left\lfloor\frac{7}{5}\right\rfloor=1$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="66" height="43" /> free window.
</p><p>Doug would get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/0/96066bbd014f0f1c524dc5d88b7d75d8d73e95c9.png" class="latex" alt="$\left\lfloor\frac{8}{5}\right\rfloor=1$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="66" height="43" /> free window. 
</p><p>This is a total of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> free windows. 
</p><p>Together, they would get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/a/b7a85487ceece443df89538301df1871fc1b7daf.png" class="latex" alt="$\left\lfloor\frac{8+7}{5}\right\rfloor = \left\lfloor\frac{15}{5}\right\rfloor=3$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="165" height="43" /> free windows. 
</p><p>So they get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/4/1/b419b91958d111b91db52e72a0e8d750e2b23174.png" class="latex" alt="$3-2=1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="72" height="13" /> additional window if they purchase the windows together. 
</p><p>Therefore they save <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/c/5/2c5e67c1d4dbb93650805cef2af0cdb49cb33660.png" class="latex" alt="$1\cdot100= \boxed{\textbf{(A) }100}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="150" height="30" />.
</p>
