<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>We have
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/a/7/ca7f72303ecda76e14992764cd8189ea495884ea.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{[ADE]}{[ABC]} = \frac{AD}{AB} \cdot \frac{AE}{AC} = \frac{19}{25} \cdot \frac{14}{42} = \frac{19}{75}.\]" width="290" height="43" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/2/6e2fdc4687b12e3806e8997a889e00dce6eb1d06.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] unitsize(0.15 cm);  pair A, B, C, D, E;  A = (191/39,28*sqrt(1166)/39); B = (0,0); C = (39,0); D = (6*A + 19*B)/25; E = (28*A + 14*C)/42;  draw(A--B--C--cycle); draw(D--E);  label(&quot;$A$&quot;, A, N); label(&quot;$B$&quot;, B, SW); label(&quot;$C$&quot;, C, SE); label(&quot;$D$&quot;, D, W); label(&quot;$E$&quot;, E, NE); label(&quot;$19$&quot;, (A + D)/2, W); label(&quot;$6$&quot;, (B + D)/2, W); label(&quot;$14$&quot;, (A + E)/2, NE); label(&quot;$28$&quot;, (C + E)/2, NE); [/asy]" width="322" height="218" />
</p><p>But <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/5/e/55eb2fcfbad02e393d8d58681100faa707370dcb.png" class="latex" alt="$[BCED] = [ABC] - [ADE]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="220" height="18" />, so
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/a/82ada7b0f131d9c26a53ef4d0ef37be11d728841.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} \frac{[ADE]}{[BCED]} &amp;= \frac{[ADE]}{[ABC] - [ADE]} \\ &amp;= \frac{1}{\frac{ABC}{ADE} - 1} \\ &amp;= \frac{1}{\frac{75}{19} - 1} \\ &amp;= \boxed{\textbf{(D) }\frac{19}{56}}. \end{align*}" width="226" height="197" />
</p><p><br />
</p><p>Note: If it is hard to understand why <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/6/d/36d273ebd006b209b3b800f87eddc9e62da0b740.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{[ADE]}{[ABC]} = \frac{AD}{AB} \cdot \frac{AE}{AC}\]" width="157" height="43" />, you can use the fact that the area of a triangle equals <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/d/aaddf799a9629a146c12d5677528ade397047727.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{2} \cdot ab \cdot \sin(C)$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="106" height="37" />.
