<p>A <a href="/wiki/index.php/Line" title="Line">line</a> passes through <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/5/7/15722de4278b855f58b51eb15a22630beaf110af.png" class="latex" alt="$A\ (1,1)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="58" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/b/4fbe368b26db2f3b9d35e3f7ff82813bafd7991a.png" class="latex" alt="$B\ (100,1000)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="104" height="18" />. How many other points with integer coordinates are on the line and strictly between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/7/7/2770f31ea6b50154b63ff2102bfdaf5a6b8a407e.png" class="latex" alt="$(\mathrm {A}) \ 0 \qquad (\mathrm {B}) \ 2 \qquad (\mathrm {C})\ 3 \qquad (\mathrm {D}) \ 8 \qquad (\mathrm {E})\ 9$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="342" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>For convenience’s sake, we can transform <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> to the origin and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" /> to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/b/82b4a1443b2809cc13170d3366b910ed95f71b70.png" class="latex" alt="$(99,999)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="66" height="18" /> (this does not change the problem). The line <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/e/57ee5125358c0606c9b588580ddfa66f83e607b7.png" class="latex" alt="$AB$" width="27" height="13" /> has the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Equation" title="Equation">equation</a> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/2/6/926c80b7e9ef14df9157ac39f5cf55b18f9c0369.png" class="latex" alt="$y = \frac{999}{99}x = \frac{111}{11}x$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="141" height="38" />. The coordinates are integers if <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/a/c/4ac081c40ade31dcfb7a02c23b367c67654be0da.png" class="latex" alt="$11|x$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="33" height="18" />, so the values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/f/c8f9ede8609198a3c325206f69856769e37a40f3.png" class="latex" alt="$11, 22 \ldots 88$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="89" height="16" />, with a total of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/2/a/c2a6cf822cb4ad0e9f258842a3522cf5eaddfb4f.png" class="latex" alt="$8\implies \boxed{\mathrm{(D)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="98" height="29" /> coordinates.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>The slope of the line is<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/2/1d28159388c2efcf765e04833ed7475745490848.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1000-1}{100-1}=\frac{111}{11},$" style="vertical-align: -15px" width="134" height="40" />so all points on the line have the form <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/1/e/21e230b792d682d8076b47a32dfacb2590ed7b13.png" class="latex" alt="$(1+11t, 1+111t)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="141" height="18" /> for some value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/d/e0d2bf360290fd61d1c1557e763f2622363b3d35.png" class="latex" alt="$t$" width="6" height="11" /> (the rise is 111 and the run is 11). Such a point has integer coordinates if and only if <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/d/e0d2bf360290fd61d1c1557e763f2622363b3d35.png" class="latex" alt="$t$" width="6" height="11" /> is an integer, and the point is strictly between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" /> if and only if <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/b/f/3bfc7a3eebbf5edf47f35bee05ba57dbae0f35b2.png" class="latex" alt="$0&lt;t&lt;9$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="72" height="13" />. Thus, there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/8/1/e81ca9c5c8715be868438500743202d6773d05bd.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{8}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="20" height="23" /> points with the required property.
-Paixiao
</p>
