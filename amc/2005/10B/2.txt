<p>A positive number <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> has the property that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/d/e/8de1a36d69a7028480a87b712a7141c651398a06.png" class="latex" alt="$x\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="25" height="14" /> of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />.  What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/d/9/4d9d502be7e035d3dabbc6798896792e563c9994.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }\ 2      \qquad \textbf{(B) }\ 4      \qquad \textbf{(C) }\ 10      \qquad \textbf{(D) }\ 20      \qquad \textbf{(E) }\ 40$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="410" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h3>
<p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/d/e/8de1a36d69a7028480a87b712a7141c651398a06.png" class="latex" alt="$x\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="25" height="14" /> means <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/c/3/9c3cf671d6f5df4b632ad29ac9a0be7b2f01566d.png" class="latex" alt="$0.01x$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="42" height="13" />, the statement "<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/5/ce5d862e51746c787b3e146180e28b9e0969bcf7.png" class="latex" alt="$x\% \text{ of } x \text{ is 4}$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="90" height="14" />" can be rewritten as "<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/c/a/9ca9179b069d8c7d8b5ac92413ddf79a266ecdda.png" class="latex" alt="$0.01x \cdot x = 4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="99" height="13" />":
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/e/88e2ff1670fc42d0a6b86dc1c210f1c83dade887.png" class="latex" alt="$0.01x \cdot x=4 \Rightarrow x^2 = 400 \Rightarrow x = \boxed{\textbf{(D) }20}.$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="332" height="30" />
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h3>
<p>Try the answer choices one by one. Upon examination, it is quite obvious that the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/d/c/7dcb0136086d149c9a91c9c0929b483b13500f0c.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D) }20}.$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="72" height="30" /> Very fast.
</p><p><br />
Solution by franzliszt
</p>
