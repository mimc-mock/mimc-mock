<p>A <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/6/3/f639dbb01f504247240bdb6c0de30ec2cf579d32.png" class="latex" alt="$\text{palindrome}$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="80" height="15" />, such as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/2/6025610adfc4989f16a016dfc9e9d97db9898060.png" class="latex" alt="$83438$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="44" height="13" />, is a number that remains the same when its digits are reversed. The numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/a/10a4f3733907d4c30d269c22a1a6f2d7c4f077ca.png" class="latex" alt="$x+32$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="50" height="14" /> are three-digit and four-digit palindromes, respectively. What is the sum of the digits of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/b/4/8b4f56b57667a40fcfe2001bed44b0ac3a9e3cb1.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 20 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 21 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 22 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 23 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 24$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h3>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is at most <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/0/0705411d92671faf1fe5602cfef8f353c7f0f83d.png" class="latex" alt="$999$" width="26" height="12" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/a/10a4f3733907d4c30d269c22a1a6f2d7c4f077ca.png" class="latex" alt="$x+32$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="50" height="14" /> is at most <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/2/c/02c5caace9b264d66671e4e634194b684cdd7695.png" class="latex" alt="$1031$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />. The minimum value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/a/10a4f3733907d4c30d269c22a1a6f2d7c4f077ca.png" class="latex" alt="$x+32$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="50" height="14" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/e/6ee927e1332358c96c62c277441c907c4f51057f.png" class="latex" alt="$1000$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />. However, the only palindrome between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/e/6ee927e1332358c96c62c277441c907c4f51057f.png" class="latex" alt="$1000$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/f/7/cf790a04eb77db1f1eff3f71af761734ef84ebaf.png" class="latex" alt="$1032$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/8/888c508062c09735bab7ff0e11e7dc80cc7580db.png" class="latex" alt="$1001$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />, which means that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/a/10a4f3733907d4c30d269c22a1a6f2d7c4f077ca.png" class="latex" alt="$x+32$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="50" height="14" /> must be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/8/888c508062c09735bab7ff0e11e7dc80cc7580db.png" class="latex" alt="$1001$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />.
</p><p>It follows that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/5/2/952151c78199467f06dd77598165b2f8fc012e35.png" class="latex" alt="$969$" width="26" height="12" />, so the sum of the digits is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/b/3/9b3104f61320b27432b8494542a5b28de9b01a6a.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(E)}\ 24}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="62" height="28" />.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h3>
<p>For <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/a/10a4f3733907d4c30d269c22a1a6f2d7c4f077ca.png" class="latex" alt="$x+32$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="50" height="14" /> to be a four-digit number, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is in between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/0/8/808b2fbca1933b6a2d68099725a80ec1caad5d50.png" class="latex" alt="$968$" width="26" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/0/0705411d92671faf1fe5602cfef8f353c7f0f83d.png" class="latex" alt="$999$" width="26" height="12" />. The palindromes in this range are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/5/2/952151c78199467f06dd77598165b2f8fc012e35.png" class="latex" alt="$969$" width="26" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/e/5/de58f4479abeeebc8fb50bd04af83e2c1bf631ae.png" class="latex" alt="$979$" width="26" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/7/3/e7312ef0815158b14fdda0634bc0be2dcf5a3d69.png" class="latex" alt="$989$" width="26" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/0/0705411d92671faf1fe5602cfef8f353c7f0f83d.png" class="latex" alt="$999$" width="26" height="12" />, so the sum of the digits of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> can be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/9/e290f2cba0ba0e11c0b91630da15a408da2fcf1c.png" class="latex" alt="$24$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/6/6/66644fd9f411299c3c1eabf151b5f601c9ef6c21.png" class="latex" alt="$25$" width="17" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/d/a/bda00d9a38a0c6bd9aac2512c6d065581882d43c.png" class="latex" alt="$26$" width="17" height="12" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/5/1051e7d41ec0b8bd688a872be17538413f4ee97b.png" class="latex" alt="$27$" width="18" height="12" />. Only <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/b/3/9b3104f61320b27432b8494542a5b28de9b01a6a.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(E)}\ 24}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="62" height="28" /> is an option, and upon checking, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d96fab55029c8ffb34af5f15e598f7541f593c.png" class="latex" alt="$x+32=1001$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="110" height="14" /> is indeed a palindrome.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h3>
<p>Since we know <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/a/10a4f3733907d4c30d269c22a1a6f2d7c4f077ca.png" class="latex" alt="$x+32$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="50" height="14" /> to be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/0/610269e2bbcf06111411ad0024b7b316b902c882.png" class="latex" alt="$1 a a 1$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="39" height="14" /> and the only palindrome that works is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/c/f/9cf3651f08f94af5e6229683abf76d1d706229b8.png" class="latex" alt="$0 = a$" width="43" height="12" />, that means <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/3/e13d6f7f6efef4f83b3b195bf6976d7f9f8395bd.png" class="latex" alt="$x+32 = 1001$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="113" height="15" />, and so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/1/431d131dc79348853523d2967a1d43d51452c771.png" class="latex" alt="$x = 1001 - 32 = 969$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="165" height="15" />. So, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> + <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> + <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> = <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/b/3/9b3104f61320b27432b8494542a5b28de9b01a6a.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(E)}\ 24}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="62" height="28" />.
~songmath20
</p>
