<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>By the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Angle_Bisector_Theorem" class="mw-redirect" title="Angle Bisector Theorem">Angle Bisector Theorem</a>, we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/1/881a95e204e4affee6408670c1da184970c5e677.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{AB}{BC} = \frac{3}{8}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="68" height="38" />. If we use the lowest possible integer values for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/e/57ee5125358c0606c9b588580ddfa66f83e607b7.png" class="latex" alt="$AB$" width="27" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/5/6c52a41dcbd739f1d026c5d4f181438b75b76976.png" class="latex" alt="$BC$" width="28" height="12" /> (the lengths of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/5/bc56862b1fdb71204894da278e42ef75de73a40b.png" class="latex" alt="$AD$" width="28" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/7/e/27e525b776c49bbe7d0ae66cfc7ab8f58f40624e.png" class="latex" alt="$DC$" width="29" height="12" />, respectively), then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/2/1/021137365a1c6ea35ebe3f63d73a2d05150e268f.png" class="latex" alt="$AB + BC = AD + DC = AC$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="237" height="14" />, contradicting the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Triangle_Inequality" title="Triangle Inequality">Triangle Inequality</a>. If we use the next lowest values (<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/5/9/9596b5a2993d0f1ce48e542106bde5b216018861.png" class="latex" alt="$AB = 6$" width="61" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/0/830de927bd8077aa97abc0e3829961d12711fe7a.png" class="latex" alt="$BC = 16$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="70" height="13" />), the Triangle Inequality is satisfied. Therefore, our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/c/f/2cf8a324ac91dc996a43ec591c840d26aae016a9.png" class="latex" alt="$6 + 16 + 3 + 8 = \boxed{33}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="166" height="23" />, or choice <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/1/b7193f66028e13f331d53ff5e6942c4b05587465.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(B)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="27" height="17" />.
</p>
