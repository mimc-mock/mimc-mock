<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Assuming we do not already know an inscribed angle is always half of its central angle, we will try a different approach. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/d/51da37d984564162c87710ca27bea422f657fb73.png" class="latex" alt="$O$" width="13" height="12" /> is the center, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/f/20f8f2f1a3ddc6b42fae25a0637e85c747875180.png" class="latex" alt="$OC$" width="28" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/a/b7a762ac8b4e23ffdd778ad6d5a8b97df8c7ce01.png" class="latex" alt="$OA$" width="28" height="13" /> are radii and they are congruent. Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/e/2/0e2d97829369bc06782c17988a22533979294d85.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle COA$" width="58" height="13" /> is an isosceles triangle. Also, note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/d/e2d87c2746bf18130f641d2c6b17a0fa902f7863.png" class="latex" alt="$\angle COB$" width="55" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/c/f/3cf72d4dd6847fd821094c422f8dcbde99783d5c.png" class="latex" alt="$\angle COA$" width="55" height="13" /> are supplementary, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/0/d/c0d83016880958075b6f571c7adb6da7c2bc0bfb.png" class="latex" alt="$\angle COA = 180 - 50 = 130^{\circ}$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="204" height="13" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/e/2/0e2d97829369bc06782c17988a22533979294d85.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle COA$" width="58" height="13" /> is isosceles, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/c/7/4c7054cee947c8430936a8e15679fdfc6f0be120.png" class="latex" alt="$\angle OCA \cong \angle OAC$" width="134" height="13" />. They also sum to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/e/c/5ec5fcabc29d46f91b1f4f628000fa40148d30a9.png" class="latex" alt="$50^{\circ}$" width="24" height="13" />, so each angle is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/6/e/16ee3ac3feaf0a603a95b83293fadb2847fb639a.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B)}\ 25}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="64" height="28" />.
