<p>The numbers from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> are placed at the vertices of a <a href="/wiki/index.php/Cube" title="Cube">cube</a> in such a manner that the sum of the four numbers on each face is the same. What is this common sum?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/2/8c2d744393c9206f825191e972e2d2024336fa3b.png" class="latex" alt="$\text{(A)}\ 14 \qquad \text{(B)}\ 16 \qquad \text{(C)}\ 18 \qquad \text{(D)}\ 20 \qquad \text{(E)}\ 24$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="376" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The sum of the numbers on one face of the cube is equal to the sum of the numbers on the opposite face of the cube; these <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> numbers represent all of the vertices of the cube. Thus the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/b/0/3b0ce5492995aedd6ae9e4634f39907e38a13254.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1 + 2 + \cdots + 8}{2} = 18\ \mathrm{(C)}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="192" height="37" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Consider a number on a vertex. It will be counted in 3 different faces, since any vertex is the intersection of three edges. Therefore, each number <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/6/b163b6898ad4bc232c7e37c4423d1190b59ee775.png" class="latex" alt="$1,2,\cdots,7,8$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="95" height="16" /> will be added into the total sum <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> times. Therefore, our total sum is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/790207ba1861b8e8a4904d7e2b20d4d35ab10fa4.png" class="latex" alt="$3(1+2+\cdots+8)=108.$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="193" height="18" /> Finally, since there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> faces, our common sum is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/e/f/befe0fabc37cfd63a22f5c5e0f596ce2146c9f88.png" class="latex" alt="$\dfrac{108}{6} = 18\mathrm{(C) }.$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="104" height="37" />
</p>
