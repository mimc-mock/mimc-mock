<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/d/c/fdc028050428615cc28ee3c1f9a67eda8e9a726c.png" class="latex" alt="$s_k$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="15" height="10" /> denote the sum of the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/8/3/183fdd96e440d7526512950f1ec491becf832db3.png" class="latex" alt="$\textit{k}$" width="8" height="12" />th powers of the roots of the polynomial <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/1/84104f35f8b36d9b6fa04a1036b7e043f57ddfb3.png" class="latex" alt="$x^3-5x^2+8x-13$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="148" height="16" />. In particular, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/c/6ecb76fbef48b875ffb5078a9c176fd378badac2.png" class="latex" alt="$s_0=3$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="48" height="15" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/2/d/a2d6db03b70676744f87cbc9784f33332e3e3d5f.png" class="latex" alt="$s_1=5$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="48" height="15" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/9/d/89df0e8b4236be93b4073587cec9546e27e0836f.png" class="latex" alt="$s_2=9$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="48" height="15" />. Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> be real numbers such that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/e/8/de8341691d962887a08594c7f1fcbb6ffbcba780.png" class="latex" alt="$s_{k+1} = a \, s_k + b \, s_{k-1} + c \, s_{k-2}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="219" height="16" /> for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/4/8e44ca2cae6be42148fe1b1fd8b5d55bd6b599bd.png" class="latex" alt="$k = 2$" width="43" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/8/9/789e7886ab2c8ab41ce25f01d6273c339aae4830.png" class="latex" alt="$....$" width="18" height="2" /> What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/b/6ebd1e0356cfe09c0d9b5459d6f1542657bb5891.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+c$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="70" height="14" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/7/c/a7c82d0be32ba32bea20d1e11e2f1dd5ecabee21.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)} \; -6 \qquad \textbf{(B)} \; 0 \qquad \textbf{(C)} \; 6 \qquad \textbf{(D)} \; 10 \qquad \textbf{(E)} \; 26$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="386" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Applying <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://artofproblemsolving.com/wiki/index.php/Newton&#39;s_Sums">Newton's Sums</a>, we have<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/a/b/0ab936b146c0392c59fa230195cfb6fa1ed6acb9.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[s_{k+1}+(-5)s_k+(8)s_{k-1}+(-13)s_{k-2}=0,\]" width="328" height="18" />so<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/d/8/3d8ebe1f551d4a709734eff0a8910b6a4344d15f.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[s_{k+1}=5s_k-8s_{k-1}+13s_{k-2},\]" width="226" height="17" />we get the answer as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/6/8e6caa69e278f6cf8dbf21ff666c4ab8ff6daed6.png" class="latex" alt="$5+(-8)+13=10$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="151" height="18" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/f/0/6f06a183d1092b27328c12a97322763ac8db97a2.png" class="latex" alt="$p, q$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="26" height="11" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> be the roots of the polynomial. Then,
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/a/2/4a2cfa1df9021f76fb72c132f05ce4691cd74dbf.png" class="latex" alt="$p^3 - 5p^2 + 8p - 13 = 0$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="179" height="18" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/f/7/6f7477e6658ea9b9a9af9f347c3d7ff030d87e34.png" class="latex" alt="$q^3 - 5q^2 + 8q - 13 = 0$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="177" height="18" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/1/7/f17f3ae91735c179865a02fe6396685be6426de2.png" class="latex" alt="$r^3 - 5r^2 + 8r - 13 = 0$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="177" height="16" />
</p><p>Adding these three equations, we get
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/8/6/886492f00cfb85467e2d007f99b8f7430b3d8c19.png" class="latex" alt="$(p^3 + q^3 + r^3) - 5(p^2 + q^2 + r^2) + 8(p + q + r) - 39 = 0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="436" height="19" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/b/9/eb9536f767769acc08f5ccbcc8856a3de72a3aa6.png" class="latex" alt="$s_3 - 5s_2 + 8s_1 = 39$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="152" height="15" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/5/e058d401a1b942f106f081ff1fcca8a8b1d42a7e.png" class="latex" alt="$39$" width="17" height="12" /> can be written as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/6/346cbb619eddaf8dd3e8065a47e1f08400b9f465.png" class="latex" alt="$13s_0$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="32" height="15" />, giving
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/5/c/75cf19159c44b9cbabfa35193a1b805ecbbb3089.png" class="latex" alt="$s_3 = 5s_2 - 8s_1 + 13s_0$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="167" height="15" />
</p><p>We are given that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/e/8/de8341691d962887a08594c7f1fcbb6ffbcba780.png" class="latex" alt="$s_{k+1} = a \, s_k + b \, s_{k-1} + c \, s_{k-2}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="219" height="16" /> is satisfied for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/4/8e44ca2cae6be42148fe1b1fd8b5d55bd6b599bd.png" class="latex" alt="$k = 2$" width="43" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/8/9/789e7886ab2c8ab41ce25f01d6273c339aae4830.png" class="latex" alt="$....$" width="18" height="2" />, meaning it must be satisfied when <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/4/8e44ca2cae6be42148fe1b1fd8b5d55bd6b599bd.png" class="latex" alt="$k = 2$" width="43" height="12" />, giving us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/1/f71c81b2ed74710e7a1ede3bf920084f306834a5.png" class="latex" alt="$s_3 = a \, s_2 + b \, s_1 + c \, s_0$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="165" height="15" />.
</p><p>Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/2/5/9258dc5ff7fee8350b3305f1e0560237d63a59b1.png" class="latex" alt="$a = 5, b = -8$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="105" height="16" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/9/7c9fd57b20f51bc00b2c081b47d8c97981e082e6.png" class="latex" alt="$c = 13$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="49" height="13" /> by matching coefficients.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/c/e/ece061c84699364b3d30e921a68f1667d7be8fc0.png" class="latex" alt="$5 - 8 + 13 = \boxed{\textbf{(D) } 10}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="170" height="28" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/f/0/6f06a183d1092b27328c12a97322763ac8db97a2.png" class="latex" alt="$p, q$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="26" height="11" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> be the roots of the polynomial. By Vieta's Formulae, we have
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/d/4/fd4e810881f140610dea3842e113f0eff01b66f6.png" class="latex" alt="$p+q+r = 5$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="105" height="16" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/1/f31598cf004c8fd714bd87b0eea46d057a002bec.png" class="latex" alt="$pq+qr+rp = 8$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="131" height="16" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/7/b/47b8214f84638548830b2b5e29e1dbd6abfb5267.png" class="latex" alt="$pqr=13$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="69" height="16" />.
</p><p>We know <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/d/9fd2ffaea39122862e213d215c1c2e6fdd7c28c6.png" class="latex" alt="$s_k = p^k + q^k + r^k$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="135" height="19" />. Consider <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/3/d/93d4dfc1d32898ed664dba23ccc6ec66d59abbc5.png" class="latex" alt="$(p+q+r)(s_k) =5s_k$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="165" height="18" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/4/d44a6f886a76b0dcc3d1cb868215ec4ce35cce40.png" class="latex" alt="$5s_k = [p^{k+1} + q^{k+1} + r^{k+1}] + p^k q + p^k r + pq^k + q^k r + pr^k + qr^k$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="494" height="20" />
</p><p>Using <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/0/2/b02056e6635cb99269c6e5d00652a0d2f1f88cd7.png" class="latex" alt="$pqr = 13$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="69" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/a/4/0a415d42dd1756342f5d4ff6d9c3b36d8c1b33ee.png" class="latex" alt="$s_{k-2} = p^{k-2} + q^{k-2} + r^{k-2}$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="203" height="19" />, we see
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/d/1cd4c5e19402ab86534562e7ea81dce9b2c64256.png" class="latex" alt="$13s_{k-2} = p^{k-1}qr + pq^{k-1}r + pqr^{k-1}$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="274" height="19" />.
</p><p>We have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/c/0/ec0045970fd7e12f2c0cbbe6acafc3457d3aa4d4.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\begin{split} 5s_k + 13s_{k-2} &amp;= s_{k+1} + (p^k q + p^k r + p^{k-1}qr) + (pq^k + pq^{k-1}r + q^k r) + (pqr^{k-1} + pr^k + qr^k) \\ &amp;= s_{k+1} + p^{k-1} (pq + pr + qr) + q^{k-1} (pq + pr + qr) + r^{k-1} (pq + pr + qr) \\ &amp;= s_{k+1} + (p^{k-1} + q^{k-1} + r^{k-1})(pq + pr + qr) \\ &amp;= 5s_k + 13s_{k-2} = s_{k+1} + 8s_{k-1}\end{split}\]" width="709" height="101" />
</p><p>Rearrange to get
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/6/5/a653b2fdf80b18ea4d77956b9f5029ac869f7195.png" class="latex" alt="$s_{k+1} = 5s_k - 8s_{k-1} + 13s_{k-2}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="221" height="16" />
</p><p>So, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/4/2d440f1d7562308404bc95161c663535f0e811bd.png" class="latex" alt="$a+ b + c = 5 -8 + 13 = \boxed{\textbf{(D) } 10}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="264" height="28" />.
</p><p>-gregwwl
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_4">Solution 4</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/a/a/faa7e4dae453f67aa78269d730f7cae70296659a.png" class="latex" alt="$r,s,t$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="38" height="15" /> be the roots of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/1/84104f35f8b36d9b6fa04a1036b7e043f57ddfb3.png" class="latex" alt="$x^3-5x^2+8x-13$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="148" height="16" />. Then:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/e/e/7eead88f3d1be6957deb7e6f28065e105046e850.png" class="latex" alt="$r^3=5r^2-8r+13$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="148" height="18" /> \\
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/e/5/3e5f5976983ff82bc5f0c9a976b06b8bcd781acd.png" class="latex" alt="$s^3=5s^2-8s+13$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="147" height="18" /> \\
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/4/8/148fac3dbb5e4884b79cc3be48b492672a665b7d.png" class="latex" alt="$t^3=5t^2-8t+13$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="141" height="18" />
</p><p>If we multiply both sides of the equation by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/d/bcdc1537798e37cfc7e1ac90e2b0d36a1e31f55a.png" class="latex" alt="$r^k$" width="15" height="15" />, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" /> is a positive integer, then that won't change the coefficients, but just the degree of the new polynomial and the other term's exponents. We can try multiplying to find <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/2/9/429749dd381e66df42eaed694b9d5d609bcd1590.png" class="latex" alt="$r^4+s^4+t^4$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="89" height="16" />, but that is just to check. So then with the above information about <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/4/b84e820e988dc3452c71b81d4e3ed189382cc1d7.png" class="latex" alt="$r^3,s^3,t^3$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="64" height="20" />, we see that:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/1/171268b45ad733c6a25de0b74262748f2b2044d6.png" class="latex" alt="$r^k=5r^{k-1}-8r^{k-2}-13r^{k-3}$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="225" height="19" />, 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/1/e/21e3487920ef6e595a51186eb783b843cc633d30.png" class="latex" alt="$s^k=5s^{k-1}-8s^{k-2}-13s^{k-3}$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="225" height="19" />,
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/9/c791fce835d86c25efc339859b91e7b3bf702b7d.png" class="latex" alt="$t^k=5t^{k-1}-8t^{k-2}-13t^{k-3}$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="217" height="19" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/e/43e3e8f291fdfc214620c0f81215635649330df6.png" class="latex" alt="$s_k=r^k+s^k+t^k$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="136" height="20" />
</p><p>Then: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/4/c946e9125dae0dd2f60bb982610f790fbd0a3d62.png" class="latex" alt="$s_k=5s_{k-1}-8s_{k-2}+13s_{k-3}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="225" height="18" />
</p><p>This means that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/9/109e36d23764256f3341a8a1004889957f184a16.png" class="latex" alt="$s_{k+1}=5s_{k}-8s_{k-1}+13s_{k-2}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="224" height="18" />, as expected. So we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/b/1/4b12cf79b626a30e1cf15b5dd2a8e282d76e12c4.png" class="latex" alt="$a=5, b=-8, c=13$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="167" height="18" />. So our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/0/f/d0f79fdbee169a7938f6741992b7f3b46c5fd818.png" class="latex" alt="$5-8+13=\boxed{\textbf{(D) } 10}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="172" height="30" />
</p><p>-IzhanAli
</p>
