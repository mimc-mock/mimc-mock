What percent of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/6/f/d6f55180c90b5e279d1cea00eee6163532dee3e8.png" class="latex" alt="$3b$" width="17" height="12" />?

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/5/3458d165faaccce0d2eb75da9dc18668c049069f.png" class="latex" alt="$a=1.5b$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="65" height="13" />, that means <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/6/1/361e8f14c461be4c9f78b06b86883a533f804ba9.png" class="latex" alt="$b=\frac{a}{1.5}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="57" height="33" />. We multiply by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> to get a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/6/f/d6f55180c90b5e279d1cea00eee6163532dee3e8.png" class="latex" alt="$3b$" width="17" height="12" /> term, yielding <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/d/d/4dd8773b090c25c4132591a64c29b1a07cd67079.png" class="latex" alt="$3b=2a$" width="59" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/6/6c6ea4756efa52a156029529aba37a305a5cb496.png" class="latex" alt="$2a$" width="18" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/0/c/70c54db490144c59cd9bcf6cd70eaa1be3189b16.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D) }200\%}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="90" height="29" /> of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Without loss of generality, let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/2/0/520953f0887a64f0d5eccb756273428e685a75df.png" class="latex" alt="$b=100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="58" height="13" />. Then, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/2/dc2f0612209d64ba5992bc217ed2b98e3dd451c9.png" class="latex" alt="$a=150$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="60" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/a/d/2ad9119f73378d8c2e42ccd0ae7a663593d9c817.png" class="latex" alt="$3b=300$" width="67" height="12" />. Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/b/5bbe99692afec8eea735e58c703673134fccee89.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{3b}{a}=\frac{300}{150}=2$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="109" height="38" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/6/f/d6f55180c90b5e279d1cea00eee6163532dee3e8.png" class="latex" alt="$3b$" width="17" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/8/b/98b70efea4cc4bc0ecea4e4e5c7b422738ee1998.png" class="latex" alt="$200\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="41" height="14" /> of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />. Hence the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/0/c/70c54db490144c59cd9bcf6cd70eaa1be3189b16.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D) }200\%}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="90" height="29" />.
</p>
