<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>We can figure out <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/2/f32625e51b01764992e4bf69015bbf88459e2b0a.png" class="latex" alt="$H = 0$" width="49" height="12" /> by noticing that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/6/a/76a2b2aaeda3bb0000156738164faab20ae9f138.png" class="latex" alt="$19!$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="21" height="13" /> will end with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> zeroes, as there are three factors of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> in its prime factorization, so there would be 3 powers of 10 meaning it will end in 3 zeros. Next, we use the fact that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/6/a/76a2b2aaeda3bb0000156738164faab20ae9f138.png" class="latex" alt="$19!$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="21" height="13" /> is a multiple of both <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/8/c6878713578626763c38433b3f4c8c2205ad0c15.png" class="latex" alt="$11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" />. Their divisibility rules (see Solution 2) tell us that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/9/d/59d613c9e7c13d4049d293e4766a6b2c1d11b248.png" class="latex" alt="$T + M \equiv 3 \;(\bmod\; 9)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="157" height="18" /> and that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/a/c/eacdb80d2fe779868f0fb3a1f7b601c4a724f412.png" class="latex" alt="$T - M \equiv 7 \;(\bmod\; 11)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="166" height="18" />. By guess and checking, we see that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/9/19922faf131a90c02802e58bf9616c5719a872ca.png" class="latex" alt="$T = 4, M = 8$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="107" height="16" /> is a valid solution. Therefore the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/d/91dede09e6dd3f73d35c5d71a66dabdd9021a200.png" class="latex" alt="$4 + 8 + 0 = \boxed{\textbf{(C) }12}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="161" height="28" />.
</p>
