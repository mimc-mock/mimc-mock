<p>A cell phone plan costs <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/3/f8366cd6196dd8a9da6d38a3e9eafb109e99d53e.png" class="latex" alt="$20$" width="17" height="12" /> dollars each month, plus <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> cents per text message sent, plus <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> cents for each minute used over <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/1/651ba418a810d971dbc8a326d49a71fe1541fae0.png" class="latex" alt="$30$" width="17" height="12" /> hours. In January Michelle sent <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> text messages and talked for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/d/4/9d450b3436120622e61813c7577d498fa1cea688.png" class="latex" alt="$30.5$" width="31" height="12" /> hours. How much did she have to pay?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/7/e/97e96960fb653ed3886300ec3499ceb9a6791143.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 24.00 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 24.50 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 25.50 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 28.00 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 30.00$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="510" height="17" />
</p><p><br />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The base price of Michelle's cell phone plan is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/3/f8366cd6196dd8a9da6d38a3e9eafb109e99d53e.png" class="latex" alt="$20$" width="17" height="12" /> dollars. 
If she sent <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> text messages and it costs <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> cents per text, then she must have spent <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/9/a39b14023c98c943554d150561aacc188c4ce26d.png" class="latex" alt="$500$" width="26" height="12" /> cents for texting, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> dollars. She talked for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/d/4/9d450b3436120622e61813c7577d498fa1cea688.png" class="latex" alt="$30.5$" width="31" height="12" /> hours, but <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/a/7/da7f5eae1323508084eaca68584b5fb7d3f1cf46.png" class="latex" alt="$30.5-30$" width="72" height="12" /> will give us the amount of time exceeded past 30 hours.
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/1/b/71b1c456b579db645dea1a7fdf7f42de05502526.png" class="latex" alt="$30.5-30=.5$" width="110" height="12" /> hours <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/3/d/03dadce9d695ab7426a2ee299c593bd049e5ce4d.png" class="latex" alt="$=30$" width="36" height="12" /> minutes.
Since the price for phone calls is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> cents per minute, the additional amount Michelle has to pay for phone calls is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cdce4dc0bf7ea9619826c672052f1ca57188564.png" class="latex" alt="$30*10=300$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="104" height="13" /> cents, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> dollars.
Adding <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/a/e/4aec2a83cd1d643979c0ef7e1ddfe7e94d77f85e.png" class="latex" alt="$20+5+3$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="80" height="14" /> dollars <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/f/bcfef9e4b248223bde2b640164f9237dae363054.png" class="latex" alt="$=28$" width="36" height="12" /> dollars <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/5/41552ceaf63ea07e1855f7d0f309e2d335b11a11.png" class="latex" alt="$=\boxed{\textbf{D}}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="45" height="23" />.
</p>
