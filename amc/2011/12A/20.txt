<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/9/28969a57466b0a1047fca37478da706c1cb8cced.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x)=ax^2+bx+c$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="157" height="19" />, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> are integers. Suppose that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/9/d/89d358367015e5ceeb10a72c78d0b627d586f3ac.png" class="latex" alt="$f(1)=0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="67" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/b/10bf8164e8f9f03ab077a50bac52fd90c77d0177.png" class="latex" alt="$50&lt;f(7)&lt;60$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="118" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/9/aa946baaeee00d574f7641e6eba3e35d08e32a2b.png" class="latex" alt="$70&lt;f(8)&lt;80$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="118" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/5/b/75b043e9b7f56cfe4ddedd2fb75ecda37df5ca25.png" class="latex" alt="$5000k&lt;f(100)&lt;5000(k+1)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="237" height="18" /> for some integer <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" />. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/8/908080b56fc36e805bfef13f09a77a76e2624126.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 1 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 2 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 3 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 4 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 5$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="348" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>From <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/d/82dd6f474fd8d40d5108934acc4ce4f98bfd3c17.png" class="latex" alt="$f(1) = 0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="67" height="18" />, we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/5/2d501f3412e3af870e232678a6b686d3cf311fc8.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+c = 0$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="102" height="14" />.
</p><p>From the first inequality, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/f/3/cf3250346ba34909d2c819c85853ba105d73896f.png" class="latex" alt="$50 &lt; 49a+7b+c &lt; 60$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="181" height="14" />. Subtracting <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/5/2d501f3412e3af870e232678a6b686d3cf311fc8.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+c = 0$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="102" height="14" /> from this gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/4/2f4dfb9c60d1614703c61c64e49a3cfe957688b3.png" class="latex" alt="$50 &lt; 48a+6b &lt; 60$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="151" height="14" />, and thus <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/2/ba26597d03e7102253e8c9162f1003777e0b8058.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{25}{3} &lt; 8a+b &lt; 10$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="137" height="37" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/b/d5b11d8228f2dba24f6725244c149411fc2176c4.png" class="latex" alt="$8a+b$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="49" height="14" /> must be an integer, it follows that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/e/e0e347d037f2a0ca0eff8c156daf041fd5732ba3.png" class="latex" alt="$8a+b = 9$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="81" height="14" />.
</p><p>Similarly, from the second inequality, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/c/b/ecb63a4c9960d460e4fb0793cb80fb08d1ca1cd7.png" class="latex" alt="$70 &lt; 64a+8b+c &lt; 80$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="181" height="14" />. Again subtracting <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/5/2d501f3412e3af870e232678a6b686d3cf311fc8.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+c = 0$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="102" height="14" /> from this gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/d/b/fdb9ca593bd1a68bd00948050dc8a80722ada915.png" class="latex" alt="$70 &lt; 63a+7b &lt; 80$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="151" height="14" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/4/0/040995a5227d07397a04f47b170b506be66f1606.png" class="latex" alt="$10 &lt; 9a+b &lt; \frac{80}{7}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="136" height="37" />. It follows from this that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/d/a3dbf6901bf76d1a84a4da02454a58a0ff35f1b9.png" class="latex" alt="$9a+b = 11$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="90" height="14" />.
</p><p>We now have a system of three equations: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/5/2d501f3412e3af870e232678a6b686d3cf311fc8.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+c = 0$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="102" height="14" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/e/e0e347d037f2a0ca0eff8c156daf041fd5732ba3.png" class="latex" alt="$8a+b = 9$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="81" height="14" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/d/a3dbf6901bf76d1a84a4da02454a58a0ff35f1b9.png" class="latex" alt="$9a+b = 11$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="90" height="14" />. Solving gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/c/2/ac219f63c5d8f2b973c2ceb9f5ef4f2438f8359a.png" class="latex" alt="$(a, b, c) = (2, -7, 5)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="150" height="18" /> and from this we find that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/1/171b4617385c9507af4a8f99059520a89fd92920.png" class="latex" alt="$f(100) = 2(100)^2-7(100)+5 = 19305$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="307" height="19" />
</p><p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/5/b659f809b3db34c7dcf22a5652af65e3b120cb54.png" class="latex" alt="$15000 &lt; 19305 &lt; 20000 \to 5000(3) &lt; 19305 &lt; 5000(4)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="424" height="18" />, we find that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/3/7a38f1ad2e2af4706a93a42722d4eb793d6ca93b.png" class="latex" alt="$k = 3 \rightarrow \boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 3}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="126" height="28" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/6/c96dd6ec1dc4ad7520fbdc78fcdbec9edd068d0c.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="34" height="18" /> is some non-monic quadratic with a root at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/c/5/4c52e02a35db152495396584bfeae1d0599cd753.png" class="latex" alt="$x=1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="42" height="12" />. Knowing this, we'll forget their silly <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> and instead write it as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/d/b1d8853574f295a837fba7180ccadc4ad5d95103.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x)=p(x-1)(x-r)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="178" height="18" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/a/7faa9f340af53616f824e108f668c09560547b24.png" class="latex" alt="$f(7)=6p(7-r)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="129" height="18" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/6/8/e68c56082db3d4756941a61d1f4f08fe46114108.png" class="latex" alt="$f(7)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="32" height="18" /> is a multiple of 6. They say <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/6/8/e68c56082db3d4756941a61d1f4f08fe46114108.png" class="latex" alt="$f(7)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="32" height="18" /> is between 50 and 60, exclusive. Notice that the only multiple of 6 in that range is 54. Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/4/a/74a16acb1676659d299afc098cfeadd82d5cfa69.png" class="latex" alt="$f(7)=6p(7-r)=54$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="173" height="18" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/0/990e97f446060e99aeab3cf175f07d1613182e29.png" class="latex" alt="$f(8)=7p(8-r)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="129" height="18" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/e/d/9ed1d18e89d8d63ccd2c5b2739aa37aea1f85f3b.png" class="latex" alt="$f(8)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="32" height="18" /> is a multiple of 7. They say <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/e/d/9ed1d18e89d8d63ccd2c5b2739aa37aea1f85f3b.png" class="latex" alt="$f(8)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="32" height="18" /> is between 70 and 80, exclusive. Notice that the only multiple of 7 in that range is 77. Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/b/9/6b922e80f9b2dad676cb108bd4ad94d10a45ad0f.png" class="latex" alt="$f(8)=7p(8-r)=77$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="173" height="18" />.
</p><p>Now, we solve a system of equations in two variables. 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/a/d/eadd50c2258c9ecec8ea3571bcb7d693b4cb652c.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} 6p(7-r)&amp;=54 \\ 7p(8-r)&amp;=77 \\ \\ p(7-r)&amp;=9 \\ p(8-r)&amp;=11 \\ \\ 7p-pr&amp;=9 \\ 8p-pr&amp;=11 \\ \\ (8p-pr)-(7p-pr)&amp;=11-9 \\ \\ p&amp;=2 \\ \\ 2(7-r)&amp;=9 \\ \\ r&amp;=2.5 \end{align*}" width="239" height="387" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/b/6/2b618888e60350f76a013db1fb0695f98192529e.png" class="latex" alt="$f(100)=2(100-1)(100-2.5)=19305 \implies k=3 \implies \boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 3}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="512" height="28" />
</p>
