<p>A small bottle of shampoo can hold <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/7/8c7f6c79f2148f0cec592c9517b40ee1b82ef22e.png" class="latex" alt="$35$" width="17" height="12" /> milliliters of shampoo, whereas a large bottle can hold <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/9/a39b14023c98c943554d150561aacc188c4ce26d.png" class="latex" alt="$500$" width="26" height="12" /> milliliters of shampoo. Jasmine wants to buy the minimum number of small bottles necessary to completely fill a large bottle. How many bottles must she buy?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/f/f5fef8175ccc76d26ec248e544f2bf220695f74f.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 11 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 12 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 13 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 14 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 15$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>To find how many small bottles we need, we can simply divide <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/9/a39b14023c98c943554d150561aacc188c4ce26d.png" class="latex" alt="$500$" width="26" height="12" /> by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/7/8c7f6c79f2148f0cec592c9517b40ee1b82ef22e.png" class="latex" alt="$35$" width="17" height="12" />. This simplifies to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/6/3/36326c3c2b3953db5ce1eb2374d34bbb82353a73.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{100}{7}=14 \frac{2}{7}.$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="90" height="37" /> Since the answer must be an integer greater than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/0/820a20de2847dfdc1bc5cffc8d904933150032bd.png" class="latex" alt="$14$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="12" />, we have to round up to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/1/9/f1965fae079a9ba2c0726c307070c2355dfcb213.png" class="latex" alt="$15$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> bottles, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/7/1/3711217e5f5d573cdedc2efeb489063c5d6ed51e.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{E}}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="24" height="23" />
</p>
