<p>Josanna's test scores to date are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/e/8/2e8e748de674bda3176ac72ea05fb2864b3cd76a.png" class="latex" alt="$90, 80, 70, 60,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="101" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/e/7fea07b9bf739f8e420081f31186264b91ced2ce.png" class="latex" alt="$85$" width="17" height="12" />. Her goal is to raise here test average at least <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> points with her next test. What is the minimum test score she would need to accomplish this goal?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/6/4/764efb9434870a93853e77e70ff75e6f02e62877.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 80 \qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 82 \qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 85 \qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 90 \qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 95$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p><p><br />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The average of her current scores is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/f/7af4b3f8897671e2879b10badf471e5deec8952e.png" class="latex" alt="$77$" width="18" height="12" />. To raise it <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> points, she needs an average of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/a/fea5fcc6d66685ee21f722efcd228eb44d38f262.png" class="latex" alt="$80$" width="17" height="12" />, and so after her <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" /> tests, a sum of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/0/1/5019ae5ab5a63c85111154e82ebcc78eedcdbe86.png" class="latex" alt="$480$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />. Her current sum is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/2/8/72864d6940ce0fce24675857208b1fa412393962.png" class="latex" alt="$385$" width="26" height="12" />, so she needs a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/8/8/588b6fa27a230c9ec4a97269ed19d306ce33b15c.png" class="latex" alt="$480 - 385 = \boxed{\mathrm{(E) \ } 95}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="163" height="29" />
</p>
