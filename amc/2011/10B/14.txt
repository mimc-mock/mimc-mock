<p>A rectangular parking lot has a diagonal of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/6/6/66644fd9f411299c3c1eabf151b5f601c9ef6c21.png" class="latex" alt="$25$" width="17" height="12" /> meters and an area of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/1/c7116bbc884d2cf49e25754864ae00d9c42d2d90.png" class="latex" alt="$168$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> square meters. In meters, what is the perimeter of the parking lot?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/1/8111bf541367c4f1a5923fe805f25927e8357ae1.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 52 \qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 58 \qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 62 \qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 68 \qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 70$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Let the sides of the rectangular parking lot be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />. Then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/c/c/2cce896956ee8292c994e58ab93f4463415187b0.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2 + b^2 = 625$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="105" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/9/6192e0d56c4206438618558a7f1a53f88f2ba7d2.png" class="latex" alt="$ab = 168$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="68" height="13" />. Add the two equations together, then factor.
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/9/1/b91a86f322ed0bf092a7c2c490bc692f91b79b7f.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} a^2 + 2ab + b^2 &amp;= 625 + 168 \times 2\\ (a + b)^2 &amp;= 961\\ a + b &amp;= 31 \end{align*}" width="234" height="69" />
The perimeter of a rectangle is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/d/2ddcaf5c848f1e7b14586dd7ce74225bc387c510.png" class="latex" alt="$2 (a + b) = 2 (31) = \boxed{\textbf{(C)} 62}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="211" height="28" />
</p>
