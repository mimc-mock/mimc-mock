<p>Josanna's test scores to date are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/e/8/2e8e748de674bda3176ac72ea05fb2864b3cd76a.png" class="latex" alt="$90, 80, 70, 60,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="101" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/a/f3a66ed6edf9c931ffceb20bd11cd1acdddc94ba.png" class="latex" alt="$85.$" width="21" height="12" /> Her goal is to raise her test average at least <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> points with her next test. What is the minimum test score she would need to accomplish this goal?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/d/d/1ddcb04e7e637d874013f2413037c10e71f59dc1.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 80 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 82 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 85 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 90 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 95$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="394" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Take the average of her current test scores, which is
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/1/5/c1521cab2e19aee7a6ad80fb78c54c4757fb4bf8.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{90+80+70+60+85}{5} = \frac{385}{5} = 77\]" width="279" height="37" />
</p><p>This means that she wants her test average after the sixth test to be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/4/2/64208b8141947233195b9c41cedc8ca7a25317b7.png" class="latex" alt="$80.$" width="21" height="12" /> Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> be the score that Josanna receives on her sixth test. Thus, our equation is
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/6/8c6e1f7450052953bc25a0006ad917b7eb71b0c8.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{90+80+70+60+85+x}{6} = 80\]" width="256" height="37" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/9/1/8916fc02a40bbe5cdfa286489582de6ede8e4e5f.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[385+x = 480\]" width="110" height="14" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/0/c/50c77746db4141356460dd69fbc15a713e186852.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[x = \boxed{95 \textbf{(E)}}\]" width="91" height="28" />
</p>
