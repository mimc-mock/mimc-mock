<p>In the expression <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/7/7/477f10f085ee0f463da5dd2d44b66b2f72a15ace.png" class="latex" alt="$c\cdot a^b-d$" width="69" height="15" />, the values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />, although not necessarily in that order. What is the maximum possible value of the result?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/e/d/4ed5e1ad462e14dc2557d7dc6ee4be5ab98e2c37.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)\ }5\qquad\mathrm{(B)\ }6\qquad\mathrm{(C)\ }8\qquad\mathrm{(D)\ }9\qquad\mathrm{(E)\ }10$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="351" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>If <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/9/c/69cefa6855d2216036df094012f7ad2a7e4e204f.png" class="latex" alt="$a=0$" width="42" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/8/0/a80d7a3ca845e6cae14b9db710b64331219ec3f7.png" class="latex" alt="$c=0$" width="40" height="12" />, the expression evaluates to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/4/3/c435877b14645e5045d3dd81fba010195c73d44a.png" class="latex" alt="$-d&lt;0$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="56" height="13" />. <br />
If <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/4/fc43f0ea59e862d7db01586894e9eea730d427b7.png" class="latex" alt="$b=0$" width="40" height="12" />, the expression evaluates to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/8/b/d8b8a1386c9fcb74bd032b4cc8f1f35578bb5f02.png" class="latex" alt="$c-d\leq 2$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="72" height="15" />. <br />
Case <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/1/791662898215b138613f4dada0a3ce54193d735f.png" class="latex" alt="$d=0$" width="42" height="12" /> remains.
In that case, we want to maximize <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/1/1c164025588ec9b3df412973bb90660c8e389209.png" class="latex" alt="$c\cdot a^b$" width="36" height="15" /> where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/6/8/168b479e89f7778932d6890c5577201a8d3a4982.png" class="latex" alt="$\{a,b,c\}=\{1,2,3\}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="144" height="18" />. Trying out the six possibilities we get that the greatest is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/f/b1f3887a8ee7139c71650d413e78bb839c895b71.png" class="latex" alt="$(a,b,c)=(3,2,1)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="135" height="18" />, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/4/b/44bd7126d900fbdd484755ff470e98694fcb4344.png" class="latex" alt="$c\cdot a^b=1\cdot 3^2=\boxed{\mathrm{(D)}\ 9}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="179" height="29" />.
</p>
