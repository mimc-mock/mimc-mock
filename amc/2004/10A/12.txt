<p>Henry's Hamburger Haven offers its hamburgers with the following condiments: ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, tomato, lettuce, pickles, cheese, and onions.  A customer can choose one, two, or three meat patties and any collection of condiments.  How many different kinds of hamburgers can be ordered?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/1/8/c18cb0659a09fa0f17aaf6df27c24ff47f25676c.png" class="latex" alt="$\text{(A) \ } 24 \qquad \text{(B) \ } 256 \qquad \text{(C) \ } 768 \qquad \text{(D) \ } 40,320 \qquad \text{(E) \ } 120,960$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="501" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>For each condiment, a customer may either choose to order it or not.  There are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> total condiments to choose from.  Therefore, there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/5/e/a5efb3bc415def343ddc4f461d5d608c5dd6c548.png" class="latex" alt="$2^8=256$" width="67" height="15" /> ways to order the condiments. There are also <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> choices for the meat, making a total of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/8/7a8c12c4a22b74b2b62d3c2264d071de4de8ba52.png" class="latex" alt="$256\times3=768$" width="109" height="12" /> possible hamburgers. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/b/e/fbefa1baa3ff77282215959679a764be4558bbb0.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\mathrm{(C)}\ 768}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="71" height="29" />
</p>
