<p>The <a href="/wiki/index.php/Polynomial" title="Polynomial">polynomial</a> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/c/7/ec79eb0fd4cdd168927d761290bff83ff5405780.png" class="latex" alt="$x^3 - 2004 x^2 + mx + n$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="175" height="16" /> has <a href="/wiki/index.php/Integer" title="Integer">integer</a> coefficients and three distinct positive zeros. Exactly one of these is an integer, and it is the sum of the other two.  How many values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> are possible?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/8/6/98625300a750996f07fd03d156018e7f09300e27.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ 250,\!000 \qquad\mathrm{(B)}\ 250,\!250 \qquad\mathrm{(C)}\ 250,\!500 \qquad\mathrm{(D)}\ 250,\!750 \qquad\mathrm{(E)}\ 251,\!000$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="594" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let the roots be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/7/6/676589d5a26f8142a0043a695e8cdc964c93f22c.png" class="latex" alt="$r,s,r + s$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="71" height="14" />, and let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/0/9901309ec73ff179b53ef6392e3112dfaaee01e3.png" class="latex" alt="$t = rs$" width="47" height="11" />. Then
</p>
<center><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/a/9aa6b4356e1b571b8c6b0273d4a5ee73ddd93031.png" class="latex" alt="$(x - r)(x - s)(x - (r + s))$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="209" height="18" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/8/f/18f8cb41503afe71a27d8834fd83f6ee33165a95.png" class="latex" alt="$= x^3 - (r + s + r + s) x^2 + (rs + r(r + s) + s(r + s))x - rs(r + s) = 0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="551" height="19" /></center>
<p>and by matching coefficients, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/0/1/10147b34725735675bfee65a376870ff33e49fd7.png" class="latex" alt="$2(r + s) = 2004 \Longrightarrow r + s = 1002$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="265" height="20" />. Then our polynomial looks like 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/b/b/fbb4aa9a18c07d29ec97438dfb06b2f218c49361.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[x^3 - 2004x^2 + (t + 1002^2)x - 1002t = 0\]" width="311" height="19" />
and we need the number of possible products <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/f/aafca7ef8318be6b23bdff9cd67d020839125b7b.png" class="latex" alt="$t = rs = r(1002 - r)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="161" height="18" />. Because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/3/543602db2bcb36cd278f9132792528ef86d5191b.png" class="latex" alt="$m=t+1002^2$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="111" height="16" /> is an integer, we also note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/d/e0d2bf360290fd61d1c1557e763f2622363b3d35.png" class="latex" alt="$t$" width="6" height="11" /> must be an integer. 
</p><p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/2/f/22ff7db3e755a8164f2344e0d3d84ea3a0fe93b3.png" class="latex" alt="$r &gt; 0$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="41" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/9/6/d968aec052b8a8f2736faf02fd8fb120489b7cff.png" class="latex" alt="$t &gt; 0$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="39" height="13" />, it follows that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/7/9/37973f4b2d08d2fc5a2bdd4691b5217eb3336561.png" class="latex" alt="$0 &lt; t = r(1002-r) &lt; 501^2 = 251001$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="290" height="19" />, with the endpoints not achievable because the roots must be distinct and positive. Because neither <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> nor <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/e/9/ae9739d3dba25ae058d11d5f49f4fa6a5100c8c5.png" class="latex" alt="$1002-r$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="66" height="13" /> can be an integer, there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/2/1c2dbcb78cd3304be3565ce90b05d63b76f87a5d.png" class="latex" alt="$251,000 - 500 = \boxed{\textbf{(C) } 250,500}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="245" height="28" /> possible values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/0/3201d265413f4dadccf88f61e80fd0e1b4e13f3e.png" class="latex" alt="$n = -1002t$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="91" height="13" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Letting the roots be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f37bba504894945c07a32f5496d74299a37aa51c.png" class="latex" alt="$s$" width="8" height="8" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/d/e0d2bf360290fd61d1c1557e763f2622363b3d35.png" class="latex" alt="$t$" width="6" height="11" />, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/9/c/99c2c80f2b2f8026db5dded79196e8b27ad0b58e.png" class="latex" alt="$t = r+s$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="69" height="13" />, we see that by Vieta's Formula's, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/c/9/0c98691102f4a6a513f3f7aa47f30c3293c6d67e.png" class="latex" alt="$2004 = r+s+t = t + t = 2t$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="227" height="14" />, and so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/1/2d1c03f2bd028cf11ad7ef50d8025edb79e1f12e.png" class="latex" alt="$t = 1002$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="66" height="13" />. Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/5/c958cab4df99fef371df0937428b055ec8587dff.png" class="latex" alt="$x-1002$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="68" height="13" /> is a factor of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/9/ba9a22e40cb03f955524dbac45dc97540f4e0ea9.png" class="latex" alt="$x^3 - 2004x^2 + mx + n$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="175" height="16" />. Letting <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/d/3/2d348bde3e15456e71734dc2c56fc7425c95927f.png" class="latex" alt="$x = 0$" width="43" height="12" /> gives that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/e/82e2a5d2518cc6fa87f952139f18c1a264c239c2.png" class="latex" alt="$1002 \mid n$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="62" height="18" /> because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/b/2/2b2f98a371d161ccfc3f8c235f64e6eb7ff41fb3.png" class="latex" alt="$x - 1002 \mid x^3 - 2004x^2 + mx + n$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="259" height="19" />. Letting <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/7/a37b7071ec0f87a7d51318e798725d5d065cbc3e.png" class="latex" alt="$n = 1002a$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="80" height="13" /> and noting that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/5/055d90246b721383e85d5939b4203b4ea79f06a4.png" class="latex" alt="$x^3 - 2004x^2 + mx + n = (x-1002)(x^2 - bx + a)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="386" height="19" /> for some <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />, we see that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" /> is the sum of the roots of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/6/9667d0ae9aac7eebb09eb7b94d1b9ce3dd443ea7.png" class="latex" alt="$x^2 - bx + a$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="90" height="16" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f37bba504894945c07a32f5496d74299a37aa51c.png" class="latex" alt="$s$" width="8" height="8" />, and so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/5/3/853433d5ae7e51b5efcc5b9a9e012889b20bb789.png" class="latex" alt="$b = 1002$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="67" height="13" />. Now, we have that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/4/f3423c181627885c5ab79b4f9bba6b0ee8c195d4.png" class="latex" alt="$x^2 - 1002x + a$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="118" height="16" /> has roots <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f37bba504894945c07a32f5496d74299a37aa51c.png" class="latex" alt="$s$" width="8" height="8" />, and we wish to find the number of possible values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />. By the quadratic formula, we see that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/0/910c7c622afd81998d260e3e8d4fe2b7fa178550.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{1002 \pm \sqrt{1002^2 - 4a}}{2} = 501 \pm \sqrt{501^2 - a}\]" width="316" height="40" /> are the two values of noninteger positive real numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/7/f37bba504894945c07a32f5496d74299a37aa51c.png" class="latex" alt="$s$" width="8" height="8" />, neither of which is equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/3/a/63a8720f439e8e8887a0746430412c323e81e13c.png" class="latex" alt="$1002$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />. This information gives us that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/7/f470e0b7901baa5d85b4adad94aef54167c6a8d8.png" class="latex" alt="$0 &lt; 501^2 - a &lt; 501^2$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="157" height="15" />, and so since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/3/833dbea31aa0e6b7d6cf4b4d803e94997505afc9.png" class="latex" alt="$501^2 - a$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="66" height="15" /> is evidently not a square, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/8/1c87478f40b885ecc0843797566b2a04b8116141.png" class="latex" alt="$501^2 - 1 - 500 = 251,001 - 500 - 1 = \boxed{\textbf{(C) } 250,500}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="416" height="28" /> possible values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/7/a37b7071ec0f87a7d51318e798725d5d065cbc3e.png" class="latex" alt="$n = 1002a$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="80" height="13" />.
</p>
