<p>Minneapolis-St. Paul International Airport is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> miles southwest of downtown St. Paul and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> miles southeast of downtown Minneapolis. Which of the following is closest to the number of miles between downtown St. Paul and downtown Minneapolis? 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/c/e/3ce424871a76dae5e655a2cd428feede650fd5e5.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)\ }13\qquad\mathrm{(B)\ }14\qquad\mathrm{(C)\ }15\qquad\mathrm{(D)\ }16\qquad\mathrm{(E)\ }17$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="388" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The directions "southwest" and "southeast" are orthogonal. Thus the described situation is a right triangle with legs <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/8455f3b5cb3b4880b8c9d782a5c1f0334db819eb.png" class="latex" alt="$8$" width="8" height="12" /> miles and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/6/fc606f7f1e530731ab4f1cc364c01dc64a4455ee.png" class="latex" alt="$10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> miles long. The hypotenuse length is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55589a9bae7c25a81b880987cc5cfca2626a9d8.png" class="latex" alt="$\sqrt{8^2 + 10^2}\approx12.8$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="135" height="18" />, and thus the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/1/f918c8ea59ae4ffbe6ae2b3f4a9bfeaf32869bf1.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\mathrm{(A)}\ 13}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="63" height="29" />.
</p><p>Without a calculator one can note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/5/5/955f1f631bc08125a83b79959fa67c8272a8b30b.png" class="latex" alt="$8^2+10^2=164&lt;169=13^2\Rightarrow\boxed{\mathrm{(A)}\ 13}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="308" height="29" />.
</p>
