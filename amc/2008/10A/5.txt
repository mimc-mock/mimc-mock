<p>Which of the following is equal to the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Product" class="mw-redirect" title="Product">product</a>
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/d/61d211dd54e4b681761aa2c6c2fea286d329138e.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\frac{8}{4}\cdot\frac{12}{8}\cdot\frac{16}{12}\cdot\cdots\cdot\frac{4n+4}{4n}\cdot\cdots\cdot\frac{2008}{2004}?\]" width="280" height="38" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/f/35fc5058c41c8cf6f88138585b64bcdad5dbd730.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 251\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 502\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 1004\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 2008\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 4016$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="466" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/4/d4426435ea6f107a3943befa7bd7d38beeefb92c.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac {8}{4}\cdot\frac {12}{8}\cdot\frac {16}{12}\cdots\frac {4n + 4}{4n}\cdots\frac {2008}{2004} = \frac {1}{4}\cdot\left(\frac {8}{8}\cdot\frac {12}{12}\cdots\frac {4n}{4n}\cdots\frac {2004}{2004}\right)\cdot 2008 = \frac{2008}{4} =$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="610" height="43" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/8/8c80fc4dae7384417061880b9e40261b8728adfc.png" class="latex" alt="$502 \Rightarrow B$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="69" height="13" />. 
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Notice that everything cancels out except for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/b/c/abc1744a459ad135efd3fd45fca7285cae133717.png" class="latex" alt="$2008$" width="35" height="12" /> in the numerator and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> in the denominator.
</p><p>Thus, the product is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/d/4fdd48641201f7d05a88da4e33a2bd6f9315a6ab.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2008}{4}=502$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="91" height="38" />, and the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/1/b7193f66028e13f331d53ff5e6942c4b05587465.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(B)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="27" height="17" />. 
</p>
