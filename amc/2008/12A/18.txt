<p>Triangle <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/2/a/e2a559986ed5a0ffc5654bd367c29dfc92913c36.png" class="latex" alt="$ABC$" width="42" height="13" />, with sides of length <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/a/e0a0db32027a732ac57d37ef2ae9bb150f65b108.png" class="latex" alt="$7$" width="9" height="12" />, has one <a href="/wiki/index.php/Vertex" title="Vertex">vertex</a> on the positive <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />-axis, one on the positive <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" />-axis, and one on the positive <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/3/b13f21416d84e13708696f34dea81026cda583c9.png" class="latex" alt="$z$" width="8" height="8" />-axis. Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/d/51da37d984564162c87710ca27bea422f657fb73.png" class="latex" alt="$O$" width="13" height="12" /> be the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Origin" title="Origin">origin</a>. What is the volume of <a href="/wiki/index.php/Tetrahedron" title="Tetrahedron">tetrahedron</a> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/9/1/b91381b367f9753ded66ab981c7451f2cb117578.png" class="latex" alt="$OABC$" width="56" height="13" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/9/9a94bcd981db19acc94bb9f2886ff3adf2cd330b.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ \sqrt{85}\qquad\mathrm{(B)}\ \sqrt{90}\qquad\mathrm{(C)}\ \sqrt{95}\qquad\mathrm{(D)}\ 10\qquad\mathrm{(E)}\ \sqrt{105}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="458" height="21" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<center><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/4/8/b48d9f2414f67d68d2e53886e0726e31626cd919.png" class="latexcenter" alt="[asy] defaultpen(fontsize(8)); draw((0,10)--(0,0)--(8,0));draw((-3,-4)--(0,0));draw((0,10)--(-3,-4)--(8,0)--cycle); label(&quot;A&quot;,(8,0),(1,0));label(&quot;B&quot;,(0,10),(0,1));label(&quot;C&quot;,(-3,-4),(-1,-1));label(&quot;O&quot;,(0,0),(1,1)); label(&quot;$a$&quot;,(4,0),(0,1));label(&quot;$b$&quot;,(0,5),(1,0));label(&quot;$c$&quot;,(-1.5,-2),(1,0)); label(&quot;$5$&quot;,(4,5),(1,1));label(&quot;$6$&quot;,(-1.5,3),(-1,0));label(&quot;$7$&quot;,(2.5,-2),(1,-1)); [/asy]" width="535" height="668" /></center>
<p>Without loss of generality, let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> be on the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> axis, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" /> be on the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" /> axis, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/3/c3355896da590fc491a10150a50416687626d7cc.png" class="latex" alt="$C$" width="14" height="12" /> be on the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/3/b13f21416d84e13708696f34dea81026cda583c9.png" class="latex" alt="$z$" width="8" height="8" /> axis, and let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/8/6/686d7ce89cc99c4c59043d8a39f04a350114464a.png" class="latex" alt="$AB, BC, CA$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="100" height="16" /> have respective lengths of 5, 6, and 7.  Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/5/b/a5b29b358e825defa3e11b7d903d43ec31e5909a.png" class="latex" alt="$a,b,c$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="41" height="16" /> denote the lengths of segments <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/3/6/f36c95028dc0dbc04be37ec2943f5be36053474b.png" class="latex" alt="$OA,OB,OC,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="105" height="16" /> respectively.  Then by the <a href="/wiki/index.php/Pythagorean_Theorem" title="Pythagorean Theorem">Pythagorean Theorem</a>,
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/6/8/6683df3a834a1d25b33dec013415df18e90564c0.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} a^2+b^2 &amp;=5^2 , \\  b^2+c^2&amp;=6^2, \\ c^2+a^2 &amp;=7^2 , \end{align*}" width="99" height="74" />
so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/9/289414b63621cafb8e5819cb21676550cda16ed4.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2 = (5^2+7^2-6^2)/2 = 19$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="209" height="19" />; similarly, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/c/e/ace0c1c2faa4c39b9f12b30c3db967a959c10dd7.png" class="latex" alt="$b^2 = 6$" width="48" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/3/5/e35dac7e2653673e7f893455c4ff01f1167ba995.png" class="latex" alt="$c^2 = 30$" width="57" height="15" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/a/b7a762ac8b4e23ffdd778ad6d5a8b97df8c7ce01.png" class="latex" alt="$OA$" width="28" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/6/0964c9cf406ef8e5ec8beaa6a92125f2d8f90460.png" class="latex" alt="$OB$" width="28" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/f/20f8f2f1a3ddc6b42fae25a0637e85c747875180.png" class="latex" alt="$OC$" width="28" height="12" /> are mutually perpendicular, the tetrahedron's volume is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/4/b74807ac0ee77d6e400564a0e5622c8fa9371d61.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[abc/6\]" width="42" height="18" /> because we can consider the tetrahedron to be a right triangular pyramid. 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/a/4/fa42628f26cbd85dad184ed745d0a3699e3afaa9.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[abc/6 = \sqrt{a^2b^2c^2}/6 = \frac{\sqrt{19 \cdot 6 \cdot 30}}{6} = \sqrt{95},\]" width="325" height="41" />
which is answer choice <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/b/82b317654f901a9c8f56585721a617396d3b050c.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\text{C}}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="23" height="23" />.
</p>
