<p>Heather compares the price of a new computer at two different stores. Store <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> offers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/a/87a8787ca8fcdf64f42e938d20fa64f63871646d.png" class="latex" alt="$15\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> off the sticker price followed by a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/4/ee458a47291ac8c044b4cd9dd5e7f39d442a3b10.png" class="latex" alt="$$90$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="26" height="14" /> rebate, and store <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" /> offers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/6/96633b73a86b9d9110e1ff824fce08def96ca296.png" class="latex" alt="$25\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> off the same sticker price with no rebate. Heather saves <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/b/a/8ba70f61b1c146935b89c7ae17eb6cd4ca444773.png" class="latex" alt="$$15$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="26" height="14" /> by buying the computer at store <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> instead of store <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" />. What is the sticker price of the computer, in dollars?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/7/417ac0932e78d7ef252b57b18a81fb0508b0af46.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ 750\qquad\mathrm{(B)}\ 900\qquad\mathrm{(C)}\ 1000\qquad\mathrm{(D)}\ 1050\qquad\mathrm{(E)}\ 1500$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="460" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h3>
<p>Let the sticker price be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />.
</p><p>The price of the computer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/f/6/ef6d099d5f69a4de87f46ec265265dff4259e64b.png" class="latex" alt="$0.85x-90$" width="82" height="12" /> at store <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/d/7/cd78b4aaa258e3771ae655efb1776b825fd54d2e.png" class="latex" alt="$0.75x$" width="42" height="12" /> at store <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" />.
</p><p>Heather saves <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/b/a/8ba70f61b1c146935b89c7ae17eb6cd4ca444773.png" class="latex" alt="$$15$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="26" height="14" /> at store <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/8/2085a2ff478c61edba8d43b2dc884524e275998b.png" class="latex" alt="$0.85x-90+15=0.75x$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="190" height="14" />.
</p><p>Solving, we find <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/4/824c0e8cd623aa15f8d266b0ed75280d1b5a113d.png" class="latex" alt="$x=750$" width="61" height="12" />, and thus the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/a/2/ca2f4283cfd441270cb90bfd2cf89a533f3dde81.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="26" height="18" />.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h3>
<p>The <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/e/f/2ef0db19f3de071176640212bf3a8c1351e4cf1d.png" class="latex" alt="$$ 90$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="26" height="14" /> at store <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/3/c336dbe8a9fdc3c818b891474db51892d69af1ef.png" class="latex" alt="$$ 15$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="26" height="14" /> greater than the additional <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/3/6/236fec243316957e078fcd0819979ff7db258a9c.png" class="latex" alt="$25\%-15\% = 10\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="145" height="14" /> off at store <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/5/ff5fb3d775862e2123b007eb4373ff6cc1a34d4e.png" class="latex" alt="$B$" width="14" height="12" />.
</p><p>Thus the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/b/5/2b56bd637cb2c936d539be1b5000a64a898af389.png" class="latex" alt="$10\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" /> off is equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/e/f/2ef0db19f3de071176640212bf3a8c1351e4cf1d.png" class="latex" alt="$$ 90$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="26" height="14" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/d/add22d9bda21e86390bc74fa4dde17730a442da7.png" class="latex" alt="$-$" style="vertical-align: 4px" width="13" height="1" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/3/c336dbe8a9fdc3c818b891474db51892d69af1ef.png" class="latex" alt="$$ 15$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="26" height="14" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/e/d5eb8726a34932d28103ce7ce17456421222603f.png" class="latex" alt="$=$" style="vertical-align: 2px" width="13" height="5" /> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/3/3/133e8b7c528260256cd0de9f8e1c2eabbf917b28.png" class="latex" alt="$$ 75$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="26" height="14" />, and therefore the sticker price is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/6/2/362384218da12b8b25ab3a0b057a7fc5e7c6b349.png" class="latex" alt="$$ 750$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="35" height="14" />.
</p>
