<p>For <a href="/wiki/index.php/Real_number" title="Real number">real numbers</a> <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />, define <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/1/7/e177c64a9bd9fbd2c9981d13826cbac308ae28ca.png" class="latex" alt="$a * b=(a-b)^2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="118" height="19" />. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/4/d44f897632f63376db40477827d960c937afc573.png" class="latex" alt="$(x-y)^2*(y-x)^2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="143" height="19" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/a/e/faec476c7b5eecf93252e43ac317c97e29cad02a.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ 0\qquad\mathrm{(B)}\ x^2+y^2\qquad\mathrm{(C)}\ 2x^2\qquad\mathrm{(D)}\ 2y^2\qquad\mathrm{(E)}\ 4xy$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="445" height="19" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/2/cb291e6d85c652622a4d9fae84cb018590486091.png" class="latex" alt="$(-a)^2 = a^2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="85" height="19" />, it follows that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/7/8/778ce670e5329492e792cbb91fdbb454f480e71f.png" class="latex" alt="$(x-y)^2 = (y-x)^2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="150" height="19" />, and 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/3/8/138e0ad8d731afe82997734cb68305608ba9622d.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[(x-y)^2 * (y-x)^2 = [(x-y)^2 - (y-x)^2]^2 = [(x-y)^2 - (x-y)^2]^2 = 0\ \mathrm{(A)}.\]" width="522" height="20" />
</p>
