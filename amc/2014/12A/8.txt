<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let the listed price be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />. Since all the answer choices are above <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/6/dc6a1da7f18069a90481296d18af7ca979fe3854.png" class="latex" alt="$\textdollar100$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="35" height="14" />, we can assume <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/8/1/681fed53ddc4b512b2acfad8135791ea6fe2e1d2.png" class="latex" alt="$x &gt; 100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="61" height="13" />. Thus the discounts after the coupons are used will be as follows:
</p><p>Coupon 1: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/e/6/fe6fa84e94c7e21e36c6e09dd2fe8087d63f7eea.png" class="latex" alt="$x\times10\%=.1x$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="114" height="14" />
</p><p>Coupon 2: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/3/f8366cd6196dd8a9da6d38a3e9eafb109e99d53e.png" class="latex" alt="$20$" width="17" height="12" />
</p><p>Coupon 3: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/c/c/9cc0a8e966557716654d550abd80cdb3e8ba4776.png" class="latex" alt="$18\%\times(x-100)=.18x-18$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="227" height="18" />
</p><p><br />
For coupon <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> to give a greater price reduction than the other coupons, we must have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/3/6e393322b0b637fad4b519f687b0b29166e68380.png" class="latex" alt="$.1x&gt;20\implies x&gt;200$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="177" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/d/6/bd6c7c7c254babf3fc99355804a334dd1bf837ec.png" class="latex" alt="$.1x&gt;.18x-18\implies.08x&lt;18\implies x&lt;225$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="359" height="13" />.
</p><p>The only choice that satisfies such conditions is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/d/0/6d0dce25aa1857785a152c79e7e8d6c81cbd6161.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ \textdollar219.95}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="105" height="29" />
