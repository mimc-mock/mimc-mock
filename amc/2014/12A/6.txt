<p>The difference between a two-digit number and the number obtained by reversing its digits is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> times the sum of the digits of either number.  What is the sum of the two digit number and its reverse?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/5/205f2da17edf43e25fc32ead41e6124d1510c55b.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }44\qquad \textbf{(B) }55\qquad \textbf{(C) }77\qquad \textbf{(D) }99\qquad \textbf{(E) }110$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="407" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let the two digits be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />. Then, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/5/6/256126fc768f9416ab2aeadc0767beb1561ba882.png" class="latex" alt="$5a + 5b = 10a + b - 10b - a = 9a - 9b$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="302" height="14" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/0/a/a0a595eced02b78e23438c2a3fa064106e1b21bd.png" class="latex" alt="$2a = 7b$" width="60" height="12" />. This yields <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/b/1/9b1838491bea2799f90f459e5a942e9dd6d7c4c7.png" class="latex" alt="$a = 7$" width="43" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/7/197213da9d06b2c5b59a10ad17f6a4aeb43d6697.png" class="latex" alt="$b = 2$" width="40" height="12" /> because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/7/8/a78210140ee0f08236c0129d621e7f9b2ed1bf56.png" class="latex" alt="$a, b &lt; 10$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="67" height="16" />. Then, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/a/61a70072b001b09ab1c8d62f788ac6fa4aa7afe5.png" class="latex" alt="$72 + 27 = \boxed{\textbf{(D) }99}.$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="151" height="28" />
</p>
