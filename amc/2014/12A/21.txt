<p>For every real number <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/4/1/441416e660d25335f27e55334ac2a06e51541c97.png" class="latex" alt="$\lfloor x\rfloor$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="23" height="19" /> denote the greatest integer not exceeding <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, and let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/d/b6d01f35e21f4dda60a2836b577dcbd218144d36.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[f(x)=\lfloor x\rfloor(2014^{x-\lfloor x\rfloor}-1).\]" width="209" height="21" />  The set of all numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> such that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/9/a/e9a30e64bd0df3bbbbd252212e1ee9dd63a91bfc.png" class="latex" alt="$1\leq x&lt;2014$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="104" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/b/d/0bd18b45fb03ac6400611a4ecb03d4f1c68b79ab.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x)\leq 1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="67" height="18" /> is a union of disjoint intervals.  What is the sum of the lengths of those intervals?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/5/355d7836eeeb58e14d0ba99e0ea8bb7662a69b6d.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }1\qquad \textbf{(B) }\dfrac{\log 2015}{\log 2014}\qquad \textbf{(C) }\dfrac{\log 2014}{\log 2013}\qquad \textbf{(D) }\dfrac{2014}{2013}\qquad \textbf{(E) }2014^{\frac1{2014}}\qquad$" style="vertical-align: -16px" width="552" height="41" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/0/2f08d31025b828f4d974161059823f37a1430642.png" class="latex" alt="$\lfloor x\rfloor=k$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="60" height="19" /> for some integer <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/1/901ea17d6554373d93231f63ed1a235c84bb3f3f.png" class="latex" alt="$1\leq k\leq 2013$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="103" height="15" />.  Then we can rewrite <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/9/6/c96dd6ec1dc4ad7520fbdc78fcdbec9edd068d0c.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="34" height="18" /> as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/8/60865d45e6005041ec3eeba5c2880e7e37793183.png" class="latex" alt="$k(2014^{x-k}-1)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="116" height="20" />.  In order for this to be less than or equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />, we need <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/c/5/9c552ebb523b7c4902dcb1292d22b7f94a658d1d.png" class="latex" alt="$2014^{x-k}-1\leq\dfrac1k\implies x\leq k+\log_{2014}\left(\dfrac{k+1}k\right)$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="370" height="43" />.  Combining this with the fact that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/b/4/eb4b45aa9acc9f13a7d1d6fbfbbe9e3e3eabfaf6.png" class="latex" alt="$\lfloor x\rfloor =k$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="60" height="19" /> gives that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/1/6/f166eefdf28e0d71694031656161e036fb398b86.png" class="latex" alt="$x\in\left[k,k+\log_{2014}\left(\dfrac{k+1}k\right)\right]$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="222" height="43" />, and so the length of the interval is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/2/d52a7e22d0a1cc4f25809029b29a4795930504fc.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_{2014}\left(\dfrac{k+1}k\right)$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="121" height="43" />.  We want the sum of all possible intervals such that the inequality holds true; since all of these intervals must be disjoint, we can sum from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/e/d/5ed8470bc717a49aafe4556c7e2c2e11ddc7b687.png" class="latex" alt="$k=1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="42" height="13" /> to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/5/ba59dfa9ae5ff4c01ce2ed06e8cba5eda691c684.png" class="latex" alt="$k=2013$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="70" height="13" /> to get that the desired sum is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/6/2/06261d18b68a17761cb48e3ae0a8834f53752c6b.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\sum_{i=1}^{2013}\log_{2014}\left(\dfrac{i+1}i\right)=\log_{2014}\left(\prod_{i=1}^{2013}\dfrac{i+1}i\right)=\log_{2014}\left(\dfrac{2014}1\right)=\boxed{1\textbf{ (A)}}.\]" width="556" height="53" />
</p>
