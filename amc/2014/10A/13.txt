src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/1/5/215b76a72a15b232d6b8930c28b4157c3066e0da.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle CMH$" width="66" height="13" /> to get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/1/0111388cd46eae79848e295363eaa65308a12428.png" class="latex" alt="$CM = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{4}.$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="93" height="43" /> We can use this to get the area of the triangle and multiply that by three since the triangles are congruent. The result follows. ~peelybonehead
</p>
