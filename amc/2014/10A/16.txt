However, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/c/7/2c7774174993ffa29a38cf577ae34d8e0a3a63cb.png" class="latex" alt="$XY$" width="30" height="12" /> is also the base of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/0/f/d0f74095dbc3ad575c310940775aa9a2e66f371c.png" class="latex" alt="$XFY$" width="44" height="12" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/f/b/7fb4e8f435a31754de597f64ff176011c223764f.png" class="latex" alt="$k = e / AB$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="78" height="18" /> where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/a/e5a649aee592a9c37d2f9b5d5239f5e06e533130.png" class="latex" alt="$AB = 1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="60" height="13" /> so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/8/0/d8079304658a9686ad905a46f4f00ffc35d282ac.png" class="latex" alt="$k = e$" width="42" height="12" />.
