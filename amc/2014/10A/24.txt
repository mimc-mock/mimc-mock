<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Let's start with natural numbers, with no skips in between.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/0/ee041ad59925cdbff29cb05ecb3fe0a5a7ee7520.png" class="latex" alt="$1,2,3,4,5,...,500,000$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="171" height="16" />
</p><p>All we need to do is count how many numbers are skipped, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" />, and "push" (add on to) <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/b/4/3b4a8a43081bca50199529ac2a4515c1400b0403.png" class="latex" alt="$500,000$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="62" height="16" /> according to however many numbers are skipped.
</p><p>Clearly, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/d/ffd9e0d03bf2acf6732aa65c2c66378cbb67d462.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{999(1000)}{2}&lt;500,000&lt;\frac{1000(1001)}{2}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="282" height="38" />. </p><p>Therefore <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/e/e/1eeb0f749cc072e6b726b3ccfb94afc564ebf386.png" class="latex" alt="$n=\frac{996(997)}{2}=496,506$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="194" height="38" />, and the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/0/4/8041a94b452f5cf4e3e5bb9a96587aca39f2736d.png" class="latex" alt="$496,506+500000=\boxed{\textbf{(A) }996,506}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="275" height="30" />.
