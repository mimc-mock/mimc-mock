<p>For real numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/e/e/9ee4b825a2e36ae093ed7be5e4851ef453b34914.png" class="latex" alt="$w$" width="13" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/3/b13f21416d84e13708696f34dea81026cda583c9.png" class="latex" alt="$z$" width="8" height="8" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/9/a/69a1b108beb9e11391b1e636b5839f88ac0d9af1.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\cfrac{\frac{1}{w} + \frac{1}{z}}{\frac{1}{w} - \frac{1}{z}} = 2014.\]" width="111" height="47" /> What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/6/1c62958e42423d619fa08c60bf6e4cb0f4ccd93e.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{w+z}{w-z}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="47" height="35" />? 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/b/f/ebf0351de397d1c6aa82fcd3f27b2829eb2d61e3.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }-2014\qquad\textbf{(B) }\frac{-1}{2014}\qquad\textbf{(C) }\frac{1}{2014}\qquad\textbf{(D) }1\qquad\textbf{(E) }2014$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="492" height="37" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Multiply the numerator and denominator of the LHS by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/8/3480aca46138a454bb35084317f530e672388798.png" class="latex" alt="$wz$" width="22" height="8" /> to get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/a/4/4a4855e08ff9b34492e9de05f68f93663cce8493.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{z+w}{z-w}=2014$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="109" height="35" />. Then since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/d/6/0d6ec3cb77c32e3bdcf4a647346a38c5c69df80b.png" class="latex" alt="$z+w=w+z$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="113" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/e/8/5e852f683d3115dd729c079a7a66ef67f9c9a4ee.png" class="latex" alt="$w-z=-(z-w)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="141" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/9/ba921346443142a5d3d5e7e1837cb9e035de1e88.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{w+z}{w-z}=-\frac{z+w}{z-w}=-2014$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="210" height="35" />, or choice <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/6/e/f6eb65962292d994ce2f9a8af63728883f256b18.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{A}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="25" height="24" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Muliply both sides by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/b/9/0b91f251ca7798b41589c93715183d225d7c29a4.png" class="latex" alt="$\left(\frac{1}{w}-\frac{1}{z}\right)$" style="vertical-align: -18px" width="81" height="45" /> to get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/d/9/cd996bf56235b0d1b28922f8721768e8c9bb7c01.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{w}+\frac{1}{z}=2014\left(\frac{1}{w}-\frac{1}{z}\right)$" style="vertical-align: -18px" width="198" height="45" />. Then, add <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/a/2/ea216cb7a370fcb3d1597149ee092f44e5ff85f4.png" class="latex" alt="$2014\cdot\frac{1}{z}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="65" height="38" /> to both sides and subtract <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/9/f/49f938eb6a7f2ead77b10e91363a4e3e08af7f40.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{w}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="16" height="37" /> from both sides to get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/b/1/db1e15c09ffafa76b037dd0758a70dcb317bf5b5.png" class="latex" alt="$2015\cdot\frac{1}{z}=2013\cdot\frac{1}{w}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="156" height="38" />. Then, we can plug in the most simple values for z and w (<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/c/b7c41d5d55f66cd4a292a064c28c0c679742c8a6.png" class="latex" alt="$2015$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/a/2fa725c98dfe19f98809033755b801810bc298aa.png" class="latex" alt="$2013$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />, respectively), and find <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/c/f5cc6127303da092eef966d08b4e8965d1ec3813.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2013+2015}{2013-2015}=\frac{2(2014)}{-2}=-2014$" style="vertical-align: -15px" width="263" height="42" />, or answer choice <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/6/e/f6eb65962292d994ce2f9a8af63728883f256b18.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{A}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="25" height="24" />.
</p>
