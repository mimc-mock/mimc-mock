<p>Danica drove her new car on a trip for a whole number of hours, averaging <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/9/b79030abeb688469b5ffc2e1c4e37fee225a529e.png" class="latex" alt="$55$" width="17" height="12" /> miles per hour. At the beginning of the trip, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96a81938ca5e71faa6894d9d52ac776c9efbe75d.png" class="latex" alt="$abc$" width="25" height="12" /> miles was displayed on the odometer, where <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96a81938ca5e71faa6894d9d52ac776c9efbe75d.png" class="latex" alt="$abc$" width="25" height="12" /> is a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />-digit number with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/d/1/dd1fa1789e3a7b9d4a9cba60f70579eb5d8b0116.png" class="latex" alt="$a\ge1$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="42" height="14" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/0/1/801b0bc0755892700273f68168f95a063853229b.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+c\le7$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="103" height="15" />. At the end of the trip, the odometer showed <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/a/82a16fbef6d82c0de43b9512df7cf77b894b22ae.png" class="latex" alt="$cba$" width="25" height="12" /> miles. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/a/c/1ac816c290967e49b43b79af7c7fbb65dc613f4a.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2+b^2+c^2$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="91" height="16" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1501b67d3d398d9ba1f9ab2e40d69f77d5c74d.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf {(A) } 26 \qquad \textbf {(B) } 27 \qquad \textbf {(C) } 36 \qquad \textbf {(D) } 37 \qquad \textbf {(E) } 41$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="397" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/8/8189a5b5a0917b8c93350827be4038af1839139d.png" class="latex" alt="$h$" width="10" height="12" /> be the number of hours Danica drove. Note that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96a81938ca5e71faa6894d9d52ac776c9efbe75d.png" class="latex" alt="$abc$" width="25" height="12" /> can be expressed as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/2/2/6228630c7c50784bb982a1c66f0fff9d3aa9777d.png" class="latex" alt="$100\cdot a+10\cdot b+c$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="141" height="14" />. From the given information, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/b/54b45fda8faf7df97955f58fa0aa404406ce67ca.png" class="latex" alt="$100a+10b+c+55h=100c+10b+a$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="305" height="14" />. This can be simplified into <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/3/4/534742d50996c7a764b728f6ab7156589f20be92.png" class="latex" alt="$99a+55h=99c$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="129" height="14" /> by subtraction, which can further be simplified into <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/7/197e6e637b560ae46ffda6d82995195dc1aac0fc.png" class="latex" alt="$9a+5h=9c$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="101" height="14" /> by dividing both sides by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/8/c6878713578626763c38433b3f4c8c2205ad0c15.png" class="latex" alt="$11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />. Thus we must have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/f/7/ef75272b8d6e50f23c94b9c7208bfde6eda53dbe.png" class="latex" alt="$h\equiv0\pmod9$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="114" height="18" />. However, if <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/4/cb4271f0a070cb70d58e70d9320bffeee1f7fb69.png" class="latex" alt="$h\ge 15$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="52" height="15" />, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/5/205451577d95c5dcca21611b1ccc6df2dde27819.png" class="latex" alt="$\text{min}\{c\}\ge\frac{9+5(15)}{9}&gt;9$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="187" height="38" />, which is impossible since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> must be a digit. The only value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/8/8189a5b5a0917b8c93350827be4038af1839139d.png" class="latex" alt="$h$" width="10" height="12" /> that is divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/f/2/bf2c9074b396e3af0dea52d792660eea1c77f10f.png" class="latex" alt="$9$" width="8" height="12" /> and less than or equal to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/0/820a20de2847dfdc1bc5cffc8d904933150032bd.png" class="latex" alt="$14$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/d/ceddbd13cedeafd3d4b97f280c97bb86340b7fcc.png" class="latex" alt="$h=9$" width="43" height="12" />. 
</p><p>From this information, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/5/5/d5527ba0b5c16c44e94016c509db3099c14388cd.png" class="latex" alt="$9a+5(9)=9c\Rightarrow a+5=c$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="216" height="18" />. Combining this with the inequalities <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/0/1/801b0bc0755892700273f68168f95a063853229b.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+c\le7$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="103" height="15" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/d/1/dd1fa1789e3a7b9d4a9cba60f70579eb5d8b0116.png" class="latex" alt="$a\ge1$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="42" height="14" />, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/9/3/d930dc2651737bd7cdde45e84249fc08c1e1f71f.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+a+5\le7\Rightarrow 2a+b\le2$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="246" height="15" />, which implies <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/0/2/502e059d1a020b368aee3a99c5669d88331d321f.png" class="latex" alt="$a=1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="42" height="12" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/4/fc43f0ea59e862d7db01586894e9eea730d427b7.png" class="latex" alt="$b=0$" width="40" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/e/b/4eb76404ee79952dcc555464b061509299789b09.png" class="latex" alt="$c=6$" width="40" height="12" />. Thus <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/6/8/568de4fed801926901b55d8e6891e7fec0112c0e.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2+b^2+c^2=1+0+36=\fbox{37 \textbf{(D)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="284" height="28" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Danica drives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/0/f5047d1e0cbb50ec208923a22cd517c55100fa7b.png" class="latex" alt="$m$" width="15" height="8" /> miles, such that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/3/073b68444caca3c51f4ce50ee7fe4e5939755e22.png" class="latex" alt="$m&gt;0$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="48" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/0/f5047d1e0cbb50ec208923a22cd517c55100fa7b.png" class="latex" alt="$m$" width="15" height="8" /> is a multiple of 55. Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/0/f5047d1e0cbb50ec208923a22cd517c55100fa7b.png" class="latex" alt="$m$" width="15" height="8" /> must have an units digit of either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/8/3/b835f2c6c592edc4583ce996f86bcc0d07ca8da5.png" class="latex" alt="$5.$" width="12" height="12" /> If the units digit of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/0/f5047d1e0cbb50ec208923a22cd517c55100fa7b.png" class="latex" alt="$m$" width="15" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/d/ced6b9b2e75857b566f1e516560c4a4d63977299.png" class="latex" alt="$0,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="16" /> then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/1/f717c40ef1d78faf6efbaef5c9e6f804d544faf3.png" class="latex" alt="$a=c$" width="42" height="8" /> which would imply that Danica did not drive at all. Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/0/b5051cfe60ec25034c70b7c7b08cfde89408639d.png" class="latex" alt="$c&gt;a.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="45" height="10" /> Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/9/5d9bed3d786b7d482a7de7bf0c7963549c0291f6.png" class="latex" alt="$|a-c|=5,$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="87" height="18" /> and because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/c/a/ecab6a0351b32673b89479081a49921c31029700.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+c\leq7, c&gt;a,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="156" height="16" /> we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/7/4/4741c0935b81ab2e9546c9b936956571687ea113.png" class="latex" alt="$(a,c)=(1,6).$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="107" height="18" /> Finally, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" /> then must be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" /> due to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/7/ce7f25d1a1259dd59d8d7aa3f5a94efb6da15272.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b+c\leq 7,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="107" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/a/1/3a16d31c1fc2c202c6acc2122eb664fca4a0b85d.png" class="latex" alt="$a^2+b^2+c^2=1^2+0^2+6^2=\fbox{\textbf{(D) }37}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="298" height="28" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>We can set up an algebraic equation for this problem.
</p><p>From what's given, we have that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/e/6ce9f81407f6754e8570af55199d96b707d13f50.png" class="latex" alt="$100c+10b+a=55x+100a+10b+c$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="305" height="14" />
</p><p>This simplifies to be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/c/6cca3f6736ddacd74475b9df0d8be3ce79cfae8b.png" class="latex" alt="$0=55x+99a-99c\implies -55x=99a-99c$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="352" height="14" />
</p><p>Factoring, we get that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/7/f7733d485ba230dddea821a1a899816b3f4fb748.png" class="latex" alt="$-55x=99(a-c)\implies x=-\frac{9(a-c)}{5}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="302" height="38" />
</p><p>Hence, notice that we want <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/1/b6108812d99997ac988be8bf113a248f7d30e071.png" class="latex" alt="$a-c=-5$" width="86" height="12" /> so that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/1/5b1d8cdec48abe8293a4169a8afce4fc8a8348e7.png" class="latex" alt="$x=9$" width="43" height="12" />
</p><p>The only pair that works for this problem that satisfies the original requirements is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/2/1/621f951230bd018717e8c0c5cd3bfd6b1f8720a6.png" class="latex" alt="$(1,6)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="38" height="18" />
</p><p>Hence, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/8/4382ce66030040ae995f9db749353623e3c696cc.png" class="latex" alt="$a=1, b=0, c=6$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="141" height="16" />
</p><p>Checking, we have that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/c/1cc6306f64bb2af54d718afac9558782c14a794e.png" class="latex" alt="$106+55(9)=601\implies 601=601$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="269" height="18" />
</p><p>Hence, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/7/9/b79bd4ba4a07ef41bcb07e2e5306e0c3769712b3.png" class="latex" alt="$1^2+0^2+6^2=37\implies\boxed{D}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="213" height="24" />
</p>
