<p>Al gets the disease algebritis and must take one green pill and one pink pill each day for two weeks. A green pill costs <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/b/3/fb3b9e046c83ca25ffa3c15f213a16571b36a47b.png" class="latex" alt="$$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="8" height="14" /><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> more than a pink pill, and Al's pills cost a total of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/7/7/f776ec2c31ad3dc4b571f7f249f3cf07ccff05a9.png" class="latex" alt="$\textdollar 546$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="35" height="14" /> for the two weeks. How much does one green pill cost?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/5/3/153bfccea757dcfef7b57d1ed10bbcb4836ddd10.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ \textdollar 7 \qquad\textbf{(B) }\textdollar 14 \qquad\textbf{(C) }\textdollar 19\qquad\textbf{(D) }\textdollar 20\qquad\textbf{(E) }\textdollar 39$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="433" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Because there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/0/820a20de2847dfdc1bc5cffc8d904933150032bd.png" class="latex" alt="$14$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="12" /> days in two weeks, Al spends <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/d/e/3de7b3f78e24de5b8f22a3ab5b7aecfd6401bd8d.png" class="latex" alt="$546/14 = 39$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="96" height="18" /> dollars per day for the cost of a green pill and a pink pill. If the green pill costs <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> dollars and the pink pill <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/b/d4baf6d4b1cfa69b68b3204b5e2eba0c5755fe20.png" class="latex" alt="$x-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="40" height="12" /> dollars, the sum of the two costs <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/a/e/5aed851e97f2f89b7ceeb6d5424aac270f956a73.png" class="latex" alt="$2x-1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="49" height="12" /> should equal <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/5/e058d401a1b942f106f081ff1fcca8a8b1d42a7e.png" class="latex" alt="$39$" width="17" height="12" /> dollars. Then the cost of the green pill <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/5/f/75f3795f8b392b98ab90ac4e5cf6ac72161c85e5.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D) }\textdollar  20}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="75" height="29" />.
</p>
