<p>How many <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/1/9/f1965fae079a9ba2c0726c307070c2355dfcb213.png" class="latex" alt="$15$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />-letter arrangements of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> A's, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> B's, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> C's have no A's in the first <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> letters, no B's in the next <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> letters, and no C's in the last <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" /> letters?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/a/0/9a01675bfb129010d994011e191874c4209b463a.png" class="latex" alt="$\textrm{(A)}\ \sum_{k=0}^{5}\binom{5}{k}^{3}\qquad\textrm{(B)}\ 3^{5}\cdot 2^{5}\qquad\textrm{(C)}\ 2^{15}\qquad\textrm{(D)}\ \frac{15!}{(5!)^{3}}\qquad\textrm{(E)}\ 3^{15}$" style="vertical-align: -22px" width="493" height="56" />

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/e/b/aeb67cf17c9938c16eff541d8de4327889fff565.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textrm{(A)}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="37" height="28" />.
</p><p>Note that the first five letters must be B's or C's, the next five letters must be C's or A's, and the last five letters must be A's or B's. If there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" /> B's in the first five letters, then there must be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/4/3/14399a9adf1c4db98905329d73293c62336e5467.png" class="latex" alt="$5-k$" width="41" height="12" /> C's in the first five letters, so there must be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" /> C's and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/4/3/14399a9adf1c4db98905329d73293c62336e5467.png" class="latex" alt="$5-k$" width="41" height="12" /> A's in the next five letters, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" /> A's and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/4/3/14399a9adf1c4db98905329d73293c62336e5467.png" class="latex" alt="$5-k$" width="41" height="12" /> B's in the last five letters. Therefore the number of each letter in each group of five is determined completely by the number of B's in the first 5 letters, and the number of ways to arrange these 15 letters with this restriction is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/4/f84db85afbb572e04172a39030921496648d8476.png" class="latex" alt="$\binom{5}{k}^3$" style="vertical-align: -22px" width="44" height="56" /> (since there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/b/4/9b47c6fc6a6d8cfc6bfb67d732a91e55c5f94b97.png" class="latex" alt="$\binom{5}{k}$" style="vertical-align: -22px" width="34" height="53" /> ways to arrange <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" /> B's and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/4/3/14399a9adf1c4db98905329d73293c62336e5467.png" class="latex" alt="$5-k$" width="41" height="12" /> C's). Therefore the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/d/82dd96492745188d12407d2b7b1a9aa2b9bf379d.png" class="latex" alt="$\sum_{k=0}^{5}\binom{5}{k}^{3}$" style="vertical-align: -22px" width="73" height="56" />.
</p>
