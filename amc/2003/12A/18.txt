<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> be a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />-digit number, and let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/6/1/0615acc3725de21025457e7d6f7694dab8e2f758.png" class="latex" alt="$q$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="8" height="11" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> be the quotient and the remainder, respectively, when <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> is divided by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />. For how many values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/1/a/31a6cd957da325a37d10bbf4c8078775c574306d.png" class="latex" alt="$q+r$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="39" height="14" /> divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/8/c6878713578626763c38433b3f4c8c2205ad0c15.png" class="latex" alt="$11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />? 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/6/a/d6ae018908e77ecc25b6ea19a260d82365a0fe5a.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A) \ } 8180\qquad \mathrm{(B) \ } 8181\qquad \mathrm{(C) \ } 8182\qquad \mathrm{(D) \ } 9000\qquad \mathrm{(E) \ } 9090$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="478" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>When a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />-digit number is divided by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" />, the first <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> digits become the quotient, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/6/1/0615acc3725de21025457e7d6f7694dab8e2f758.png" class="latex" alt="$q$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="8" height="11" />, and the last <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> digits become the remainder, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" />. 
</p><p>Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/6/1/0615acc3725de21025457e7d6f7694dab8e2f758.png" class="latex" alt="$q$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="8" height="11" /> can be any integer from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/0/0705411d92671faf1fe5602cfef8f353c7f0f83d.png" class="latex" alt="$999$" width="26" height="12" /> inclusive, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> can be any integer from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" /> to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/1/a313bf06349648df7be807bee95b651604ce7a7e.png" class="latex" alt="$99$" width="17" height="12" /> inclusive. 
</p><p>For each of the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/a/e/eaed782d24999db75d0f9c1135c53df2692d99ce.png" class="latex" alt="$9\cdot10\cdot10=900$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="122" height="13" /> possible values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/6/1/0615acc3725de21025457e7d6f7694dab8e2f758.png" class="latex" alt="$q$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="8" height="11" />, there are at least <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/6/e060b19f919ceeb7dc0093a9199fa9df6ab5a046.png" class="latex" alt="$\left\lfloor \frac{100}{11} \right\rfloor = 9$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="84" height="43" /> possible values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> such that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce04a7d147d142fb4def4f61f8c4596eb67e1c5.png" class="latex" alt="$q+r \equiv 0\pmod{11}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="152" height="18" />. 
</p><p>Since there is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> "extra" possible value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> that is congruent to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/c/edc20bccf186adffa7225f53d842ad2a27c63266.png" class="latex" alt="$0\pmod{11}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="88" height="18" />, each of the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/9/0597aea89941058d9acf9bf570b94bf66ec2a4b4.png" class="latex" alt="$\left\lfloor \frac{900}{11} \right\rfloor = 81$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="93" height="43" /> values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/6/1/0615acc3725de21025457e7d6f7694dab8e2f758.png" class="latex" alt="$q$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="8" height="11" /> that are congruent to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/c/edc20bccf186adffa7225f53d842ad2a27c63266.png" class="latex" alt="$0\pmod{11}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="88" height="18" /> have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> more possible value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/5/b55ca7a0aa88ab7d58f4fc035317fdac39b17861.png" class="latex" alt="$r$" width="8" height="8" /> such that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce04a7d147d142fb4def4f61f8c4596eb67e1c5.png" class="latex" alt="$q+r \equiv 0\pmod{11}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="152" height="18" />. 
</p><p>Therefore, the number of possible values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> such that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce04a7d147d142fb4def4f61f8c4596eb67e1c5.png" class="latex" alt="$q+r \equiv 0\pmod{11}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="152" height="18" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/9/4/a942b7ae2e436560956335561fafcb6027a39b6a.png" class="latex" alt="$900\cdot9+81\cdot1=8181 \Rightarrow\boxed{(B)}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="241" height="29" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Notice that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/0/6/b0643329458b2e2a3b374bf5ec7a6671d912fc64.png" class="latex" alt="$q+r\equiv0\pmod{11}\Rightarrow100q+r\equiv0\pmod{11}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="362" height="18" />. This means that any number whose quotient and remainder sum is divisible by 11 must also be divisible by 11. Therefore, there are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/d/bad2052ae97b7ca1559e80b981d67232fd2f6590.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{99990-10010}{11}+1=8181$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="207" height="38" /> possible values. The answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/a/e5a6a985938157c3ac74eb9ebcecd26518fad15e.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B) }8181}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="83" height="28" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/8/3/18396b1d04475fed55fd7c1b75cd4d85133210be.png" class="latex" alt="$abcde$" width="43" height="12" /> be the five digits of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" />. Then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/c/5/3c5050b5d0f0b56e5aa00079f614bc21ba672d87.png" class="latex" alt="$q = abc$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="58" height="16" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/4/7/247f6c0d737a321109a2d277133be6dad904ff43.png" class="latex" alt="$r = de$" width="50" height="12" />. By the divisibility rules of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/8/c6878713578626763c38433b3f4c8c2205ad0c15.png" class="latex" alt="$11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/9/6/1969567f071b08585c3097680bb818fbfa3bf77e.png" class="latex" alt="$q = a - b + c \pmod{11}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="182" height="18" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/a/3/da3e359713680d4954a3218063bd9cb92767e167.png" class="latex" alt="$r = -d + e \pmod{11}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="167" height="18" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/a/f/6af4e78ddc74587858d0e20246529086b9d06e84.png" class="latex" alt="$q + r = a - b + c - d + e = abcde = n \pmod{11}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="378" height="18" />. Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" /> must be divisble by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/8/c6878713578626763c38433b3f4c8c2205ad0c15.png" class="latex" alt="$11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />. There are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/a/f/1af6d6fcc0af356e07310226cede1ef944c0b20f.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{99990 - 10010}{11} + 1 = 8181$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="207" height="38" /> five-digit multiples of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/6/8/c6878713578626763c38433b3f4c8c2205ad0c15.png" class="latex" alt="$11$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" />, so the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/a/e5a6a985938157c3ac74eb9ebcecd26518fad15e.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(B) }8181}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="83" height="28" />.
</p>
