<p>What is the sum of the reciprocals of the roots of the equation 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/2/8/728a7010f846fd918f2d05e56aefc796a369be97.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2003}{2004}x+1+\frac{1}{x}=0$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="151" height="38" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/5/c/75c14e7d7bbe404873c673c55cc78c6f9eeecf8a.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A) \ } -\frac{2004}{2003}\qquad \mathrm{(B) \ } -1\qquad \mathrm{(C) \ } \frac{2003}{2004}\qquad \mathrm{(D) \ } 1\qquad \mathrm{(E) \ } \frac{2004}{2003}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="479" height="38" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Multiplying both sides by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />: 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/b/c/5bc781199f3316cffcd40810c05bd7472420b6f9.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2003}{2004}x^{2}+1x+1=0$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="164" height="38" />
</p><p>Let the roots be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />. 
</p><p>The problem is asking for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/8/f/58f1665f3be54fd614260af6d176fecd72aab347.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{a}+\frac{1}{b}= \frac{a+b}{ab}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="115" height="37" />
</p><p>By <a href="/wiki/index.php/Vieta%27s_formulas" title="Vieta&#39;s formulas">Vieta's formulas</a>: 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/9/e/59e7662fa67e715ce264624906e3604b0ee01322.png" class="latex" alt="$a+b=(-1)^{1}\frac{1}{\frac{2003}{2004}}=-\frac{2004}{2003}$" style="vertical-align: -19px" width="220" height="44" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/3/11368560a0a115da4b7caaa750cd36708ceb742f.png" class="latex" alt="$ab=(-1)^{2}\frac{1}{\frac{2003}{2004}}=\frac{2004}{2003}$" style="vertical-align: -19px" width="183" height="44" />
</p><p>So the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/d/2fdbc86de7388a60457406fc20a76040ac28b656.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{a+b}{ab}=\frac{-\frac{2004}{2003}}{\frac{2004}{2003}}=-1 \Rightarrow\boxed{\mathrm{(B)}\ -1}$" style="vertical-align: -19px" width="267" height="47" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Dividing both sides by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/6/b/76b2878564ab19b80abb21ba964abe90fe120846.png" class="latex" alt="$x^2$" width="16" height="15" />, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/6/f46712a0e825110c6a934734055443452714d01f.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2003}{2004}+\frac 1x+\frac{1}{x^2}=0$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="153" height="38" />, 
</p><p>we see by <a href="/wiki/index.php/Vieta%27s_formulas" title="Vieta&#39;s formulas">Vieta's formulas</a> that the sum of the roots is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/c/f/0cf81de55e5cf42f2f84b32216edfa3efedf1901.png" class="latex" alt="$-1  \Rightarrow\boxed{\mathrm{(B)}\ -1}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="127" height="29" />.
</p>
