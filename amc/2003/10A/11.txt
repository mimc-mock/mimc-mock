<p>The sum of the two 5-digit numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/3/2/5322783541d38808b55ad243579cd21a44808eb4.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="65" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/d/6/cd63c853f80c9ccc826c02bc6a08fe30cfe2e770.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="65" height="13" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/b/d/1bdc4ea63d007bb275fec32b35ad1e8d2537aaab.png" class="latex" alt="$123422$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="53" height="13" />. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/9/4/894e204b6dc2e7997d3492fd2e6033c873848b46.png" class="latex" alt="$A+M+C$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="91" height="14" />? 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/c/e5cb6d0898106abb1e5a0f803fa674cf5d9d8f56.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A) \ } 10\qquad \mathrm{(B) \ } 11\qquad \mathrm{(C) \ } 12\qquad \mathrm{(D) \ } 13\qquad \mathrm{(E) \ } 14$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="388" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/1/e/a1ee71230a250817c2d7c6904096c01167a8f6b9.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC10+AMC12=123422$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="231" height="14" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/6/1/a61f58b474fa5f99145000cb84b34ee110499e10.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC00+AMC00=123400$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="231" height="14" /> 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/5/c/f5c0607ef0fc7be34832d00c47c8867a86294c47.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC+AMC=1234$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="177" height="14" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/4/4/e441132608dac7727ec9fd73fa5c9ea442e7deac.png" class="latex" alt="$2\cdot AMC=1234$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="130" height="13" /> 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/b/6/4b65d19de498768f3ce6b38e94635643d309f996.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC=\frac{1234}{2}=617$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="163" height="37" />
</p><p>Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/1/5d1e4485dc90c450e8c76826516c1b2ccb8fce16.png" class="latex" alt="$M$" width="19" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/3/c3355896da590fc491a10150a50416687626d7cc.png" class="latex" alt="$C$" width="14" height="12" /> are digits, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/f/0/af04466ac38b7b0d0f451b3280c38f861ea13822.png" class="latex" alt="$A=6$" width="46" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/0/6/306fbc27de8a2ff35f7de8b08c56d55d69de903b.png" class="latex" alt="$M=1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/2/172453ab3ca0c4b95b5993f46fb8e9552e302546.png" class="latex" alt="$C=7$" width="47" height="12" />. 
</p><p>Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/f/4/3f4961e95c73f2dc4e5179d03502fe00e7271e07.png" class="latex" alt="$A+M+C = 6+1+7 = \boxed{\mathrm{(E)}\ 14}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="274" height="29" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>We know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/d/6/cd63c853f80c9ccc826c02bc6a08fe30cfe2e770.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="65" height="13" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> more than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/3/2/5322783541d38808b55ad243579cd21a44808eb4.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="65" height="13" />. We set up <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/f/87f1997ea5de902ff4ee02a0ba38476779dc45a6.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC10=x$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="100" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/4/8/b48b3444904a45c0d360afa8a18130c710e245b6.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC12=x+2$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="131" height="14" />. We have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/2/6/726230bef9956bca22b78fdceb2d1b1b65c81ebf.png" class="latex" alt="$x+x+2=123422$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="152" height="14" />. Solving for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" />, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/1/e01099b90ae64cfd064d34e580211789e2ae97ac.png" class="latex" alt="$x=61710$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="79" height="13" />. Therefore, the sum <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/8/4/48449f34912cd114713e1c0fae0ec23af22ffec5.png" class="latex" alt="$A+M+C= \boxed{\mathrm{(E)}\ 14}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="178" height="29" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>Consider the place values of the digits of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/3/2/5322783541d38808b55ad243579cd21a44808eb4.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="65" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/d/6/cd63c853f80c9ccc826c02bc6a08fe30cfe2e770.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="65" height="13" />. 
</p><p>When we add <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/3/2/5322783541d38808b55ad243579cd21a44808eb4.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="65" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/d/6/cd63c853f80c9ccc826c02bc6a08fe30cfe2e770.png" class="latex" alt="$AMC12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="65" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/8/3281f042316c3229797f581210fbbdf57758e94e.png" class="latex" alt="$C + C$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="53" height="16" /> must result in a units digit of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />, meaning <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/3/c3355896da590fc491a10150a50416687626d7cc.png" class="latex" alt="$C$" width="14" height="12" /> is either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/0/a/e0a0db32027a732ac57d37ef2ae9bb150f65b108.png" class="latex" alt="$7$" width="9" height="12" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/1/5d1e4485dc90c450e8c76826516c1b2ccb8fce16.png" class="latex" alt="$M$" width="19" height="12" /> is odd, this means a ten was carried over to the next place value from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/8/3281f042316c3229797f581210fbbdf57758e94e.png" class="latex" alt="$C + C$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="53" height="16" />, and thus <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/d/f/ddf96892e4c0821f127102dee41517a58d9086cc.png" class="latex" alt="$C = 7$" width="47" height="12" /> (as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/f/61fd6faa4e8b9ffc9a63b3737fd0fcbe4be54770.png" class="latex" alt="$7 + 7 = 14$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="85" height="15" /> and the ten is carried over). Now, we know 3 is the units digit of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/b/3/8b363a105c19d585a5a98651d81052c2b236a55d.png" class="latex" alt="$M + M + 1$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="95" height="16" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/1/5d1e4485dc90c450e8c76826516c1b2ccb8fce16.png" class="latex" alt="$M$" width="19" height="12" /> is either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/0/1/601a7806cbfad68196c43a4665871f8c3186802e.png" class="latex" alt="$6$" width="8" height="12" />. Again, we must look at the digit before <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/1/5d1e4485dc90c450e8c76826516c1b2ccb8fce16.png" class="latex" alt="$M$" width="19" height="12" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" />. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> is even, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/1/5d1e4485dc90c450e8c76826516c1b2ccb8fce16.png" class="latex" alt="$M$" width="19" height="12" /> must be less than <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />, or else the ten would be carried over. Ergo, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/1/5d1e4485dc90c450e8c76826516c1b2ccb8fce16.png" class="latex" alt="$M$" width="19" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />. Nothing is carried over, so we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/c/b6c627548ab28c7908688ced84a0465f9077dc45.png" class="latex" alt="$A + A = 12$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="94" height="16" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/8/818297b710359102c7cd36d1ce433f64657cc77d.png" class="latex" alt="$A = 6$" width="46" height="13" />. Therefore, the sum of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/1/5d1e4485dc90c450e8c76826516c1b2ccb8fce16.png" class="latex" alt="$M$" width="19" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/3/3/c3355896da590fc491a10150a50416687626d7cc.png" class="latex" alt="$C$" width="14" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/1/9/c1923de5844a2931170f9c9fca60d871e99b9c67.png" class="latex" alt="$6 + 1 + 7 = \boxed{\mathrm{(E)}\ 14}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="159" height="32" />.
</p>
