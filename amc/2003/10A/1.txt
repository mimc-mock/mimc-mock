<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_4">Solution 4</span></h2>

The sum of odd numbers is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/9/e/d9ea4b69b5de8a8129a67009cb273b12c16d59ed.png" class="latex" alt="$O=1+3+5+...+4005$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="205" height="14" />. When compared to the sum of odd numbers, we see that each of the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" />th term in the series of even numbers differ by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" />.
