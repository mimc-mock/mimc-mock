<p>Part of the graph of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/3/4/834d0850803426a3c40637713c80b5985a1a205f.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x) = ax^3 + bx^2 + cx + d$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="207" height="19" /> is shown. What is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />?
</p><p><a href="/wiki/index.php/File:2003_12B_AMC-20.png" class="image"><img alt="2003 12B AMC-20.png" src="https://wiki-images.artofproblemsolving.com//8/8e/2003_12B_AMC-20.png" width="147" height="161" /></a>
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/b/c/fbc0b505d5b755f2e171d6a7b53ecf9d89ccfede.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A)}\ -4 \qquad\mathrm{(B)}\ -2 \qquad\mathrm{(C)}\ 0 \qquad\mathrm{(D)}\ 2 \qquad\mathrm{(E)}\ 4$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="387" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h3>
<p>Since
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/6/b/86bdc06d99812833a76429475ddd32d620da7e1d.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} -f(-1) = a - b + c - d = 0 = f(1) = a + b + c + d \end{align*}" width="403" height="18" />
</p><p>It follows that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/8/9/d89f784f9f2a39b3605ab93f991192119994e3ad.png" class="latex" alt="$b + d = 0$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="72" height="14" />. Also, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/1/1/31139d7a4d51ea3096d40a646a7f2d59fcc1e49d.png" class="latex" alt="$d = f(0) = 2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="100" height="18" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/2/e/02e283e0930872e09f7f48b4f6b876e5fb674bb9.png" class="latex" alt="$b = -2 \Rightarrow \mathrm{(B)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="109" height="18" />.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h3>
<p>Two of the roots of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/c/57ccf8d123ba4259e29fba55d9b008874e071492.png" class="latex" alt="$f(x) = 0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="68" height="18" /> are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/f/7cfbd35086ffefb83637d42166582b43c8a1ff4a.png" class="latex" alt="$\pm 1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="22" height="12" />, and we let the third one be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" />. Then 
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/9/8/498460502d7b55f1151403b1cda71afbffe6e06d.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[a(x-1)(x+1)(x-n) = ax^3-anx^2-ax+an = ax^3 + bx^2 + cx + d = 0\]" width="507" height="19" />
Notice that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/7/a/97a304d413d59296c23d220fd018d6aa8df06abf.png" class="latex" alt="$f(0) = d = an = 2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="145" height="18" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/8/f/d8f6f363fe9e6b20e9b4fcda6a39b4275adcbb52.png" class="latex" alt="$b = -an = -2 \Rightarrow \mathrm{(B)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="168" height="18" />.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h3>
<p>Notice that if <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/8/1/68159ebfcc5bbf2be5b5df2e0a74a9a21a1d73f5.png" class="latex" alt="$g(x) = 2 - 2x^2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="115" height="19" />, then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/0/5/805f72d665bc73f35318fcd73a8c776e242f8076.png" class="latex" alt="$f - g$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="42" height="16" /> vanishes at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/c/c/3cc55afeea4667e0bd33893f74152cddbc75acff.png" class="latex" alt="$x = -1, 0, 1$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="91" height="16" /> and so
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/7/f/a7f8101b936e4ce7a95605d3d35a94edd409e454.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[f(x) - g(x) = ax(x-1)(x+1) = ax^3 - ax\]" width="340" height="19" />
implies by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/6/b/76b2878564ab19b80abb21ba964abe90fe120846.png" class="latex" alt="$x^2$" width="16" height="15" /> coefficient, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/3/8/e38e2567712dddf3dc0ab761bc7f797e49be0d63.png" class="latex" alt="$b + 2 = 0, b = -2 \Rightarrow \mathrm{(B)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="189" height="18" />.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_4">Solution 4</span></h3>
<p>The roots of this equation are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/0/2/202ae1a8013fde4392b0396515be3dd334d690b1.png" class="latex" alt="$-1, 1, \text{ and } x$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="96" height="15" />, letting <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> be the root not shown in the graph. By Vieta, we know that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/e/a/7eaa4f69e36dfbc102e7da4aa298514a14e5d3d0.png" class="latex" alt="$-1+1+x=x=-\frac{b}{a}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="172" height="37" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/9/3/69356b96cdc59c0a2563cdff8b0480036eb791ed.png" class="latex" alt="$-1\cdot 1\cdot x=-x=-\frac{d}{a}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="168" height="37" />. Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/c/e/1cecddc1ed78c8a6a20e6cec73083bc06ba14cef.png" class="latex" alt="$x=\frac{d}{a}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="46" height="37" />. Setting the two equations for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> equal to each other, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/0/3/f0310a4ff366c2d8208bbdbe0bc5e475b99dbb26.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{d}{a}=-\frac{b}{a}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="64" height="37" />. We know that the y-intercept of the polynomial is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/8/8/588e07a0cfc1e617ac5d6552a759b3011aa96d6c.png" class="latex" alt="$d=2$" width="42" height="12" />. Plugging in for <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/09298a15f54bcffa99d15656941da6c79f590587.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{2}{a}=-\frac{b}{a}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="64" height="37" />.
</p><p>Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/e/b/deb0322b5ca7560dc1e4c16eb391e1a887c35dc5.png" class="latex" alt="$b=-2 \Rightarrow \boxed{B}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="110" height="24" />
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_5">Solution 5</span></h3>
<p>From the graph, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/a/1/5a1a33e6d802d44c82b87de08fe38ecf73657f14.png" class="latex" alt="$f(0)=2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="67" height="18" /> so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/8/8/588e07a0cfc1e617ac5d6552a759b3011aa96d6c.png" class="latex" alt="$d=2$" width="42" height="12" />. Also from the graph, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/7/c/e7ca5dc29b26a89e50b36fe9b3df584579666472.png" class="latex" alt="$f(1)=a+b+c+2=0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="192" height="18" />. But we also have from the graph <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/3/a/53a82cac352f393a73c261ef1d021b53e514b3ea.png" class="latex" alt="$f(-1)=-a+b-c+2=0$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="220" height="18" />. Summing <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/4/a34bee821a0dd72805c9f516e4b3ef3f0a3601cb.png" class="latex" alt="$f(1)+f(2)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="88" height="18" /> we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/2/8c279253881c9b2413ebf1af0ddd4613d16d9c19.png" class="latex" alt="$2b+4=0$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="81" height="14" /> so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/2/e/02e283e0930872e09f7f48b4f6b876e5fb674bb9.png" class="latex" alt="$b = -2 \Rightarrow \mathrm{(B)}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="109" height="18" />.
</p><p>Solution by franzliszt
</p>
