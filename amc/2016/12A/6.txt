<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>We are trying to find the value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/9/fc97ef67268cd4e91bacdf12b8901d7036c9a056.png" class="latex" alt="$N$" width="16" height="12" /> such that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/a/01aa824a81edf8a95847fd9b5ebcbe2f59bbe118.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[1+2+3\cdots+(N-1)+N=\frac{N(N+1)}{2}=2016.\]" width="388" height="38" /> Noticing that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/d/0/5d07012ef3aa0df44bd782365e63f3937c87b54f.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{63\cdot 64}{2}=2016,$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="117" height="37" /> we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/5/6/256a688d9718ce4e96c34b9afe2fd016ff4cfa17.png" class="latex" alt="$N=63,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="63" height="16" /> so our answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/e/d/5ed405eae1eddf1928cc3e656910a140220de687.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D) } 9}.$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="59" height="28" />
</p><p>Notice that we were attempting to solve <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/3/b/13b248c23712c017b01d8a36061b8745aaea036f.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{N(N+1)}{2} = 2016 \Rightarrow N(N+1) = 2016\cdot2 = 4032$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="392" height="38" />. Approximating <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/3/c/93cf76f47a82a23b4294f3c62e1c63eaed8a33a2.png" class="latex" alt="$N(N+1) \approx N^2$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="125" height="19" />, we were looking for a perfect square that is close to, but less than, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/3/9/6395e1f759197dbcfc89b4990a2e34d913af689a.png" class="latex" alt="$4032$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/5/f/55f2f307fc58e9a6098c72deacd3210675e5a88c.png" class="latex" alt="$63^2 = 3969$" width="85" height="15" />, we see that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/3/d/a3d74b8525d04430ebaa21bcf4fef89e7331eba8.png" class="latex" alt="$N = 63$" width="58" height="12" /> is a likely candidate.  Multiplying <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/0/e/40e5991b1548b2bbf070bd71bce2fe696278ad14.png" class="latex" alt="$63\cdot64$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="49" height="13" /> confirms that our assumption is correct.
