<p>How many <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" />-digit positive integers (that is, integers between <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/e/6ee927e1332358c96c62c277441c907c4f51057f.png" class="latex" alt="$1000$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/5/0/450352c42d8f7a2b16abf3323453aa1ab13cbe0d.png" class="latex" alt="$9999$" width="35" height="12" />, inclusive) having only even digits are divisible by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/4/0/64048ae4dca5e9c398e5a419c1839f85b165558b.png" class="latex" alt="$5?$" width="17" height="12" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/7/c/07cb8ceef3c4faa4f3ea6e5b2487fb7d17a9657e.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) } 80 \qquad \textbf{(B) } 100 \qquad \textbf{(C) } 125 \qquad \textbf{(D) } 200 \qquad \textbf{(E) } 500$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="434" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>The units digit, for all numbers divisible by 5, must be either <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />. However, since all digits are even, the units digit must be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" />. The middle two digits can be 0, 2, 4, 6, or 8, but the thousands digit can only be 2, 4, 6, or 8 since it cannot be zero. There is one choice for the units digit, 5 choices for each of the middle 2 digits, and 4 choices for the thousands digit, so there is a total of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/6/5/4655358ca3700e183e0ed29eed8b27e32f684104.png" class="latex" alt="$4\cdot5\cdot5\cdot1 = \boxed{\textbf{(B) } 100} \qquad$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="173" height="28" /> numbers.
</p>
