They take turns playing, with Bela going first. At his first turn, Bela chooses any real number in the interval <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/5/8/a585433224883cbcd304f34129a152b97d5ed96e.png" class="latex" alt="$[0, n]$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="36" height="18" />.
