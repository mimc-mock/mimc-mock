<p>Suppose that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> is a finite set of positive integers. If the greatest integer in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> is removed from <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" />, then the average value (arithmetic mean) of the integers remaining is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/f/4/4f4b8590f8aa9f9894b3891dd5dede0e354fa04e.png" class="latex" alt="$32$" width="17" height="12" />. If the least integer in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> is also removed, then the average value of the integers remaining is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/7/8c7f6c79f2148f0cec592c9517b40ee1b82ef22e.png" class="latex" alt="$35$" width="17" height="12" />. If the greatest integer is then returned to the set, the average value of the integers rises to <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/8/7/78792fe4680a164f537ea72a4f900e4471433c3b.png" class="latex" alt="$40$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" />. The greatest integer in the original set <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/4/d/94db15b430439ad5b3c80bfacfef0c6499ede2ee.png" class="latex" alt="$72$" width="17" height="12" /> greater than the least integer in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" />. What is the average value of all the integers in the set <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" />?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/f/7/6f70efd93527dc889ea7428b3db70c8b6b67bdb3.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A) }36.2 \qquad \textbf{(B) }36.4 \qquad \textbf{(C) }36.6\qquad \textbf{(D) }36.8 \qquad \textbf{(E) }37$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="455" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Let the lowest value be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/5/9/859ccf4cd60c7bc6b8fa1afc9a42dc811a826d6f.png" class="latex" alt="$L$" width="12" height="12" /> and the highest <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/2/6e28ce12d49d39f160d5a0ef54077fc98e4b9d2b.png" class="latex" alt="$G$" width="14" height="12" />, and let the sum be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/e/a/3ead47fb9fb4a4c273feee398f72ff2a09702b84.png" class="latex" alt="$Z$" width="13" height="12" /> and the amount of numbers <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/4/174fadd07fd54c9afe288e96558c92e0c1da733a.png" class="latex" alt="$n$" width="10" height="8" />. We have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/3/9/739ec2fe12cbde0881a8d7424ef485069fa5c93b.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{Z-G}{n-1}=32$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="96" height="38" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/1/8/a189db6918996382ce55d3f0b03d9df95d67e2ed.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{Z-L-G}{n-2}=35$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="130" height="37" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/3/f/e3fa90fe452f83bf96d021c96a81eea4090d5487.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{Z-L}{n-1}=40$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="94" height="38" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/6/546f2047f5447edd5c12d0f18ccad0a6d85d003d.png" class="latex" alt="$G=L+72$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="90" height="14" />. Clearing denominators gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/f/5/9f564dd7b0237624a500b1135c22876d6135615b.png" class="latex" alt="$Z-G=32n-32$" width="143" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/3/ad3f374b8eb7587f7cde7ffa29ca79fb9b9be54b.png" class="latex" alt="$Z-L-G=35n-70$" width="178" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/c/adc23102b684ac7202cd9a00bfa92db67fc8593b.png" class="latex" alt="$Z-L=40n-40$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="141" height="13" />. We use <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/6/546f2047f5447edd5c12d0f18ccad0a6d85d003d.png" class="latex" alt="$G=L+72$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="90" height="14" /> to turn the first equation into <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/8/7/e87183d5c065168e1de231b1ab1a9b1098cf32c7.png" class="latex" alt="$Z-L=32n+40$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="141" height="14" />. Since <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/3/1/131b26ff9de68192808515699f927199ff9d227f.png" class="latex" alt="$Z-L=40(n-1)$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="149" height="20" /> we substitute it into the equation which gives <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/c/dcc1b21210db29332b1a7d9ce088fd457f23aeee.png" class="latex" alt="$n=10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="13" />. Turning the second into <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/8/6/f86869be0a880409057cdc2a223f58924264f109.png" class="latex" alt="$Z-2L=35n+2$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="141" height="14" /> using <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/4/6/546f2047f5447edd5c12d0f18ccad0a6d85d003d.png" class="latex" alt="$G=L+72$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="90" height="14" /> we see <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/6/1/b61fc4c58331cd3570e13aa7e0416fd6df5acc47.png" class="latex" alt="$L=8$" width="45" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/d/9/8d900775b7d7158079c1c1da3aa4baa7648e322d.png" class="latex" alt="$Z=368$" width="64" height="12" /> so the average is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/d/b/bdba479047f39eacc9ce67ca73d7e59a33a472b7.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{Z}{n}=\boxed{\textbf{(D) }36.8}$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="123" height="39" /> ~aop2014
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> be the greatest integer, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" /> be the smallest, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/3/b13f21416d84e13708696f34dea81026cda583c9.png" class="latex" alt="$z$" width="8" height="8" /> be the sum of the numbers in S excluding <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/6/e/26eeb5258ca5099acf8fe96b2a1049c48c89a5e6.png" class="latex" alt="$x$" width="10" height="8" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" /> be the number of elements in S.
</p><p>Then, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/a/a/aaa2eb7aa98178a74a0b205e11561667e59baf97.png" class="latex" alt="$S=x+y+z$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="109" height="16" />
</p><p>First, when the greatest integer is removed, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/c/7/4c790342658146aaf1a93abf16821c7d97ad582b.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{S-x}{k-1}=32$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="90" height="38" />
</p><p>When the smallest integer is also removed, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/4/0/b40b4b84247d2724862abe71b148648606a5cbe4.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{S-x-y}{k-2}=35$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="122" height="37" />
</p><p>When the greatest integer is added back, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/a/e/bae20812c8fe38afb0fb9c75842e3d510d4b2e96.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{S-y}{k-1}=40$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="89" height="38" />
</p><p>We are given that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/1/0/7100961f9f3c39b8a64793f0af32baa76be96fc9.png" class="latex" alt="$x=y+72$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="84" height="15" />
</p><p><br />
After you substitute <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/1/0/7100961f9f3c39b8a64793f0af32baa76be96fc9.png" class="latex" alt="$x=y+72$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="84" height="15" />, you have 3 equations with 3 unknowns <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/2/2f2e8e8dd78b26ddc9ac278cc179101d30644cae.png" class="latex" alt="$S,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="15" height="16" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/9/2/092e364e1d9d19ad5fffb0b46ef4cc7f2da02c1c.png" class="latex" alt="$y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="9" height="11" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/3/b/d3bc4fd393da616626868cd212417004b07848a8.png" class="latex" alt="$S-y-72=32k-32$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="176" height="16" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/4/8748c05dc156245d6f03952f1a207386deed434d.png" class="latex" alt="$S-2y-72=35k-70$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="185" height="16" />
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/f/1/5f1e636fbadf56fd3e0032130a431c39d5c98e89.png" class="latex" alt="$S-y=40k-40$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="136" height="16" />
</p><p>This can be easily solved to yield <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/a/f/3afd9b066c6c1fb6ef4383cb93bbabe6521c36d2.png" class="latex" alt="$k=10$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="52" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/a/4/8a4808fab877e290bdc0e68ce61cc8a852fd397b.png" class="latex" alt="$y=8$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="42" height="16" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/c/8/ec88bb150f9c2f6da0b68982ee1984b55409948f.png" class="latex" alt="$S=368$" width="63" height="12" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/6/3/a63588d48cc9015ddcfc1560cf86e98b33b51972.png" class="latex" alt="$\therefore$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="11" height="10" /> average value of all integers in the set <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/e/0/ee0ed7a840e6619394952f139a2b1e77eac66453.png" class="latex" alt="$=S/k = 368/10 = \boxed{\textbf{(D) }36.8}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="234" height="30" />
</p><p>~ SoySoy4444

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_3">Solution 3</span></h2>
<p>We should plug in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/f/3/cf347491b110306078dd4dd2cb37c64f3764ab17.png" class="latex" alt="$36.2$" width="31" height="12" /> and assume everything is true except the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/7/8c7f6c79f2148f0cec592c9517b40ee1b82ef22e.png" class="latex" alt="$35$" width="17" height="12" /> part. We then calculate that part and end up with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/e/a/aea67138856a9e932a93e69ec7586fa5b6bfed62.png" class="latex" alt="$35.75$" width="40" height="12" />. We also see with the formulas we used with the plug in that when you increase by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/a/2/6a270a7be617f39b92e4821298c4555cffe496c0.png" class="latex" alt="$0.2$" width="22" height="12" /> the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/e/a/aea67138856a9e932a93e69ec7586fa5b6bfed62.png" class="latex" alt="$35.75$" width="40" height="12" /> part decreases by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/f/6/df6ee426e2e724a4e7255dd71bdd7dbd6c75ab04.png" class="latex" alt="$0.25$" width="31" height="12" />. The answer is then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/8/7/c874f8f03a77c10f663b3e2ebbc303d944507a30.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D) }36.8}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="81" height="30" />. You can work backwards because it is multiple choice and you don't have to do critical thinking. ~Lopkiloinm

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_4">Solution 4</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/7/9/179da0ec9ea4d36881248f7f1380dacc78fc91c2.png" class="latex" alt="$S = \{a_1, a_2, a_3, \hdots, a_n\}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="180" height="18" /> with <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/9/9/59948e6975ac1352f9b31405e6a3c74319803884.png" class="latex" alt="$a_1 &lt; a_2 &lt; a_3 &lt; \hdots &lt; a_n.$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="191" height="12" /> We are given the following: <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/e/c/aecd91e93199e4289661420c0a40e01ad4e4587b.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[{\begin{cases} \sum_{i=1}^{n-1} a_i = 32(n-1) = 32n-32, \\ \sum_{i=2}^n a_i = 40(n-1) = 40n-40, \\ \sum_{i=2}^{n-1} a_i = 35(n-2) = 35n-70, \\ a_n-a_1 = 72 \implies a_1 + 72 = a_n. \end{cases}}\]" width="272" height="96" /> Subtracting the third equation from the sum of the first two, we find that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/3/8/9385e377c1d9466ec465f5d7af6c38e350b5ed64.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\sum_{i=1}^n a_i = \left(32n-32\right) + \left(40n-40\right) - \left(35n-70\right) = 37n - 2.\]" width="451" height="47" /> Furthermore, from the fourth equation, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/a/1/da1d47f0e7fcf8936f7f9dafdd35b3c433092df5.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\sum_{i=2}^{n} a_i - \sum_{i=1}^{n-1} a_i = \left[\left(a_1 + 72\right) + \sum_{i=2}^{n-1} a_i\right] - \left[\left(a_1\right) + \sum_{i=2}^{n-1} a_i\right] = \left(40n-40\right)-\left(32n-32\right).\]" width="628" height="53" /> Combining like terms and simplifying, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/f/0/df050ae567b006f1bcec0e63ca273e30f0e3ba55.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[72 = 8n-8 \implies 8n = 80 \implies n=10.\]" width="311" height="13" /> Thus, the sum of the elements in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/5/e/95e710db2a2946b781265c3893fa721111ee7b80.png" class="latex" alt="$37 \cdot 10 - 2 = 368,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="136" height="16" /> and since there are 10 elements in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/f/2/2f2e8e8dd78b26ddc9ac278cc179101d30644cae.png" class="latex" alt="$S,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="15" height="16" /> the average of the elements in <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/2/ad28c83c99a8fd0dd2e2e594c9d02ee532765a0a.png" class="latex" alt="$S$" width="12" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/6/6c62f3f2645836939d98cc364962558443a3d553.png" class="latex" alt="$\tfrac{368}{10}=\boxed{\textbf{(D) }36.8}$" style="vertical-align: -11px" width="129" height="30" />
</p><p>~peace09
