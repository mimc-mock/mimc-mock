The scores of the players are as follows: Ravon--<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/4/8/f480123fdee29c93742d132dcdab8439a72e45d6.png" class="latex" alt="$11,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="22" height="15" /> Oscar--<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/0/5/4054552638cf9f29e857a1306bd3ec31df3c19b6.png" class="latex" alt="$4,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="15" /> Aditi--<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/4/43420adcd0fce9dce3978d53b97a8e0262b58da4.png" class="latex" alt="$7,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="13" height="15" /> Tyrone--<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/4/b/e4bb513e21508306b77c013aae74e9c4b165e9fc.png" class="latex" alt="$16,$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="22" height="16" /> Kim--<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/5/d/55d9112910335dfe813b84b8d9db66a08dcf5a2f.png" class="latex" alt="$17.$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="21" height="12" /> Which of the following statements is true?

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>By logical deduction, we consider the scores from lowest to highest:
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/c/d/5cd0a4bf14e244fc13e2e25f59cecc341553196f.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\begin{align*} \text{Oscar&#39;s score is 4.} &amp;\implies \text{Oscar is given cards 1 and 3.} \\ &amp;\implies \text{Aditi is given cards 2 and 5.} \\ &amp;\implies \text{Ravon is given cards 4 and 7.} &amp;&amp; (\bigstar) \\ &amp;\implies \text{Tyrone is given cards 6 and 10.} \\ &amp;\implies \text{Kim is given cards 8 and 9.} \end{align*}" width="453" height="114" />
Therefore, the answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/1/c/c1c8108bd8a0f627fb7304e8a2c4b3990b8265c9.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) }\text{Ravon was given card 4.}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="223" height="28" />
</p><p>Certainly, if we read the answer choices sooner, then we can stop at <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/5/875816dba06e32edf79fcfbb793af13f73bdca9c.png" class="latex" alt="$(\bigstar)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="29" height="18" /> and pick <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/0/7/f07759fa176d8edab201426b075c105d704ff275.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(C)}.$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="32" height="17" />
</p><p>~smarty101 ~smartypantsno_3 ~SmileKat32 ~MRENTHUSIASM
