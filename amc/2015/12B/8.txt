<p>What is the value of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/d/e/3de42ac9f00a49db557f56672d137491ae9f3bfc.png" class="latex" alt="$(625^{\log_5 2015})^{\frac{1}{4}}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="102" height="22" />&#160;?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/2/9123c6861615bbff4b77a8e0d9774474701447a6.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\; 5 \qquad\textbf{(B)}\; \sqrt[4]{2015} \qquad\textbf{(C)}\; 625 \qquad\textbf{(D)}\; 2015 \qquad\textbf{(E)}\; \sqrt[4]{5^{2015}}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="478" height="21" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/3/7/7378d9a73365e327087bec6042d3f9913d7bcf35.png" class="latex" alt="$(625^{\log_5 2015})^\frac{1}{4} = ((5^4)^{\log_5 2015})^\frac{1}{4} = (5^{4 \cdot \log_5 2015})^\frac{1}{4} = (5^{\log_5 2015 \cdot 4})^\frac{1}{4} = ((5^{\log_5 2015})^4)^\frac{1}{4} = (2015^4)^\frac{1}{4} = \boxed{\textbf{(D)}\; 2015}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="813" height="28" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>We can rewrite <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/a/f/0af98ec58f2842c8c63286efedff7a660f09ac8b.png" class="latex" alt="$\log_5 2015$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="69" height="17" /> as as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/d/81dac0f07d9f38db1fc714ad3113c0840dafda4a.png" class="latex" alt="$5^x = 2015$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="77" height="13" />. Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/f/2/0f2c9fc4fa447a202904998b67951c2c01506905.png" class="latex" alt="$625^{x \cdot \frac{1}{4}} = 5^x = \boxed{2015}.$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="166" height="23" />
</p>
