</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/6/a/d6a7859473b5a9a2876881ba52f6c5df99257def.png" class="latex" alt="$\angle ZAC=\angle OAY=90^{\circ}$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="186" height="15" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/0/570b6c52ce38164f1bd9790fb104e2b2b809c5f9.png" class="latex" alt="$\angle ZAC+\angle BAC=\angle OAY+\angle BAC$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="291" height="16" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/c/3/ec38645a1ccd163342a150577a230a355a019401.png" class="latex" alt="$\angle ZAB=\angle CAY$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="136" height="14" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/4/5/845b843d039b3ed0cdd7560e0d6b31b9a6a10ddd.png" class="latex" alt="$AZ=AC$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="81" height="14" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/6/b/86b6fc482a7f05d25af1ec88b4039c4af2f432d0.png" class="latex" alt="$AB=AY$" width="80" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/6/0/a603a9d17340ba61269d46b1005b9e6a8ef9e088.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle AZB \cong \triangle ACY$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="143" height="18" /> by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/d/8/4d8879abf0d7fbb06bc388aa9ab096a5c37c9bb8.png" class="latex" alt="$SAS$" width="37" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/1/9/a193d2215b5a33b8cb782a6512d25033404d2867.png" class="latex" alt="$BZ=YC$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="83" height="14" />. Because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/9/7/a973843efc3fd9c387ac614968d2cee18ba80553.png" class="latex" alt="$AZ \perp AC$" width="79" height="13" />,  <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/b/34bf6aa6151eba7ce116beb631d68283084f2b2b.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle ACY$" width="58" height="13" /> is a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/a/7/6a7dfa8a2a90d521b2b03f5b82a1fb9f85dce88c.png" class="latex" alt="$90^{\circ}$" width="24" height="13" /> rotation about point <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/9/019e9892786e493964e145e7c5cf7b700314e53b.png" class="latex" alt="$A$" width="13" height="13" /> of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/9/6/2960ebcade6f4c0f0052c17e250496a78008e866.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle AZB$" width="57" height="13" />. So, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/8/7/28739f84bab973d95f6cd642d30d2e403d5eb37a.png" class="latex" alt="$BZ \perp YC$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="83" height="14" />.
</p><p>Because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/0/cb02062cd93f5f57ecbd505b6a1cab416ecd67b6.png" class="latex" alt="$OZ$" width="28" height="12" /> and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/a/f/8afe773cd5709ee6b1f9f84680829802825cfab9.png" class="latex" alt="$OY$" width="28" height="12" /> is the radius of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/4/c/d4c748db2ab4a67c0aa0dace8827ba7cbc3a48aa.png" class="latex" alt="$\odot O$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="27" height="14" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/2/e/72e422aa0f67ed331ce52391d8f0778f5e09f069.png" class="latex" alt="$OZ=OY$" width="80" height="12" />. Because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/d/51da37d984564162c87710ca27bea422f657fb73.png" class="latex" alt="$O$" width="13" height="12" /> is the midpoint of hypotenuse <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/e/57ee5125358c0606c9b588580ddfa66f83e607b7.png" class="latex" alt="$AB$" width="27" height="13" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/1/f/91f63d35ffa438da6f29a72e305aed23482eea05.png" class="latex" alt="$OB=OC$" width="81" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/d/5/8d5574489788ffea01304724e34a1f46056e66c6.png" class="latex" alt="$BZ=CY$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="83" height="14" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/d/d/bdd0887a6f450b93b81f4a97bbd3bd8d02806f52.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle OBZ \cong \triangle OCY$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="144" height="18" /> by <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/2/e/22e28d9bde02e365b67760c06b05e9003ca002c0.png" class="latex" alt="$SSS$" width="36" height="12" />. Because <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/1/a/61a780fa8612225cb0e6f57ee782ba96c3c38abc.png" class="latex" alt="$BZ \perp CY$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="83" height="14" />,  <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/e/5/9e5e5f8e31c437a3c6fd5dbfb5738fab13c8d140.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle OCY$" width="59" height="13" /> is a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/a/7/6a7dfa8a2a90d521b2b03f5b82a1fb9f85dce88c.png" class="latex" alt="$90^{\circ}$" width="24" height="13" /> rotation about point <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/1/d/51da37d984564162c87710ca27bea422f657fb73.png" class="latex" alt="$O$" width="13" height="12" /> of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/7/417f87175b283ea33aa358fd1418c7bdd5d121fd.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle OBZ$" width="58" height="13" />. So, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/1/5/0156dc52a79c5093dc8b3320227043b00eaf5a84.png" class="latex" alt="$OB \perp OC$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="84" height="14" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/d/6/dd66039b552c2055742ff221ec5d1304ef0c48f5.png" class="latex" alt="$\angle COB = 90^{\circ}$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="108" height="15" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/e/2/8e204682dc7fda2e1de6a2b73c88631064deaca6.png" class="latex" alt="$OC=OB$" width="81" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/f/3/ef30d35e81e31da464c6e8238de6a0ba6fe54c50.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle OCB$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="61" height="18" /> is isosceles right triangle, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/2/c/92cb0db393d17eff46da7f7923860d4050515704.png" class="latex" alt="$\angle ABC=\angle OBC=45^{\circ}$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="188" height="15" />. So, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c3a2d2224f7d163b46d702132425d47828bf538.png" class="latex" alt="$\triangle ABC$" width="58" height="13" /> is isosceles right triangle.
</p><p>Therefore, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/c/a/6ca45cd9db25683832e86770991bb6cdb4a652ce.png" class="latex" alt="$AC=BC=\dfrac{12}{\sqrt{2}}=6\sqrt{2}$" style="vertical-align: -17px" width="191" height="41" />, the perimeter is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/3/a/43ad0b01c3187c3af7c440d8db54f03f85470474.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 12+12\sqrt{2}}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="128" height="33" />.
</p><p>~<a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://artofproblemsolving.com/wiki/index.php/User:Isabelchen">isabelchen</a>
