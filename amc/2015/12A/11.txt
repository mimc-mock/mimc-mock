<p>On a sheet of paper, Isabella draws a circle of radius <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />, a circle of radius <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" />, and all possible lines simultaneously tangent to both circles. Isabella notices that she has drawn exactly <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/7/cb745f62ddf5625f47a60b8976c60faebcf74f43.png" class="latex" alt="$k \ge  0$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="43" height="15" /> lines. How many different values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" /> are possible?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/9/e/a9e5d67e14004975196e98e028a195857a2b8881.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 2 \qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 3 \qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 4 \qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 5\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 6$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="348" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Isabella can get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" /> lines if the circles are concentric, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> if internally tangent, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> if overlapping, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/c/d/7cde695f2e4542fd01f860a89189f47a27143b66.png" class="latex" alt="$3$" width="8" height="12" /> if externally tangent, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/c/c7cab1a05e1e0c1d51a6a219d96577a16b7abf9d.png" class="latex" alt="$4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="9" height="12" /> if non-overlapping and not externally tangent. There are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/a/c7a42524741cb8781a00318850f8d3183f191ac7.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(D)}\ 5}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="55" height="28" /> values of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/c/3/8c325612684d41304b9751c175df7bcc0f61f64f.png" class="latex" alt="$k$" width="9" height="12" />.
</p>
