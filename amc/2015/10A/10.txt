<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>The first thing one would want to do is place a possible letter that works and then stem off of it.  For example, if we start with an <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" />, we can only place a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" /> next to it.  Unfortunately, after that step, we can't do too much, since:
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/7/b/97bbd2f79277568625f5969ae4fee4c0e94260c6.png" class="latex" alt="$acbd$" width="35" height="12" /> is not allowed because of the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/f/8/df89f73c5de42c1c12b2792feb1bb38b836ce405.png" class="latex" alt="$cb$" width="16" height="12" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/4/1/c41e3abba19d247984a92930c074def7f27c927a.png" class="latex" alt="$acdb$" width="35" height="12" /> is not allowed because of the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/6/e/f6e1a0789128066a6b03855158dafabb8d84f63b.png" class="latex" alt="$cd$" width="17" height="12" />. 
</p><p>We get the same problem if we start with a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" />, since a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" /> will have to end up in the middle, causing it to be adjacent to an <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" />.
</p><p>If we start with a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/3/8136a7ef6a03334a7246df9097e5bcc31ba33fd2.png" class="latex" alt="$b$" width="8" height="12" />, the next letter would have to be a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/6/a/96ab646de7704969b91c76a214126b45f2b07b25.png" class="latex" alt="$d$" width="9" height="12" />, and since we can put an <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/d/c7d457e388298246adb06c587bccd419ea67f7e8.png" class="latex" alt="$a$" width="9" height="8" /> next to it and then a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" /> after that, this configuration works.  The same approach applies if we start with a <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/3/7/3372c1cb6d68cf97c2d231acc0b47b95a9ed04cc.png" class="latex" alt="$c$" width="8" height="8" />.
