<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Because the two points are on a diameter, the center must be halfway between them at the point (4,3). The distance from (0,0) to (4,3) is 5 so the circle has radius 5. Thus, the equation of the circle is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/f/a/ffa79d0332c30c40c49c351d0e10ebf961a1a7a6.png" class="latex" alt="$(x-4)^2+(y-3)^2=25$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="189" height="19" />. 
</p><p>To find the x-intercept, y must be 0, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/f/6/df6623767aed4e6a2cd402f14a43cb602a5738bb.png" class="latex" alt="$(x-4)^2+(0-3)^2=25$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="189" height="19" />, so <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/e/f/6efcf1c23d87566285606b771ac830d509bfd764.png" class="latex" alt="$(x-4)^2=16$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="105" height="19" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/0/cb035cec3c9df265242091700b0a6e326fb2f894.png" class="latex" alt="$x-4=4$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="75" height="12" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/9/0/b/90b09bb8c206d2394301e2cc5ee2c533b4c71b92.png" class="latex" alt="$x=8, \boxed{D}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="79" height="24" />.
