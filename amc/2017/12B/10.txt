<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>WLOG, let there be <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/5/9/e59e2c6e83eb78cca610a5fd4070ae01c8d4ae60.png" class="latex" alt="$100$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="26" height="13" /> students. <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/0/2/302e99c1c7cd91a6ae371139e5142ccef5977dca.png" class="latex" alt="$60$" width="17" height="12" /> of them like dancing, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/8/7/78792fe4680a164f537ea72a4f900e4471433c3b.png" class="latex" alt="$40$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="13" /> do not. Of those who like dancing, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/1/8/1183a12017e48198b3352789cc39b223b17945d6.png" class="latex" alt="$20\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/d/f/edf074831eb5bc9e61d6d6e09f525a86e3068f6a.png" class="latex" alt="$12$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="17" height="12" /> of them say they dislike dancing. Of those who dislike dancing, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/7/0/870303feff6f491471061a7402eba3e326ba84f2.png" class="latex" alt="$90\%$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="32" height="14" />, or <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/e/5/ce5d3cbb9ab0a992ed503c2d4499e4f4af2f9d65.png" class="latex" alt="$36$" width="17" height="12" /> of them say they dislike it. Thus, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/8/3285fc87dec0cfd734d0a95633ca14a4d9ec7034.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{12}{12+36} = \frac{12}{48} = \frac{1}{4} = 25\% \boxed{\textbf{(D)}}$" style="vertical-align: -14px" width="245" height="38" />
</p>
