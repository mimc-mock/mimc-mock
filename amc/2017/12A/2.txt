<p>The sum of two nonzero real numbers is 4 times their product. What is the sum of the reciprocals of the two numbers?
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/5/7/1/571c1026e49506d70e6e271bdb9920253b289b54.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 1\qquad\textbf{(B)}\ 2\qquad\textbf{(C)}\ 4\qquad\textbf{(D)}\ 8\qquad\textbf{(E)}\ 12$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="357" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/2/5/32581661cddf01ad30c1e36efe72f99eb0a0adca.png" class="latex" alt="$x, y$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="27" height="11" /> be our two numbers. Then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/2/9/1/291e4f819fc3a5992cd76aecd7b326a9552861a0.png" class="latex" alt="$x+y = 4xy$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="95" height="15" />. Thus, 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/9/bc91cce43523068dfd1acbe77fe825fa70a274ea.png" class="latex" alt="$\frac{1}{x} + \frac{1}{y} = \frac{x+y}{xy} = 4$" style="vertical-align: -16px" width="154" height="40" />. 
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/9/9/f99012341ff701c012589a6972d9c4f8e866285d.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{ \textbf{C}}$" style="vertical-align: -5px" width="25" height="23" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>We can let <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/1/1/811204e3412eaf90240c65f8327e1815d2a0cc80.png" class="latex" alt="$x=y.$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="47" height="11" /> Then, we have that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/7/2/c7296ba1cce3c43f17bdd21e696e73473838e998.png" class="latex" alt="$2x=4x^2$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="72" height="17" /> making <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/5/7/457915e9eda7e372c41e2152cd2465f21a0e97c4.png" class="latex" alt="$x=\tfrac{1}{2}.$" style="vertical-align: -7px" width="52" height="23" /> The answer is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/1/3/b13f7020ccbf4fca0a3beb9346d90353a05a635a.png" class="latex" alt="$\dfrac{2}{x}=4=\boxed{ \textbf{C}}.$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="104" height="38" /> ~ <a href="/wiki/index.php/User:Solasky" title="User:Solasky">Solasky</a> (<a href="/wiki/index.php?title=User_talk:Solasky&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="User talk:Solasky (page does not exist)">talk</a>)
</p>
