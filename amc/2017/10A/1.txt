<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p>Notice this is the term <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/4/a/34ace040475607683853fcfd4960a8f7fa6678c7.png" class="latex" alt="$a_6$" style="vertical-align: -2px" width="15" height="10" /> in a recursive sequence, defined recursively as <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/b/3/cb38b691689e24599957cab7e87c28bb71f8dce5.png" class="latex" alt="$a_1 = 3, a_n = 2a_{n-1} + 1.$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="181" height="16" /> Thus:
<img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/5/d/b5d5c0648d386af95131a5512af608dc33d8e429.png" class="latexcenter" alt="\[\begin{split} a_2 = 3 \cdot 2 + 1 = 7.\\ a_3 = 7 \cdot 2 + 1 = 15.\\ a_4 = 15 \cdot 2 + 1 = 31.\\ a_5 = 31 \cdot 2 + 1 = 63.\\ a_6 = 63 \cdot 2 + 1 = \boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 127} \end{split}\]" width="210" height="127" />

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Starting to compute the inner expressions, we see the results are <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/a/7/7a711b005719fb0af8c0e5ad7a3346d3ca46ceb5.png" class="latex" alt="$1, 3, 7, 15, \ldots$" style="vertical-align: -3px" width="97" height="16" />. This is always <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/c/e/dce34f4dfb2406144304ad0d6106c5382ddd1446.png" class="latex" alt="$1$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="8" height="12" /> less than a power of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />. The only admissible answer choice by this rule is thus <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/4/a/74a03f5777e45771a80acad785593ba85298cba7.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 127}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="73" height="28" />.

The largest power of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" /> in the series is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/0/5/e/05e228ac5bee7b9ffb60cacf50f3f2c7c31f8038.png" class="latex" alt="$64$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="18" height="13" />, so the sum is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/4/a/74a03f5777e45771a80acad785593ba85298cba7.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C)}\ 127}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="73" height="28" />.
