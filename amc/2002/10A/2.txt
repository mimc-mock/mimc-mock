<p>Given that a, b, and c are non-zero real numbers, define <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/f/f/6ff06fed70fd5b2f7f88c1e62e631f5707659fd7.png" class="latex" alt="$(a, b, c) = \frac{a}{b} + \frac{b}{c} + \frac{c}{a}$" style="vertical-align: -12px" width="161" height="37" />, find <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/e/b/3ebc7325af4f1831e00aa2c54dea23ba6084c8f6.png" class="latex" alt="$(2, 12, 9)$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="65" height="18" />.
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/c/c/e/ccefe446ac26b1d2ff26fd59aaaea8ce281b5a83.png" class="latex" alt="$\textbf{(A)}\ 4 \qquad \textbf{(B)}\ 5 \qquad \textbf{(C)}\ 6 \qquad \textbf{(D)}\ 7 \qquad \textbf{(E)}\ 8$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="348" height="17" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solutions">Solutions</span></h2>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_1">Solution 1</span></h2>
<p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/9/2/3923a8b8b7fc17a1c39e7603ce84b0dd905840c2.png" class="latex" alt="$(2, 12, 9)=\frac{2}{12}+\frac{12}{9}+\frac{9}{2}=\frac{1}{6}+\frac{4}{3}+\frac{9}{2}=\frac{1}{6}+\frac{8}{6}+\frac{27}{6}=\frac{36}{6}=6$" style="vertical-align: -13px" width="497" height="38" />. Our answer is then <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/9/4/394e31b6e19ba6e9c14f340669915c86d068a16a.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) }6}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="55" height="28" />.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution_2">Solution 2</span></h2>
<p>Without computing the answer exactly, we see that <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/0/f/b0fcfc243f87bf536da606161ebd84bd6bd4bd59.png" class="latex" alt="$2/12=\text{a little}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="108" height="18" />, <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/5/6/7561279759aa9123f89e2cc01eae00fe9bf65dbd.png" class="latex" alt="$12/9=\text{more than }1$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="149" height="18" />, and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/d/2/4d2a339e63348244e06c466735dea80d91cdffca.png" class="latex" alt="$9/2=4.5$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="74" height="18" />.
The sum is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/e/f/6/ef684f341e03d8868d567c5b98fa8810d1a697b5.png" class="latex" alt="$4.5 + (\text{more than }1) + (\text{a little}) = (\text{more than }5.5) + (\text{a little})$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="458" height="18" />, and as all the options are integers, the correct one is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/9/4/394e31b6e19ba6e9c14f340669915c86d068a16a.png" class="latex" alt="$\boxed{\textbf{(C) }6}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="55" height="28" />.
</p>
