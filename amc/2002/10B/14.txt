<p>The number <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/e/9/4e9de7501af1f289ba226fca90b6d1194af77518.png" class="latex" alt="$25^{64}\cdot 64^{25}$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="76" height="15" /> is the square of a positive integer <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/9/fc97ef67268cd4e91bacdf12b8901d7036c9a056.png" class="latex" alt="$N$" width="16" height="12" />. In decimal representation, the sum of the digits of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/9/fc97ef67268cd4e91bacdf12b8901d7036c9a056.png" class="latex" alt="$N$" width="16" height="12" /> is
</p><p><img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/1/2/9/129c58dc0f5511a544b9285d6e9ca3c8885ac11a.png" class="latex" alt="$\mathrm{(A) \ } 7\qquad \mathrm{(B) \ } 14\qquad \mathrm{(C) \ } 21\qquad \mathrm{(D) \ } 28\qquad \mathrm{(E) \ } 35$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="378" height="18" />
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Solution">Solution</span></h2>
<p>Taking the root, we get <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/d/0/a/d0abc68766718b29c4adecacd99ea0f3f5fde835.png" class="latex" alt="$N=\sqrt{25^{64}\cdot 64^{25}}=5^{64}\cdot 8^{25}$" style="vertical-align: -1px" width="215" height="18" />.
</p><p>Now, we have <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/8/2/6/826e40915d01ffa89d3d042d7156f68d1988cdec.png" class="latex" alt="$N=5^{64}\cdot 8^{25}=5^{64}\cdot (2^{3})^{25}=5^{64}\cdot 2^{75}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="287" height="19" />.
</p><p>Combining the <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/4/1/c/41c544263a265ff15498ee45f7392c5f86c6d151.png" class="latex" alt="$2$" width="8" height="12" />'s and <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/7/9/0/79069377f91364c2f87a64e5f9f562a091c8a6c1.png" class="latex" alt="$5$" width="8" height="12" />'s gives us <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/3/5/8/358cbc9777547b31f9e0a8458d47d833666462fd.png" class="latex" alt="$(2\cdot 5)^{64}\cdot 2^{(75-64)}=(2\cdot 5)^{64}\cdot 2^{11}=10^{64}\cdot 2^{11}$" style="vertical-align: -4px" width="340" height="20" />. 
</p><p>This is the number <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/a/d/e/ade582b767f93cb8ade6938238a0d0e79ec5c62f.png" class="latex" alt="$2048$" style="vertical-align: 0px" width="35" height="13" /> with a string of sixty-four <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/b/c/1/bc1f9d9bf8a1b606a4188b5ce9a2af1809e27a89.png" class="latex" alt="$0$" width="8" height="12" />'s at the end. Thus, the sum of the digits of <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/f/c/9/fc97ef67268cd4e91bacdf12b8901d7036c9a056.png" class="latex" alt="$N$" width="16" height="12" /> is <img src="//latex.artofproblemsolving.com/6/5/4/654103fb6320bd26428cc4e254fe7eae588b38eb.png" class="latex" alt="$2+4+8=14\Longrightarrow\boxed{\mathrm{ (B)}\ 14}$" style="vertical-align: -10px" width="216" height="29" />
</p>
